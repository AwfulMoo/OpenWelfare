MT items payable to service providers

Process MT items not listed in the MBS or DVA Allied Health Fee Schedules but payable to service providers

This table describes how to process items not listed in the MBS or DVA Allied Health Fee Schedules, but payable to service providers.

Note:

  • when the service is not itemised as an MT item it must be rejected with RSN code S 578 'Item number cannot be determined from information supplied'
  • when 3-DVA ITM? is displayed, check item rules in table below. Use PI18 if MT item is payable according the business rules

    MT

    item number

    Fee type and processing instructions

    Requires Prior Financial Authorisation (PARS Approval)

    MT02, MT04, MT06

    Non-MBS (Low Cost); Non-MBS (Medium Cost); Non-MBS (High Cost)

    Item numbers used for when a DVA approved Medical service is not covered by an MBS item number.

    Requires Prior Financial Authorisation.

    • check PARS for an approval
      • when Prior Financial Authorisation is:
        • present - pay service with PI 18
        • not present - reject service with RSN code S 279
    • not covered by the current approval - reject service with RSN code S 283

    Fees must only be paid up to the amount that is approved in the prior financial authorisation. Where services are claimed above the fee approved by DVA, enter RSN code 288 and pay approved amount. Services claimed below the approved fee are paid as claimed.

    Payment of assistant surgeon fee in relation to MT06 approved amount

    • Check history for MT06 amount on same date of service
    • Follow the standard business rules for item 51303
    • At PEND stage, enter 'P158'
    • In the FEE field, enter the approved amount for item MT06
    • The system will automatically calculate 20% of the fee
    • Pay the service

    MT16

    Photodynamic Therapy

    Equivalent to MBS item 30196

    Prior Financial Authorisation is not required.

    Services are paid for item MT16 for the amount that is claimed by the provider, up to an amount of $8,000.

    MT item maximum fee amount

    • MT16 - $8,000. DVA approval is required for claims above $8,000 – refer claim to VAP helpdesk.

    Restrictions:

    Where a claim is received for both an MT16 and 30196 on the same date of service, check if the 2 treatments are different. For example, where one treatment is to remove a skin cancer (30196) and the other is to provide treatment (PDT) to a different skin cancer.

    Are the claims different?

    • Yes, pay with 'VASI' and PI 42
    • No, reject with RSN 159

    If separate areas are indicated for treatments of skin cancer (PDT), the restriction can be overridden.

    Check QITI for White Card eligibility.

    No up to $8000

    MT20

    Non Eligible MRI Machines

    Equivalent to MBS items 63001 – 63561

    Prior Financial Authorisation is not required.

    Services are paid for item MT20 for the amount that is claimed by the provider, equivalent to the MBS MRI item supplied, up to an amount of $690.

    Rules:

    • the relevant MBS item number (63001 - 63561) must be noted. Where the MBS equivalent has not been supplied, the service should be rejected using RSN 746
    • the amount charged must be checked against the MBS fee for the equivalent item and the fee paid according to the full Medicare schedule fee. When the fee quoted is not equivalent to the MBS fee for:
    • manual claims lower than MBS fee - the fee can be changed to the MBS equivalent fee. Service Officers must apply RSN 288 - Fee paid in accordance with departmental agreed rates
    • manual claims greater than MBS fee - reject with reason code 504 - Charge keyed is incorrect or missing
    • online claims - staff must reject with reason code 504 - Charge keyed is incorrect or missing
    • providers must have specialty code C01 118 or C01 218 to claim. Providers are eligible when they have code C01 047 or C01 046. Key VPUI,provider number (do not include alpha) to check specialty codes:
    • when error 4-NOT SPEC is returned, check VPQI,provider number (do not include alpha) to verify provider’s other locations and check provider’s specialty codes
    • does the service provider’s other locations have the specialty codes C01 046, C01 047, or C01 118 present and open for the date of service being claimed?
    • Yes - send a provider update request to the VAP Policy Helpdesk to have the provider location updated to include the specialty code added with the effective date no earlier than 1 September 2012. The Resources page contains a link to contact details for Veterans' Affairs Processing (VAP)
    • No - when none of the stated specialty codes is present or open at the date of service, reject the item with RSN code S141

    No

    MT50

    Non-MBS Pathology

    This MT item is payable only to Approved Pathology Practitioners (APPs) for services not listed/described in the MBS.

    APPs must claim for listed services via MBS Item numbers where applicable. MT items are payable only for APPs when the service is not covered by the MBS.

    Prior Financial Authorisation is not required.

    Services are paid for item MT50 for the amount that is claimed by the provider, up to an amount of $150.00.

    Rules:

    • claims must be made by an APP working at a recognised Approved Pathology Authority (APA), upon referral from a General Practitioner (GP), Local Medical Officer (LMO) or recognised specialist for pathology services
    • APPs must have specialty code C02 021 to claim. Key VPUI,provider number (do not include alpha) to check specialty codes
    • APPs must supply a clear description of the test performed. The Resources page contains a link to MT50 pathology test abbreviations. When the service is not clearly described, reject the item with RSN code S 746
    • both White and Gold card holders are eligible for this item. The date of service must be equal to or greater than 1 February 2013
    • this item is used to cover all types of pathology tests not listed in the MBS as long as a clear and accurate description of the test is supplied on the invoice. The charge is limited to the corresponding MBS fee
    • APPs must claim for listed services via MBS item numbers where applicable. Where a valid MBS item number is notated on the claim and the item is payable under normal MBS assessing rules, process the claim using that item number rather than as an MT50.

    No
