For Services Australia social workers only.

This page contains information about Social Work Information System (SWIS) activities.

Non-casework fields for SWIS

This table outlines the non-casework fields in the SWIS.

Item

Description

1

Office activity + Read more ...

To view or add office activities (commonly referred to as non-casework activities):

  • Launch workflow
  • Expand 'Social Work Information System (SWIS)' in the left hand Menu Bar
  • Select 'Office Activity (SWIS)'

This will open the Office Activity main screen.

2

Activity type + Read more ...

This field displays the type of activity undertaken. The available categories and their definitions are:

Community Outreach/Networking

  • Participation in local interagency meetings and forums
  • Visits to a community or local/state/territory government agency to promote Services Australia services/initiatives
  • Developing/maintaining relationships with a network of agencies in the community

Community Partnerships

  • Work in developing/maintaining partnerships with community, non-government or government agencies at local, area or national level

Income Security Policy Development and Feedback

  • Social workers providing feedback about various aspects of the impact income security policy has on Services Australia customers 'up the line' through agency channels
  • Participation in developing income security policy

Management Activities

  • Participation in a range of management activities at Centrelink Service Centre, Smart Centre Call, Area or National level, including quality checking activities related to the Social Work Quality Assurance Framework and Quality Call Listening

Training/Professional Development

  • Providing and/or receiving training, professional development to social work and non-social work staff

Note: supervision is recorded in SmartCoach, not SWIS.

3

Activity description + Read more ...

This field displays the breakdown descriptions available for each of the Activity Types. The available categories are:

Community Outreach/Networking

Includes sub-categories:

  • Emergency/Disaster Response
  • Interagency Meeting
  • Outreach Service
  • Participation in Special Events
  • Relationship Building
  • Steering Committees

Community Partnerships

Includes sub-categories:

  • Development of New Community Partnerships
  • Group Work
  • Information/Feedback on Programs and Services
  • Ongoing management of community partnerships

Income Security Policy Development and Feedback

Includes sub-categories:

  • Research Projects/Meetings
  • Submissions
  • Working Groups

Management Activities

Includes sub-categories:

  • Business Planning/Performance Analysis
  • Developing/Monitoring Service Delivery
    • Select this option if recording quality checking activities. See Recording Quality checks in SWIS
  • Local Assessment Panel/Customer Aggression Discussion
  • Service Centre/Smart Centre meetings
  • Staff Selections
  • Zone Meetings

Training/Professional Development

Includes sub-categories:

  • Induction/Orientation
  • Providing Training/Professional Development

Note: supervision is recorded in SmartCoach, not SWIS.

4

Additional notes + Read more ...

This field is optional and holds any additional notes that the social worker may want to record about an action and/or activity undertaken.

These notes can be accessed by the Business Manager of social work services and can be very useful to demonstrate the types of work being undertaken.
