Determining dates for reassessments and updates

Table 1

Step

Action

1

Request for reassessment or update request received + Read more ...

Determine the date of event (DOV) that the income and/or assets information relates to:

  • Check the RCA Institution Summary (RIS) screen for the aged care entry details
  • If a care recipient is RCA/CUR after an auto means assessment, and is lodging an Aged Care Calculation of your cost of care, the date of effect will be taken as the date of entry into care, and details will be used to correct the auto assessment

Is the income and/or assets date of event after the date of entry into aged care?

  • Yes, go to Step 2
  • No:
    • date of event before or equal to entry to care, go to Step 3
    • no entry date recorded, go to Step 4

2

Care recipient advising change in circumstances + Read more ...

The care recipient's circumstances have changed from a date after the date of entry into care.

An update is needed:

  • Check if evidence is needed for the change in circumstances, see Verifying income and assets
  • Update the income and/or assets:
    • The date of event (DOV) for updates is the date the change occurred
    • The date of receipt (DOR) is the date of contact/lodgement of form
  • The coding of income and assets for aged care purposes aligns with the Social Security Act 1991. For further information, see:
    • Coding income and assets for Centrelink payments and services
    • Financial Investments
    • Exempt income and assets for Centrelink payments
    • Assessing deprivation/gifting
    • Foreign income and assets
    • Aged care means assessment with total assessable assets equal to or greater than $10,000,000 (and applicable assessable income)
  • If an urgent review of aged care costs has been requested, contact Aged Care Technical Peer Support (TPS) for ad hoc review request

Procedure ends here.

3

Request for reassessment received + Read more ...

Aged Care skilled staff only can complete the rest of the process (reassessments from date of entry into care). Non Aged Care staff - send a detailed ACTDOC using the ACTDOC/CALLBACK guided procedure to region RCA explaining why reassessment is required.

Is the income and assets information from the date of entry into care and entry is on or after 1 July 2014?

  • Yes, go to Step 4
  • No, assessed under the Pre 1 July 2014 scheme (care recipient is grandfathered)
    • See Aged Care means assessment - Aged care means assessment - residential care pre 1 July 2014 - income assessment and Aged Care means assessment - Residential Care pre 1 July 2014 - assets assessment
    • Procedure ends here

4

Reassessment from date of entry or pre entry assessment + Read more ...

Complete a reassessment when:

  • a means assessment is means not disclosed (MND)
  • a request is received to correct the income and/or assets used in a previous means assessment including for previous period of care
  • an Aged Care Calculation of your cost of care has been lodged after an auto means assessment has been completed

Use the following dates:

  • The date of event (DOV) for the reassessment is the date of entry into care or DOV used in original means assessment if care recipient has not yet entered care
  • The date of receipt (DOR) is the date of contact/ lodgement date of form/information

In Customer First, does the care recipient have a status of RCA/CUR?

  • Yes:
    • RCA/CUR-DSS or RCA/CUR-SFR, go to Step 5
    • RCA/CUR-MTA, go to Step 6
  • No, go to Step 7

5

Care recipient is RCA/CUR-DSS or RCA/CUR-SFR + Read more ...

Is the care recipient still in care with the same provider?

  • Yes, go to Step 6
  • No, See Step 1 in Table 2

6

Reassessment of initial means assessment + Read more ...

Care recipient is correcting the means assessment from the date of entry:

  • Confirm the Aged Care Calculation of your cost of care has been completed correctly and signed by the care recipient or authorised third party. See Aged care - consent and disclosure
  • Use the Aged Care Calculation of your cost of care 'checklist' to confirm all necessary supporting documentation is provided

Reassessment will be completed at first point of contact unless contact is by phone and:

  • further information is required, or
  • the reassessment is not able to be completed during the call due to the length of processing

Can this reassessment be completed?

  • Yes, see Table 2 for actioning reassessments in Customer First
  • No, further information is required:
    • Index an RCA/NCL or RCA/HOM using current date as DOR contact/lodgement date of form/information
    • Issue RFI using Process Direct – refer Requesting information (CLK)
    • Complete Fast Note - Auto text – Aged Care > Assessment > Progress of Aged Care Assessment with details of information requested
    • Hold claim for 21 days plus an allowance for the letter to be received
    • Procedure ends here
    • Add the keyword of RCAPRI to refer for urgent processing - an additional ACTDOC is not required
    • procedure ends here
  • No, means assessment requires hand off for processing:
    • Index an RCA/NCL or RCA/HOM with DOR as date of contact/ lodgement date of form/information
    • Add the keyword of RCAPRI to claim to refer for urgent processing
    • Complete Fast Note with reasons for hand off of claim
    • Procedure ends here

7

No RCA/CUR status - reassessment required + Read more ...

Care recipient was previously in care and is providing information to reassess the original means assessment.

To determine if a care recipient is discharged/deceased, check:

  • Aged Care Staff Portal (ACSP):
    • Care recipient summary will show periods in care
    • Care recipient – Event Summary will show departure reasons
    • Banner in ACSP will display when a care recipient is deceased
  • Customer First:
    • DL screen for any notification of death, and
    • DEA screen for coding that indicates this has occurred

Is the care recipient discharged from care?

  • Yes, and is deceased in Customer First:
    • Unable to update income and assets. A manual assessment is needed
    • Create a Fast Note. Select Auto Text option > Aged Care > Enquiry > Aged Care Discharged CR - Man Let Request
    • Include the reasons for the manual assessment referral
    • Procedure ends here
  • Yes, and not deceased in Customer First:
    • Update the income and assets
    • Update the RCAA screen with the details of the principal home
    • Confirm details on RCIRC screen are correct
    • Go to the BA screen and code Service Reason: RCA, Act: RES, Date of effect: blank
    • Complete the Source: PHO or form type and Date of Receipt: date of contact/lodgement of form
    • When the activity is finalised, RCA shows as CAN
    • See Step 2 in Table 2 for actioning reassessments in Customer First
  • No, still in care with same provider or yet to enter care:
    • Means assessment needs completing
    • See Aged care means assessment - preliminary checks
    • Procedure ends here

Actioning reassessments in Customer First

For aged care skill tagged staff only.

Table 2

Step

Action

1

Check the Means Not Disclosed Indicator field + Read more ...

The care recipient can correct a means assessment or provide details after a means assessment is finalised as means not disclosed (MND):

  • Go to the RMND screen
  • Check the Means Not Disclosed Indicator field

Care recipient currently assessed as:

  • MND field: Y
    • Insert C to correct Means Not Disclosed line from original date
    • Change Means Not Disclosed Indicator to N
    • Update the RMND screen to MND status N
    • Complete the Source: PHO or form type and Date of Receipt: date of contact/lodgement
    • Go to the BA screen and code Service Reason: RCA, Act: REA, Date of effect: date of entry
  • MND field: N
    • Go to the BA screen and code Service Reason: RCA, Act: REA, Date of effect: date of entry/date of change
    • Complete the Source: PHO or form type and Date of Receipt: date of contact/lodgement of form

2

Complete income and assets reassessments + Read more ...

Reassess the income and assets using the DOV and DOR from Table 1.

Select the income and asset screens to code:

  • Use the Residential Care Task Selector (RCTS) to choose the screens
    • if data already exists on a screen, a Y will appear on the RCTS screen
    • check this data for accuracy
  • Or manually go to the screens individually

For assistance coding means assessment details for:

  • Residential Care, see the Aged care means assessment - residential care post 1 July 2014 - post-entry process
  • Home Care, see the Aged care means assessment - home care post-entry process

Is the income and assets reassessment for residential care?

  • Yes, go to Step 3
  • No, go to Step 4

3

Confirm assets outcome on RCAD - residential care only + Read more ...

Check the RCAA Assessment Determination (RCAD) screen to confirm the correct dates and asset amounts are recorded:

  • RCAD should reflect the assets updated for the care recipient
  • For care recipients with a partner this is:
    • 50% of the total combined assets recorded
    • regardless of the actual ownership percentage of the asset

Discharged from care

RCAD may not display the correct asset information for care recipients discharged from care. To confirm dates and amounts coded for these care recipients:

  • use the Income & Assets Menu on the Customer First Workspace page
  • select the Pension income, assets and rate link to display a summary of income and assets
  • select 'Next' to view previous dates if needed

Is this care recipient currently in care?

  • Yes, go to Step 4
  • No, go to Step 5

4

Confirm means assessment data transmitted to aged care systems + Read more ...

Complete a check of the means assessment data (if needed).

Check the RCA event on the EV List (XEV) screen:

  • The XEV screen displays income and asset data transmitted to aged care systems in the refresh activity

Confirm the means assessment data is correct including:

  • RCINC line states:
    • date of entry into care, or
    • each date of change
  • RCINC line should not contain 4 $0 entries in line unless:
    • RIAS shows $0 income, or
    • care recipient is eligible for ITF/MTCF/ITCF to be $0 (i.e. dependent child)
  • RCSTAT line states:
    • date of entry into care, or
    • each date of change

Is the XEV screen displaying income and asset data as expected?

  • Yes, go to Step 5
  • No,
    • Review coding of date and amounts
    • For further assistance, contact Technical Peer Support (TPS)
    • Procedure ends here

5

Attach Fast Note to the update activity + Read more ...

When the income and/or assets coding is complete:

  • Exit the activity
  • Attach a Fast note to record the assessment outcome:
    • Use a Fast Note. Select Auto Text option > Aged Care > Assessment > RES Care Assessment - FIN to document the assessment outcome
    • Link the Fast Note to the open RCA/REA activity

6

Assessment result + Read more ...

When the Fast Note is complete:

  • Select (S) the RCA/REA activity from the Activity List (AL) screen
  • Go to the Assessment Result (AR) screen
  • Check the results on AR and confirm:
    • the RCA status remains the same
    • any arrears or overpayments calculated are correct based on DOV and DOR
    • check the RCAD screen as this will reflect the assets outcome of the care recipient

Is the care recipient currently in care with the same service provider?

  • Yes, go to Step 7
  • No:
    • Care recipient has not entered care (RCA/CUR-MTA), go to Step 8
    • Reassessment is for previous period in care, go to Step 9

7

Follow up action + Read more ...

  • Ongoing fees are immediately adjusted by the system for residential care recipients who were previously MND
  • For all other care recipients:
    • check if an ad hoc review has been requested to immediately adjust fees
    • see Aged care reviews - ad hoc reviews for more information
  • Any refunds calculate and pay at the next quarterly review (QR)
  • Use the Aged Care Fee Calculator on the Resources page to determine if the care recipient is low means or not low means
  • If resident status has been corrected from date of entry, create a Fast Note. Select Auto Text option > Aged Care > Enquiry > Aged Care Manual Adjustment Request. Refer to Aged care reviews - quarterly reviews for information

Check for any current financial hardship (hardship) assistance reductions:

  • ACMPS - check Hardship & Income Suspension tab
    • Locate care recipient record
    • Under Hardship and income suspension tab, select Hardship summary

If the care recipient has a current hardship assessment:

  • use Fast Note. Select Auto Text option > Aged Care > Hardship > Aged Care Hardship Enquiry
  • include a summary of
    • reason for referral
    • changes that have been advised
    • any documents given or requested
  • record details of the changes to income and/or assets advised
  • Procedure ends here

8

Reassessment of pre entry means assessment + Read more ...

Care recipient has corrected income and assets for a pre entry assessment. Updated income and assets are used to determine fees once the care recipient enters care.

Check ACMPS>Communication History tab to view original pre entry assessment letter. If updates made to income and assets change the care recipient's resident status, a new pre entry letter will be automatically issued. A manual pre entry letter can be issued once income and assets information have been received in ACMPS.

If the care recipient or authorised third party has requested a new pre entry assessment letter:

  • Go to ACMPS in Fees and Charges role
  • In the care recipient record:
    • go to Means Tested Assessments tab
    • check that updates have been received in Means Test Assessments
    • copy the care recipient ACMPS ID
  • To generate a pre entry letter:
    • in the side menu, select Documents>Manual Letter Generation
    • enter ACMPS ID, select care type and letter required
    • see Resources for a link to Aged Care Letter Codes and Descriptions
  • Under Primary Details for the Letter, enter the MTA ID that contains the updated income and assets, fields will populate from MTA data
  • Select Generate Letter

Procedure ends here.

9

Care recipient no longer in care - complete referral for a manual adjustment + Read more ...

When the income and/or asset updates are complete:

  • advise the care recipient or authorised third party that further investigation is needed
  • create a Fast Note. Select Auto Text option > Aged Care > Enquiry > Manual Adjustment Referral and include:
    • a summary of the updates made
    • the reason for referral
    • details of entry and discharge dates
