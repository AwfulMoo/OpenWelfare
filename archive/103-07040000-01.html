Using the Tax Year to Date (TXYTD) screen 103-07040000

This document explains how to correctly use the Tax Year to Date (TXYTD) screen. Services Australia has payment information held on a customer's Centrelink record, which provides details of taxable and reportable non-taxable amounts, the tax withheld from payments and the Remote Area Allowance (RAA) amounts.

On this Page:

Access to payment summary details

Centrelink payment summary details for all customers who receive a reportable Centrelink payment within the financial year are electronically transferred to the Australian Taxation Office (ATO) if they are pre-filled to a customer's income tax return. For more information, see Electronic transfer of Payment Summary details to the Australian Taxation Office (ATO).

Note: customers can access their Payment Summary through a range of self service options.

Tax Year to Date (TXYTD) screen

The TXYTD screen allows staff to retrieve a customer's payment details, as they will appear (in summary form) on a Centrelink payment summary for a given period.

The income details provided on the screen uses the same payment details that are produced on Centrelink payment summaries and takes into account information such as overpayments, benefit transfers and other tax adjustments.

Any future payments are not to be included in the retrieval of information.

The TXYTD screen does not replace payment summaries but is able to be used to cross check the payment details made to a customer who is currently receiving or has received reportable payments.

The TXYTD screen can be accessed in Customer First or Process Direct.

In Customer First, the TXYTD screen shows details for payment summary periods via a web browser hyperlink using Microsoft Internet explorer. Details before 1 July 2003 will show directly on the TXYTD screen.

In Process Direct the TXYTD screen shows payment summary year to date details from the start of the current financial year until the end of the customer’s most recent payment period. Payment information for previous financial years is not available in Process Direct.
