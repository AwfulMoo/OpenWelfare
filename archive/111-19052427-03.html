Smart Centres QCF variations

Table 1: This table describes the process for submitting QCF variations in Smart Centres.

Item

Description

1

Guidelines for Team Leaders + Read more ...

Approved Smart Centre variations are located on the Quality Call Framework (QCF) Portal. The Resources page has a link.

Approved variations to the Sampling policy must be lodged by the last day of the quarter:

  • Open the Sampling Variation Tool on the QCF Portal
  • Submit

Note: an Out of Scope variation is only for staff who do make or receive external customer calls. An Out of Scope variation is not used for call evaluations for other purposes, such as back on track or learning and development. For calls listened to for back on track or learning and development, select No, when answering “Is this evaluation in scope for Quality Call Framework” in the QCF tool.

2

Org Unit Variation + Read more ...

Smart Coach shows that a staff member has a requirement to be listened to however they aren’t on the phone.

Where all staff in a Org Unit do not make or receive calls a Org Unit variation can be applied. The Org Unit Sampling Variation Request form needs to be completed and sent to the Quality Call Framework mailbox to arrange coding. The Resources page has a link to the Contact details.

3

SmartCoach + Read more ...

Variations will generally show the following day, except when the variation was entered on a Friday or over a weekend. These will display in Smart Coach on Monday.

The data is loaded into Smart Coach by 2pm (EST) the day after the variation was entered into the online form.

Do not lodge another variation. Duplicate variations will cause issues with the display in Smart Coach. For example if a variation is lodged to reduce the requirement to 5 and then a subsequent variation is lodged for the same 5 requirement, a count of 10 will display as required in Smart Coach. Duplicated variations require manual deletion and cause significant and unnecessary rework.

If variation/s are not showing in Smart Coach, check the Important Messages - SmartTools & CAR-T Agent to ensure no issues with data loads.

4

Additional Variations + Read more ...

Any requests to amend an existing variation, or for a new variation, need to be sent to Portfolio Operations Quality, who will coordinate the requests. See the Resources page for a link to the Quality and Technical Support homepage.

Variations can cover individuals, Teams, location, Footprint, Branch, Program and/or Work Type.

Calibration for accreditation and re-accreditation

Table 2: This table describes the process for completing the calibration for accreditation and re-accreditation.

Item

Description

1

Calibration exercise + Read more ...

Once the accreditation or re-accreditation process and test has been complete, go to the Quality Call Framework Smart Centre Portal and select the Calibration and Aim for Accuracy link.

Open the Smart Centre Calibration instructions, located under the Current Calibration Exercise.

Listen to the call and submit the calibration activity using the Calibration Form.

Once the calibration is submitted, an automated email will be sent. Forward the email to the Portfolio Operations Quality team.

Once the data has been received (weekly) the Portfolio Operations Quality team will send feedback against the baseline standard by email.

Note: the results will not affect the accreditation. They are for learning and development and to assist in clarifying how the person performed against a set standard.
