Classification guidelines for Classification Support

Table 1: This table is a support document for Classification Support Officers who do manual classification of Centrelink customer documents. It provides information for documents escalated to Classification Support.

Form code or document type

Classification Support information

Aged Care forms starting with AC (the most common ones being AC019, AC021 and AC022)

Forward a copy of the image to the Aged Care Liaison team. Classify document as F9 and finalise work item as Image requires administrative processing.

Appointment of a nominee – Residential Aged Care, Department of Social Services

Forward a copy of the image to the Aged Care Payments team. Classify document as F9 and finalise work item as Image requires administrative processing.

Blank forms and pages

If there is not enough information to identify the customer record, follow existing protocols to Orphan the document.

Classify the document using the form code and finalise the work item as Image contains insufficient information to classify and click Save. A message will appear to say that the work item has been unassigned

For images that are completely blank with no identifying features received on their own, classify the document as F9. Finalise the work item as Image contains insufficient information to classify and click Save. A message will say the work item has been unassigned.

If the document was received with other documents for the customer, attach the image to the customer record. This will enable staff inform the customer the form received was blank.

Continence Aids Payment Scheme (CAPS) related documents

Send documents by email to the CAPS team. Classify document as F9 and finalise work item as Image requires administrative processing.

DAF (Deny Access) Records

Refer to section on Restricted Access.

FA083 - Application for Approved Care Organisation status

These forms should not be attached to records. The ACO team will create the ACO CRN and action as appropriate. Send FA083 by email to the Approved Care Organisations processing team. Classify document as FA083 and finalise work item as Image requires administrative processing.

Form not in drop down box (matrix)

Email the Workload Management team to request interim classification process and include an example of the form. Do not finalise work item until advice is received.

IMS code letters from Business Integrity Officers (participation fax queue only)

TPSO to email to Random Sample Surveys (RSS) Programme. Classify document as F9 and finalise work item as Image requires administrative processing.

Inappropriate material

Ensure your Team Leader has reviewed the image and follow the process outlined in Requesting deletion of a digital image to have the image deleted. Pending deletion, classify document as F9 and finalise work item as Image requires administrative processing.

Medicare documents (other than Advice of Death)

If the document was received by post or scanned in a service centre and under 70 days old, lodge a rescan request.

Email the image to Digitisation Operations if the document:

  • is over 70 days old
  • was uploaded by the customer
  • was received by fax

Public Trustee documents

Where the letter refers to multiple customers, attach to the Public Trustee’s record and then advise the relevant Program Team/s by email with a copy of the letter.

Restricted Access

National Queue action:

  • keep the CRN field blank
  • leave the form type in
  • finalise work item as Image requires administrative processing

FileNet action to declare the image:

  • Follow the process outlined in FileNet (IBM Content Navigator) for Digital Images
  • Email the National Restricted Access team and include:
    • Customer Reference Number (CRN)
    • Scan date
    • Form code the image has been classified as Document ID

SA116 - Advice of Death

If there is not enough information to identify the customer record, email the image to the Medicare Eligibility and Enrolments team to request a search for the customer in the Medicare system.

Classify document as SA116A and finalise work item as Image requires administrative processing.

SA389 - Centrelink Business Application

SA389 forms received on their own and where we cannot locate a record, must be emailed to National Business Gateway Business Applications. When received with SA445 and we cannot find a record, email National Business Gateway Business Applications.

Classify document as SA389 and finalise work item as Image requires administrative processing.

SA391 - Centrelink Business Application - Confirmation eService

Email document to the Income Management Payment and Contact team (IMPACT). Classify document as SA391 and finalise work item as Image requires administrative processing.

SA436 - Centrelink Business Application - Electronic Verification of Rent Community Housing Organisations

Email document to Income Management Payment and Contact team (IMPACT). Classify document as SA436 and finalise work item as Image requires administrative processing.

SA445 - Business Online Details

SA445 forms received on their own and a record cannot be located email to National Business Gateway.

When received with SA389 and a record cannot be located email to National Business Gateway Business Applications.

Classify document as SA445 and finalise work item as Image requires administrative processing.

SA448 - Business Application for Income Management Deductions

Email document to Income Management Payment and Contact team (IMPACT). Classify document as SA448 and finalise work item as Image requires administrative processing.

SA450 - Essential Medical Equipment - Declaration

Form has been mapped directly to the CSQ.

Retrieval of the original document is not required. If document is to be sent back to the customer, print a copy and post to the customer.

Classify as SA450 and attach to customer record.

SA475 - Business change of details

These cases are not to be referred to NBG. Forward any enquiries to Income Management Payment and Contact team (IMPACT).

Classify document as SA475 and finalise work item as Image requires administrative processing.

SI041 - Request by a Child Protection Agency for information from Centrelink (SI041)

Email document to Public Interest Team.

Classify document as F9 and finalise work item as Image requires administrative processing.

SI042 - Request by a Child Protection Agency for information from Centrelink (SI042)

Email document to Public Interest Team.

Classify document as F9 and finalise work item as Image requires administrative processing.

SI043 - Request by a Child Protection Agency for information from Centrelink (SI043)

Email document to Public Interest Team.

Classify document as F9 and finalise work item as Image requires administrative processing.

SS460 - BasicsCard Merchant Applications

Email document to Income Management Payment and Contact team (IMPACT). Classify document as SA460 and finalise work item as Image requires administrative processing.

SU461 - Voluntary Work (Request for Organisational Approval)

Form has been mapped directly to the CSQ and should be emailed to Voluntary Organisation team. Classify document as SA461 and finalise work item as Image requires administrative processing.

SU704 - Community Housing Transition Rent Certificate

Document is to be sent back to the customer. Print a copy and post to the customer. Classify document as SU704 and finalise work item as Image requires administrative processing.

SU709 - Confirmation of Imprisonment for payment

Email form to the Prison Servicing Helpdesk. Classify document as SU709and finalise work item as Image requires administrative processing.

Subpoenas

Before escalating to Classification Support, team leaders will send subpoenas needing action by email to the Subpoena team.

Forward to the Subpoena team by email or internal mail:

  • subpoenas needing action, or
  • receipts from Courts noting that documents have been received

Classify document as F9 and finalise work item as Image requires administrative processing.

Tasmanian Freight Equalisation Scheme letters and Bass Strait Passenger Equalisation forms – all forms from TAS002 to TAS022

Email the document to Tasmanian Transport Programmes. Classify document as F9 and finalise work item as Image requires administrative processing.
