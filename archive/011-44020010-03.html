Documents needed + Read more ...

Customers must provide the following documents for each person on the enrolment form:

  • Current passport or Immicard
  • Proof they have applied for permanent residency. This must be:
    • From the Department of Home Affairs, and have details about the category of their visa, or
    • An email from a Migration Agent from a digital copy, phone, tablet or laptop

Service Officers must complete a VEVO check as information provided by Immigration link may not be able to determine the person’s eligibility.

Note: VEVO will show ‘Applicant for PR’ as ‘No’. This is a known issue with DHA/VEVO. The enrolment can continue.

Eligibility for Medicare + Read more ...

A person is eligible for Medicare when they:

  • have lodged an application for a combined partner visa 309/100 or 820/801, and
  • are in Australia to live, and
    • holds a valid visa
    • has permission to work, or
    • has a spouse, parent or child that is an Australian citizen, New Zealand citizen that lives in Australia, or holds a permanent resident visa

Eligibility commencement date is:

  • Temporary Partner Visa Offshore 309: date of arrival in Australia (to live)
  • Temporary Partner Visa Onshore 820: date application was lodged with Department of Home Affairs

Where the temporary visa subclass 309 or 820 was not granted at the time the application for permanent residency was lodged, eligibility will commence from the date the:

  • applicant came to Australia to live (offshore), or
  • application for permanent residency was lodged (onshore)

The applicant does not need to wait until the visa subclass 309 or 820 is granted to be eligible.

Temporary Partner visa subclass 309 (offshore)

Table 3

Step

Action

1

Eligibility for Medicare + Read more ...

To enrol an applicant for Partner visa (subclass 309) register the customer for Medicare.

Key the following entitlement details:

  • Entitlement Type: Conditional Migrant
  • Entitlement Start Date: date the applicant entered Australia to live
  • Entitlement End Date: 12 months
  • Entitlement End reason: entitlement ceased

Record all documents in CDMS when registering customers Medicare entitlement.

Permanent Partner visa subclass 100 (offshore)

Table 4

Step

Action

1

Eligibility for Medicare + Read more ...

To enrol an applicant for Partner visa (subclass 100) register the customer for Medicare.

Key the following entitlement details:

  • Entitlement Type: Migrant
  • Entitlement Start Date:
    • where the visa was granted onshore and the date granted
    • where the visa was granted offshore and the date of entry into Australia
  • Entitlement End Date: not applicable (open Medicare card)

Record all documents in CDMS when registering customers Medicare entitlement.
