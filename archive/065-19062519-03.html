Assessing and coding debt transfer of previous members of a couple

This table describes the steps to follow when assessing and coding transfer of a HEAS debt based on a Court ruling.

Step

Action

1

Module S provided + Read more ...

Has the Module S been completed and coded?

  • Yes, go to Step 2
  • No, undertake actions as required in Assessing the Separation details Mod S and online updates

Note: missing information should be requested via a Q999 including a copy of the Court Order or ruling granting transfer to one person of:

  • the HEAS debt, and
  • the HEAS security

2

Check supporting court documents + Read more ...

Check court documents:

  • for evidence that grant full ownership of the HEAS security to one person (transferee), and
  • that the responsibility for repayment of the HEAS debts of transferor (person named as no longer owning the part of the security) is given to the transferee
  • make sure the title on property is in accordance with court documents

Is the binding agreement for debt and property transfer evident in court documents provided?

  • Yes, cease loan payments to both go to Step 3
  • No, cease loan payments to both and request information via Q999

3

Contact both persons named in Mod S and advise action being taken + Read more ...

The transferor and transferee must both be contacted as part of this process. Contact the transferor first (if known)

Consent of both persons is required and must be provided at the contact in order to code using the instructions below.

Contacting the transferor

When contacting the transferor confirm that they agree to the agency transferring their debt to the transferee based on legal documents provided.

Advise transfer of their HEAS debt balance will be nil after the terms in the Court document are recorded.

Does transferor want loan payment to restart?

  • Yes, and new security with sufficient equity has been offered
    • Restate Terms and Conditions
    • Advise requirements to provide completed Module R, along with signed written request (Statement)
    • When all documents are provided request valuation after establishing equity of more than $10,000 based on client's estimate
    • Document details with the OAPL17 Fast Note annotate as appropriate
    • Go to Step 5
  • No, create DOC with OAPL17 Fast Note annotate as appropriate

4

Contact transferee and advise action taken advise + Read more ...

Prior to contact review available equity and payability of the HEAS to transferee based on consolidated debt of both persons.

Contact and advise transferee of:

  • available equity on security based on consolidated debt amount
  • outcome of payability check, and projected debt amount
  • terms and conditions of participation
  • requirement to provide consent to record transfer of the HEAS security and debt of transferor to their record

Consent of both persons is required and must be provided at the contact in order to code using the instructions below.

Is the customer Age Pension age, has adequate equity and wants loan payment to continue?

  • Yes, customer meets all 3 requirements. Code the following:
    • Update the percentage share owned in the property on both records: Note: Error E607CQ may occur. See Step 5 in the Assessing income and assets when a business has been sold table for workaround
    • Add outstanding balances of loan components of the transferor to the record of the transferee
    • Zero all loan components on record of the transferor
    • Restart loan
    • Record details with Fast Note HEAS debt zeroed separation annotate as appropriate
    • Mail Q999
    • Procedure ends here
  • No, 1 or more conditions not met. Add balances of loan components for the transferor to the record of the transferee and mail Q999. Record details with Fast Note: HEAS debt zeroed separation and annotate as appropriate. If the following applies take the necessary action:
    • Below Age Pension age: Recovery action will commence immediately and interest is accruable until all debts repaid. Procedure ends here
    • Age Pension age reached and does not offer additional security or want loan payment: Advise interest will be charged until full debt amount is repaid. Procedure ends here
    • Age Pension age reached and offering another security: restate terms and conditions. New legal costs will be charged for registering caveat or charge, advise requirements to provide completed Module R with signed written request (statement). When all documents are provided request valuation after establishing adequate equity to cover current debt and future payments. Go to Step 5

5

Real estate details + Read more ...

Make sure the property or properties being used as security for the loan are coded on the Real Estate Details (RE) screen and property ownership is correct on both records.

If the property being used for security of the loan is the principal home then this also needs to be recorded on the RE screen.

When the principal home is coded onto the RE screen code the Current house and Curtilage field with the same value as the Current Market Value field. This makes sure the principal home is not used in the ordinary assets test, should the customer revert back to an automatic rate in the future. Assessing and coding the Real estate details Mod R provides detailed coding instructions.

Note: the property ID number from the Real Estate/Business Summary (REBS) screen is recorded on the Loan Details (PLS/HEAS) screen.

Transmission of title

A transmission of title is required if:

  • a court order creates a formal property settlement, and
  • one member of the couple is to take full ownership of the security property

Use the relevant transmission of title template on the Resources page.

Go to Step 6

6

Coding loan details using a new loan ID if last loan payment is less than 13 weeks + Read more ...

  • Restart the HEAS with new loan ID on PLS screen in Customer First
  • Go to the PLS screen via:
    • the Pensions Task Selector (PTS) screen and select the Loans Scheme field, or
    • coding 'PLS' in the Nxt field, then pressing [Enter]
  • Enter all details on the following fields, overtyping where required:
    • Effect Date: defaults to current date
    • Loan Top up Type: defaults to maximum rate top-up
    • Loan Top up Amount per fortnight: if applicable
    • Loan Security Value:
    • Customer Advised Max Amount $: if applicable
    • Guaranteed/Nominated Amount:
    • 100% PLS: defaults to 'N', change to 'Y' if customer is a loan-only customer
    • CLK Loan Property IDs: defaults to 'N'
  • Enter details in Source and Date of Receipt fields

7

Caveats/Charges + Read more ...

As soon as the payment is current, a tasking request must be sent to the Property and Home Equity Access Scheme Legal mailbox. This is to instruct the contracted solicitors for the registration of caveats/charges and/or transmission of title on the item(s) of real estate nominated by the customer for the security of the loan.

See the Resources page for tasking request template.

8

Reply from Solicitors + Read more ...

The solicitors will advise once the caveats/charges have been completed. Record these details on a DOC on the customer's record.

The solicitors will also advise Legal Services Division of the legal costs involved in registering the charge.

These costs are recoverable from the customer. The cost will be added to the outstanding loan amount and interest will be charged at the same rate of the loan.

Once the Legal Services Division advise of the recoverable legal costs, update the PLS/HEAS screen.

9

Coding legal costs + Read more ...

On the PLS/HEAS screen, update the following fields:

  • Costs $: the solicitors' charges
  • Source
  • DOR

Finalise the activity.

A batch laser advise (BLA) advising the customer of the costs to be paid by them will issue automatically.

10

Charges added to the loan balance + Read more ...

Legal costs are automatically added to the customer's Current Balance$ field if:

  • the customer has advised that they wish to have the solicitors' charges added to their loan, or
  • no reply is received from the customer
