Claiming Medicare benefits by person on the same Medicare card

How a Medicare benefit is paid when a person on the same Medicare card

Table 1: This table lists the ways in which a Medicare benefit is paid when a person on the same Medicare card attends a service centre on behalf of the claimant.

Where the claimant:

the Medicare benefit is paid:

Explanation

has bank account details stored

EFT to the claimant.

The person on the same Medicare card may sign the printed receipt, even though it displays the bank account details of the claimant.

does not have bank account details stored

EFT to the claimant.

All manual patient claims will be paid via EFT from 1 July 2016. Where the claimant does not have valid EFT details stored in CDMS or cannot provide valid temporary or permanent EFT details at the time the claim is submitted , the payment for the claim will be ‘HELD’ until EFT details have been provided.
