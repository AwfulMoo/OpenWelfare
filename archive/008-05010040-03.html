Completing a Mobility Allowance review form

Item

Description

1

Activity the customer is undertaking + Read more ...

The customer is required to advise the Services Australia what activity they are undertaking, such as:

  • gainful employment
  • vocational training
  • independent living/life skills training
  • voluntary work
  • combination of vocational training, voluntary work, gainful employment and independent living skills
  • job search activities

Customers participating in Disability Employment Services (DES) Disability Management Service satisfy the eligibility requirement for MOB through their participation for the standard rate of Mobility Allowance, and the number of hours is not taken into account. For customers receiving an income support payment, participation is verified either:

  • through the interface with the Department of Education, Skills and Employment system, or
  • by viewing the customers current Job Plan

Income received

Customers receiving the higher rate of MOB will be required to report their income to verify wages are at or above the relevant minimum wage. While MOB is not income tested, if:

  • hours worked falls below 15 hours per week as reported by the customer, higher rate MOB recipients will enter a 12 week higher rate Period of Grace
  • hours worked falls below 32 hours over a 4 week period, the customer will enter a 12 week standard rate Period of Grace

If payment of an income support payment is precluded due to income/hours worked, the customer may still be eligible for the higher rate of MOB.

Incapacity exemption

If the customer lodges a medical certificate which exempts them from the Mutual Obligation Requirements, they may lose qualification for MOB because they no longer meet the Mobility Allowance travel test.

2

Details of the employer or training organisation + Read more ...

In this question, the customer needs to provide:

  • details of their employer
  • the number of hours they spent on the activity over a 2 and 4 week period

Customers receiving the higher rate of MOB will also be required to report their income to verify wages are at or above the relevant minimum wage.

Vocational or independent living/life skills training

If the customer is undertaking vocational or independent living/life skills training they need to provide:

  • the details of the organisation
  • the number of hours they spent on the activity over a four week period
  • the start and end date of the course

Any recreational component of the independent living/life skills training is disregarded.

3

Provision of vehicle under the Vehicle Assistance Scheme from the Department of Veterans' Affairs (DVA) + Read more ...

If the customer has been provided with a car under the Vehicle Assistance Scheme from DVA they need to provide:

  • the date the vehicle was received, and
  • proof they still have the vehicle

MOB is not payable if the customer is in receipt of a DVA car.

GST free vehicle

The purchase of a GST free vehicle does not affect qualification for MOB. (For information on purchasing GST-free cars refer customers to the Australian Taxation Office).

4

Your statement + Read more ...

Customers need to check the form is completed and they have read the statement before signing (including electronic signature) the review form.

Note: if any concerns about the authenticity of the electronic signature, discuss with a supervisor and complete a Suspected Fraud referral if appropriate. Do not document specific details of the allegation(s) on the customer's record. Escalate to Level 2 Helpdesk if further assistance is required.
