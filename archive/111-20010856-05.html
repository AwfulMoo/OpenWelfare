QMA error codes

Please note: Error codes Q00011 – Q00032 listed below are proficiency impacting when selected under the “Other errors identified” field on the QMA Summary, with the exception of Q00015 and Q00024.

Table 1

Error

Definition

This error applies when…

Q00001

Mismatch with information in claim/ evidence/ discussion with customer

The activity has been updated and does not match the supporting information.

Q00002

Field updated without appropriate supporting evidence

The information coded requires supporting evidence and none is available.

Q00003

Date coded does not match qualification date

The date coded does not match the qualification date per rules for the benefit/payment type.

Q00004

Incorrect field selected

The incorrect drop down field has been selected.

Q00005

Information transcribed incorrectly from source (keying error)

The Service Officer has missed the field or coded it wrong. For example:

  • transposed the date (23/12 rather than 12/23)
  • incorrect spelling in free text fields
  • code/abbreviation incorrectly keyed
  • asset amount keyed incorrectly ($123 000 rather than $12 300)

Q00006

Supporting legislation or policy not taken into account, or incorrectly interpreted (decision error)

The staff member made a decision not supported by legislation or policy or operational messages. The actions should be supported by Operational Blueprint.

Q00007

Manual calculation is incorrect

A staff member made a manual calculation and it is incorrect.

Q00008

Event date does not match earliest deemed date.

The event date does not match the earliest deemed date as per business rules.

Q00009

THP Score calculation incorrect

The calculation of a THP score is incorrect. This error applies to Carer Payment and Carer Allowance.

Q00010

Carer score calculation incorrect

The calculation of a Carer score is incorrect. This error applies to Carer Payment and Carer Allowance.

Q00011

Catch all error - arrears, current and or future rate incorrect

The staff member has not correctly calculated the ongoing rate or arrears, or the future rate is not correct.

Q00012

Catch all error - circumstances assessed incorrectly

All customer circumstances have not been assessed correctly. An error has been identified but no other error is appropriate.

Q00013

Catch all error - POI details incorrect

Proof of identity details have not been recorded correctly or identify confirmation not completed.

Q00014

Catch all error - Correspondence Nominee incorrect

The customer has indicated they have a nominee and it has not been added.

Q00015

Catch all error - Getting it Right – Minimum DOC standards

The decision DOC has not been completed correctly and the Service Officer has not explained their decision.
Note: this is a non-proficiency impacting error

Q00016

Catch all error - person incorrect

The work item/ claim has been actioned on the incorrect record.

Q00017

Catch all error - benefit type incorrect or customer not eligible

The customer has been granted the incorrect benefit or another payment is more appropriate.

Q00018

Catch all error - record linking not completed or incorrect

The appropriate records have not been linked correctly.

Q00019

Catch all error - Payment Nominee details not updated

The customer has indicated they have a nominee and it has not been added.

Q00020

Catch all error - additional information required

The staff member should not have assessed the claim as information is still outstanding.

Q00021

Catch all error - referral required

A specialist referral is required and it has not been completed.

Q00022

Catch all error - inappropriate outcome

The outcome from the work item is incorrect, e.g. the claim has been granted when it should have been rejected or vice versa.

For compliance, this error applies when the assessment result is incorrect, e.g. debt amount incorrect, debt period incorrect, no debt outcome presents when a debt should exist.

Q00023

Catch all error - incorrect date of event

The date of event is coded incorrectly

Q00024

Catch all error - work item requires ICT Service Desk intervention

The work item status needs to be reset from QMA to In Process and therefore held for ICT referral.
Note: this is a non-proficiency impacting error

Q00025

Catch all error - an update is required to the Assurer Record or AoS Case

An update is required on the assurer record or Assurance of Support (AoS) case.

Q00026

Catch all error - booking links not complete or incorrect

An update is required to a travel management booking.

Q00027

Catch all error - one-off payment incorrect

A one off payment is made via OOP and it is incorrect.

Q00028

Catch all error - Rent Assistance rate incorrect

Incorrect details have been coded on the AC screen that results in the wrong rate of rent assistance being paid.

Q00029

Catch all error - Benefit Transfer incorrectly assessed

This error applies when an incorrect decision was made on the Record Transfer Decision screen

Q00030

Catch all error - Residency incorrectly assessed or customer not residentially qualified

This error applies to emergency payment claims when a customer’s residency status is not reviewed or established including where not all residence data is coded.

Q00031

Catch all error - Incorrect Emergency event or period assessed

This error applies to emergency payment claims when an incorrect Emergency event or period was assessed.

Q00032

Catch all error - Customer’s identity requirements not sufficient to access claim

This error applies to emergency payment claims when a customer’s identity status is not checked, identity documents are not verified or identity exemption reviews are not coded.
