PRODA Access - general

Dear [insert client's name]

Thank you for your enquiry.

To lodge a claim in DVA Webclaim on behalf of the service provider requires that you have your own PRODA account, and the service provider must delegate access to you.

If you have a PRODA account, and the service provider has delegated access to you, please advise us and we will investigate the issue further. Please note delegation requests only last up to 12 months and need renewing annually.

If you have a PRODA account but the service provider has not granted you access, please advise him or her to:

  • log on to HPOS
  • select My Details tile
  • select My delegates
  • add new delegate

A delegation request can also be lodged by:

  • log on to HPOS
  • select the My Providers tile
  • select Request Delegation
