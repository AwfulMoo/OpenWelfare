In Aged Care Management Payment System (ACMPS), the Home Care Account tab displays all the home care account entries for a care recipient.

To locate a care recipientâ€™s home care account in ACMPS:

  • Log on to ACMPS
  • Select Care Recipient Search option from the top menu

  • Key Care Recipient ID or search by surname, first name and DOB

  • Select the relevant care recipient

    • The Home Care Account tab displays all the home care account entries for the relevant care recipient
