Completing the Partner details (MOD P) 106-07110060

The page contains an explanation for some of the more complex questions on the MOD P; questions such as name, address and contact details have been left out. Where further forms are required, the customer's partner should be directed to the Forms page on Services Australia website to download the form. The Resources page contains a link to the website.

MOD P questions

Item

Question

1

Q1-11 - About you + Read more ...

The customer must provide the following details about themselves at questions 1-11:

  • their Customer Reference Number (CRN) if known
  • full name
  • date of birth
  • relationship status (Married, Registered relationship or De facto relationship)
  • any change to their phone number
  • Partner Permission to Enquire
  • whether they are in receipt of or claiming Carer Allowance, Commonwealth Seniors Health Card or Low Income Health Care Card only
  • whether they are in receipt of Family Tax Benefit and/or Child Care Subsidy only
  • any change to their address or accommodation since they have last told Services Australia (which may require return of Mod AC)
  • any change to their income or assets as a result of becoming partnered (which may require return of MOD IA)
  • whether their partner is in receipt of an income support payment, if applicable (which may require the return of a MOD IA)

2

Q12-82 - About your partner + Read more ...

The customer must provide the following details about their partner at questions 12-82:

  • Customer Reference Number (CRN)
  • full name
  • if they have been known by any other name
  • gender
  • date of birth
  • if an interpreter is required when dealing with the agency
  • preferred spoken and written language
  • contact details
  • if they live or will live in the same home as the customer
  • their address if they don’t live with the customer
  • reason why they are not living with the customer (if applicable)
  • the period they’re not living with the customer (if applicable)
  • if they are currently claiming or receiving an income support payment or Low Income Health Care Card from Services Australia
  • any change to their income or assets as a result of becoming partnered (which may require the completion and return of MOD IA)
  • if they are of Aboriginal or Torres Strait Islander descent (this is a voluntary question)
  • the country they currently live in
  • their travel details
  • their citizenship details
  • their Tax File Number (TFN)
  • if they have any income and/or assets (in and/or outside Australia) and a list of possible income/assets for them to select. Selection/s may result in the requirement to complete and return a MOD IA
  • any employment related income
  • any business interests
  • any compensation
  • any other money received
  • any bank accounts
  • any assets
  • any superannuation or financial assets
  • any other income and assets

3

Q83-88 - About your family + Read more ...

The customer and their partner must provide the following details about their family at questions 83-88:

  • any children or students in their care under 20 years of age
  • whether they are in receipt of Family Tax Benefit and/or Child Care Subsidy for the child(ren) in their care
  • taxable income for Family Tax Benefit and/or Child Care Subsidy purposes
  • eligible Child Care Subsidy hours
  • any shared care arrangements with someone other than the current partner

4

Q89 - Checklist + Read more ...

A checklist is provided to assist the customer and/or their partner in determining what supporting documentation must be lodged with the MOD P.

5

Q90 - Privacy Notice + Read more ...

The customer and partner should read this information regarding their privacy and personal information.

6

Q91 - Declaration + Read more ...

The customer and partner should read the declaration and both the customer and partner are required to sign and date the form.
