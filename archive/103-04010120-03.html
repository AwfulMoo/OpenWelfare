Approving requests to participate in the Group Payment Scheme

Step

Action

1

Group payment request received + Read more ...

An organisation contacts Services Australia asking to commence participation in the Group Payment Scheme for the residents in its care or customers whose financial affairs they manage.

  • If organisation contacts, send an email to the Seniors, Carers, Nominees and Bereavement Programme with contact details for the organisation and the group payment section will make contact. For contact details, see Bereavement. To view the actions the group payment section will take, go to Step 2.
  • If customer/organisation provides completed:
    • 'Please send my payment to an institution - Group Payment' (SS270) form or
    • 'Authorising a person or organisation to enquire or act on your behalf' (SS313) form,
    • go to Step 3

2

Approval of organisation to participate in the Group Payment Scheme + Read more ...

The Senior, Carers, Nominees and Bereavement Programme will undertake the following actions:

  • Email a Service Level Agreement (SLA) for completion to the organisation
  • When the SLA has been returned, have it signed by the delegated officer
  • Scan the signed form and email back to the organisation. Request the organisation’s bank account and contact details
  • Create a new TPP CRN for the organisation and attach the SLA
  • Contact the Payments Helpdesk to create a new 3 digit group home code in RDF with bank account details and to link this to the TPP CRN
  • Advise the organisation when the action has been completed. Staff from the organisation must create a Provider Digital Access (PRODA) and link it to Business Hub to access the schedules

3

Organisation approved for group payment and SS270 and SS313 forms completed + Read more ...

When the organisation is approved for group payments and provides the completed SS270 and SS313 forms, process the returned forms.

For processing details for SS270, see Group payment schedules and payments to organisations.

For processing details for SS313, see Adding or rejecting a nominee request.

4

Group payment schedules available via Business Hub + Read more ...

Group payment schedules will be available via Letters in Business Hub.

The Resources page contains a link to a listing of organisations approved to receive their schedules online. Use the organisation's three digit Group Payment code to locate the organisation on the listing.

For further information, see Group payment schedules and payments to organisations.

Note: if there is a problem with delivery of schedules, lodge a mySupport request immediately.

Has the organisation requested a paper copy of the schedule?

  • Yes, go to Step 5
  • No, procedure ends here

5

Organisation requests a copy of the schedule + Read more ...

If the organisation requests a copy of the schedule, a reprint is available via the History Summary (HS) screen on the organisation's record for up to 62 days after the date of payment.

The schedules will be available in Letters in business Hub for 2 years. However, searches can only be conducted in 3 month blocks.

The organisation will have a record on the Customer's Centrelink record with a stakeholder code and service reason code of 'TPP'.

  • reissue the letter from the HS screen
  • record details on a DOC
