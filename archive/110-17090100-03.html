Employment Income Confirmation (EIC) and Check and Update Past Income (CUPI) Operational Blueprint pages are under review. Compliance Officers must seek advice from the Policy Engagement Team before actioning an EIC or CUPI. Contact the team by the CADO Service Desk page.

This page contains the process for Compliance Officers (CO) who are actioning returned correspondence for compliance interventions. This includes the check and update past income (CUPI) service, Employment Income Confirmation (EIC), Manual Income Confirmation (MIC), Business Income and Bank Interest (BIBI) and Business and other income types (BOIT).

4

Customer conversation + Read more ...

Authenticate the customer before releasing any information.

If the customer is contacting about a suspension, update their address and reissue the contact letter before transferring the customer to the relevant business area for restoration actions to be completed.

Confirm the customer's current address. The suggested dialogue is:

'I am contacting you today to confirm and update your current address. We made an attempt to contact you by letter which was unsuccessful. Can you please confirm your current mailing address?'

Check all the customer's addresses (including postal address), and phone numbers (including mobile number) and record their current contact details using the Change in Contact Details workflow in Customer First.

See Updating address details.

Tell the customer 'The letter we sent was to advise you that you need to confirm or update your employment income online. This relates to a difference in the employment income held by the ATO and the employment income you declared to Centrelink. The letter outlines the steps you need to take. A new letter will be sent by registered mail'

Note: in all instances, attempt to progress and finalise the intervention while the customer is on the phone.

Can the intervention be progressed over the phone?

  • Yes:
    • CUPI or BOIT, go to Step 5
    • EIC, MIC or BIBI, go to Step 6
  • No, reissue the first contact letter:
    • CUPI or BOIT, see Step 2 in Table 8
    • EIC, MIC or BIBI, see Step 3 in Table 8

5

Complete the intervention by phone for CUPI or BOIT + Read more ...

In Process Direct:

  • select
  • select Registered Post Tracking
  • from the Outcome dropdown menu, select Returned to Sender
  • in the Address confirmed or updated? field, select either Confirmed or Updated
  • select OK to change the Integrated Review status back to Open

Note: where the intervention is progressed in the same customer contact as completing the work item, the initial notification letter will not be reissued.

For more information on progressing the intervention for:

  • CUPI, see Check and update past income (CUPI) service
  • BOIT, staff undertaking BOIT interventions refer to the Pilot documentation

Procedure ends here.

6

Complete the intervention by phone for EIC, MIC or BIBI + Read more ...

In Customer First, select:

  • the Registered Post Tracking guided procedure
  • the correspondence status Returned to Sender, then select Next
  • either Confirmed or Updated in the Is the customer record updated with the correct address to reissue the initial notification to? field
  • Finish to change the review status back to Open

Note: where the intervention is progressed in the same customer contact as completing the work item, the initial notification letter will not be reissued.

For more information on progressing the intervention for:

  • EIC, see the Assisted tab in EIC online compliance interventions
  • MIC, see Initiation and customer contact for MIC interventions
  • BIBI, see Initiation and customer contact for Business Income and Bank Interest (BIBI) interventions

Procedure ends here.

7

Confirm address update has not been received + Read more ...

Has the customer provided a new address?

  • Yes, go to Step 8
  • No:
    • if the Returned to Sender (Integrated Review) has a status of For Manual Action with the reason Income Support Benefit Suspension, go to Step 9
    • for all other reasons, procedure ends here

8

Has the initial contact letter been reissued? + Read more ...

Check the Search Outbound Correspondence screen in Customer First to confirm if the first contact letter has already been reissued.

Has the letter been reissued?

  • Yes, procedure ends here
  • No, reissue the first contact letter:
    • CUPI or BOIT, see Step 2 in Table 8
    • EIC, MIC or BIBI, see Step 3 in Table 8

9

Suspension or cancellation + Read more ...

End date all address types from today's date. This will automatically suspend or cancel payment for the reason Whereabouts Unknown (WUK).

In Customer First:

  • go to Address History (ADH) screen
  • select most current HOME address
  • select Continue
  • in the End date field, enter today's date
  • in the Source field, enter 'LETTER'
  • in the Receipt Date field, enter today's date
  • repeat the process for the most current POSTAL address
  • check Assessment Result (AR) activity
  • review the SUS or CAN result
  • select Continue to finalise the activity

Record decisions in the intervention DOC. See the Resources page for the documenting requirements for registered mail processing for compliance interventions.

Note: generate a Display on Access (DOA) DOC on the customer record to ensure if/when the customer contacts or comes back onto payment, their address and payments are restored before being referred to the Compliance Risk Branch. See the Resources page for the Documenting requirements for registered mail processing for compliance interventions which includes the Display on Access DOC.

Procedure ends here.

Make 2 genuine attempts to contact the partner on all available numbers. Follow the phone contact requirements.

Note: the work item will not be able to be actioned for the second contact attempt until the following day.

Record all attempts to contact the partner in Notes.

Was contact successful?

  • Yes, go to Step 8
  • No, go to Step 7

7

Contact with partner is not successful + Read more ...

Record all contact attempts in Process Direct:

  • Select Yes for Did you call all of their contact numbers?
  • Select No for Did you successfully speak with the customer on the phone?
  • Select Done

Complete any outstanding actions on the customer's record.

Procedure ends here.

8

Conversation with the partner + Read more ...

Confirm the customer's current address. The suggested dialogue is:

'I am contacting you today to confirm your current address. We made an attempt to contact you by letter that was unsuccessful. Can you please confirm your current mailing address?'

Check all the customer's addresses (including postal address), and phone numbers (including mobile number) and record their current contact details using the Change in Contact Details workflow.
