Raising the debt

Table 2:

Step

Action

1

Debt Action script + Read more ...

Use the Debt Action script to raise the debt.

The debt action script:

  • Generates a SS340 Account Payable if the debt does not include Carer Supplement (CS). Amend information on the OPAOD screen, to suit the customers' circumstances
  • Generates a Q417 manual debt advice when the debt includes CS. Send the advice ensuring the letter specifies the amount of CP debt and CS debt. See Sending Account Payable letters

Is the script available and working?

  • Yes, follow the prompts, go to Step 3
  • No, follow the manual procedure, go to Step 2

2

Raise the debt + Read more ...

To raise the debt:

  • action on the Debt Management and Information System (DMIS)
  • send the account payable letter to the customer
  • finalise the activity and note the Debt ID
  • go to Step 3

3

Document all details of the debt + Read more ...

Include the Debt ID number in the summary line.

See Creating, reviewing and deleting documents (Fast Note, Callbacks, ACTDOCs and DOA DOCs) and Online Document Recording (ODR).

Note: if the debt is less than $50 the system will automatically waive the debt.

4

Determine if the customer's tax details need adjustment for their Payment Summary record + Read more ...

Note: the payment summary (Group Certificate) record of the customer must be adjusted regardless of whether or not they are the payee.

Did the excess payment (negative adjustment) match the overpayment amount raised?

  • Yes, adjustment of the Payment Summary is not required. Procedure ends here
  • No, adjust the tax details
