CDBS eligibility for the relevant FTB Part A claim period

Table 1: This table describes the period of CDBS eligibility for the relevant Family Tax Benefit (FTB) Part A claim period.

Item

Lump Sum claim period

1

2019/2020

FTB Part A claim must be made by 30 June 2021

CDBS notification for 2021

Note: the customer needs to be eligible for the FTB Part A payment to be eligible for CDBS.

2

2019/2020

FTB Part A claim made between 1 July and 31 December 2021.

CDBS Notification for 2022.

Note: the customer needs to be eligible for the FTB Part A payment to be eligible for CDBS.

CDBS eligibility requirements

Table 2: This table describes the qualifying payments for either parents/guardian or children/teenagers.

Item

Qualifying payments

1

Parent/guardian or their partner gets:

  • FTB Part A
  • Parenting Payment (PP)
  • Double Orphan Pension (DOP)

2

Child/teenager gets:

  • Family Tax Benefit (FTB) Part A
  • Youth Allowance (YA)
  • ABSTUDY
  • Disability Support Pension (DSP)
  • Parenting Payment (PP)
  • Special Benefit (SpB)
  • Carer Payment (CP)

Actioning data that has been unmatched

Table 3: This table describes the action to take where it is confirmed that data has been unmatched.

Item

Data owner and action

1

Medicare system

Example

different:

  • date of birth
  • name

Action

The details can be updated, see Amend a person’s Medicare enrolment details

Note: the person receiving the government payment is the person who can make the enquiry.

  • Where the child/teenager is the person in receipt of ABSTUDY, Youth Allowance or Special Benefit payment, they can make the enquiry
  • Where the child/teenager is part of a family/guardian receiving FTB Part A, the parent/guardian can make the enquiry if they are the recipient of the payment

2

Centrelink system

Example

different:

  • date of birth
  • name

Action

Medicare cannot update these details.

The child/teenager's parent/guardian must contact Centrelink to update the child's/teenager's details. Updates to customer details are transmitted to Medicare on a daily basis.

Note: enquiries relating to the government payment the parent/guardian is receiving must be directed to Centrelink. Enquiries relating to eligibility for CDBS must be directed to CDBS Help desk.

3

Department of Veterans' Affairs (DVA)

Example

different:

  • date of birth
  • name

Action

Services Australia cannot update these details.

The child/teenager's parent/guardian must contact DVA to update the child/teenager's details.

Note: enquiries relating to the young person's eligibility under the Veterans' Children Education Scheme (VCES) and Military Rehabilitation and Compensation Act Education and Training Scheme (MRCAETS) must be forwarded to DVA.
