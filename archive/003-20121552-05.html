Pandemic Leave Disaster Payment - Northern Territory

Claim form

Period of self-isolation, quarantine or caring that starts:

  • between 9 December 2021 and 17 January 2022 (inclusive) Claim for Pandemic Leave Disaster Payment (EM252) form
  • before 9 December 2021, (that is, up to and including 8 December 2021), take a claim for this period over the phone using the verbal claim template. If the customer is not able to submit a claim via phone, use the Claim for Pandemic Leave Disaster Payment - Northern Territory form (EM230) form
