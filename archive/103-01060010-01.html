Death of an income managed customer 103-01060010

This document outlines the processes to be followed upon the death of an income managed customer.

Legal Personal Representative

When notified of an income managed customer's death, Service Officers are to ask the person notifying of the death if they know the customer's Legal Personal Representative (LPR). Where known, documentary evidence is to be provided to Services Australia.

Release of information

When a person advises they are acting on behalf of a deceased customer, and they request details of the remaining Income Management account balance, Service Officers are to verify the person is one of the following before any information is given:

  • the deceased customer's Legal Personal Representative (LPR)
  • next of kin, or
  • the person who has paid for the deceased customer's funeral expenses

If verification is not possible, an application can still be made for the residual funds, providing they cannot be paid directly to the customer's last known payment destination, or that of their payment nominee (if applicable).

Disbursing residual balances

When an income managed customer passes away, the full residual balance will be disbursed to the last known payment destination on the customer's record when the Notification of Death guided procedure is completed.

If the customer had a current payment nominee, the full balance will be disbursed to the payment nominee.

IMPACT investigation

Where there are returned payments from a financial institution, the residual balance will remain in the deceased customer's Income Management account, and the options available for disbursement will be pursued by the Income Management Payment and Contact Team (IMPACT).

IMPACT will liaise with the relevant Zones to investigate how residual funds can be disbursed.

Cultural considerations

Service Officers are to be aware of cultural considerations which need to be considered as customers from different cultural or religious backgrounds may react differently to the death of a loved one. For more information, see Cultural considerations when someone dies.

Late notification of death

Where funds have been credited to a customer's Income Management account after their date of death (payments continuing to be made due to late notification), this portion of income managed funds can be used to offset the debt incurred due to the late notification of death.

The Resources page contains links to the Income Management Policy helpdesk webform, Income Management intranet page, and contact details for the Income Management Payment and Contact Team (IMPACT).
