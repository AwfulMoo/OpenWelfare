Tax exemption calculation examples

These amounts may not be current. They are examples only. For current payments, see Rates and thresholds.

Examples of how the calculate tax exemptions

Table 1

Example

<Description>

1

Example of taxation exemption calculation for Lump Sum Bereavement Payment (LBP)

Age Pensioner couple receiving $393.00 each per fortnight with Energy Supplement of $10.50 and Pension Supplement of $47.40 (includes $29.90 non-taxable components) each per fortnight. No other add-ons received. To calculate the Tax exemption when 1 of the couple dies:

Calculate the Lump Sum Bereavement Payment (LBP)

Combined Member of a Couple Rate is $450.90 x 2 x 7 = $6312.60

New Single Rate is $470.70 plus $13.90 plus $62.90 = $547.50 x7 =$3832.50

LBP is $6312.60 - $3832.50 = $2480.10

Calculate the Tax Free Amount (TFE)

Survivor's Non Taxable Amount if partner had not died is ($10.50 + $29.30) x 7 = $278.60

Deceased partner's gross amount if they had not died is $450.90 x 7 = $3156.30

TFE is $3156.30 plus $278.60 = $3434.90

As the LBP is less than the TFE, the entire LBP is tax free, so no tax adjustment is needed.

2

Example of taxation exemption calculation for ordinary payment

Age Pensioner couple receiving $400.00 excluding supplementary amounts, each per fortnight. Deceased partner had earnings. To calculate the Tax exemption for surviving partner:

Calculate the Tax Free Amount (TFE)

Combined Member of a Couple Rate is $400x 2 = $800

New Single Rate is $843.60 excluding supplementary amounts

$843.60 - $800 = $43.60 x 7

TFE is $305.20 to be deducted from customer's tax record

3

Partner Bereavement Payment (PBV) taxation exemption calculation, where both members of couple received JSP

JSP customer received PBV at pregnant rate, where pregnancy end is <14 weeks. Deceased partner was also in receipt of JSP.

Daily rate $49.86 consists of:

  • Basic rate $39.70
  • Energy Supplement $0.63
  • Rent Assistance $9.80

Energy supplement (ES) and rent assistance (RA) are not taxable. RA is maximum rate.

Calculate the Partner Bereavement Payment (PBV)

Daily rate x 14 x 3 + $1000

$49.86 x 14 x 3 + $1000

= $3,094.12

Calculate the Tax Free Amount (TFE)

Survivor's Non Taxable Amount if partner had not died is ($7.90 ES + $64.60 RA) x 7 = $507.50

Note: survivor's non-taxable amount is at partnered rate

Deceased partner's gross amount if they had not died is $698.04 x 7 = $4886.28

TFE is deceased partner's rate plus survivor's non-taxable amount

$4886.28 + $507.50 = $5,393.78

As the PBV $3,094.12 is less than the TFE $5,393.78, the entire PBV is tax free so no tax adjustment is needed.

4

PBV taxation exemption calculation, where customer claimed JSP

Customer received PBV at non-pregnant rate when JSP claim granted. Deceased partner was not in receipt of any income support payments

Daily rate $49.86 consists of:

  • Basic rate $39.70
  • Energy Supplement $0.63
  • Rent Assistance $9.80

Energy supplement (ES) and rent assistance (RA) are not taxable. RA is maximum rate.

Calculate the Partner Bereavement Payment (PBV)

Daily rate x 14 x 4.5

$49.86 x 14 x 4.5

= $3,141.18

Calculate the Tax Free Amount (TFE)

Survivor's Non Taxable Amount if partner had not died is nil, as customer was not in receipt of any payments.

Deceased partner's gross amount if they had not died is also nil, as they were not in receipt of any payments

TFE is deceased partner's rate plus survivor's non-taxable amount

As both are nil, the TFE is nil

The entire amount of PBV $3,141.18 is taxable, so a tax adjustment may be required.
