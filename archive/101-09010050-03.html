Add or remove characteristics for Centrelink Confirmation eServices (CCeS) businesses 101-09010050

For Program Support Managers only

This page contains the process for the Third Party Programmes team and National Business Gateway to add or remove characteristics for a CCeS business.

Adding or removing CCeS characteristics

This table describes the process for staff to add or remove CCeS characteristics from a CCeS business record. Updates can be made in Customer Record or Customer First.

Step

Action

1

Business requests to change its CCeS characteristics + Read more ...

All requests by a CCeS business to add or remove characteristics to its profile must be in writing from an authorised officer.

If the request is to add new characteristics, the business must detail the additional information required and why it is needed. The Third Party Programmes team (TPP) must ensure there is a legitimate need for the additional customer information before proceeding to add characteristics to a business record. If there is any doubt, evidence should be requested from the business.

Reminder: a CCeS business should only be given access to the minimum amount of customer information required to enable it to confirm entitlement to a concession, rebate or service.

2

Access the business record + Read more ...

Access the business record in the following ways:

  • Coding 'B' in the Env field, 'ISS' in the System field and entering the business record using its Customer Reference Number (CRN)
  • Conduct a search for the business if the CRN is unknown, using the Organisation Name Search (ON) screen or the Advanced Organisation Search (OASP) page

3

View the Org Characteristic Profile + Read more ...

  • Navigate to the OSAS screen. Select ‘S’ against the CCS agreement and then select 'Continue' or [Enter]
  • On the OSAB screen, select ‘Y’ against View Org Characteristic Profile and then select 'Continue' or [Enter]
  • On the OCTS screen, select ‘S’ against the Characteristic Enquiry Group to be updated:
    • Centrelink Characteristics
    • DVA Characteristics
    • Superannuation Characteristics. Note: staff will not need to select or update this group. It has default characteristics that are fixed and cannot be changed or updated)
  • Select 'Continue' or [Enter]

Note: if staff need to update characteristics for multiple Characteristic Enquiry Groups, code the updates separately for each group. However, this can be done within the same activity.

4

Org Characteristic Market Sector (OCMS) screen + Read more ...

The OCMS screen allows staff to view or update the market sector that applies to a CCeS business. The market sector generally matches the business category allocated to the business within their CCeS contract.

When a business requires updates to their characteristics, the market sector should not be changed.

If the business currently has:

  • standard characteristics, go to Step 5
  • non-standard characteristics, go to Step 6

Note: historical pages and information can be viewed by staff by typing in the number of the page they want to go to in the Page field.

5

Business has standard characteristics + Read more ...

If the business has a standard characteristic profile and staff are updating (adding or removing), update the OCMS screen with the following:

  • ‘S’elect the market sector relevant to the business
  • Enter ‘N’ in the Standard (Y/N) field’
  • Enter details in the Source field, DOR field and ‘C’ in the Action field
  • Select 'Continue'
  • Enter ‘Y’ in the Confirm field
  • Select 'Continue' to go to the Org Characteristics Details (OCCD) screen

(Any change to characteristics in a standard profile will change the business to a non-standard characteristic profile).

Go to Step 7.

6

Business has non-standard characteristics + Read more ...

If the business has a non-standard characteristic profile and staff are updating characteristics (adding or removing):

  • Leave all fields blank on the OCMS screen
  • Select ‘Continue' or [Enter] to go to the Org Characteristics Details (OCCD) screen

Go to Step 7.

7

Org Characteristic Details (OCCD) screen + Read more ...

The OCCD screen allows a user to view or update the business’ characteristic profile. For existing CCeS agreements, a ‘Y’ is displayed next to the characteristics that currently apply to the business:

  • ‘Payment Type’ characteristics (e.g. PEN/NSS/ABY etc.) are displayed in the top section of the screen
  • Other characteristics (e.g. HCC/PCC/Address etc.) are then displayed at the bottom

If:

  • adding new characteristics, go to Step 8
  • removing characteristics, go to Step 9

8

Adding characteristics + Read more ...

To add new characteristics, on the OCCD screen:

  • select ‘S’ against all existing characteristics the business already has (as indicated by a ‘Y’), and
  • select ‘S’ against any new characteristics to be added
  • Code Source field ‘INT’, DOR field ‘T’ and Action field ‘I’
  • Select ‘Continue' or [Enter]
  • Enter ‘Y’ in the Confirm field
  • Select ‘Continue' or [Enter]
  • Go to Step 10

Important: there are often multiple pages of characteristics on the CCS record. When updating characteristic profiles, check all pages and select all existing characteristics. To scroll through multiple pages, use any of the following options:

  • type the number in the Column field
  • the PF11 and PF10 keys (in Customer Record), or
  • the Move Right and Move Left buttons (in Customer First)

Tip: if selecting the Date Paid To, Payment End Date, Payment Status and/or Date of Grant characteristics, at least one Payment Type characteristic must be selected or an error will present.

9

Removing characteristics + Read more ...

To remove characteristics (if they are no longer required):

  • Select ‘S’ only the existing characteristics that are to remain
  • Do not select any characteristics that are to be removed (if they are selected in error, they will not be removed)
  • Code Source field ‘INT’, DOR field ‘T’ and Action field ‘I’
  • Select ‘Continue' or [Enter]
  • Enter ‘Y’ in the Confirm field
  • Select ‘Continue' or [Enter]
  • Go to Step 10

Important: there are often multiple pages of characteristics on the CCS record. When updating characteristic profiles, check all pages and select all characteristics. To scroll through multiple pages, use any of the following options:

  • type the number in the Column field
  • the PF11 and PF10 keys (in Customer Record), or
  • the Move Right and Move Left buttons (in Customer First)

10

Org Work Unit Summary (OWUS) screen + Read more ...

Staff may also need to update the work unit details (if one exists) when adding or removing characteristics on a business record via the OWUS screen.

The OWUS screen allows staff to view a summary of work units that are currently attached to a business. Note: new work units cannot be added to a business record.

Navigate to the OWUS screen.

Are there existing work units on the OWUS screen?

  • Yes. Code the following:
    • On the OWUS screen, select ‘S’ the CCS Work Unit. Select ‘Continue' or [Enter]
    • On the OWUD screen, select ‘Continue' or [Enter]
    • On the OCTS screen, select ‘S’ against the appropriate Characteristic Enquiry Group (Centrelink or DVA). Select ‘Continue' or [Enter]
    • On the OCCD screen:
      if adding characteristics, select ‘S’ against all existing characteristics the business already has, as indicated by a ‘Y’, as well as any new characteristics just added
      if removing characteristics, select ‘S’ only the existing characteristics that are to remain. Do not select any characteristics that are to be removed (if they are selected in error, they will not be removed)
    • Code footer details with Action field ‘C’ and select ‘Continue' or [Enter]
    • Confirm ‘Y’ and select ‘Continue' or [Enter]
    • Go to Step 11
  • No. Select ‘Continue' or [Enter] and go to Step 11

11

Updates required to additional Characteristic Enquiry Groups + Read more ...

Are there other Characteristic Enquiry Groups to be updated?

  • Yes. Repeat Steps 3 to 10 in this table ensuring to select the next group to be updated via the OCTS screen
  • No. Select ‘Continue' or [Enter] and go to Step 12

12

Complete the update on the Assessment Results (AR) screen + Read more ...

Navigate to the AR screen and complete the activity to finalise updates.

13

Notify the business + Read more ...

The business can be notified by email that the request to add or remove characteristics is complete.

14

DOC the record + Read more ...

Add a DOC to the record to detail the updates made, including the name of the authorised officer who made the request to change the characteristics.
