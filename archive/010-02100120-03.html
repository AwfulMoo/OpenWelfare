Step

Action

Eligibility for Fares Allowance for ABSTUDY + Read more ...

Note: an Australian Apprentice is not entitled to FAA for the Australian Apprenticeship.

Is the customer eligible for FAA for ABSTUDY?

  • Yes, go to Step 2
  • No, see Step 3 in the Eligibility for FAA for vacation travel table

2

Course requirements + Read more ...

Is the student undertaking a course that is:

  • tertiary or secondary non-schooling level, and
  • more than 1 semester in length?

Check the Education Course Details (EDC) screen:

  • Course Level field to determine the level of course, and
  • Course Start Date and Course End Date field to determine the length of course

Does the course meet the required level and course length?

  • Yes, go to Step 3
  • No, see Step 3 in the Eligibility for FAA for vacation travel table

3

Check if student is residing away from permanent home + Read more ...

Vacation travel is only available to students who are entitled to commencement and end of study travel to move away from their permanent home to undertake a course of study.

Students undertaking Away from Base activities may qualify for alternative assistance for Away from Base travel.

Is the student residing away from their permanent home address in order to undertake an Away from Base activity?

  • Yes, see Step 3 in the Eligibility for FAA for vacation travel table
  • No, go to Step 4

4

Has dependant travel been accessed + Read more ...

Check the previous Bookings in the Travel Management Application (TMA) in Process Direct to determine if the student has utilised dependant's travel in the year of study to bring their dependent family members to live with them at the study location.

Note: vacation travel is not available to students whose dependants have joined them at the study location, as there is no need to travel to the permanent home location to reunite a family.

Has dependant travel been used in the year of study?

  • Yes, see Step 3 in the Eligibility for FAA for vacation travel table
  • No, go to Step 5

5

Study commencement + Read more ...

Has the student commenced study in the course for the year?

Note: vacation travel can only be accessed after the student has commenced study in the course for the year.

  • Yes, go to Step 6
  • No, see Step 3 in the Eligibility for FAA for vacation travel table

6

Travel requirements + Read more ...

Is the student travelling between the study location and the permanent home address?

  • Yes, see Step 1 in the Eligibility for FAA for vacation travel table
  • No, go to Step 7

7

Approval of vacation travel + Read more ...

Advise the student that vacation travel is usually approved only between the permanent home and the study location.

Does the student have circumstances that allow travel to be approved to or from a location other than the permanent home address or usual study location?

  • Yes, see Step 1 in the Eligibility for FAA for vacation travel table
  • No, see Step 3 in the Eligibility for FAA for vacation travel table

Eligibility for FAA for vacation travel

Table 2: This table describes the circumstances under which an ABSTUDY tertiary or secondary non-schooling student can be eligible for FAA for vacation travel during the study year.

Step

Action

1

Check if student is eligible for FAA for vacation travel + Read more ...

Check if the student has previously claimed FAA for this travel this year as vacation travel may only be claimed once per year for a course.

Has FAA for vacation travel already been claimed?

  • Yes, go to Step 3
  • No, go to Step 2

2

Eligible for FAA + Read more ...

Advise the student, that they are approved and entitled to undertake vacation travel. Determine if the student requires FAA to be:

  • authorised and booked in advance of travel, or
  • reimbursed after the travel has been undertaken The student will be required to complete an ABSTUDY Fares Allowance Claim (SY032). Procedure ends here.

3

Ineligible for FAA + Read more ...

Advise the student they are not eligible for FAA for vacation travel.

If the travel request has been received by ABSTUDY Smart Centre telephony, record details of discussion on a DOC. Explain their review and appeal rights. See Initial contact about a decision and the review of decision process.

If the travel request has been received by the ABSTUDY Smart Centre Travel Team, finalise the Travel Booking status as 'Rejected' in the Travel Management Application (TMA). Record details of the decision in the Notes field. See Coding pre-booked Fares Allowance (FAA) entitlements for ABSTUDY and ABSTUDY Pensioner Education Supplement (PES)

Check if the student's circumstances will entitle them to access alternate FAA travel types.

Record details on a DOC.
