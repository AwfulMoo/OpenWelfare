1

Undertake proof of record ownership

Always check the customer's identity when dealing with requests to change payment destination and issuing refunds to former customers.

As well as the normal authentication checks, ask the customer:

  • to state their current payment destination details (before the requested change). This can be either BSB code and account number, or branch name and account number
  • the approximate amount of their last Services Australia payment to that account. There are extra checks for customers turning 18 who want to change payment into their own bank account
  • if they are a JobSeeker Payment customer, if they had casual earnings on their last report:
    • employer's name
    • wages declared (accept approximate figures)
  • if applicable, their child’s middle name and date of birth

There are no set questions. Take extra care and make sure the customer can answer questions that are not common knowledge to another person.

The customer may not know the organisation's account details if the current payment destination is a rehabilitation centre, correctional centre trust account or respite nursing home. The customer can state their previous payment destination details and the amount of their last payment to that account. This does not include by group schedule.

If the customer cannot provide these details or the details are incorrect, ask more questions before updating the account details. See Step 7 of Authenticating a customer.

Create Q888

In Customer Record

Check the Nominee Linking Summary (NOLS) screen to see if the customer has a correspondence nominee. If they do, issue the same Q888 letter to the correspondence nominee.

  • Go to the OLA Accept Service Reasons (OASR) screen
    • Key Service Reason, e.g. JSP, PEN,
    • Key 'Q888' in Letter Code field and press [Enter]
  • On the Letter Display (LDT) screen
    • Key 'OPT' in Command field and press [Enter]
    • The Letter Display Options (LDO) screen displays
  • The LDO screen shows options for variable text or data to include in the letter
    • Select options by keying these numbers in the field at the end of each line
      PHN008: 1
      CLORTP: 2
      NOMCLI: 2 (if the customer has a correspondence nominee, key 1)
      NOMCUS: 1
      FRTX1: 1
      FRTX2 to FRTX6: 2
      PPLACT: 2
      SSALF: 7
      CHAT: 2
      REVCHG: 2
      APP999: 2
      ABSAIC: 1
      DAYWKS: 1
      PRVACT: 1
    • For free text paragraphs, key '1' next to paragraphs needed, '2' next to those not needed. Make sure to complete the field for each line
  • In the Command field, key 'DATA' and press [Enter]
  • The Variable Data (LDD) screen displays
    • Key in the phone number field
    • In free text paragraphs enter the required text, per items below
    • Make sure contact information and other details in the letter are correct

Refer to Quality Development Officer (QDO) for quality checking. The QDO will then finalise the letter and issue to the customer.

  • On the LDT screen, code 'HS' in the Nxt field and press [Enter]. The HS screen will display letter status of PR (printed)
