Cancel or withdraw online claim using Centrelink online account

Table 1

Step

Action

1

Access Centrelink services myGov account + Read more ...

Customer accesses My online claims page to:

  • Cancel their started claim, go to Step 2
  • Withdraw their submitted claim:
    • For all SAP (non-legacy) online claims, go to Step 3
    • For leqacy claims submitted:
      in the past 14 days, go to Step 3
      more than 14 days ago, the customer must contact to request a Service Officer withdraw the claim, procedure ends here

2

Cancel claim + Read more ...

The customer is to select the Cancel claim option.

A warning tells them if they cancel their claim they will not be able to:

  • view the claim details
  • continue with their claim
  • undo this action

If they accept the declaration and cancel the claim:

  • My online claims page displays information about the cancelled claim including:
    • claim ID
    • claim type
    • date it was cancelled
  • They cannot select it to view further details
  • Claim information will not store or transfer to the customer's Centrelink record. A history of the claim activity can be viewed in the customer's record

Procedure ends here.

3

Withdraw claim + Read more ...

The customer is to select the Withdraw claim option.

A warning tells them if they choose to withdraw their claim, they cannot:

  • reinstate it
  • appeal the decision

If the customer accepts the declaration and withdraws their claim:

  • Confirmation displays (see Resources page for more information)
  • My online claims page displays information about the withdrawn claim including:
    • claim ID
    • claim type
    • date it was withdrawn
  • They cannot select it to view further details
  • Claim details are can be viewed in the customer's record. The claim status updates to withdrawn
  • The system will create a DOC on the customer's record stating they have withdrawn their online claim

4

Auto system action + Read more ...

The system searches for any claim activities associated with the claim type:

Are all expected claim activities still open on a PER record?

  • Yes, go to Step 5
  • No, go to Step 6

5

All claim activities open on PER record + Read more ...

The system automatically:

  • Cancels all claim activities for the claim type
  • Creates a DOC stating the customer has withdrawn their online claim. DOCs have a keyword of CANOCXXX where XXX is the claim type

Procedure ends here.

6

Manual Follow-up (MFU) required + Read more ...

The system will create a Cancel Claim Follow-up ACTDOC for claim activities that have:

  • a status of Completed, Cancelled or Submitted
  • been determined

ACTDOCs have a keyword of CANOCXXX where XXX is the claim type.

Action Required

  • Investigate claim record
  • If the claim is determined, contact customer to discuss withdrawal

For more information, see:

  • Withdrawal of claims
  • Not effective claims or withdrawal of claim for Family Tax Benefit or Stillborn Baby Payment

Cancel or withdraw online claim using the Express Plus Centrelink mobile app

Table 2

Step

Action

1

Access Express Plus Centrelink mobile app + Read more ...

To access the My Claims screen in the app:

  • select Menu
  • select My Claims

This will display a list of claims.

Claims will not display on this screen until the customer has:

  • reviewed their claim
  • accepted the declaration
  • moved to the Next steps page from within their Centrelink online account

If the customer wants to:

  • cancel a started claim, go to Step 2
  • withdraw a submitted claim, go to Step 3

2

Cancel claim + Read more ...

The customer selects the cancel claim option.

A warning tells them if they cancel their claim they will no longer be able to:

  • view the claim details
  • continue with their claim

They cannot undo this action.

If they accept the declaration and cancel the claim:

  • Your Claims page displays information about the cancelled claim including:
    • claim ID
    • claim type
    • start date
    • date cancelled
  • Claim information will not store or transfer to the customer's Centrelink record. A history of the claim activity can be viewed in the customer's record

Procedure ends here.

3

Withdraw claim + Read more ...

    • date withdrawn
  • They cannot select it to view further details
  • Claim details are can be viewed in the customer's record. The claim status updates to Withdrawn

The system will create a DOC on the customer's record stating they have withdrawn their online claim.
