Video Chat appointments

Item

Description

1

Use of video chats + Read more ...

Video chat is currently being used for a number of services, including:

  • Confirm your identity appointments
    • JobSeeker Payment
    • Youth Allowance (Student or Jobseeker) online claims only
  • Social worker appointments
  • Random Sample Surveys (RSS)
  • Assessment Services (Job Capacity Assessments), and
  • Financial Information Service (FIS) Officer interviews

Note: new customers will receive an option to book a video chat appointment to confirm their identity. All other video chat appointments are verbally offered by trained staff.

2

Customers eligible for video chat appointments + Read more ...

Video chat appointments are not available to all customers. Customers must be triaged by trained staff. To receive the option to attend a video chat the customer must meet the following eligibility requirements:

  • do not need an interpreter
  • are not a restricted access customer
  • are registered for secure online mail and electronic messaging
  • are not a nominee lodging a claim on behalf of a customer

The customer must not have an open Confirm your identity video chat appointment to be eligible for a confirm your identity video chat appointment.

3

Customers contact about a video chat appointment + Read more ...

  • Use the Appointment screen in Process Direct to view appointment details
  • Notes are optional and may not be visible on the customer record
  • Customers will receive a myGov confirmation letter for their booked video chat appointment
  • Appointments will display within the customers Centrelink online account and Express Plus Centrelink mobile app
  • Customers can join their video chat appointment through the URL at the bottom of their myGov confirmation letter, or
  • Customers can select the Join meeting button. This button will display on the day of the appointment in their Centrelink online account or Express Plus Centrelink mobile app. Note: customers may need to download the Teams app when using a mobile device
  • Staff can assist customers to book a Confirm your identity video chat appointment for eligible online claims only
  • Only selected staff will book all other video chat appointment types

Staff must use Process Direct to manage video chat appointments.

4

Helping customers to view their video chat appointments + Read more ...

Staff can help customers view their scheduled video chat appointments in:

myGov:

  • sign into their myGov account
  • select Inbox from the menu bar
  • select the Centrelink video appointment letter

Centrelink online account:

  • sign into their myGov account
  • select Services from the menu bar
  • select Centrelink from their linked services
  • select Documents and Appointments
  • select Manage Appointments

Express Plus Centrelink mobile app:

  • sign into their Express Plus Centrelink mobile app
  • on the home page, select Appointment Details
  • select the Video Chat appointment

5

Customer request a video chat appointment + Read more ...

Staff must use Process Direct to create video chat appointments.

Only eligible customers completing an online claim to confirm their identity can request a video chat appointment.

To book a video chat appointment customers must:

  • have a recorded address
  • be subscribed to myGov inbox
  • be subscribed to online letters

Staff should only book a video chat appointment where the customer meets all criteria below:

  • lodges an eligible online claim
  • needs to complete identity confirmation as per the 'Next Steps' stage, and
  • meets the eligibility requirements in the above section

If a customer requests to book a video chat appointment to confirm their identity:

  • use the Appointments Icon to create video chat appointment:
  • select the Create new appointment icon
  • select Appointment Type Video Chat (Trial only)
  • select Appointment Channel Video Conference
  • select Appointment Reason Confirm your identity
  • select Location Virtual Service Centre
  • customer time zone: select the customer's time zone based on their location
  • select an available time and Save. Do not book an appointment less than 90 minutes ahead of the current Australian Eastern Standard Time

The appointment will now display on the Booked Appointments table and the customer will receive a myGov confirmation letter.

Tell the customer:

  • they may be unable to submit their claim until their identity is confirmed, and
  • to have their photographic identity documents on hand for their video chat appointment

DOC the conversation on the customer's record.

6

Customer requests to reschedule video chat appointment + Read more ...

Staff must use Process Direct to reschedule video chat appointments.

If a customer requests to reschedule a video chat appointment for confirm your identity:

  • use the Appointments icon to reschedule video chat appointment
  • select the appropriate Booked Appointments row
  • select the Reschedule selected appointments icon. Do not book an appointment less than 90 minutes ahead of the current Australian Eastern Standard Time (AEST)
  • select reason for reschedule Customer Request
  • select the new Appointment Details from available times and Save

The appointment times will display in the customer's local time zone.

Tell the customer:

  • they may be unable to submit their claim until their identity is confirmed, and
  • to have their photographic identity documents on hand for their video chat appointment

DOC the conversation on the customer's record.

If a customer requests to reschedule any other video chat appointment type, refer the customer to their myGov confirmation letter. The letter outlines the relevant phone number for the customer to contact to discuss.

7

Customer requests to cancel a video chat appointment + Read more ...

Staff must use Process Direct to cancel video chat appointments.

If a customer requests to cancel a video chat appointment for confirm your identity:

  • use the Appointments icon to cancel video chat appointment
  • select the appropriate Booked Appointments row
  • select the Change selected appointment icon
  • select Cancelled from New Status
  • select the appropriate reason in Cancellation Reason and Save

Tell the customer they:

  • may not be able to submit their new claim until their identity linkage is completed
  • will not receive confirmation that the appointment was cancelled, and
  • must attend a service centre to complete their 'Prove your identity' task in order to submit their claim

DOC the conversation on the customer's record.

If a customer requests to cancel any other video chat appointment type, refer the customer to their myGov confirmation letter. The letter outlines the relevant phone number for the customer to contact to discuss.

8

Technical issues + Read more ...

If customers experience technical issues attending a video chat, use the video chat issues form to lodge a mySupport request. The Resources page contains links to mySupport and the video chat issues form.

Open mySupport before opening the form and include the:

  • Customer Reference Number (CRN)
  • device customer was using
  • letter or access point the customer was using to attend

If staff experience technical issues Creating, Rescheduling or Cancelling a video chat, use the video chat issues form to lodge a mySupport request.

Open mySupport before opening the form and include the:

  • CRN
  • appointment reason for the video chat
  • cancellation reason
