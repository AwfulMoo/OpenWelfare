Assess claim for multiple services on the same day for the same patient

Table 1

Step

Action

1

What items are being claimed? + Read more ...

  • multiple of the same item (e.g. 2 x 91721) for the same date of service, go to Step 2
  • different items for the same date of service (e.g. 91721 & 23), go to Step 3

See Assessing MBS item restrictions for more information.

2

Multiple claims for same item on same day + Read more ...

The provider is to include text with their claim showing there was a reasonable lapse of time between the services.

Does the claim include text showing separate times (e.g. 9:00 am and 2:00 pm)?

  • Yes, follow the instructions in the return message to pay the claim
  • No, follow the instructions in the return message to reject the claim

The procedure ends here.

3

Multiple claims for different items on the same day + Read more ...

The provider is to include text with their claim that shows the services:

  • were for unrelated conditions, or
  • there was a reasonable lapse of time between them

Does the claim include text (e.g. unrelated, separate and/or 10:30 am and 2:00 pm)?

  • Yes, follow the instructions in the return message to pay the claim
  • No, follow the instructions in the return message to reject the claim

The procedure ends here.

Check eligibility for treatment services

Table 2: this table describes the process to check a patient’s eligibility for mental health treatment services if affected by bushfires (91000 – 91731).

Step

Action

1

Complete Security check + Read more ...

Note: proceed only if the caller meets the security check.

Advise the caller that the eligibility information provided is based on the patient’s Medicare claiming history and is subject to change if there are claims pending.

See Perform security check when a health professional or their staff call about a claim table.

2

Retrieve the patient’s history + Read more ...

  • Key NHSI,CARDNUMBER,PATIENT and press [Enter]
  • Press [PF9]. The patient PIN is displayed

Note: check history by PIN in all instances to ensure the complete claiming history is returned, not just the history associated with 1 card.

3

Check for mental health treatment items on the patient’s history + Read more ...

Count the number of mental health treatment services on the patient’s claims history since 1 January.

  • Key NHSI,PIN,ITEM/91000-91731,SORT/DOS and press [Enter]

Have less than 10 services been provided since 1 January (current year)?

  • Yes, advise the caller that the patient is eligible for a mental health treatment service. The process ends here.
  • No, advise the caller that the patient is not eligible for these mental health treatment service items. The process ends here.
