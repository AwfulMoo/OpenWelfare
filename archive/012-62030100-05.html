Accepted

Circumstance outside of Approved Supplier’s reasonable control

Original prescription lost due to poor housekeeping. For example:

  • prescriptions lost during a move,
  • forgot to detach the pharmacy copy when dispensing,
  • dispensed medicine to a patient in good faith expecting the prescription to be handed over later

Cannot be processed

These situations can be considered as poor administrative practice by the Approved Supplier. These are not outside of reasonable control
