                Mutual obligation pause and resumption dates

                These tables outline historical mutual obligation pause and resumption dates in response to COVID-19 outbreaks.

                Note: Job seeker compliance does not apply when mutual obligation requirements are paused.

                Before using this table, check if there are current Operational Messages in place covering the failure date and location. Where there is no current Operational Message for the failure date being reviewed, continue to the process below.

                How to use this table

                • Each state and territory has a separate table
                • The dates affecting some or all locations of the state or territory are listed and are coordinated with paused and resumption dates
                • To find if mutual obligations were paused at the time of the failure:
                  • Go to the table for the relevant state/territory
                  • In the paused/resumed columns, find the period that covers the failure date
                  • If the failure date is not covered by a paused period, the customer was subject to mutual obligation requirements
                  • If the failure date is covered by one of the paused periods, check the LGA column to see if the job seeker's location was affected
                  • where the job seeker's location was affected on the failure date, check the 'details' column for specific mutual obligation pause information
                  • where the job seeker's location was not affected, the customer was subject to full mutual obligation requirements

                Some locations were affected by multiple COVID-19 outbreaks. This means that in these areas mutual obligation and job seeker compliance arrangements will have multiple pause and resumption dates

                Full mutual obligation requirements paused for all job seekers

                Partial mutual obligation requirements

                When a state/territory exits extended lockdown and has achieved 70% vaccination rate, the following mutual obligation activities resume:

                • attend Provider Appointments
                • to agree to a new or amended Job Plan within 2 days

                Full mutual obligation requirements

                When a state/territory that has been in an extended lockdown reaches a vaccination target of 80% second dose vaccination rate, full mutual obligation requirements resume.

                Job search requirements (or Points Period requirements if applicable) resume for each job seeker from the start of their next full reporting period after their state or territory's resumption date.

                Note: this phased approach does not apply to CDP.
