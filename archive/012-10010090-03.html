View or print practice payment history

Table 1

Step

Action

1

Log into systems + Read more ...

Log on to these systems:

  • PIP Online, or
  • WIP - Practice Stream Online

2

Search for the practice + Read more ...

On the Practice Search screen:

  • Key the Practice ID
  • Select Search. The Search Results will show

Check if the practice name and address on the practice profile match the practice details.

If the details match, go to Step 3

If they do not match, check the company or trading name:

  • Select the Practice ID. The Practice Summary screen will show

  • Select Ownership Details from the drop down. The Ownership Details screen will show

Do these details match the practice details?

  • Yes, go to Step 3

3

View the payment history + Read more ...

View the practice’s payments:

  • Select Payments from the Main menu
  • Select Payment History from the drop down. The Payment History screen will show

To view a PIP payment history, under Filter criteria:

  • Select the relevant payment year from the Select Year drop down
  • Select Get Details
  • The payment records for the relevant payment year will show

To view a WIP - Practice Stream payment history, under Filter criteria:

  • Select the relevant payment month from the Calculation Month drop down
  • Select Search
  • The payment records from the relevant payment month will show

The Payment Status column will show the status of a payment.

Do not tell the practice the payment amount unless the payment status is Paid.

The Payment Status Types table on the Resources page has a list of payment status types.

4

View payment details + Read more ...

To view a PIP payment, under PIP Payments:

  • Go to the relevant payment month from the Calculation Quarter column
  • Select View from the Details column. The Payment Details screen will show

  • Go to the relevant:
    • payment month from the Calculation Month column, and
    • payment type from the Payment Type column
  • Select View Details from the Action column. The Payment Details screen will show

5

View a Quarterly Payment Advice + Read more ...

To view a PIP payment advice, under PIP Payments:

  • Go to the relevant payment month from the Calculation Quarter column
  • Select View PDF from the Payment Advice column
  • A ‘What do you want to do with PIP Quarterly Payment Advice?’ message will show
  • Select Open. The Quarterly Payment Advice will show

To view a WIP - Practice Stream payment advice, under Payment History:

  • Go to the relevant:
    • Payment month from the Calculation Month column, and
    • payment type from the Payment Type column
  • Select View PDF from the Action column. A ‘What do you want to do with PIP Quarterly Payment Advice?’ message will show
  • Select Open. The Quarterly Payment Advice will show

6

Print a Quarterly Payment Advice + Read more ...

To print the Quarterly Payment Advice:

  • Right mouse click on the Quarterly Payment Advice
  • Select Print
  • Select the relevant print options
  • Select Print

Check Indigenous Health Payments decrease + Read more ...

Indigenous Health Sign on payment

If there is an Indigenous Health Sign on payment decrease, advise the practice that this is a 1 off practice sign-on payment to register for the PIP IHI.

The sign-on payment is paid in the next payment quarter after the Indigenous Health Incentive (IHI) approval date. The approval date is located on the Incentive Summary screen.

Indigenous Health Patient registration

If there is an Indigenous Health patient registration decrease, advise the practice:

  • all patient registrations submitted at least 7 days before the point-in-time date are actioned
  • patient registrations submitted within 7 days of the point-in-time date may contribute to the next quarterly payment calculation
  • all patient registrations submitted after the point-in-time date will contribute to the next quarterly payment calculation
  • all registered patients can be checked via HPOS
  • a practice who registers a patient first in the calendar year will received the $250 payment. Patients who contribute to the Indigenous Health Patient registration payment can be checked via the quarterly payment advice

The practice may have changed their participation:

  • On the Incentive Summary screen, select View from the History column to check the incentive history
  • Check if the practice is approved or withdrawn from the IHI during the relevant payment quarter

Indigenous Health Outcomes payment tier 1 and 2

If there is an Indigenous Health Outcomes payment tier 1 or 2 decrease, advise the practice:

  • this payment is based on the practice’s billed services. See Application for Practice Incentives Program (PIP) Indigenous Health Incentive (IHI)
  • that if the payment:
    • is 0, the practice is not eligible for payment
    • has decreased, the payment has been calculated accordingly for the quarter

Refer to the IHI guidelines for more information about payment calculations. The Resources page has a link.

The practice may have changed their participation:

  • On the Incentive Summary screen, select View from the History column to check the incentive history
  • Check if the practice is approved or withdrawn from the IHI during the relevant payment quarter

Note: the Indigenous Health Outcomes payment is paid in the February payment quarter
