Assessing eligibility

Table 1

Step

Action

1

Eligible for Masters and Doctorate Award + Read more ...

The student must:

  • meet the eligibility criteria to receive the Masters and Doctorate Award, and
  • be receiving or have received at least $1.00 of entitlement (during the academic year they are claiming) of one of the following:
    • Living Allowance
    • ABSTUDY Pensioner Education Supplement (PES)
    • Before 1 January 2022, a Defence Force Income Support-like payment under the Veterans Entitlements Act 1986

If:

  • Yes, go to Step 2
  • No, go to Step 6

2

Moving to another location + Read more ...

Is the student required to move to another location to take up their Masters and Doctorate studies?

  • Yes, go to Step 3
  • No, go to Step 6

3

Removal costs + Read more ...

Removal costs are costs to cover:

  • the use of a removalist company, or
  • truck hire, if they choose to carry out their own removal

Payment can be made:

  • to the removal/hire truck company on receipt of their invoice, or
  • as a reimbursement to the student on submission of original receipts

Relocation Allowance does not include the costs of establishing the customer's new home e.g. bonds.

4

Fares to travel to their place of study + Read more ...

If student/partner/dependent(s) requires assistance for fares, they will be paid for an economy or student concessional airfare to the study location. See Masters and Doctorate travel for ABSTUDY.

If they travel by surface transport, the payment will be the airfare equivalent, or actual costs, whichever is the lesser.

Note: this allowance is paid as an alternative to Fares Allowance. Once relocated, there is no further travel entitlement to return to the former home location.

5

Removal costs and/or fares + Read more ...

Does the student need help with removal costs and/or fares (as determined in Steps 4 and 5)?

  • Yes, see Step 1 in Table 2
  • No, go to Step 6

6

Not eligible for Relocation Allowance + Read more ...

  • Tell the student they are not eligible for Relocation Allowance
  • Record details on a DOC

Procedure ends here.

Coding Relocation Allowance

For ABSTUDY Smart Centre Processing staff only

Table 2

Step

Action

1

Refer to ABSTUDY Smart Centre Processing Team + Read more ...

Service Officers not in the ABSTUDY Smart Centre Processing Team:

  • Collect the details needed for the assessment
  • Send details to the ABSTUDY Smart Centre Processing Team
    Use Fast Note - select Auto text, use Students, ABSTUDY & Apprentices > Update > ABSTUDY Action request > Confirm
  • Tell the customer the ABSTUDY Smart Centre Processing Team will make the decision
  • Procedure ends here

ABSTUDY Smart Centre Processing Team, go to Step 2.

2

Complete the Supplementary Education Payments (EPSP) screen + Read more ...

Determine the amount of Relocation Allowance payable.

On the EPSP screen, update:

  • Event Date, key the date the action is to take place from
  • Payment Type, key 'REL'
  • Reason, leave blank
  • Amount Claimed, key the amount of money student has claimed for
  • Amount Paid, key the amount which is to be paid to the student
  • Payee, key either 'CUS' (customer) or 'TPT' (third party)
  • Claim Details, key details which are relevant to the student's situation relating to the payment they are claiming. Note: this is an optional free text field
  • Frequency, key details to reflect the frequency the student's requested payment is to be paid. This is usually a one off payment for this particular allowance

Is the payment being directed to a third party?

  • Yes, go to Step 3
  • No, go to Step 4

3

Third party payment + Read more ...

If payment was to a third party, the system will go to the Payment Instruction Summary (PIPS) screen. Complete the following fields:

  • Add New Payee, key 'Y', or
  • Link to Organisation, key 'Y'

The system will flow to the screens to input the third party details for either the person or the organisation.

4

Advise customer of outcome + Read more ...

  • Tell the student how much Relocation Allowance they will be paid
  • Record details on a DOC
