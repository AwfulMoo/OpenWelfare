Date of lodgement/contact

Date of receipt (DOR), Marital Status (MS)

Prior to entry into care and is also prior to the DOR of the auto sourced activity

Date of entry: 8 April

Auto activity DOR: 12 April

Aged Care Calculation of your cost of care form DOR: 1 April, scanned on 22 April.

Auto claim activity to be cancelled and Residential Care Assistance (RCA) claim re-indexed from 1 April (Aged Care Calculation of your cost of care DOR).

MS screen to be completed prior to claim using DOR 12 April (auto DOR) to ensure correct arrears for single rate of income support payment (ISP).

After entry into care but it is prior to the DOR of the auto sourced activity or where no auto source RCA/NCL has been created

Date of entry into care: 1 February

Aged Care Calculation of your cost of care DOR: 5 February, scanned on 15 February.

Auto claim activity DOR: 10 February.

Auto claim to be cancelled and RCA claim re-indexed using DOR 5 February (DOR of form). As the Aged Care Calculation of your cost of care was lodged prior to the auto activity being created, means assessment details need to be coded using the date of receipt of form to enable correct date of effect when updating MS screen, income and assets.

After entry into care and on the same date as the DOR of the auto sourced activity

Date of entry: 4 November

Auto activity DOR: 8 November.

Aged Care Calculation of your cost of care DOR: 8 November.

Means assessment can be completed using auto activity/Aged Care Calculation of your cost of care DOR (8 November).

MS screen – can also be updated in auto activity as entitled to illness separated rate of ISP from 8 November (first notification).

After the DOR of the auto sourced activity

Date of entry: 30 September

Auto activity DOR: 2 October

Aged Care Calculation of your cost of care DOR: 10 October

Means assessment can be completed using auto activity DOR (2 October).

MS screen – can also be updated in auto activity as entitled to illness separated rate of ISP from 2 October (first notification).

Date of entry into Residential Care is 30 September, auto RCA/NCL DOR is 2 October, and Aged Care Calculation of your cost of care DOR is 10 October. Means assessment can be completed in auto RCA/NCL activity as this was received prior to the Aged Care Calculation of your cost of care.

All income and asset updates will also be made using the DOR of the auto activity, even though the form was lodged after this date as that becomes the deemed date of claim.

An auto sourced activity has been created, or where a verbal means assessment is undertaken after the DOR of the auto activity

Date of entry: 15 April

Auto activity DOR: 17 April

Phone contact DOR: 22 April

Means assessment to be coded using the auto activity, including any updates to MS screen or income and assets information.

No auto sourced activity has been created or where a verbal means assessment is undertaken and no auto sourced activity has been created

Date of entry: 9 December

Phone contact DOR: 16 December

No auto activity has been created. Index RCA/NCL from date of contact – 16 December.
