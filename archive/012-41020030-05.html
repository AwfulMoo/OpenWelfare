Registration details

An applicant must have been awarded certification of either of the following from an approved registered training organisation (RTO):

  • Certificate III (equivalent or higher) in Aboriginal and/or Torres Strait Islander Primary Health Care, or
  • Certificate III (equivalent or higher) in Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Health - before 1 July 2012
  • If the RTO is not listed below, conduct an RTO search to confirm the current status of the training organisation. If the search confirms the organisation is a registered training organisation, the certificate can be accepted

If certification is from an unapproved RTO, applicant's qualification may still be an equivalent or higher one. Applicant must provide:

  • qualification certificate, and
  • independent assessment by an approved RTO advising qualification is equivalent or higher
