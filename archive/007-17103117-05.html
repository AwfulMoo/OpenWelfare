Enrolment scenarios

This table provides scenarios of various enrolment status, outcomes and action required.

Item

Scenario

1

Disputed enrolment + Read more ...

Jennifer enrols child Evie at Bright Futures Child Care Centre.

Jennifer logs onto their online account via myGov to view the Complying Written Agreement (CWA). Jennifer notices Bright Futures has Evie only attending 3 days per week, when Evie will be attending 5 days. Jennifer disputes the CWA and speaks to the Child Care Centre. Bright Futures corrects the CWA and resubmits the enrolment.

2

Rejected enrolment + Read more ...

Tanya notices their Centrelink online account has tasks relating to Complying Written Agreements (CWA's) for Tanya's children Loren and Keira for Freedom Child Care Centre. Tanya has not enrolled the children in childcare. Tanya rejects the CWA's.

3

Received enrolment + Read more ...

Wendy calls the Families and Parenting line and speaks to Sam about childcare.

Sam looks at the enrolment tab in Process Direct and notices the enrolment is showing as Received. Sam advises Wendy:

  • to speak to Milestones Child Care Centre as William and Daniel's enrolment is not complete/missing some information
  • that CCS entitlement cannot commence until Milestones Childcare Centre update this information and resubmit the enrolment (CWA)
  • to confirm this information via Centrelink online account after the centre has updated the enrolment

4

Ceased enrolment + Read more ...

Scenario 1 - Automatically Ceased

Child Tommy has not attended their Child Care Centre for 14 weeks due to a medical procedure.

Tommy's last day at the Centre was the 5 April 2021.

The 'ceased' date is Monday 5 July 2021 (14 weeks).

The Enrolment end date is Sunday 4 July 2021. (The date immediately prior to the ceased date).

Tommy's parent speaks to the Child Care Centre and re-enrols Tommy as Tommy is now fully recovered and able to attend Child Care.

The new enrolment date is the 5 July 2021.

As the previous enrolment is 'ceased', a new enrolment is created. This will appear as 2 separate enrolments.

Scenario 2 - Extend Enrolment Event

Child Lincoln has not attended their Child Care Centre for 14 weeks due to a medical procedure.

Lincoln's last day at the Centre was the 5 April 2021.

The 'ceased' date is Monday 5 July 2021 (14 weeks).

Extend enrolment event is in place from 15 March 2021 to 31 July 2021 and this applies to Lincoln's enrolment.

Lincoln's enrolment will not end on the 4th of July 2021 as there is an Extend enrolment event in place.

Lincoln's 14 weeks non-attendance count will start from 1 August 2021, after the end of the Extend enrolment event period.

Scenario 3 - Arrangement ended

Terry-Anne's children Mia and Kira are enrolled at Woolamookooka Out of Hours School Care (OOSH). Mia and Kira have been accepted to train at the School of Dance for gifted Dancers commencing 21 July 2021. Terry-Anne's children will not attend OOSH for this period. Terry-Anne decided to un-enrol Mia and Kira for this period.

Mia and Kira's last day at OOSH will be the 20 July 2021. The arrangement end date is the 20 July 2021.

Enrolments will cease from the 21 July 2021.
