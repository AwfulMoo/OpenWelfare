To assess a customer’s LGA

  • Open the Queensland Live Feed App
  • Place the address given by the customer in the search box
  • Select the correct address that fills in the drop down
  • In the Layers menu on the right:
    • for AGDRP and NZ DRP, select Declared LGAs - AGDRP – Floods (Feb 2022)
    • for DRA and NZ DRA, select Declared LGAs - DRA – Floods (Feb 2022)
  • If the address given by the customer is:
    • in a declared LGA
      - for AGDRP and NZ DRP it will be shaded in red
      - for DRA and NZ DRA it will be shaded in purple
      - the LGA name will show in the middle of the LGA. Note: staff may need to zoom out until the LGA name shows
    • not in a declared LGA, to identify the LGA name, scroll down the Layers menu on the right and select LGA 2020

Note: if there are concerns about the validity of an address provided, see Emergency payments - preventing fraud to conduct an address search.

Continue to assess the claim:

  • Eligibility for AGDRP and NZ DRP
