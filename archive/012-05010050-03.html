This page contains the process to delete electronic Medicare claims.

On this Page:

Delete patient claim

Delete entire bulk bill claim

Delete individual bulk bill voucher (Medicare online only)

Delete patient claim

Note:

  • Deleting an Easyclaim patient claim will delete all services (items) transmitted in the claim. Ensure to notify caller that all services will be deleted.
  • Electronic claims can only be deleted on the same day of processing.

Delete patient claim in Mainframe

Table 1: This table describes how eBusiness Service Officers delete Online, Medicare Easyclaim patient claims and Medicare Patient Claim Webclaims in Mainframe.

Step

Action

1

Perform security check + Read more ...

Perform security check over the phone for all claim deletions requested from practices or providers. See Verify caller identity for eBusiness Service Centre enquiries.

2

Log on to Mainframe

3

Verify claim deletion request + Read more ...

Request from caller:

  • Patients Medicare number
  • Date of Service
  • Item number (s)
  • Provider number

Can claim be deleted?

  • Yes, go to Step 4
  • No, see Handle claims that cannot be deleted (Resources)

4

Enter claim details + Read more ...

For Medicare Online claims, Medicare Easyclaims and Medicare Patient Claim Webclaims:

  • Key NQEI, DA, Medicare card number and press [Enter]
  • Is claim showing as WAITPEND?
    • Yes, go to Step 6
    • No, go to Step 5

5

Check claim status + Read more ...

If the claim status is:

  • paid, the claim must be reversed
    • Check details in request are correct and key 'R' in the S field
    • Press [Enter]. Result: the original claim will be displayed
    • Key 'R' to reverse and press [Enter]
    • Press [F2] to go back to NQEI screen
    • WAITPEND, go to Step 6

6

Update WAITPEND claim + Read more ...

  • Key 'C' in the S field and press [Enter]
  • Select 3 (Req Del-Requested deletion by provider) and press [Enter]
  • Key 'U' to update
  • Press [F2] to go back to NQEI screen. Go to Step 7

7

Enter text message and update + Read more ...

  • Key 'T' in the S field and press [Enter]
  • Key a text message and press [Enter]. For example: 'Incorrectly billed, requested deletion by name'
  • Key 'U' to update

Delete entire bulk bill claim

Note:

  • Deleting an Easyclaim bulk bill claim will delete all services (items) transmitted in the claim.
  • Electronic claims can only be deleted on the same day of processing.

Delete entire claim (Medicare Online, Medicare Easyclaim and Medicare bulk bill webclaim)

Table 2: This table describes how Service Officers delete entire Medicare Online bulk bill claims, Medicare Easyclaim bulk bill claims and Medicare Bulk Bill Webclaims.

Step

Action

1

Perform security check + Read more ...

Perform security check over the phone for all claim deletions requested from practices or providers. See Verify caller identity for eBusiness Service Centre enquiries.

2

Log on to Mainframe

3

Verify claim deletion request + Read more ...

Request from caller:

  • Medicare number
  • Patient name
  • Date of service
  • Item number (s)
  • Payee Provider number
  • Claim ID

Can claim be deleted?

  • Yes, go to Step 4
  • No, see Handle claims that cannot be deleted (Resources)

4

Action claim type + Read more ...

For Medicare Easyclaims:

  • key 'NHSI, Medicare card number' and press [Enter]
  • press [F2] to find claim identifier (ID)
  • key 'DQRI, payee provider number, claim id' and press [Enter]

For Medicare Online claims:

  • key 'DQRI,CUST/minor customer id or payee provider number, claim id' and press [Enter]

For Medicare Bulk Bill Webclaims:

  • key 'NHSI, Medicare card number' and press [Enter]
  • press [F2] to find claim identifier (ID)
  • key 'DQRI, payee provider number, claim id' and press [Enter]

5

Display DCEI screen + Read more ...

Key 'C' in S column and press [Enter].

Result: DCEI,provider number,claim id,DOL=date of lodgement screen is displayed.

6

Action claim status + Read more ...

If claim:

  • is in PEND status (except WAITBKGD), go to Step 8
  • was PAID (processed) on same date, over key DCEI with 'DSDI' and press [Enter]

Result: DSDI, provider number, claim id, DOL=date of lodgement screen is displayed.

7

Over key DSDR + Read more ...

  • Key 'R' to reverse and press [Enter]
  • Over key DSDR with 'DAPI, provider number, claim id, DOL=date of lodgement,609' and press [Enter]
  • Go to Step 9

8

Over key DQRI + Read more ...

Over key DQRI with 'DAPI, provider number, claim id, DOL=date of lodgement, 609' and press [Enter].

9

Confirm claim has been rejected + Read more ...

Press [F2] to go back to the DQRI screen to confirm the claim has been rejected.

Note: the claim status should be REQ-DEL.

Delete individual bulk bill voucher (Medicare online only)

Note: electronic claims can only be deleted on the same day of processing, excluding claims sent to the payment run.

Delete individual vouchers for Medicare online bulk bill claims

Table 3: This table describes how eBusiness Service Officers delete individual vouchers for Online bulk bill claims

Step

Action

1

Perform security check + Read more ...

Perform security check over the phone for all claim deletions requested from practices or providers. See Verify caller identity for eBusiness Service Centre enquiries.

2

Log on to Mainframe

3

Verify claim deletion request + Read more ...

Request from caller:

  • Medicare number
  • Patient name
  • Date of service
  • Item number (s)
  • Payee Provider number
  • Claim ID

Can claim be deleted?

  • Yes, go to Step 4
  • No, see Handle claims that cannot be deleted (Resources)

4

Display DCEI screen + Read more ...

Key:

  • 'DQRI, CUST/minor customer id or payee provider number, claim id' and press [Enter]
  • 'C' in S column and press [Enter]

Result: DCEI,provider number,claim id,DOL=date of lodgement screen is displayed.

5

Action claim status + Read more ...

For:

  • PEND status (except WAITBKGD) claims:
    • Over key DCEI to 'DAPI, provider number, claim id, DOL=date of lodgement'
    • Go to Step 6
  • PAID (processed) on same date claims:
    • Over key DCEI with 'DSDI' and press Enter. Result: DSDI,provider number,claim id,DOL=date of lodgement screen is displayed
    • Key 'R' to reverse and press [Enter]
    • Over key DSDR with 'DAPI,provider number,claim ID,DOL=date of lodgement'
    • Go to Step 6

6

Delete individual vouchers in a claim + Read more ...

  • Press [F8] in claim until the voucher to be deleted is found
  • Check all voucher details before deleting:
    • Medicare card number
    • Patient name
    • Provider number
    • Item numbers
    • Date of service
  • Press Tab to go to the:
    • P column and over key 6 with 'S'
    • RSN column and key the rejection code '609' for all items in voucher
  • Press [Enter]

7

Finalise claim after individual voucher deleted + Read more ...

For:

  • UNPAID or PEND claims (showing NO ERRORS OK TO PAY):
    • Over key DAPI with 'DPPI' and press Enter. Result: 'DPPI,provider,number,claim ID, DOL=date of lodgement' screen is displayed
    • Key 'P' to pay by over keying the I with a 'P' in the DPPI control line
    • Press [Enter] to return claim to PAID-SD status
  • REVERSED PAID claims:
    • Over key DAPI with 'DPPI' and press Enter. Result: DPPI,provider number,claim ID,DOL=date of lodgement screen is displayed
    • Key 'P' to pay and press [Enter] to return claim to PAID-SD status

Note: if a claim cannot be finalised (showing MORE ERRORS), go to Step 8.

8

Handle claim that cannot be finalised + Read more ...

When a claim cannot be finalised, eBusiness Service Centre Service Officers must email Assessing and Benefits using the below template:
