Scenario

Quarterly Review outcome

Income and asset update received same month as QR, service provider is up to date with claims:

  • 5 March – service provider lodges February claim which is finalised and paid
  • 10 March – income and asset changes updated in SPARC
  • 15 March – QR implemented
  • 20 March – date of effect for new set fees and CPI
  • March QR will calculate any refund for the period ending 28 February (last claim completed)
  • Service provider will receive the refund when the March claim is lodged from 1 April onwards
  • Any refund owing for a period in March will be calculated in the July QR

    Income and asset update received same month as QR, service provider is up to date with claims:

    • 10 August – income and asset changes updated in SPARC
    • 2 September – service provider lodges August claim which is finalised and paid
    • 20 September – date of effect for new set fees and CPI
    • 25 September – QR implemented
  • September QR will calculate any refund for the period ending 31 August (last claim completed)
  • Service provider will receive the refund when the September claim is lodged from 1 October onwards
  • Any refund owing for a period in September will be calculated in the January QR
    		•

    Income and asset update received same month as QR, service provider is 3 months behind with claims:

    • 10 September – service provider lodges August claim which is finalised and paid
    • 15 October – income and asset changes updated in SPARC
    • 1 January – date of effect for new set fees
    • 11 January – QR implemented
    • 15 February – service provider lodges outstanding claims
  • January QR will calculate any refund for the period ending 31 August (last claim completed)
  • 15 February – the September claim will be processed first, paying any refunds from the September QR
  • Service provider will receive refunds when all outstanding claims are received and finalised
