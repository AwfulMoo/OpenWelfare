Validate EBPRP claim form before sending to EBPRP Processing Team

Table 1: This table describes how a Service Officer validates an External Breast Prostheses Reimbursement Program (EBPRP) claim form before scanning via the Multi-Function Device (MFD) scanning process.

Step

Action

1

Check claim form + Read more ...

Customers can lodge an EBPRP claim form via email, post and at service centres.

Note: Service Officers must validate claim forms received at service centres (via the drop-box or handed directly to a Service Officer).

Ensure all sections of the form have been completed including:

  • customer details
  • prosthesis side (left, right or both)
  • postal address details
  • EFT account details
  • details relating to other financial assistance (for example, private health fund payments)
  • proof of purchase or receipt attached
  • customer declaration signed

Scan EBPRP claims submitted at service centres via the Multi-Function Device (MFD) scanning process daily.

Is the claim form correct for EBPRP?

  • Yes, go to Step 3
  • No, go to Step 2

2

Claim form incorrect + Read more ...

Customer present in service centre

Where the customer has supplied an incorrect claim form for EBPRP and they are in the service centre:

  • download an EBPRP claim form from the Services Australia website
  • ask the customer to complete the form for re-submission
  • receipt the claim to record details in CDMS. Go to Step 4
  • scan the form and supplied documents via the MFD scanning process

Customer not present in service centre

Where the customer has supplied an incorrect claim form for EBPRP and they are not in the service centre:

  • scan the form and the supplied documents via the MFD scanning process
  • the EBPRP processing team will contact the customer

3

Claim form correct + Read more ...

Check the claim form is completed.

If all the sections of the form are complete, go to Step 4.

If the EBPRP claim form is incomplete and:

  • the customer is present in the service centre, ask the customer to complete the required sections
  • was placed in the drop-box:
    • contact the customer for the relevant information. Services Officers must be sensitive to the circumstances surrounding the claim
  • if phone contact is unsuccessful, receipt the claim in the Consumer Directory Maintenance System (CDMS) and send all the documents via the MFD scanning process. Note: only the EBPRP Processing Team have authority to access and issue EBPRP letters and other correspondence to customers.

4

Record details in Consumer Directory Maintenance System (CDMS) + Read more ...

Record lodgement details in CDMS. Check customer restrictions in CDMS when verifying the Medicare card and follow the appropriate procedures with the message that is received.

Record receipt of the claim in the Personal Comment field:

  • note the breast ticked on the claim form as either:
  • (L) – Left. For example, if a claim is submitted for the left breast, record as (L) EBPR Claim Rcvd
    • (R) - Right
  • Both. For example, if a claim is submitted for a double breast surgery, record as (B) EBPR Claim Rcvd
  • key EBPRP Claim Received
  • date stamp the application form with date of lodgement

5

Confirm details in CDMS + Read more ...

Confirm the following details in CDMS:

  • EFT account details
  • address
  • telephone number

6

Update details from the claim form + Read more ...

Update details as required from the EBPRP claim form.

Note: if the EFT account details have been captured previously by Services Australia via an alternative notification source, do not make any changes to the notification source unless the banking details on the EBPRP claim form are different to those stored in the system.

7

Prepare documents for scanning + Read more ...

Prepare the documents for the MFD scanning process

Prepare documents for the EBPRP Processing Team - same day

Table 2: This table describes how a Service Officer prepares documentation for the EBPRP Processing Team. Note: send claims to the EBPRP Processing Team on the same day they are received. The KPI for processing EBPRP claims is 10 days.

Step

Action

1

Photocopy documents + Read more ...

Photocopy original receipt or proof of purchase and return to customer if they are in the service centre.

2

Stamp copies + Read more ...

Stamp every photocopied page with ‘Original Document Sighted’.

3

Endorse copies + Read more ...

Endorse photocopy of original documents with:

  • Service Officer's P number
  • date
  • office location

4

Attach copies to claim form + Read more ...

Attach certified copies to the claim form and return original receipt or proof of purchase to the customer.

5

Receipt the claim + Read more ...

Receipt the claim and check/update CDMS as required. See Step 4 in Table 1.

6

Send documents to EBPRP Processing Team + Read more ...

Scan the documents as per the MFD scanning process.
