Applying Alternative Identity review periods

Step

Action

1

Review process for Alternative Identity + Read more ...

Alternative Identity Assessment reviews are recorded on the Identity Confirmation Dashboard and display the:

  • Alternative Identity Reason
  • Identity Status
  • Reminder Date, and
  • Review Date (end date)

Alternative Identity categories and review periods will vary dependant on the customer circumstances.

The system will generate a letter advising the customer to lodge further documents if:

  • an Alternative Identity review code requiring a review was applied to the customer record, and
  • their identity documents are still outstanding 2 to 4 weeks before the review end date

Note: when actioning work item in Work Load Manager (WLM) do not select the Process button in the WLM item at this stage.

During the review process, manually update the WLM item status to Completed, if:

  • Identity Status of Confirmed is achieved
  • Request for Information (RFI) letter is issued
  • Alternative Identity assessment is completed
  • A decision is made to cancel/suspend a payment

To action a review, go to Step 2.

If the customer contacts within 13 weeks of being Cancelled (CAN) or Suspended (SUS) for not providing identity documents, go to Step 12.

If the customer contacts after 13 weeks of being CAN or SUS or Rejected (REJ) for not providing identity documents, go to Step 13.

2

Actioning a review for Alternative Identity + Read more ...

When the customer lodges identity documents, code and verify them using the Identity Confirmation Dashboard.

If all identity documents have been verified and linkage (photo check) completed, ensure the Finish button is selected to achieve a ‘Confirmed’ identity status. The Alternative Identity Review status will automatically be updated to ‘Completed’.

If identity documents and/or linkage is still needed to confirm the customer’s identity, go to Step 3.

3

Identity documents or linkage still required + Read more ...

Check if the customer has any documents uploaded to their record.

Code all documents using the Identity Confirmation Dashboard.

Can the agency help the customer to lodge the required documents?

  • Yes,
    • Help customer to get and lodge the documents
    • Procedure ends here if other identity documents and linkage is not required
  • No, go to Step 4

4

Check letter was sent + Read more ...

Check if the reminder letter or other advice was sent to the customer 2 to 4 weeks before the review fell due.

Was the letter sent to the customer?

Check CORRO screen in Process Direct for the Confirmation of Identity letter. This screen may indicate that the 'Do Not Send FILTER' was on for the letter. If FILTER was on, the letter was not sent.

  • Yes, go to Step 5
  • No:
    • Review outstanding identity requirements and any vulnerabilities that may impact the customer's ability to complete their identity requirements. If appropriate, send a new Request for Information (RFI) to the customer using Process Direct
      If the customer is vulnerable, consider contacting the customer or their nominee by phone before a RFI is issued to:
      - confirm the customers identity status by coding identity document details, or
      - complete and Alternative identity assessment, by phone in the Identity Confirmation Dashboard
    • Note: if the review has expired and, identity is still outstanding, go to Step 5

5

Check customer's circumstances + Read more ...

Check the Identity Confirmation Dashboard. Has the customer already provided the required identity documents and completed linkage?

  • Yes, the customer's Identity Status should be Confirmed. Ensure the Finish button is selected to achieve a 'Confirmed' identity status
    Procedure ends here
  • No, a Gathering Documents (GD) review will update to For Manual Action (FMA) in the Identity Confirmation Dashboard when a customer uploads an Identity document. Check the review status in the Identity Confirmation Dashboard, if it shows a review status as
    • FMA, go to Step 8
    • GD, continue with customer contact:
      - if a contact telephone number is available for the customer, go to Step 6
      - if a contact telephone number is not available for the customer, go to Step 7

6

Contact telephone number available + Read more ...

Contact the customer by phone and complete Proof of Record Ownership (PoRO). Service Officers must have a conversation with the customer about their circumstances.

Ask if:

  • they are able to upload their documents now
  • they need more time to get documents
  • the agency can help them to get or lodge identity documents, or
  • they are unable to get the required documents

If the customer:

  • can give additional identity documents with more time, complete a new Alternative Identity assessment by phone. This
    • is completed in the Identity Confirmation Dashboard
    • allows them more time to get the identity documents and/or
    • allows them more time to complete linkage

Advise the customer of their obligations, due date and consequences of potential cancellation and DOC the record to indicate why more time is required.

  • cannot get identity document/s due to their circumstances, complete a new Alternative Identity Assessment by phone to apply a non-reviewable Alternative Identity code
    • do not re-apply a reviewable Alternative Identity code where the customer cannot meet identity requirements

If the customer:

  • cannot attend in person, see Supporting Identity Confirmation for customers unable to attend in person
  • can lodge the required identity documents but is unable to complete linkage, See Alternative identity
  • cannot be contacted by phone, go to Step 7

7

Customer cannot be contacted by phone + Read more ...

Customer has not provided identity documents

  • If the customer has not provided sufficient identity documents, the payment will automatically suspend/cancel when the review becomes due. Procedure ends here

Customer has provided identity documents

  • Check for scanned identity documents
  • Code and verify the documents in the Identity Confirmation Dashboard
  • If the customer has not provided sufficient identity documents, the payment will automatically suspend/cancel when the review becomes due. Procedure ends here as GD will be 'in process' in the Dashboard
  • The review will be displayed under the Transactions icon in Process Direct

Procedure ends here.

8

For Manual Action (FMA) review - allocated work item in Workload Manager (WLM) + Read more ...

When allocated an FMA review, check the Identity Confirmation Dashboard and check for any scanned identity documents.

If Commencement of Identity (CoI), Primary Use in Community (PUIC) and Secondary Use in Community (SUiC) identity documents:

  • can be verified, check linkage, is completed, go to Step 9
  • are not verified and linkage is not completed, go to Step 10
  • are not verified and linkage is complete, go to Step 11

Note: where care receivers are required to provide identity documents, Service officers must always check the customer and care receiver records for scanned identity document.

9

Identity documents verified, linkage is completed + Read more ...

CoI, PUiC and/or SUiC identity documents can be verified, and photo ID box checked:

  • Yes, code remaining identity documents and select Finish button to achieve a confirmed identity status. The FMA review status will auto complete. DOC the record. Procedure ends here
  • No, and the customer is on a payment, check that reminder letter was sent 2 to 4 weeks prior to end of review. If letter was:
    • sent, suspend the customer payment. DOC the record to show why the payment was suspended
    • not sent, send RFI letter to be returned in 21 days using Process Direct. DOC the record to show why the RFI was sent

10

Identity documents are not verified and linkage is not completed + Read more ...

CoI, PUiC and/or SUiC identity documents are not verified, and photo ID box not checked. If payment is:

  • cancelled. Procedure ends here
  • current, check if reminder letter was sent 2 to 4 weeks prior to end of review. If letter was:
    • sent and there is no scanned identity document, suspend the customer payment (FRC)
    • not sent, send RFI letter to be returned in 21 days using Process Direct. DOC the record to show why the RFI was sent

11

Identity documents are not verified and linkage is complete + Read more ...

CoI, PUiC and/or SUiC identity documents are not verified, and photo ID box checked make 2 genuine attempts to contact by phone, if contact is:

  • successful, customer can get identity documents. Code remaining identity documents and select Finish button to achieve a confirmed identity status. FMA review status will auto complete. DOC the record. Procedure ends here.
  • successful, but customer cannot give the remaining identity documents over the phone:
    • suspend payment, unless customer has vulnerabilities or if customer no longer possesses identity documents. See Alternative Identity
    • if suspended, advise the customer the payment is suspended and will resume within 13 weeks if remaining identity documents are provided or if Alternative Identity is completed. DOC the record
  • unsuccessful after the second attempt, check if reminder letter was sent 2 to 4 weeks prior to end of review. If letter was:
    • sent, suspend the customer payment
    • not sent, send RFI letter to be returned in 21 days using Process Direct. DOC the record to show why the RFI was sent

12

Customer contacts within 13 weeks of CAN or SUS + Read more ...

Normal review procedures apply if within 13 weeks of the CAN or SUS action, the customer:

  • attends a service centre, or
  • contacts a Smart Centre

Restore the customer's payment if they:

  • supply the required documents and linkage, or
  • complete an Alternative Identity assessment, which may include, a further review using a new Alternative Identity assessment in the Identity Confirmation Dashboard
    Note: staff must restore payment before completing a new Alternative identity assessment. The process restarts from the date the new assessment is completed

See the References page for the date of effect of favourable determination resulting from review.

Procedure ends here.

13

Customer contacts after 13 weeks of CAN/SUS/REJ + Read more ...

After 13 weeks, the payment cannot be restored. Customers should be encouraged to reclaim and to complete their Identity requirements.

Customers with a Confirmed Identity status, are not required to prove their identity. They must only be authenticated.

In some circumstances, staff may consider re-indexing a claim. See Indexing a customer's claim on the system.

See the References page for the date of effect of favourable determination resulting from review.

Procedure ends here.

If the customer has completed their Identity requirements and the re-indexed claim cannot be completed due to identity issues:

  • check the Identity Confirmation Dashboard workflow is finalised with the Finish button
  • escalate to a Technical Support Officer
  • TSO and/or SSO can request further assistance from Identity Policy Helpdesk. See Resources page for contact details
