Extension of 13 Week NEIS Pre-Training and development

There may be occasions where the job seeker needs more than 13 weeks of NEIS Pre-Training approved activity because they are still seeking council approval or are requiring some business licensing before they can commence their business under Department of Employment and Workplace Relations (DEWR) NEIS/Self-Employment Assistance Agreement.

For these job seekers, an extension of the 13 weeks NEIS Pre-Training activity can be arranged until they are able to commence in NEIS/Self-Employment Assistance.

The NEIS/Self-Employment Assistance Panel Members updates the expected end date of the NEIS/Self-Employment Assistance training in DEWR systems and this information is then reflected on the Activity and Exemption (AEX) screen.

The job seeker has to advise their Workforce Australia or Disability Employment Services provider in person of the extension and the provider is required to update their Job Plan.

Services Australia links

The Department of Employment and Workplace Relations (DEWR) Workforce Australia helpline will assist with general NEIS/Self-Employment Assistance enquiries and advise the job seeker of any local NEIS/Self-Employment Assistance providers near them. This phone number is located in Department of Employment and Workplace Relations.

NEIS/Self-Employment Assistance Helpdesk: where conflicting information about NEIS/Self-Employment Assistance participation or Allowance is received, contact the Participation Programmes Team.

Job seekers wishing to participate in NEIS/Self-Employment Assistance are to be referred by Workforce Australia or Disability Employment Services providers. Services Australia staff are not to refer customers directly to a NEIS/Self-Employment Assistance provider.
