Frequently asked questions

Table 3

Question

Answer

Are Mental health treatment services counted over a calendar year or rolling 12 months?

Calendar year - defined as the period between and including 1 January to 31 December.

How many treatment services can a patient access?

Patients can access up to:

  • 20 individual psychological treatment services
  • 10 group psychological treatment services

Note: these claiming limits apply each calendar year, regardless of how many are on the referral.

Do patients need a referral to receive treatment services?

Yes, patients must have a referral for treatment services under their MHTP.

Note: you can also refer your patient to another practitioner for FPS.

Can patients have an eating disorder treatment and management plan and a MHTP?

Patients should generally only have one treatment and management plan in place at any given time.

Treatment services provided under a GPMHTP count towards the claiming limit for the EDP.

Health professionals may be subject to post-payment audits by the Department of Health and should ensure they are claiming Medicare benefits appropriately.

See Treatment and management plans for eating disorders (including anorexia nervosa) for more information.

Can patients have a MHTP and a chronic disease management plan?

Yes, patients can have both plans in place if they are clinically relevant to the patient.

My patient is a resident in an aged care facility, are there any MHTP MBS items?

What mental health care plan & review items are available for patients in Residential Aged Care Facilities?

Yes, access to mental health services was expanded to patients in a residential aged care facility on 10 December 2020. There is a factsheet on MBS Online which lists all of the item numbers and requirements.

My patient has had 10 treatment services already this year? Are there items I can still bill for treating them when I go residential aged care facilities?

Yes additional mental health services under Better Access are available to residents of Aged Care Facilities. See the factsheets on MBS Online for the items that are available and more information.

If I visit my patients in residential aged care facilities and bill mental health services, is there anything else I can bill for having to travel from my consulting rooms?

Yes there are 3 flag fall items. See the factsheets on MBS Online for the items that are available to your profession and for more information.

If I visit my patients in residential aged care facilities and bill mental health services, can I bill a flag fall item for each patient I see?

No, the flag fall items are only available for the initial attendance at one Residential Aged Care Facility on one occasion.

If I see my patients in multiple residential aged care facilities, can I only bill a flag fall item once?

You can bill a flag fall item for the initial or first patient at each of the Residential Aged Care Facilities, per occasion.
