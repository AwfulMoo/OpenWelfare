Dear Sir/Madam

We tried unsuccessfully to contact you today regarding the government’s Paid Parental Leave scheme.

Please contact us at your earliest convenience on 131158 to discuss the scheme. You will need to have your ABN or Centrelink Reference Number available when you call.

For specific information in relation to the Paid Parental Leave Scheme for employers, please refer to servicesaustralia.gov.au > Home > Organisations > Business > Employer Resources > Paid Parental Leave Scheme for Employers > Resources > Paid Parental Leave scheme Employer Toolkit.

Service Officer
