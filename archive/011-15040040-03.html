This page contains the process required to terminate an existing case in Compensation Case Management System (CCMS).

Decide to terminate existing case in CCMS

Table 1

Step

Action

1

Decide to terminate existing case in CCMS + Read more ...

Does one of the below reason’s apply?

  • If the case was incorrectly registered or incorrect details keyed
    • for example the wrong person has been selected as the injured person or the incorrect date of injury or illness has been entered
    • go to Step 2
  • If written notice received that advised:
    • the claim has been discontinued or withdrawn, or
    • a court or compensation authority had dismissed the claim and evidence such as court or relevant documents provided
    • go to Step 2
    • do not terminate if denied liability, use standard case notes where possible to note this is not the reason to terminate, procedure ends here
  • If the claimant advised that they have not made a claim for compensation or do not intend to do so:
    • the compensation payer does not need to confirm
    • go to Step 2
  • If the claim is not notifiable for example, the claim has settled for $5000 or less (including all costs):
    • obtain written confirmation from the injured person, claimant, their authorised solicitor, the compensation payer or their solicitor/agent
    • once obtained, go to Step 2
  • If the Notice of judgment or settlement (MO022) has been received with a date of injury or illness earlier than the registered date of injury or illness registered:
    • see General information for Medicare Compensation Recovery and decide whether to register a new case
    • to register a new case, go to Step 2
    • otherwise continue with existing case and procedure ends here
  • If a Medicare history statement request (MO026) has been received with a new date of injury or illness that is different to that on the registered date of injury or illness:
    • confirm by phone with the compensation payer
    • once confirmed, go to Step 2
  • None of the above applies:
    • do not terminate case
    • use standard case notes where possible that did not meet termination reasons
    • procedure ends here

Terminate existing case in CCMS

Table 2

Step

Action

1

Answer questions in sequence in CCMS + Read more ...

In the Case Termination screen in CCMS, scroll to the bottom to finalise actions and answer the questions in sequence.

Note: do not select or tab through the fields - Service Officers must double select a choice or Press [Enter] to answer questions.

  • Refer case to Supervisor? - Select No and Press [Enter]
  • Is this case a class action suit? - Select No and Press [Enter]
  • Verify claimant's address? - Select No and Press [Enter]
  • Do you want to close this case? - Select Yes and Press [Enter]
  • Select finalisation type? From the drop down list, select one of the following options and Press [Enter]:
    • incorrect registration (where wrong person registered)
    • claim termination (where the legal process was discontinued, withdrawn, dismissed or settled under $5,000 where no notice of settlement is supplied and the information is received in a letter or email, claimant has advised they are not making a claim)
  • Date the notice received? Enter the date stamp on the document received or the date the case was incorrectly registered and Press [Enter]
  • Update CICS registration? - select the black arrow and Press [Enter]. Do not release the work form yet, go to Step 2

A system message is returned to confirm 'completed'.

2

Re-register case on CCMS + Read more ...

Does the case need to be re-registered on CCMS?

  • Yes, use the notification document and/or the claim details on the terminated case to register the case correctly:
    • print a copy of all documents on the terminated case
    • register the case and obtain new Work Identification Number (WIN)
    • scan all documents to the new WIN, go to Step 3

See Register Medicare Compensation Recovery claims for assistance.

  • No, go to Step 3

3

Add a case note + Read more ...

If the Case Notes screen has not opened automatically, select the Add notes button from the list on the right hand side of the main CCMS screen.

  • The top box shows all notes previously recorded on this case
  • In the blank bottom box, record the actions taken, information requested and from whom and reasons for a decision. Use standard case notes where possible
  • Select Add to save notes. A confirmation box will appear, select 'Yes' to add case note
  • Press [F4] to permanently terminate the case
