Business rules for compensation charges

Table 1

Item

Description

1

CMM - Compensation charge to compensation payer

The debt is raised:

  • under section 1184G when the compensation payer is under preliminary notice
  • for both the customer and their partner due to ongoing and arrears of periodic payments, or for the customer due to getting a lump sum settlement

2

CMB - Compensation charge to compensation payer

This debt is raised:

  • to the compensation payer under section 1184H when the compensation payer is under preliminary notice but they did not:
    • notify of a settlement and/or periodic payment, or
    • comply with a recovery notice (they failed to comply with section 1184D)

Raise any overpayment incurred by the partner under section 1223(1).

3

CMC - Compensation charge to customer

This debt is raised to the customer under section 1184F when:

  • ongoing and/or arrears of periodic payments are paid, or
  • lump sum settlement received, and
  • no preliminary notice was issued to the compensation payer, or
  • the customer failed to notify of compensation payments

Raise any overpayment incurred by the partner under section 1223(1).

4

CMA - Compensation charge to customer

This debt is raised to the customer under section 1184I when:

  • compensation payer is not under preliminary notice, and
  • due to the customer getting a lump sum arrears of periodic compensation for a fixed period. That is, the specific dates of the periodic payments are known

Raise any overpayment incurred by the partner under section 1223(1).

Recipient Assessment Benefit/Pension Rate: field

Valid Codes:

  • BENEFIT: Maximum basic member of a couple rate of allowance
  • PENSION: Maximum basic member of a couple rate of pension

If the customer is partnered, enter either the:

  • compensation recipient’s current payment stream, or
  • partner’s current payment stream

Recipient Assessment CAP Qualified: field

This field records if the customer is:

  • deemed to be qualified, and
  • getting a compensation affected payment (CAP) at the time of the incident

If the customer lodges a claim for payment or becomes CAP Qualified:

  • do a new assessment
  • change the CAP Qualified field to Yes

How to request CMS02 access through ICT Security Portal (ISP)

Step

Action

1

CMS02 access can be requested by selecting the ICT Security Portal (ISP) from the intranet homepage.

On the Application page, under Request Management heading:

  • Select Request Resource tile to open the Request Access to Resource form
  • Select Access for: select the appropriate option
  • StartDate: leave default date
  • EndDate: leave default date
  • Complete Reason box: ‘required for basic compensation updates’
  • Category: select Mainframe Applications and highlight Compliance Applications
  • Filter By: Name
  • Containing: CMS02*
  • Select [Search]
  • Search Results: highlight CMS02 – ASSESSOR (ISP Category: Mainframe Applications – COMPLIANCE APPLICATIONS)
  • Select: right arrow [>] to move resource to Resources to Request box
  • Resource Details will show
  • Select Mainframe: ALL MAINFRAME DOMAINS
  • Select [Submit]
