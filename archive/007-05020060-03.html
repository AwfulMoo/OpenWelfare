Rejection and not effective codes for Dad and Partner Pay (DAP) 007-05020060

  • Background
  • Process

Expand all


This page contains a list of specific codes which are used to confirm the status of NEF or REJ for Dad and Partner Pay claims. Once DAP status is made NEF or REJ it cannot be reassessed. A new claim activity needs to be processed to assess the customer's DAP entitlement.

Rejected (REJ):

Where:

  • an effective DAP pre-birth/placement claim was made and has an ASSessed status of POB/POP before 1 July 2023, and
  • proof of birth/placement is submitted, and
  • the actual DOB/DOP is on or after 1 July 2023

Not Effective (NEF):

  • DAP Pre-birth/ placement claim where the expected date of birth/ placement is on or after 1 July 2023 and the claim date of receipt is on or after 1 July 2023

OR

  • DAP post birth claim is ASS-ASD and actual date of birth/ placement is on or after 1 July 2023
  • DAP claim is started before 1 July 2023 and continued and submitted on or after 1 July 2023 and the actual date of birth/placement is on or after 1 July 2023
