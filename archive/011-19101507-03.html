Check eligibility for Treatment and Management Plan items and psychological treatment services

Table 1: This table describes the process for checking patient eligibility for an eating disorder treatment and management plan (items 90250-90257,90260-90263 and 92146-92167) and eating disorder psychological treatment services (items 82352-82383, 90271-90282, 92182-92200 and 93076-93137).

Step

Action

1

Advise caller of online options + Read more ...

Advise the caller:

  • GPs can use the patient care plan history search in HPOS to view their patient's care plan history
  • Health professionals can use the MBS Item Online Checker in HPOS to see if their patient is eligible for an MBS item
  • All patients can access their Medicare claims history through their Medicare online account or Express Plus app

Patients and health professionals can follow instructions on the Services Australia website.

If they want to continue with the call, go to Step 2.

2

Complete Security check + Read more ...

Note: proceed only if the caller meets the security check.

Advise the caller that the eligibility information provided is based on the patient’s Medicare claiming history at the time of their enquiry and is subject to change if there are claims pending.

See Perform security check when a health professional or their staff call about a claim table in Perform telephone security check for Medicare health professionals.

3

Retrieve the patient’s history + Read more ...

Key ‘NHSI,CARDNUMBER,PATIENT’ and press [Enter].

Press [PF9].

The patient PIN is displayed.

Note: check history by PIN in all instances to ensure the complete claiming history is returned, not just the history associated with one card.

4

Check for eating disorder treatment and management plan items on the patient’s history + Read more ...

Key ‘NHSI,PIN,ITEM/90250-90257,SORT/DOS’ and press [Enter].

Key ‘NHSI,PIN,ITEM/90260-90263,SORT/DOS’ and press [Enter].

Key ‘NHSI,PIN,ITEM/92146-92167,SORT/DOS’ and press [Enter].

What service is the eligibility check for?

  • Plan items, go to Step 5
  • Treatment items, go to Step 7

5

Review result of patient history search for eating disorder plan item + Read more ...

Does the patient history display a paid claim for a plan item with a date of service in the last 12 months?

  • Yes, go to Step 6
  • No:
    • Advise the caller that the patient may be eligible for the relevant plan item
    • Remind the caller that this information is based on the patient's Medicare claims history for today's date. Claims that have not been processed yet may affect their eligibility.
    • Procedure ends here

6

Advise the caller of the patient's eligibility for eating disorder plan item + Read more ...

Advise the caller that the patient is not eligible for an eating disorder treatment plan item.

Note: Service Officers can advise when the patient's last plan item was if the caller asks.

Procedure ends here.

7

Continue investigation for patient eligibility of eating disorder treatment item + Read more ...

Does the patient history display a paid claim for a plan item with a date of service in the last 12 months?

  • Yes, go to Step 8
  • No, advise the caller that the patient is not eligible for eating disorder psychological treatment items as there is no current eating disorder planning items on the patients history. Procedure ends here

See also: Mental health treatment plans (MHTP) under Medicare (Better Access initiative) if the patient has a mental health treatment plan.

8

Advise caller of the patient's eligibility for treatment items + Read more ...

Patients are eligible for up to 40 eating disorder psychological treatment services under their EDP.

Eating disorder treatment services includes initial individual and group psychological treatment services provided under the Better Access initiative.

Count the number of MHTP and eating disorder psychological treatment items on the patient's claims history since the plan date:

  • Key NHSI, PIN, ITEM/283-287, SORT/DOS and press [Enter]
  • Key NHSI, PIN, ITEM/371-372, SORT/DOS and press [Enter]
  • Key NHSI, PIN, ITEM/2721-2727, SORT/DOS and press [Enter]
  • Key NHSI, PIN, ITEM/91818-91821, SORT/DOS and press [Enter]
  • Key NHSI, PIN, ITEM/91842-91845, SORT/DOS and press [Enter]
  • Key NHSI,PIN,ITEM/91166-91176,SORT/DOS and press [Enter]
  • Key NHSI,PIN,ITEM/91181-91188,SORT/DOS and press [Enter]
  • Key NHSI,PIN,ITEM/80000-80021,SORT/DOS and press [Enter]
  • Key NHSI,PIN,ITEM/80100-80171,SORT/DOS and press [Enter]
  • Key NHSI,PIN,ITEM/82352-82383,SORT/DOS and press [Enter]
  • Key NHSI,PIN,ITEM/90271-90282,SORT/DOS and press [Enter]
  • Key NHSI,PIN,ITEM/92182-92200,SORT/DOS and press [Enter]
  • Key NHSI,PIN,ITEM/93076-93103,SORT/DOS and press [Enter]
  • Key NHSI,PIN,ITEM/93110-93137,SORT/DOS and press [Enter]

Does patient history display more than 40 services?

  • Yes, advise the caller that the patient is not eligible for psychological treatments services under their EDP
  • No, advise the caller:
    • the patient may be eligible for an eating disorder psychological service
    • there is a requirement for regular reviews of the eating disorder treatment and management plan and appropriate referral is required before providing treatment services. Direct callers to MBS Online for full descriptions and explanatory notes
    • this information is based on the patient's Medicare claims history for today's date. Claims that have not been processed yet may affect their eligibility

Note: if they ask, Service Officers can tell the caller how many services the patient has had under their care plan and how many they have remaining. Capping limits apply to some items under the Better Access initiative. See the Resources page for the Eligibility for treatment services infographic.

Does a patient history display a paid item within the above ranges?

  • Yes, go to Step 5
  • No:
    • Advise the caller that the patient is not eligible for eating disorder dietetic treatment services as there is no current eating disorder treatment and management plan on the patient's history
    • Remind the caller that this information is based on the patient's Medicare claims history for today's date. Claims that have not been processed yet may affect their eligibility
    • Procedure ends here

5

Advise caller of the patient’s eligibility + Read more ...

Patients are eligible for up to 20 dietitian treatment services if they have a current EDP.

Count the number of dietetic treatment items on the patient's claims history since the plan date.

  • Key NHSI,PIN,ITEM/10954,SORT/DOS’ and press [Enter]
  • Key NHSI,PIN,ITEM/82350-82351,SORT/DOS and press [Enter]
  • Key NHSI,PIN,ITEM/93074,SORT/DOS and press [Enter]
  • Key NHSI,PIN,ITEM/93108,SORT/DOS and press [Enter]

Does patient history display more than 20 services?

  • Yes, advise the caller the patient is not eligible for dietetic services
  • No, advise the caller the patient may be eligible for dietetics treatment services

Remind the customer that this information is based on the patient’s Medicare claims history for today’s date. Claims that have not been processed yet may affect their eligibility.

Note: if they ask, Service Officers can tell the caller how many services the patient has had under their care plan and how many they have remaining.

Service Officers can also advise the caller that if the patient has used all the services under the existing referral, they need to get a new referral from their GP.
