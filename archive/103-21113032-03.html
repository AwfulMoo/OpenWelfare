Participant requires access to IM funds, including funds held for disbursement after commencing on the CDC

Table 1: This table provides the process for CDC participants to gain access to their IM funds that are held for disbursement after commencing on the CDC (including access to ‘available’ funds).

Step

Action

1

Participant contacts to access IM funds + Read more ...

Participants who have transferred from Income Management to the Cashless Debit Card may have residual funds in their IM account that they need to access.

Participants do not need to provide a reason for their requests, as the funds remain restricted either under IM or CDC legislation.

Check the following to confirm the participant is on CDC:

  • Benefit Status line for ‘IQN’
  • Debit Card Trial Assessment Summary (Q2AS) screen to check the CDC start date and confirm the participant has completed their waiting period
  • Payment Summary (PS) screen for ‘multi desti’, to show the participant’s payments are split between the CDC and regular account

Is the participant on CDC?

  • Yes, go to Step 2
  • No, for customers
    • in their waiting period who would like to bring their CDC start date forward, see Cashless Debit Card (CDC) - changing initial waiting periods
    • in the Northern Territory that have not transitioned for the CDC but are interested in moving to the CDC, see Income Management (IM) to Cashless Debit Card (CDC) Transition in the Northern Territory
    • interested in volunteering for the CDC, see Cashless Debit Card (CDC) Volunteers
    • all other customers
      In Customer First, DOC the customer's record. Use Fast Note - select Auto Text, use Generic > Cashless Debit Card > CDC Transition Enquiry. Procedure ends here
      In Process Direct, add a Note on the record. Refer to Resources and use the ‘CDC Participation Enquiry’ template when creating a note on the customer’s record. Procedure ends here

2

Check the IM Balance + Read more ...

Run the Expense Management Summary workflow and check the participant’s:

  • current IM balance
  • available IM balance
  • scheduled disbursements to the CDC account

Does the participant have funds in their IM account or scheduled disbursements to the CDC account?

  • Yes, go to Step 3
  • No, advise the participant of their IM balance. Procedure ends here

3

Disbursement Options + Read more ...

While participants do not need to provide a reason for their requests, it is important to discuss the participant’s needs as the options below can vary on when the participant receives the funds.

Participants can choose one or more of the following options. To disburse the available IM funds:

  • to the BasicsCard (funds are available immediately), go to Coding Income Management expenses Table 7
  • by recalling BasicsCard funds to the IM account for transfer to the CDC account (funds are available immediately), go to Recall of funds from a BasicsCard to a customer's Income Management account Table 1
  • via a One off Expense (funds are transferred to Third Party Organisations (TPO’s) within 24 to 48 hours), go to Coding Income Management expenses Table 2
  • to the CDC account via an Unrestricted Cash Payment (funds are available within 3 business days), go to Unrestricted Cash Payments (UCPs) Table 3
  • by requesting access to held funds that are due to disburse to the CDC account (funds are available the following business day). Go to Step 4

4

Accessing Held Funds + Read more ...

If a participant requires access to their held funds, email the CDC Transition Team for action.

Subject line: URGENT access needed to held funds

Email Body:

  • Participant's CRN:
  • Name:
  • Amount: Remaining funds to be disbursed (both held and available funds)
  • Other Details:

Advise the participant that funds can take approximately 24 hours to arrive in their CDC account. Procedure ends here.
