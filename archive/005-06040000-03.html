Coding a PPE authority on a customer’s record

Step

Action

1

A customer contacts to update their PPE status + Read more ...

A Status Resolution Support Services (SRSS) payment customer contacts to update their PPE status. The customer may wish to authorise another person (including their partner) or organisation to enquire on their behalf, amend or end PPE details. When appointing a PPE, check the customer is aware of what information can be given to the PPE.

Note: PPU and nominee arrangements do not apply for SRSS as the payment is not paid under social security law.

Confirm customer's identity before any updates are made.

What PPE activity is required?

  • Record a new PPE, go to Step 2
  • Amend an existing PPE, go to Step 2
  • Cancelling an existing PPE, go to Step 5

2

Appointing a PPE + Read more ...

Who is the customer giving permission to enquire?

  • the customer's partner, go to Step 4
  • not the customer's partner, go to Step 3

3

Obtain details of customer's PPE authority + Read more ...

If the SRSS customer is appointing their Home Affairs contracted service provider as their PPE, information can be obtained verbally from the customer.

In all other situations, issue the Status Resolution Support Services Authorising a person or organisation to enquire of your behalf (SU698) form for the SRSS customer to complete.

To record a new PPE authority:

  • use Fast Note - select Auto text, use Data Quality > Updates > Third Party Permitted to enquire, or
  • type 'third' into the Search field:
    • select Third Party Permitted to Enquire from the available menu options
    • check there is a tick in the DOA DOC. This automatically populates the DOA DOC field with the correct literal of 'PPE', which ensures the DOC does not expire before the authority ends. See Creating documents (Fast Note, Callbacks and ACTDOCs)

If the Fast Note is not available, manually record the PPE authority by recording/updating the following details using a Display on Access (DOA) DOC:

Note: select 'PPE' reason in the DOA DOC field and extend the expiry date to ensure the DOC does not expire before the authority ends.

  • name/organisation
  • contact person (if an organisation)
  • relationship to customer
  • date of birth (if an individual)
  • password
  • daytime phone number
  • address
  • period: Indefinite or from / / to / /
  • any limitations to the authority
  • the customer's acceptance of PPE

Procedure ends here.

4

Update partner PPE to give authority + Read more ...

If a customer wants to give authority to their partner, add a new line when updating the marital status. Do not delete or correct existing entries unless they were originally incorrect.

  • code a new line on the Marital Status (MS) screen
  • Event Date: field - use today's date
  • Marital Status: field - restate the marital status
  • PPE (Cust/C): - update indicator to 'Y' for customer to record authority for partner to enquire
  • if the partner wants to provide authority for the customer, update this in the:
    • customer's record - update PPE (Ptnr/P): indicator to 'Y', or
    • partner's record - swap to the partner's record then update the PPE (Cust/C): indicator to 'Y'
  • record source and receipt date
  • select Continue
  • navigate to the Assessment Results (AR) screen to finalise activity
  • read the privacy statement regarding collection of PPE information to the customer and their partner. See Privacy Statement on Resources page

Procedure ends here.

5

Cancelling PPE Authority + Read more ...

Who previously had PPE authority?

  • the customer's partner, go to Step 6
  • someone other than the customer's partner, go to Step 7

6

Cancelling details of PPE + Read more ...

In the customer's record, go to the Marital Status (MS) screen and make the following updates:

  • code the relevant date in the Event Date: field
    • if the cancellation of PPE is due to a separation, the authority will automatically cease once the separation has been processed on the customer's record
    • otherwise, the date is the date of notification
  • restate the marital status in the Marital Status: field
  • update PPE: indicator to 'N' for customer (C) to cancel authority for partner to enquire
  • select Continue
  • navigate to the AR screen to finalise activity
  • record details on a DOC and finalise the activity

If the PPE authority is also recorded in a Display on Access (DOA) DOC, the DOC must be expired.

Procedure ends here.

7

PPE for non-partners is recorded using a Display On Access DOC + Read more ...

To cancel the PPE status, expire the DOC.

Ending the PPE status in Customer First:

  • annotate the DOC to advise customer requested PPE arrangement to cease
  • highlight the PPE DOA DOC
  • from the 'More' tab, select 'expire or extend'
  • complete the expiry date field with the date PPE is to cease
  • select continue to finalise
