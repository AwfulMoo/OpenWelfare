Process and calculate compensation payments

Item

Category

1

Compensation online Estimator + Read more ...

See Resources for a link to the Compensation Estimator.

On the Compensation Estimator page:

  • select Use the compensation estimator at the bottom of the page

2

Compensation payment details screen + Read more ...

To calculate the compensation estimate complete the following fields:

Proposed gross lump sum amount: field.

  • Key the gross lump sum amount including any:
    • medical costs
    • legal costs, and
    • other payment for non economic loss that will be paid for this incident

Total amount of lump sum advances for the same incident: field.

  • Key any lump sum payments paid in advance for the same incident

Have you received any periodic payments?

  • Yes, will go to the Payback of periodic compensation payments
  • No, will go to What is the date of the incident?

Payback of periodic compensation payments: field.

  • Key the amount of wages to be paid for the gross settlement amount

Has this payment ceased?

  • Yes, key the date the periodic payments were last paid
  • No, will use the date the estimate is calculated

What was the date after periodic payments ceased?

Key the date to which periodic compensation were last paid. If none, key the date which lost earnings or lost capacity to earn commenced

Select Submit. This will show the Compensation payments results details screen.
