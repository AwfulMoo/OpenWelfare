Example – zeroing off arrears when granting claim for PPL or DAP

Table 3: an example of how to zero arrears.

Item

Description

1

Correcting a PPL child’s date of birth resulting in incorrect arrears

A Service Officer identifies that a child's date of birth was incorrectly recorded as 27 July instead of 18 July. The Service Officer corrects the date of birth which results in the customer’s PPL changing to PPL/REJ and a PPL overpayment debt shell is being created.

The Service Officer contacts debt recovery and the debt is set to $0.00.

To correct the PPL status, the claim is processed as a PPL claim outside of PPL claim timeframes. The original claim information is added to a new claim using a SOA shell in Process Direct or the Assisted Customer Claim (ACC) within Customer First with a DOR: of today's date (being the date that the review is taking place).

If the claim rejects ‘C12’, an override is required.

Once the claim coding has been completed the Service Officer will need to refer the claim for an override.

Once the override has been coded, the claim is returned to the Service Officer who checks and prepares to finalise the claim. No contact is made to confirm details as no payment will be issued.

PPL payment is being generated in the activity, however because the debt was set to zero it is not appropriate to pay the customer again.

To zero off the PPL arrears,

For Customer First:

  • Place an 'R' on the PPL result line to navigate to the Rate Component (RAC) screen
  • Select relevant periods on the Daily Rate Component (RAC) and key ‘0’ in the Man Adj $ field and ‘ADJ’ in the Man Adj Rsn field
  • Select the arrears and reduce to $0.00

Once the all arrears are removed, return to the Assessment Results (AR) screen to finalise the claim.

For Process Direct:

  • Key ‘RAC’ in the SuperKey field to navigate to the Customer Daily Rate Component (RAC) screen
  • Go to the relevant date range for the debt that was generated by the rejection activity and key ‘0’ in the $ Man Adj field and ‘ADJ’ in the Man Adj Reason field for the relevant dates

Once the all arrears are removed, return to the Entitlement (ELD) screen to finalise the claim.
