Assessing and coding an Identity Review Period or Alternative Identity for a customer unable to attend in person

Table 1

Step

Action

1

Customer is unable to attend in person + Read more ...

When a customer is affected by an exception circumstances staff must determine if the customer:

  • needs an Identity review applied by phone, or
  • an Alternative Identity assessment completed by phone

Note: documents that are verified with an issuing authority through DVS provide a higher level of assurance in the claimed identity.

Coding and assessing Alternative Identity by phone may result in some identity categories coded as ‘ZZ Cannot provide’ or ‘YY will provide later’.

To achieve a higher level assurance when completing Alternative identity staff MUST request and code available identity documents that can be verified in DVS before accepting documents that are not.

For example only:

  • Commencement of Identity - ZZ cannot provide, when customer does not possess a document and the SS257 process is not applicable.
  • Primary use in Community - Driver licence when available over a Proof of Age Card which is not verified)
  • Secondary use in Community - Medicare card when available over a bank card which is not verified)

Refer to Identity documents A to Z which shows documents that are sent to DVS.

This assessment may result in a reviewable or non-reviewable outcome and supported by a verified document where possible.

Does the customer have a valid reason for not attending or is an Identity Review Period appropriate?

  • Yes, go to Step 2
  • No,
    • tell the customer or nominee how they can provide documents by phone, online or in person. Also that the customer can complete linkage or an Alternative Identity assessment in person at a service centre or Agent site, or
    • if applicable by Video chat to complete linkage
    • procedure ends here

2

Determine when the customer can attend in person + Read more ...

If the customer:

  • because of exceptional circumstances, can never attend in person, go to Step 3
  • can attend within the Identity Review Period, and an identity review period is appropriate by phone, go to the Identity Confirmation Dashboard
  • select Identity Confirmation from the Task Selector
  • select Start
  • if available, code and verify identity documents for 1 or 2 identity categories
  • select Will provide later for categories where identity documents are not available
  • select Next
  • answer the questions to apply the relevant review period and give the customer additional time to complete Identity Confirmation
  • advise customer to provide all outstanding identity documents including the photo linkage document in person at a Service Centre within this period
  • Note: request customers to complete linkage via Video chat where applicable
  • where a customer provides all three required identity documents, do not code the SUiC document until the Identity Review Period has been applied
  • if all three identity documents have been coded, follow the Identity SME escalation process to request deletion of the SUiC document. Apply the Identity Review Period and then recode the SUiC details

Procedure ends here.

3

The customer will never be able to attend in person + Read more ...

Use the Identity Confirmation Dashboard to complete an Alternative Identity assessment with the customer by phone.

For customers that can provide all required identity documents:

  • go to the Identity Confirmation Dashboard in Process Direct
  • select the CoI and PUiC identity documents provided from each category
  • select Cannot provide for the SUiC identity documents
  • code and verify CoI & PUiC documents
  • select Next
  • complete question set in Alternative Identity assessment workflow
    Note:     to access the Alternative Identity assessment do not code the SUiC document. If all 3 identity documents have been coded, see Identity SME escalation process to request deletion of the SUiC document. The SUiC document can only be coded once the alternative identity assessment has been applied

For customers unable to provide the required identity documents

  • select available identity documents from CoI, PUiC or SUiC category
  • select Cannot provide for the remaining categories
  • select Next
  • code and verify available identity documents
  • complete question set in Alternative Identity assessment workflow

This will

  • apply an appropriate alternative identity category for the customer's circumstances, and
  • allow any outstanding claims for payments and services to be granted without generating future reviews

Procedure ends here.

Identity documents lodged for customers unable to attend in person

Table 2

Step

Action

1

Identity Status + Read more ...

Check the customer's Identity Status under the Snapshot icon in Process Direct.

Is the customer's identity status 'Confirmed'?

  • Yes,
    • identity documents are not required for this customer
    • procedure ends here
  • No, go to Step 2

2

Identity documents lodged + Read more ...

Customers unable to attend an office or Agent site in person can lodge identity documents through various channels when certain criteria are met.

If documents are lodged:

  • by a Correspondence Nominee (check the NORS screen for confirmation) or a person who has Express Authority or Implied Authority. Go to Step 3
  • by a third party with no Nominee access or implied authority, go to Step 4
  • online, go to Step 5
  • by mail, go to Step 6
  • via a Rural Financial Counsellor (RFC), go to Step 7
  • by phone, go to Step 9

3

Documents lodged by correspondence nominee or person with express or implied authority + Read more ...

Find out why the customer cannot attend in person. A valid reason is required when a third party is lodging identity documents. Discuss when and if the customer may be able to attend.

Does the customer have a valid reason for not attending?

  • Yes,
    • DOC the valid reason and discussion
    • Go to Step 8
  • No,
    • Customer must attend the office in person
    • Advise the nominee
    • DOC the record
    • Procedure ends here

4

Documents lodged by a third party + Read more ...

Do not accept documents from a third party for identity purposes.

Discuss a nominee arrangement and if appropriate, issue form. See Appointing nominees for more information.

Procedure ends here

5

Documents lodged online + Read more ...

  • Documents can be lodged online via the Upload documents service
  • For online claims, including Circumstance Change Monitor (CCM) aligned claims
  • Make sure the documents are legible, go to Step 8

6

Documents lodged by mail + Read more ...

Identity documents must be originals. If copies are received, advise the customer to attend in person with the original documents. Extra time may be given to the customer. See Coding identity documents.

Update the documents using the Identity Confirmation Dashboard in Process Direct at the first point of contact. Otherwise, scan documents to the customer's record.

Return original documents to the person or authorised nominee via registered mail.

Note: do not encourage customers to send documents by post due to the risk of documents becoming lost.

Go to Step 8

7

Documents received via Rural Financial Counsellor (RFC) + Read more ...

If and only when the customer is attending a financial counselling appointment, the RFC can sight, copy and certify the identity documents to be used for Identity Confirmation.

Go to Step 8.

8

Check Identity documents + Read more ...

Compare the name and date of birth on the person’s identity documents with the Customer Personal Detail Summary (CPDS) screen.

Note: there should only be one date of birth on the CPDS screen. If there is more than one, find out which date of birth is correct. Update the CPDS so that all names have one date of birth and DOC details of the determination. See Verifying the date of birth or age of a customer or child.

Are the name and date of birth the same?

  • Yes, go to Step 9
  • No,
    • make sure it is the correct record
    • if name has changed, complete the name change process. See Changing the Legal name of customers or their children
    • make sure there is an identity document linking the two different names together. For example, a marriage certificate
    • record all name variations on the CPDS screen
    • go to Step 9

9

Coding identity documents + Read more ...

Code identity document details through the Identity Confirmation Dashboard. See Coding identity documents.
