Check eligibility for GP management plan (GPMP) items

Table 1: this table describes the process for checking patient eligibility for GPMP items (721, 229, 92024, and 92055).

Step

Action

1

Advise caller of online options + Read more ...

Advise the caller of their options to check a patient's eligibility:

  • GPs can use the patient care plan history search in HPOS to view their patient's care plan history
  • Health professionals can use the MBS Items Online Checker in HPOS to see if their patient is eligible for an MBS item
  • All patients can access their Medicare claims history through their Medicare online account or Express Plus app

Patients and health professionals can follow instructions on the Services Australia website.

If they want to continue with the call, go to Step 2.

2

Complete Security check + Read more ...

  • For health professionals, see Health professional or their staff call about a claim
  • For consumers, see Perform security check to authenticate Medicare record

Note: proceed only if the caller meets the security check.

Advise the caller that the eligibility information provided is based on the patient's Medicare claiming history at the time of the enquiry and is subject to change if there are claims pending.

3

Retrieve the patient's history + Read more ...

Key 'NHSI,CARDNUMBER,PATIENT' and press [Enter].

Press [PF9].

The patient PIN displays.

Note: check history by PIN in all instances to ensure the complete claiming history is returned, not just the history associated with one card.

4

Check for CDM items on the patient's history + Read more ...

  • Key 'NHSI,PIN,ITEM/229-233' and press [Enter]
  • Key 'NHSI,PIN,ITEM/721-732' and press [Enter]
  • Key 'NHSI,PIN,ITEM/92024-92103' and press [Enter]

Does patient history display a paid item within the above ranges?

  • Yes, go to Step 5
  • No, advise the caller that the patient is eligible for a GP management plan
    • Remind the caller that this information is based on the patient's Medicare claims history for today's date. Claims that have not been processed yet may affect eligibility
    • Procedure ends here

5

Advise caller of the patient's eligibility + Read more ...

Item 721, 229, 92024, and 92055 cannot be claimed within:

  • 12 months of an item 721, 229, 92024 and 92055

or

  • 3 months of an item 231, 232, 233, 724, 726, 727, 729, 731, 732, 92024, 92026, 92027, 92028, 92057, 92058 and 92059

Does the patient history display any paid items in the last 12 months or 3 months as per the above item ranges?

  • Yes, advise the caller that the patient is not eligible for a GP management plan item
  • No, advise the caller that the patient is eligible for a GP management plan item

Note: if the caller asks, Service Officers can advise when the patient's last plan item was.

Remind the caller that this information is based on the patient's Medicare claims history for today's date. Claims that have not been processed yet may affect their eligibility.

Does the patient history display a paid group assessment item in this calendar year?

  • No, advise the caller that the patient is not eligible as they have not had a group assessment service.
    • Procedure ends here
  • Yes, go to Step 7

7

Check for group treatment services on the patients history + Read more ...

All patients are eligible for a maximum of 8 allied health group services each calendar year.

Patients in a RACF are also eligible for 2 additional physical therapy services (item 93620).

  • Key 'NHSI,PIN,ITEM/81105,SORT/DOS' and press [Enter]
  • Key 'NHSI,PIN,ITEM/81115,SORT/DOS' and press [Enter]
  • Key 'NHSI,PIN,ITEM/81125,SORT/DOS' and press [Enter]
  • Key 'NHSI,PIN,ITEM/93285,SORT/DOS' and press [Enter]
  • Key 'NHSI,PIN,ITEM/93613-93615,SORT/DOS' and press [Enter]

Does the patient history display 8 paid group treatment items in the calendar year?

  • Yes, advise the caller that the patient is not eligible for the item
    • To check eligibility for RACF additional physical therapy group treatment item 93620, go to Step 8
  • No, advise the caller that the patient is eligible for an item in the above range
    • the patient is not eligible for RACF additional physical therapy item 93620 as they have not completed the initial course of group treatment
    • Remind the caller that this information is based on the patient's Medicare claims history for today's date. Claims that have not been processed yet may affect their eligibility
    • if the caller asks, Service Officers can advise how many services the patient has had under their care plan and how many they have remaining
    • Procedure ends here
