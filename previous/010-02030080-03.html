This page contains information to assist Service Officers assess eligibility for the ABSTUDY Tertiary Award.

Assessment procedure for ABSTUDY Tertiary Award

This table describes the steps to follow to assess eligibility for the ABSTUDY Tertiary Award and provides information on additional benefits and allowances for approved students.

Step

Action

1

Meeting the general eligibility criteria for ABSTUDY + Read more ...

Does the student/Australian Apprentice (apprentice or trainee) meet the general eligibility criteria for ABSTUDY?

  • Yes, go to Step 2
  • No, go to Step 12

2

Meeting the definition of an Australian Apprentice (apprentice or trainee) or a student in an approved mainstream or Indigenous course + Read more ...

Does the customer meet the definition of an Australian Apprentice (apprentice or trainee) or are they a student enrolled in an approved mainstream or Indigenous course?

  • Yes, go to Step 3
  • No, go to Step 12

3

Minimum school leaving age requirements + Read more ...

Has the student/Australian Apprentice reached the minimum school leaving age as defined by the relevant State/Territory education authority or has provided evidence that they have been granted exemption from the State/Territory education authority in order to attend TAFE or another tertiary institution?

  • Yes, go to Step 4
  • No, go to Step 12

4

Student or Australian Apprentice + Read more ...

Is the customer a student or an Australian Apprentice?

  • If a student, go to Step 5
  • If an Australian Apprentice, go to Step 10

5

Post-secondary course + Read more ...

Is the student studying a post-secondary course?

  • Yes, go to Step 6
  • No, go to Step 12

6

Startup Year Course + Read more ...

See Startup Year Courses for further information.

Is the student studying a Startup Year Course?

  • Yes, go to Step 7
  • No, go to Step 12

7

STARTUP-HELP loan + Read more ...

See the Resources page for link to the StudyAssist website for more information on STARTUP-HELP loans.

Has the student been selected for a STARTUP-HELP loan by their higher education provider?

  • Yes, go to Step 8
  • No, go to Step 12

8

Studying a full-time workload + Read more ...

Is the student studying a full-time workload (i.e. the normal amount of full-time study) or qualifies for a study load concession?

  • Yes, go to Step 9
  • No, go to Step 12

9

Satisfactory progress + Read more ...

Is the student making satisfactory progress for the purposes of ABSTUDY?

Note: if checking eligibility at or prior to new claim stage, advise student of this requirement for retaining eligibility.

  • Yes, go to Step 10
  • No, go to Step 12

10

Living in Australia or eligible for overseas study provisions + Read more ...

Is the student/Australian Apprentice either:

  • living in Australia while studying/training, or
  • eligible for assistance under the overseas study provisions?
  • Yes, go to Step 11
  • No, go to Step 12

11

Customer meets the eligibility criteria for ABSTUDY Tertiary Award + Read more ...

Advise the customer they are considered to meet the eligibility criteria for ABSTUDY Tertiary Award.

A student approved for the ABSTUDY Tertiary Award may qualify for the following benefits:

  • Living Allowance or Pensioner Education Supplement
  • Incidentals Allowance
  • Additional Incidentals Allowance
  • Fares Allowance
  • Rent Assistance
  • Remote Area Allowance
  • Pharmaceutical Allowance
  • Away From Base assistance
  • Additional Assistance
  • Relocation Scholarship
  • Student Start-up Loan

An Australian Apprentice approved for the ABSTUDY Tertiary Award may qualify for the following benefits:

  • Living Allowance
  • Incidentals Allowance
  • Rent Assistance
  • Remote Area Allowance
  • Pharmaceutical Allowance
  • Additional Assistance

Also advise them to lodge a claim for ABSTUDY, if they have not already done so.

Record details on a DOC.

Procedure ends here.

12

Customer does not meet the eligibility criteria for ABSTUDY Tertiary Award + Read more ...

Advise the customer they are not considered to meet the eligibility criteria for ABSTUDY Tertiary Award.

  • Investigate eligibility for an alternative payment

  • Record details on a DOC

Note: while general advice based on information provided by the customer can be given over the phone, the customer should be encouraged to test their eligibility in person, particularly in cases of discretionary decisions.

For more information on claims see: Claiming ABSTUDY.
