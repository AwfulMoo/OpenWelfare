Composite items – example 1

Benefits should be billed under composite item 39128 where a health professional fits a patient with:

  • a spinal catheter (item 39125), and
  • an infusion pump which is filled with a therapeutic agent to manage chronic intractable pain (item 39126)

Example of composite items - group T8, subgroup 3

Table 1: This table provides an example of composite items from group T8, subgroup 3.

Item

Group T8 – Surgical operations, Subgroup 7 – Neurosurgical Pain Relief

39125

Intrathecal or epidural SPINAL CATHETER

insertion or replacement of, and connection to a subcutaneous implanted infusion pump. For the management of chronic intractable pain (Anaes.) (Assist.)

Fee: $310.10 Benefit: 75% = $232.60

39126

INFUSION PUMP

subcutaneous implantation or replacement of, and connection of the pump to an intrathecal or epidural catheter, and filling of reservoir with a therapeutic agent or agents, with or without programming the pump. For the management of chronic intractable pain (Anaes.) (Assist.)

Fee: $376.55 Benefit: 75% = $282.45

39128

INFUSION PUMP

subcutaneous implantation of, AND intrathecal or epidural SPINAL CATHETER insertion of, and connection of pump to catheter, and filling of reservoir with a therapeutic agent or agents, with or without programming the pump. For the management of chronic intractable pain (Anaes.) (Assist.)

Fee: $686.65 Benefit: 75% = $515.00

Composite items – example 2

Benefits are payable under composite item composite item 58112 (x-ray of the spine - two regions) and not the individual items when:

  • items 58100 (x-ray of cervical spine), and
  • 58106 (x-ray of lumbo-sacral spine) are performed by the one health professional

Example composite items - group I3, subgroup 4

Table 2: This table provides an example of composite items from group I3, subgroup 4.

Item

Group I3 – Diagnostic radiology, Subgroup 4 – Radiographic examination of spine and report

58100

Spine - cervical (R)

Fee: $68.75

Benefit: 95% = $65.35, 85% = $58.45

58106

Spine - lumbosacral (R)

Fee: $78.85

Benefit: 95% = $74.95, 85% = $67.05

58112

Note: an account issued or a patient assignment form must show the item numbers of the examinations performed under this item.

Spine - 2 examinations of the kind mentioned in items 58100, 58103, 58106 and 58109 (R)

Fee: $99.60

Benefit: 95% = $94.65, 85% = $84.70

Composite items – example 3

Benefits are payable under composite item 41554 (mastoidectomy, intact wall technique, with myringoplasty and ossicular chain reconstruction) and not the individual items when:

  • items 41539 (ossicular chain reconstruction), and
  • 41551 (mastoidectomy, intact wall technique, with myringoplasty) are performed by the same health professional
