Update relationship details service - partnered to single

This table describes how to help customers update their relationship details using their Centrelink online account.

Item

Description

1

Using this service + Read more ...

Some customers are not eligible to use the Update relationship details service. For information on who is eligible to use the service, see the Eligible customers in the Background tab.

The Update relationship details service starts when the customer uses one of the following options:

  • select the MENU > My details > Personal and contact details > My profile > Update relationship details
  • select the MENU > My details > Personal and contact details > Update relationship details, or
  • uses the Centrelink online account What are you looking for? search function

To cancel a started separation details update, the customer must select Cancel update. A message will display:

You have chosen to cancel your separation update.

  • You will need to start your update again
  • After it is cancelled, the update cannot be viewed or continued
  • This action cannot be undone

The customer can select Cancel update to confirm or Continue update if they no longer want to cancel the service. When cancelled, the online separation update cannot be viewed or continued. No information is recorded.

The customer can start a new Update relationship details service at any time.

2

Update relationship details progress + Read more ...

The Your separation update has been started page displays 5 steps that the customer must complete in order:

  • Step 1 Your circumstances
  • Step 2 Your accommodation details
  • Step 3 Your financial details
  • Step 4 Review and confirm
  • Step 5 Next steps

The customer cannot start a new step until the previous one is done.

The customer begins by selecting Start in each step as they move through them.

Each step contains modules with relevant questions. For each set question the customer can select Previous to go back to the previous question. When each module is completed and the customer reaches the end of each step, they are returned to the Your separation update has been started page to start the next step.

As each step is completed, the customer has the option to return to a previously completed step to change their answers by selecting the Edit option. The customer can navigate directly to the module they want to update by selecting the relevant tab.

Customers can navigate between pages. If the answer to a question is entered incorrectly, an amber warning icon or red error will appear to advise them how to enter the answer correctly.

The update does not have to be completed in one session. Customers can leave the Your separation update has been started page at any time and responses will be saved. The update can be continued later. If the started update has not been submitted within 13 weeks, it will cancel. The customer will need to start their update again.

3

Customer’s circumstances + Read more ...

Questions will depend on the customer's circumstances and the payment they receive. This could include:

  • separation details
  • contact details
  • address details
  • child details
  • contact and address details of one referee

4

Accommodation details + Read more ...

Questions will depend on the customer’s basic accommodation and living arrangements. This could include:

  • home ownership details
  • life interest details
  • paying for accommodation details (rent, fees, etc.)
  • sharer details

5

Financial details + Read more ...

These questions allow the customer to give or confirm details about their financial income and asset details. This could include:

  • payment destination details
  • savings
  • other assets
  • other updates to income and assets

6

Review and confirm + Read more ...

The Confirm details page includes a summary of all information provided by the customer, so they can check the details they have provided are correct. This information displays in sections, e.g. Your circumstances.

The customer can select the Update option in each section to change their answers. This will take them back to the relevant step to review/update the question and answer.

Declaration

When all information is correct, the customer must accept the ‘Declaration’ by ticking the box. This means a signed form is not needed.

Confirm information

After the customer accepts the declaration, they can select Confirm information. They are advised that when selected, it saves all the details they have provided and they will not be able to change their answers after this.

7

Next steps - uploading documents to verify change of circumstance + Read more ...

In the Next Steps step, the customer must complete Required tasks before they can submit their update.

The customer can select Get Form in each task to go to that form page on the Services Australia website to download, complete and save to their desktop.

After saving the completed form, they can select Upload in the task to upload and submit it.

Customers can also take photos of the completed forms and upload them using the Upload documents service in the Express Plus Centrelink mobile app.

In the Next steps section, select the Upload option next to each task.

If their documents are not ready, the customer can return later to upload them by:

  • going to the Update relationship details service in their Centrelink online account, and
  • accessing their started separation update

They can also upload these documents using the Express Plus Centrelink mobile app.

The system will cancel their separation update if all required documents have not been provided within 14 days of the date of request.

If they are unable to upload their documents, the customer can:

  • mail the documents, or
  • take them to a service centre

8

Submit update + Read more ...

Customers must complete Required tasks by providing requested documents.

The relationship update cannot be submitted if the Required tasks are not complete. When all tasks are completed, the Required status will change to Done. The customer can then select Submit to finalise their relationship details update.

9

Viewing separation details entered online

In Customer First + Read more ...

  • go to the Document List (DL) page
  • check for a DOC titled Cus Relationship update with content to advise the customer has submitted a relationship update online
  • if there is no DOC, check Process Direct

In Process Direct + Read more ...

  • go to the customer's record through the Customer Summary tile
  • check Transactions > All Transactions and Notes tiles
  • when the Update relationship service is used:
    • Transactions>All Transactions - check for a transaction titled Submitted xx/xx/xxxx Received xx/xx/xxxx – Customer Update – Advise Separation. Additional information may be recorded, depending on the customer’s circumstances, for example, customer is separated under one roof or safety concerns exist
    • Notes - check for a DOC titled Cust. Relationship update with content to advise the customer has submitted a relationship update online

Separation will be actioned when allocated to an appropriately skilled Service Officer.
