This page contains assessment examples.

On 24 April 2013 Services Australia is assessing whether the following individuals who reside in the Northern Territory would be subject to Income Management under the Disengaged Youth (DEY) measure. The Service Officer will look at the number of weeks, since 24 October 2012, that each individual has been receiving a relevant payment.

Assessment examples

Income Management Scenarios

This table contains different scenarios for Income Management under the Disengaged Youth measure.

Scenario

Description

1

Customer not eligible for IM under the Disengaged Youth measure + Read more ...

John has received Youth Allowance for the period 7 December 2012 to 25 January 2013 (7 weeks) and Newstart Allowance from 3 April 2013 to 24 April 2013 (3 weeks). John would not be subject to Income Management under the Disengaged Youth measure as he has only been in receipt of a relevant payment for a total of 10 weeks in the previous 26 weeks

Newstart Allowance was replaced by JobSeeker Payment (JSP) on 20 March 2020.

2

Customer in receipt of YA and NA over the past 26 weeks + Read more ...

Theo has been in receipt of Youth Allowance and Newstart Allowance over the past 26 weeks:

  • Youth Allowance from 1 November 2012 to 14 December 2012 (6 weeks)
  • Newstart Allowance from 7 February to date (24 April 2013) (11 weeks)

Theo would be eligible for Income Management under the Disengaged Youth measure as he has been on payments for more than 13 weeks during the past 26 week period

Newstart Allowance was replaced by JobSeeker Payment (JSP) on 20 March 2020.
