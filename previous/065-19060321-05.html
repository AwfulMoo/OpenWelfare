This page contains information about cancellation and rejection reasons for the Scheme.

Legend for cancellation and rejection codes

Table 1: This table describes a legend to assist in interpreting cancellation and rejection codes for the Home Equity Access Scheme.

Item

Description

1

Cancellation (CAN)

  • Cancellations for the Scheme claims are actioned in Customer First or Customer Record
  • Cancelled records can sometimes be manually or automatically restored
  • Before cancelling a customer's payment, care should be taken to establish that the customer has lost entitlement for that payment
  • Manual cancellations for claims are recorded via the HEAS Details (PLS) screen. Do not use the Benefit Action (BA) screen to cancel loan payments

2

Rejections (REJ) of claims

  • Rejections for claims are actioned in Process Direct
  • A new claim activity must be completed to get the customer on payment if the decision to reject was incorrect
  • Customers may withdraw their claims voluntarily or may have lodged an inappropriate or incorrect claim. In all these circumstances, claims under the Scheme are taken to have never been lodged. See Assessing Home Equity Access Scheme applications for more details
  • Rejections can never be restored
  • Manual rejections are recorded via the HEAS Details (PLS) screen. Do not use the Benefit Action (BA) screen to reject Scheme applications

3

Rejections of advance payments requests

  • Rejections for advance payment requests are actioned in Process Direct via the HEAS Advance (HEASADV) screen
  • Staff can withdraw an advance payment request at the customer’s request
  • See Advance payments for Home Equity Access Scheme loans for more details

4

Initiation

  • M - Manual cancellation or rejection codes that can be manually input via the Reject/Cease Reason field on the HEAS Details (PLS) screen
  • A - cancellation or rejection codes that automatically occur on the system based on coding input by staff or a system generated reassessment
    • The automated cancellation or rejection reason for a claim displays in the Reject/Cease Reason field on the HEAS Details (PLS) screen upon completion of the activity
    • The rejection reason for advance payments displays on the HEAS Advance (HEASADV) screen in Process Direct

5

Starting fortnightly payments

  • M - can be manually restored by updating the HEAS Details (PLS) screen. Some restorations may also require updating the customer’s circumstances. Do not use the Benefit Action (BA) screen to restore loan payments
  • A – can be automatically restored
  • N – cannot be restored
