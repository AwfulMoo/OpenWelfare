Do not follow this procedure for self-isolation, quarantine or caring periods that start on or before 17 January 2022. See Processing Pandemic Leave Disaster Payment Claims in Customer First.

If allocated an online claim or Assisted Customer Claim, do not process it in Customer First. If staff are allocated one and not trained in processing in Process Direct, they should speak with their team leader or Service Support Officer (SSO) for help reallocating the work item.

Customers can claim online again from 8am Wednesday 20 July 2022. If a customer calls, tell them to visit https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/pandemic-leave-disaster-payment for how to claim online. If a customer does not have access to online services, they can claim over the phone using Assisted Customer Claim (ACC) from 9am on the same date.

There are different claiming periods depending on the self-isolation, quarantine or caring period claimed. See the Resources page.

If a customer has lodged a claim online or using ACC that has not yet been finalised, they cannot lodge another one. If a customer calls to ask why they cannot lodge more than one claim, tell them:
‘You can lodge your next claim once you have received confirmation from Services Australia on the claim outcome of your previous claim.’

A claim can be rejected with no further customer contact if:

  • a customer lodged a fifth or subsequent claim, and
  • an RFI letter was sent requesting evidence to support that claim, and
  • they withdrew that fifth or subsequent claim or that claim was rejected as the customer ‘Failed to Provide Information’ (FPI) and
  • they then lodged a new fifth or subsequent claim without the evidence previously requested
    • To reject the claim using the rejection reason Do not meet conditions of payment, go to Step 34

  • lodged a fifth or subsequent claim after a previous withdrawal, and
  • has provided all evidence previously requested to support previous claims paid and their current claim

Has a decision already been made on the customer's claim for the exact same claim period? + Read more ...

A new claim decision cannot be made when a customer is trying to claim for the exact same claim period.

Note: if a customer has been paid for an overlapping period this is checked later. If a customer has been rejected for an overlapping period, this should be considered a new claim and assessed accordingly.

If a decision has already been made for the exact same claim period, the customer would have received a decision letter. This tells them they can request an explanation (review) of the decision. New information can be discussed as part of that process and a new decision made.

To check if a decision has been made on the customer's claim for the exact same claim period, key ‘DL’ in the Super Key field. Check the claim period in any relevant:

  • Finalisation notes
  • Pandemic Leave Disaster Payment Grant Fast Note
  • Pandemic Leave Disaster Payment Reject Fast Note
  • DEP DOCs
  • CSO CLM EXG PLDP review ACC run DOC

If there is a CSO CLM EXG PLDP review ACC run DOC that includes the exact same claim period, the claim must be granted because of a completed explanation (review) of a decision. Select Next to progress through the claim to the Claim Decision screen. Go to Step 25.

Customer has an ISP status of SUS/IMP or CAN/IMP + Read more ...

If a customer did not have an ISP status of SUS/IMP or CAN/IMP (this would have been identified at the Check ISP step above), go to Step 12.

Customers with an ISP status of SUS/IMP or CAN/IMP may be in gaol.

Customers cannot receive the Pandemic Leave Disaster Payment if they are in gaol.

Key ‘DL’ in the Super Key field to go to the customer’s Document List (DL) screen to check for a DOC that confirms the customer has been released from gaol.

If there is no DOC, more details are needed from the customer to progress their claim, see Table 3.

Customer details needed:

  • the name of the facility they were released from,
  • the date they were released and
  • their MIN (Master Index Number), this is their prison reference number

Is the customer in gaol?

  • Yes, reject the claim
  • No, go to Step 13
