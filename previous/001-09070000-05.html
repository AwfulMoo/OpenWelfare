Job Plan activities

The following tables describe Job Plan Categories, activities and Services Australia access to add (A) activities to a Job Plan or only view (V) activities.

Service Officers have access to add many activities for Provider Managed job seekers. Service Officers should only negotiate or update a Job Plan for a provider managed job seeker where there are valid reasons for doing so, and the provider has requested the changes.

It is appropriate however, to negotiate a Job Plan for a provider managed job seeker if the activities being included will fully meet a job seekers mutual obligation requirements and result in the job seeker becoming Centrelink managed.
