Understanding the Customer Line Item Display

Table 1

Column name

Purpose

St

The icon shows as:

  • green for closed
  • red for open

Document No

This is the invoice, payment or adjustment reference number.

Documents have a different prefix based on their type.

  • 18 numbers for invoices
  • 11 numbers for payments
  • 30 numbers for refunds
  • 19 numbers for invoice reversal (credit memo)
  • 10 numbers for accounting adjustments (that is, transfers between customers)

Ty

This is a 2 digit alpha-numeric code to signify the transaction document type.

  • DR for invoices
  • DT for payments
  • KA for refunds
  • DR for invoice reversal (credit memo)
  • AB for accounting adjustments (for example, transfers between customers)

Posting Date

Date the document was loaded to SAP
