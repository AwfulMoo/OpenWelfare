Cancellation and suspension codes for Widow B Pension (WidB) and Wife Pension (WP) 099-10030010

  • Background
  • Process
Expand all


This file is for historical assessments/information only.

This page contains information about the codes and procedures used for recording payment cancellation or suspension information on a Widow B Pension (WidB) or Wife Pension (WP) customer's record. The list of reason codes is preceded by a legend explaining the codes.

On this Page:

Legend - Explanation of reason code fields

Explanation of reason codes fields used on this page

Table 1: This table describes the legend for the subsequent tables, and explains the codes and acronyms used.

Item

Description

1

Code

Three letter code used in the pension system (PEN).

2

Description

Expanded meaning of the code.

3

Valid action codes and initiation

Action - indicates whether a code can be used to suspend or cancel

SUS = suspension

CAN = cancellation

Initiation:

Manual = reason codes that can be manually input on the Benefit Action (BA) screen

Auto = cancellation, suspension or rejection codes that automatically occur on the system

4

Payment type

Advises which payments use that code. WID = Widow B Pension, WFA = Wife Pension where their partner is in receipt of an Age Pension, WFD = Wife Pension where their partner is in receipt of a Disability Support Pension (DSP).

5

Restoration

Cannot be restored after 19 March 2020

WP and WidB cannot be restored after 19 March 2020. All payments current as at 19 March 2020 will be cancelled on 20 March 2020 with reason:

  • XWP, or
  • XWO (WP customers overseas under Age Pension age)

WP who are in a Bereavement Period spanning 20 March 2020 can continue to be paid WP until the end of the Bereavement Period. WP can be restored if suspended during the Bereavement Period where appropriate.

Who could restore - up to 19 March 2020 (historical information)

Indicates whether the WidB or WP if suspended may be restored at a Smart Centre or only at a service centre. The Document List (DL) screen should always be checked for information regarding the suspension as in some cases it may not be appropriate to restore at the first point of contact. For information on restoration see Restoration of Partner Allowance (PA) and Widow Allowance (WA).

These payments can no longer be granted, so where the payment has been cancelled the customer will need to test their eligibility for a different payment.

Rejected records can never be restored.
