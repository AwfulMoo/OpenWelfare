Unique Identifier

A health professional who is registered in more than 1 profession with Ahpra will only have 1 Unique Identifier but will have multiple Ahpra registration numbers with the exception of dental hygienists, dental therapists and oral health therapists who will maintain the same registration number if qualified in more than one dental profession.

Suppression Information

Table 2

Heading/alert

Details included

Description/meaning

Ahpra Information is suppressed

The health professional’s information will not show up in the window for most staff when a suppressed record is viewed.

Ahpra records are suppressed if the National Board had decided that publishing information about a health professional would cause a serious risk to the health professional’s health and safety.

Supplementary Information

This is the heading for supplementary information.

Type

Will show as 1 of the below:

  • Condition
  • Endorsement
  • Notation
  • Undertaking

This will show the type of supplementary information.

  • Conditions, which restrict a health professional’s practice, are placed on a record by a National Board
  • Endorsements will show an extension to a health professional’s scope of practice
  • Notations describe the scope of a health professional’s practice
  • Undertakings reflect something a health professional has agreed to do or not to do. Unlike a condition, undertakings are voluntary

Sub Type

Text

This is the sub type of supplementary information.

If a sub type is not included in the supplementary information this will show as Not provided.

Detail

Text

This is the detail received about the supplementary information. This can only contain a maximum of 1920 characters.

Start

DD/MM/YYYY

This is the start date of the associated condition, endorsement, notation or undertaking.

End

DD/MM/YYYY

This is the end date of the associated condition, endorsement, notation or undertaking. An end date may not be present on current supplementary information records.

Ahpra Registration Status

Table 5: This table describes the Ahpra registration statuses shown in the window.

Note: this information is for internal use only. Direct any questions from health professionals or other external parties about Ahpra registration statuses, for a particular provider or general information to Ahpra.

Status

Status will display as

Registered – Current

The registration type is currently within the registration period. Health professionals registered with Ahpra need to renew their registration(s) annually.

Registered – Late Period

The renewal date for this registration has passed and an application for renewal has not been received by Ahpra.

This status is considered current for Medicare purposes until the end of the late period.

Registered – In Force

The renewal date for this registration has passed but an application for renewal has been received by Ahpra.

This status is considered current for Medicare purposes.

Unregistered – Failed to Renew

The end of the late period for this registration was reached and an application for renewal was not received by Ahpra.

Unregistered – Refused

That an application for renewal of registration was received and a decision was made to refuse to renew the registration.

Unregistered – Cancelled

That a decision was made to cancel the registration.

Unregistered – Suspended

That the registration type has been suspended.

Unregistered – Surrendered

The health professional has surrendered the registration type.

Unregistered – Deceased

Ahpra has received advice that the health professional is deceased.

Unregistered – Void

The decision for this registration type has been rescinded by Ahpra.

Unregistered – Withdrawn

The registration was withdrawn by the health professional.
