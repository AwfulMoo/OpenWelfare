Note:

  • Service Officers may need to refine their search results if the 'Your search matched X care recipients. Only 200 care recipients are displayed in care recipient ID order below.   • select Care recipient details in the Search by* field
  • complete the below fields:
    • Care recipient ID* - if searching by Care recipient ID, the mandatory asterisk against the Family name field disappears
