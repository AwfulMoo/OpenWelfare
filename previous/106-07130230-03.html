Rejecting a PES or ABSTUDY PES claim

Step

Action

1

Check that customer does not meet qualifications + Read more ...

Ensure the customer either:

  • does not qualify (does not meet eligibility for PES, eligibility for ABSTUDY PES, payability or obligations) and the decision to reject the claim is correct, or
  • wishes to withdraw their claim

See the References page for links to the Social Security Guide and the ABSTUDY Policy Manual about qualifications, payability and obligations.

Record all information provided on the claim to allow the system to reject claim automatically. If claim does not reject automatically, follow workflow to manually reject.

2

Ensure residence information is recorded + Read more ...

The customer's residence qualification will be automatically assessed as long as the residence information is correctly recorded.

Note: residence information must still be recorded if the rejection is for any other reason. If residence details are not recorded, the system will override any other rejection and the claim will reject for a residence related reason.

Is the customer's claim to be rejected for a reason other than residence?

  • Yes, go to Step 3
  • No, go to Step 4

3

Single principal carer - qualifying payment not current + Read more ...

Is the customer's claim to be rejected for reason Qualifying Payment not current (CQP) and the customer appears to be a single principal carer?

  • Yes, check the principal carer status on the Principal Carer Circumstances Details (PCCD) screen:
    • if coded 'No' and it appears they are eligible, see Principal carer of a dependent child to assess and code the PCCD screen
    • if eligible, continue to assess the claim
  • No, reject the claim. Go to Step 4

4

Code the rejection of claim + Read more ...

Code the rejection on the system:

  • in the Nxt field, key 'SVPES'
  • in the Sys field, key 'ISS'
  • press [Enter]

The customer's PES record status must be either New Claim (NCL) or Assessed (ASS) for the rejection activity to be accepted. If this is not the case, index the new PES claim before rejecting:

  • go to the Benefits Action (BA) screen
  • in the Svc Rsn field, key benefit type:
    • 'EPF' for PES or
    • 'EPA' for ABSTUDY PES
  • in the Action field, key 'REJ'
  • for the Reason field, select an appropriate rejection code by using field help '?', or check cancellation, suspension and rejection reason codes. Only use rejection code ‘Other’ (OTH) if no other rejection code is appropriate. For example, to reject ABSTUDY PES claims where a student is not entitled to ABSTUDY PES due to receiving other government assistance for the same period
  • in the Effect date field, key date of decision

Note: PES claimants who have previously completed a Master or Doctorate degree do not qualify for assistance irrespective of the course they are currently studying. The Education Course Details (EDC) screen should be coded with the details of the course the customer is currently claiming for and then a manual rejection undertaken on the BA screen for the reason, Masters or Doctorate Complete 'MDC'.

5

Finalise the activity + Read more ...

If an education entry payment (EdEP) is made to a person, and the person does not pay the enrolment fees for the course in relation to which the education entry payment was made, in the calendar year the education entry payment was made, the amount of the education entry payment made is a debt due to the Commonwealth. If this has occurred, see Raising Education Entry Payment (EdEP) debts.

Go to the Assessment Results (AR) screen.

Finalise the activity by keying 'Y' in the Finished field and press [Enter].

6

Check if a manual rejection reason letter is required + Read more ...

A manual letter will be required if a Manual Follow-up (MFU) activity has been created on the Activity List (AL) screen after the rejection activity is finalised.

Go to the AL screen, if there is:

  • an MFU requiring a manual letter, go to Step 7
  • no MFU, record the details of decision on a DOC. Procedure ends here

7

Send a manual letter + Read more ...

Generate a manual letter (Q134) on the Letter Display (LD) screen and complete it by giving the reason(s) for rejecting the claim and advising of the applicant's right of review and appeal. The letter will be sent to the applicant.

Record the details of decision on a DOC. If the rejection reason is FRC or FSD, the decision DOC must list all documents not provided at the time of the rejection.

Is the customer receiving Carer Payment (CP)?

  • Yes, go to Step 8
  • No, procedure ends here

8

Carer Payment (CP) + Read more ...

CP customers claiming PES may tell Services Australia of a change in their circumstances. This may impact their ongoing entitlement to CP.

After the PES claim has been finalised for a CP customer, including rejecting the claim, Service Officers processing PES claims must alert CP-trained Service Officers that a customer has started studying:

  • use the  Fast Note  - select  Auto text, use  Carers  >  Update  >  Commenced study – CPAS to be updated
  • select  Confirm
  • ensure all relevant information is recorded in the  DOC
  • select  Continue
  • do  not  close the  DOC  created by the workflow
