Examples of known customers

List of known customers

This table describes the role types which can be known customers to Services Australia and the list of numbers they can give to prove this.

Healthcare providers can do all different types of business with Services Australia, to do certain types of business they have to identify who they are. Service Officers can verify the numbers they supply have been used previously with Medicare to do with other business and therefore are known to Services Australia.

Role type

Proof of known customer status

Australian Health Practitioner Registration Agency (Ahpra) registrant
  • Healthcare Provider Identifier – Individual (HPI-I)
  • Ahpra registration ID
  • Provider Digital Access (PRODA) ID

  • RO identifier
  • Services Australia Public Key Infrastructure (PKI) Individual Certificate registration authority (RA) number
  • Certificate Manager for a Services Australia PKI Site Certificate RA number
  • PRODA ID

  • Certificate Manager for a Services Australia PKI Site Certificate RA number
