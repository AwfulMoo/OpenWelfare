Changing an address for AIC applicants or students

Portions of this process are done by Assistance for Isolated children (AIC) Scheme Smart Centre processing staff only.

Step

Action

1

Change of address for applicant or student + Read more ...

Assistance for Isolated Children (AIC) involves any of following payments:

  • AIC Boarding Allowance (BA)
  • Additional Boarding Allowance (ABA)
  • Second Home Allowance (SHA)
  • Distance Education Allowance (DED)
  • AIC Pensioner Education Supplement (PES)

When an applicant advises:

  • a change to the student's term address only, go to Step 11
  • a change of theirs and the student's address (for example, principal family home) complete the following:
    • on the applicant’s record, update the Address Details (AD) screen, Accommodation (AC) screen, and Telephone Details (TD) screen’
    • the system will create a Manual Follow-up (MFU) on all AIC student records attached to the applicant for the AIC Smart Centre to action address updates

Service Officers must ensure all updates and coding on AIC records are completed in the ISS system. Use the Service reason ‘SVAIC’

2

Change to student's living arrangements + Read more ...

Changes to a student's living arrangements will generally cause a change of allowance or rate.

For staff not trained in AIC Processing, if the customer is advising of a change in circumstances:

Use Fast Note - select Auto Text, use Students, ABSTUDY and Apprentices > Update > AIC update action DOC.

Service Officers must include:

  • relevant dates the applicant advises the change in circumstances occurred, and
  • details of the new circumstances

This will create an open work item for AIC Processing staff.

For AIC Processing staff, contact the applicant by phone if required. Ask for the details of the student's new circumstances. Go to Step 3.

3

Action any Manual Follow-ups (MFUs) + Read more ...

For Assistance for Isolated Children (AIC) Scheme Smart Centre Processing staff only.

Check if the student still meets an AIC isolation condition in relation to the new principal family home address.

The applicant may still be eligible for a payment under the AIC Continuity of schooling concession where:

  • the family has a new address (for the principal family home), and
  • the student no longer meets one of the AIC isolation conditions

For example, family has moved to a non-isolated area.

This concession applies where the student continues to:

  • attend the same school, or
  • be enrolled at the same distance education institution

Does the student still meet an AIC isolation condition in relation to the new principal home address, or satisfy the AIC Continuity of schooling concession?

  • Yes, go to Step 5
  • No, go to Step 4

4

Cancel the AIC Application + Read more ...

For Assistance for Isolated Children (AIC) Scheme Smart Centre Processing staff only.

Cancel the AIC application from the date the principal family home address was changed.

Record details on a DOC. Include all relevant Act and AIC Guidelines references.

Procedure ends here.

5

Check student's circumstances + Read more ...

For Assistance for Isolated Children (AIC) Scheme Smart Centre Processing staff only.

If the student:

  • attends the same school or is still enrolled at the same distance education institution and has had no change in their term living arrangements, go to Step 6
  • is now studying by distance education, go to Step 7
  • is now boarding away from home, go to Step 8
  • is now living in a Second Family Home, go to Step 9

6

Student attends same school or is still enrolled at the same distance education institution and has had no change in their term living arrangements + Read more ...

For Assistance for Isolated Children (AIC) Scheme Smart Centre Processing staff only.

Tell the applicant the student continues to be eligible for the AIC under the continuity of schooling concession to the end of the year (or the end of the following year if in Yr 11 and continuing to Yr 12).

On the EIID screen code:

  • Event Date: date of change
  • Nearest Approp Govt school: code appropriate government school closest the new home address
  • Distance from home to: code distance in kilometres from the new home address to the nearest appropriate government school
  • Deemed Isolation: COS Continuity of schooling
  • leave all other fields blank
  • select ‘I’ to insert new details

To ensure that AIC does not continue beyond the current year, on the EIID screen code:

  • Event Date: code 01/01 of the following calendar year
  • Deemed Isolation: leave blank
  • Select ‘I’ to insert new details

Record decision on a DOC. Procedure ends here.

7

Check Distance Education Allowance (DED) eligibility + Read more ...

For Assistance for Isolated Children (AIC) Scheme Smart Centre Processing staff only.

Check if the student is eligible for Distance Education Allowance (DED).

Go to Step 10.

8

Check eligibility for AIC Boarding Allowance + Read more ...

For Assistance for Isolated Children (AIC) Scheme Smart Centre Processing staff only.

Check if the student is eligible for  Boarding Allowance (BA/ABA).

Go to Step 10.

9

Check AIC Second Home Allowance eligibility + Read more ...

For Assistance for Isolated Children (AIC) Scheme Smart Centre Processing staff only.

Check if the student is eligible for Second Home Allowance (SHA).

Go to Step 10.

10

Updating EILA and EDC screens + Read more ...

For Assistance for Isolated Children (AIC) Scheme Smart Centre Processing staff only.

Update details on the EILA, EDC and EIID screens, as relevant:

Go to Step 12.

11

Updating the TER address + Read more ...

For Assistance for Isolated Children (AIC) Scheme Smart Centre Processing staff only.

If the student has changed the term address update the TER address on the Address Details (AD) screen on the student’s record.

Go to Step 12.

12

Update the Payment Instruction (PIPI) screen + Read more ...

For Assistance for Isolated Children (AIC) Scheme Smart Centre Processing staff only.

On the student’s record, go to the address on the PIPI screen:

  • Select the Default Payee
  • Update the Payment Instructions Payee Details (PIPD) screen with the new address and Payee and account details (if necessary)
  • Applicant Field:
    • Code a 'Y' for letters to be sent via the address coded as HOME or POS on the Address Details (AD) screen
    • Code a 'N' for a letter to be sent to the third party
  • Finalise the activity on the AR screen
  • Record details on a DOC. Include any relevant Act or AIC Guidelines references

Letters being issued to a third party will show payment dates and rates.
