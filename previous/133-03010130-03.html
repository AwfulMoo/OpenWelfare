Cancel or withdraw online claim using Centrelink online account

Table 1

Step

Action

1

Access Centrelink online account through myGov + Read more ...

Customers can sign in to their Centrelink online account through myGov. Once signed in, the customer needs to select MENU>Payments and claims>Claims>My Online claims. To:

  • cancel their started claim, go to Step 2
  • withdraw their submitted claim:
    • For all online claims on the current platform, go to Step 3
    • For online claims submitted on the old platform in the past 14 days, go to Step 3
    • For online claims submitted on the old platform more than 14 days ago, the customer must contact Services Australia to request their claim to be withdrawn. Procedure ends here

2

Cancel claim + Read more ...

If the customer selects the Cancel claim option, a warning tells them:

  • cancelling their claim means they no longer want to submit this claim for a payment, and
  • if they decide to apply for a payment after cancelling this claim they need to complete and lodge a new claim online
  • after the claim is cancelled, it cannot be viewed or continued
  • this action cannot be undone

If the customer selects Cancel claim:

  • the My online claims page displays, showing information about the cancelled claim including:
    • claim ID
    • claim type
    • date cancelled
  • they cannot select it to view further details
  • claim information will not store or transfer to the customer's Centrelink record

A history of the claim activity can be viewed in the customer's record.

Procedure ends here.

3

Withdraw claim + Read more ...

If the customer selects the Withdraw claim option, a warning tells them:

  • withdrawing their claim means they no longer want to submit this claim for a payment
  • if they decide to apply for a payment after withdrawing this claim they will need to complete and lodge a new claim
  • after the claim is withdrawn, it cannot be reinstated
  • they cannot appeal this decision

If the customer selects I understand and wish to withdraw this claim then selects Withdraw claim:

  • a confirmation message shows
  • the My online claims page displays, showing information about the withdrawn claim including:
    • claim ID
    • claim type
    • date withdrawn
  • they cannot select it to view further details
  • the claim status updates to withdrawn
  • the system will create a DOC on the customer's record stating 'Claim ID: XXXXXXXXXX is now withdrawn and deemed not to have been made'

Claim details can be viewed in the customer's record.

Procedure ends here.

4

Auto system action + Read more ...

The system searches for any claim activities associated with the claim type:

Are all expected claim activities still open on a PER record?

  • Yes, go to Step 5
  • No, go to Step 6

5

All claim activities open on PER record + Read more ...

The system automatically:

  • Cancels all claim activities for the claim type
  • Creates a DOC stating the customer has withdrawn their online claim. DOCs have a keyword of CANOCXXX where XXX is the claim type

Procedure ends here.

6

Manual Follow-up (MFU) required + Read more ...

The system will create a Cancel Claim Follow-up ACTDOC for claim activities that have:

  • a status of Completed, Cancelled or Submitted
  • been determined

ACTDOCs have a keyword of CANOCXXX where XXX is the claim type.

Action Required

  • Investigate claim record
  • If the claim is determined, contact customer to discuss the withdrawal

For more information, see:

  • Withdrawal of claims
  • Not effective, rejection, or withdrawal of claim for Family Tax Benefit or Stillborn Baby Payment

Cancel or withdraw online claim using the Express Plus Centrelink mobile app

Table 2

Step

Action

1

Access Express Plus Centrelink mobile app + Read more ...

Sign in to access the My claims screen in the app:

  • from the home screen, select More
  • select My claims

The My claims screen shows a list of incomplete, withdrawn, submitted and completed claims.

Claims will not show on this screen until the customer has:

  • reviewed their claim
  • accepted the declaration
  • moved to the Next steps page in their claim

If the customer wants to:

  • cancel a started claim, go to Step 2
  • withdraw a submitted claim, go to Step 3

2

Cancel claim + Read more ...

If the customer selects the Cancel claim option (for the claim type), a Declaration screen tells them they will no longer be able to:

  • view the claim details
  • continue with their claim

If they select I accept and submit and cancel the claim:

  • a confirmation alert shows
  • the My claims screen shows information about the cancelled claim, including:
    • claim ID
    • claim type
    • date cancelled
  • claim information will not store or transfer to the customer's Centrelink record

A history of the claim activity can be viewed in the customer's record.

Procedure ends here.

3

Withdraw claim + Read more ...

If the customer selects the claim from the My claims screen, then the Withdraw claim option, a Declaration screen tells them if they choose to withdraw their claim (for the claim type) they cannot:

  • reinstate it
  • appeal this decision

When the customer selects I accept and submit and withdraws their claim:

  • a confirmation alert shows
  • the My claims screen displays, showing information about the withdrawn claim including:
    • claim ID
    • claim type
    • date withdrawn
  • they cannot select it to view further details
  • the claim status updates to Withdrawn
  • the system will create a DOC on the customer's record stating 'Claim ID: XXXXXXXXXX is now withdrawn and deemed not to have been made.'

Claim details can be viewed in the customer's record.

Procedure ends here.

4

Auto system action + Read more ...

The system searches for any claim activities associated with the claim type.

Are all expected claim activities still open on a PER record?

  • Yes, go to Step 5
  • No, go to Step 6

5

All claim activities open on PER record + Read more ...

The system automatically:

  • cancels all claim activities for the claim type
  • creates a DOC stating the customer has withdrawn their online claim. DOCs have a keyword of CANOCXXX where XXX is the claim type

Procedure ends here.

6

Manual Follow-up (MFU) required + Read more ...

The system will create a Cancel Claim Follow-up ACTDOC when any of the claim activities have:

  • a status of Completed, Cancelled or Submitted
  • been determined

ACTDOCs have a keyword of CANOCXXX where XXX is the claim type.

Action Required:

  • Investigate claim record
  • If the claim is determined, contact customer to discuss the withdrawal
