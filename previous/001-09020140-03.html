Information sections on the Participation Summary screen

Table 1: This table describes information sections on the Participation Summary screen.

Item

Description

1

Key Information section + Read more ...

The Key Information section lists the following details about the customer:

  • if they are exempt from mutual obligation requirements
  • their current participation category, for example, 'JSP/YA/SPL WKCAP 30+ hrs/wk' or 'Voluntary'
  • their expected job search efforts, that is, the number of job search efforts required each fortnight
  • if they are connected to a provider
  • any failures, for example, shows outstanding and completed compliance actions
  • who they are managed by, that is, Services Australia or provider managed

2

Current Providers section + Read more ...

The Current Providers section has details of the:

  • job seeker's Deferred Referral Information
  • job seeker's provider type
  • name (Site Code)
  • address and phone number of the provider
  • date they commenced/suspended/ceased with the provider
  • their current status with the provider, and
  • if applicable, the customer's suspension end date

The Service Officer can access further information via the 'Link to the Online Diary' and the 'Link to Deferred Referrals' links located alongside this section.

3

Employment Provider Services Referral Information + Read more ...

The Employment Provider Services Referral Information provides details of:

  • the job seeker's current stream
  • the job seeker's status in Employment Provider Services, e.g. suspended
  • the status date of effect which provides the date of the last change in status
  • more information which will provide context sensitive information such as the job seekers phase or the reason the job seeker was exited

There is a link to the 'Employment Provider Services Referral History' immediately beneath the 'Employment Provider Services Referral Information' section. Selecting the link will go to the 'Employment Provider Services Referral History' page.

4

Subsidy section + Read more ...

The Subsidy section includes the following:

  • Subsidy type (e.g. Youth Bonus Wage Subsidy)
  • Start date
  • End date

If the customer is not currently commenced or receiving a subsidy then the Subsidy twisty will display as collapsed with message 'No Current Information'. A 'History' twisty within the Subsidy section will display the complete customer's subsidy participation history.

5

Reporting information section + Read more ...

The Reporting information section includes details regarding the customer's:

  • reporting type
  • the approved reporting channel, and
  • the current reporting frequency

6

Job Seeker Registration Information section + Read more ...

The Job Seeker Registration Information section provides the:

  • Job Seeker ID
  • Job Seeker status
  • type of job seeker registration type (for example, fully eligible)
  • in some cases it will provide 'More Information' (for example, whether the customer is identified as Vulnerable Youth/Vulnerable Youth (Student))

The Service Officer can access further information or update current job seeker registration details via the 'View JSCI/Run Looking for Work' link located underneath the 'Job Seeker Classification Instrument (JSCI)' section.

7

Job Seeker Classification Instrument (JSCI) section + Read more ...

The Job Seeker Classification Instrument (JSCI) section provides the JSCI result and status.

The Service Officer can access further information or update current details via the 'View JSCI/Run Looking for Work' link under this section.

8

Employment ESAt Referral Request History section + Read more ...

The Employment ESAt Referral Request History section provides the history and status of ESAt referral requests received from the Employment system (RFL/UPD activities).

If an ESAt request has been manually withdrawn, the reason for the withdrawal will display in this section.

9

Current Plan section + Read more ...

The Current Plan section lists details about the customer's Job Plan, such as:

  • the date signed, that is, the date the job seeker signed their current plan
  • who it was negotiated by, for example, either Centrelink and the service centre name, or the jobactive organisation name
  • the channel the job seeker used to agree to their plan, e.g. face to face or online
  • participation category, that is, the job seeker's participation category at the time the plan was negotiated, for example, 'Parents on PP 15 hrs/wk'

The Service Officer can select the twisty to go to the Job Plan status page. Current and historical plans can be viewed on this page and new Job Plans can be negotiated using the Job Plan workflow.

10

Activity Type/Exemption Information section + Read more ...

The Activity Type/Exemption Information section provides information to the Service Officer on:

  • what type of activity the job seeker is undertaking
  • any exemptions granted
  • activity/exemption start dates, and
  • activity/exemption end dates

The Service Officer can also access information about full-time short course(s) which have been approved by the job seeker's Employment Services Provider(s) by selecting the 'View Short Course Details' link.

11

Non-Applied Exemption Information section + Read more ...

The Non-Applied Exemption Information section provides information to the Service Officer on:

  • any Non-Applied Exemptions decisions made
  • the types of exemptions that were not applied (not granted), for example, Major Personal Crisis
  • Non-Applied Exemption decision dates

12

Participation Compliance section + Read more ...

The Participation Compliance section provides the Service Officer with information regarding the job seeker's compliance history including:

  • the Event Date of any compliance action
  • the Status Date of the compliance action
  • the compliance action type
  • reason and status, and
  • any penalty periods

The Service Officer can access further information or action outstanding compliance action via the 'Link to Participation Compliance' link located alongside this section.

Referral information and indicator sections on the PS Screen

Table 2: This table describes referral information and indicator sections on the Participation Summary screen.

Item

Description

1

Community Development Program Referral Information + Read more ...

The Community Development Program Referral Information section will display a job seeker's current Community Development Program (CDP) referral information.

  • Placement type:
    • CDP Community Volunteer
    • CDP Fully Eligible
  • Placement Status - Pending; Commenced; Suspended; Exited, Transferred. Will be blank if there is no status
  • More Information/Exit reason - if status is Exited - the exit reason will be displayed

2

Referral Summary section + Read more ...

The Referral Summary section shows:

  • which program/provider the job seeker has been referred to
  • the Date Of Event this occurred
  • what is their status (for example, referred), and
  • the reason (for example, awaiting contact)

The Service Officer can access further information via the 'Referral Summary' link located alongside this section.

3

Vulnerability indicator + Read more ...

The Vulnerability Indicator section displays current Vulnerability Indicators where the job seeker has provided sufficient evidence to support their circumstances and the requirements for a Vulnerability Indicator have been met. The system will automatically create a Vulnerability Indicator based on information recorded on the customer record.

4

Homelessness indicator + Read more ...

The homelessness indicator section will display where the job seeker has a homelessness indicator on their record. This section provides the Service Officer with current information.

The Service Officer can access the Homelessness Indicator History (HIH) screen via the 'View Homelessness History' link. When the Service Officer has finished with the screen, they will be returned to the Participation Summary screen.

Employment Services Action Items relevant to the customer

Table 3: This table describes Employment Services Action Items that may be relevant to the customer.

Item

Description

1

Failure to attend ESAt + Read more ...

The wording of the action items is 'This customer may be vulnerable and has failed to attend their Employment Services Assessment (ESAt). Please contact customer to arrange a new appointment'.

This action item notifies Assessment Services to reschedule an appointment.

This navigates to the ESAt Attendance workflow.

2

The customer should be referred for an ESAt and have a suitable appointment booked + Read more ...

There are a number of action items which indicate an appointment should be booked for an ESAt. The reason the appointment was not booked will also display if applicable.

These items will be actioned by Assessment Services.

This action item will not display if the 'Failure to attend ESAt' action item is displayed.

3

Referral to Provider + Read more ...

The customer should be referred to an Employment Services Provider and have a suitable appointment booked.

This action item notifies the Service Officer to action any outstanding referrals to an Employment Services Provider and immediately schedule an initial appointment with the provider.

This navigates to the Online Diary.

4

Action referral to DES + Read more ...

This action item notifies the Service Officer of an outstanding referral to a Disability Employment Services (DES) provider.

This item does not have a link attached; the Service Officer will need to navigate manually to update this referral.

5

Re-engagement appointment with jobactive organisation + Read more ...

Customer must have a re-engagement appointment with their Employment Service Provider, please schedule this appointment.

This action item notifies the Service Officer that the job seeker requires a re-engagement appointment with their jobactive provider when a period of suspension from stream services has ended.

By selecting the action item the Service Officer will go to the Online Diary where they should book the next available appointment for the job seeker.

6

Action Referral to Transition to Work (TtW) + Read more ...

This action item notifies the Service Officer of an outstanding referral to a TtW provider and have a suitable appointment booked.

By selecting the action item the Service Officer will go to the appropriate screens/workflows.

7

Referral to AMEP/SEE + Read more ...

Action referral to AMEP/SEE.

This action item notifies the Service Officer of an outstanding referral to an Adult Migrant English Program or Skills for Education and Employment provider.

By selecting the action item the Service Officer will go to the Referral Summary page.

8

Action referral to ADE and update customer's Job Plan where appropriate + Read more ...

This action item notifies the Service Officer of an outstanding referral to an Australian Disability Enterprise (ADE).

By selecting the action item the Service Officer will go to the Create Referrals workflow.

Item

Description

Action Post Approved Activity Review + Read more ...

By selecting the action item the Service Officer will be taken to the appropriate screens to conduct this review.

2

Check Outstanding Participation Service Components + Read more ...

This action item notifies the Service Officer of any outstanding Participation Items.

This item does not have a link attached; the Service Officer will need to manually complete any outstanding items.

Creation or reclassification of JSCI required + Read more ...

This action item is displayed when the job seeker requires the Looking for Work workflow to be run for the first time or the Looking for Work workflow to be re-run due to a change in circumstances.

This navigates to the Looking for Work workflow.

4

Employment Services Provider has reported a change in circumstances for <reason/s> + Read more ...

Complete JSCI with this job seeker.

This action item is displayed when a jobactive provider has reported a change of circumstances for a job seeker. The Looking for work workflow should be run and the JSCI completed.

Up to nine reasons can be displayed in the action item.

This item does not have a link attached; the Service Officer will need to manually navigate to the Looking For Work workflow.

5

Customer has a <appointment type> appointment booked for <appointment date> but offer now if available. + Read more ...

This action item is displayed when a QTI or QTR appointment is booked between the system date and 14 days in the future.

This item does not have a link attached; the Service Officer will need to manually select the appointment and run the Service Profiling questionnaire.

6

Customer's recent earnings suggest employment may be approved as meeting mutual obligation requirements. If approving, update AEX screen and determine if a Job Plan update is required. Do not update the Job Plan if the customer is a DES participant + Read more ...

This action item notifies the Service Officer the job seeker may be fully meeting their mutual obligation requirements through paid work.

By selecting the action item the Service Officer will navigate to the Job Plan Status page.

See Paid work as an approved activity.

Action Items relevant to a Job Plan

Table 5: This table describes Action Items relevant to a Job Plan.

Item

Description

1

AEX screen + Read more ...

AEX screen updated with <activity type literal>. Ensure Job Plan is updated to include job seeker's current activity.

Note: this is when the study activity is auto populated on the AEX screen as a result of study being coded on other screens such as Short Course Details, EDC/NES.

This Action Item will be displayed where the job seeker's record has:

  • at least one of the following current Activity Types (displayed on the Activity and Exemption Summary (AEX) screen):
    • Short Course (SHD), or
    • Jobseeker Full Time Student (STF), or
    • Jobseeker Part Time Student (SPT)

and

  • the current Job Plan does not include a Study - Part Time or Full Time (ET59) Activity

The Action Item will display the Activity Type codes that are recorded on the customer's AEX screen.

This Action Item navigates to the Job Plan Workflow.

The Service Officer is required to update the Job Plan to reflect the study details on the AEX screen.

2

Job Plan updated with study activity + Read more ...

Ensure course/study details are also recorded.

This action item will be displayed where the job seeker's record has:

  • a current Job Plan which was negotiated by Centrelink, and
  • the current Job Plan includes a Study - Part Time or Full Time (ET59) compulsory activity, and
  • the current Job Plan does not include EM53, EM54 or EM55 compulsory activity, and
  • does not have one of the following Activity Types (as displayed on the AEX screen) with start dates and end dates that exactly match ET59 compulsory activity in the Job Plan:
    • Short Course (SHD), or
    • Jobseeker Full Time Student (STF), or
    • Jobseeker Part Time Student (SPT)

This Action Item navigates to the Activity and Exemption Summary (AEX) screen. Check if the correct approved activity is already coded on this screen with the same start and end dates. If not, the Service Officer is required to record the course/study details on the job seeker's record on appropriate screens, for example, Short Course Details, EDC/NES. Once details are recorded, check the AEX screen to ensure that the correct approved activity is populated on this screen.

3

Job Plan updated with PCW/TRWC<15 hrs per week + Read more ...

Ensure QTI is recorded on AEX

This action item will be displayed where the job seeker's record has:

  • a current Job Plan which was negotiated by Centrelink, and
  • the current Job Plan includes an 'Attend Quarterly Appointment' AI02 or 'Reduced Capacity Requirements' AI08 Activity, and
  • does not have a current Quarterly Interview (QTI) Activity recorded (as displayed on the AEX screen)

This Action Item navigates to the AEX screen.

The Service Officer is required to update the AEX screen with the Quarterly Interview (QTI) activity.

4

Job Plan started but not completed + Read more ...

Ensure the Job Plan is completed appropriately

This Action Item will be displayed where the customer has a WAP/PLN Activity 'STA'rted on the Assessment List (AL) screen.

This Action Item navigates to the Job Plan Workflow.

The Service Officer is required to negotiate a Job Plan.

5

Customer is Centrelink managed and has no current Job Plan + Read more ...

Negotiate a Job Plan with customer

This Action Item will be displayed when the job seeker is:

  • Services Australia managed, and
  • Has mutual obligation requirements - JobSeeker Payment (JSP), Youth Allowance (YA), Special Benefit (SpB), or has compulsory participation requirements (Parenting Payment), and
  • does not have a current Job Plan

This Action Item navigates to the Job Plan Workflow.

The Service Officer is required to negotiate a Job Plan.

6

Job Plan action is required + Read more ...

Ensure Job Plan is updated to include referral activities as per the AEX screen.

This Action Item will be displayed when the job seeker is:

  • Services Australia managed, and
  • coded on the AEX screen one of the following programs:
  • Australian Disability Enterprises (ADE)
    • Adult Migrant English Program (AME)
    • Skills for Education and Employment (DLN)
  • the current Job Plan does not have the same activity included as compulsory activity, or
  • there is a corresponding activity on the Job Plan but the end date does not match that of the referral

This Action Item navigates to the Job Plan Workflow.

The Service Officer is required to update the Job Plan to reflect the information on the AEX screen.

7

Job Plan requires at least one compulsory activity + Read more ...

Ensure Job Plan is updated taking into consideration the job seeker's participation category.

This Action Item will be displayed where the job seeker is:

  • Services Australia managed, and
  • has a current Job Plan that does not include at least one compulsory activity appropriate to the customer's participation category, or
  • has a current Job Plan that does not include a compulsory activity

This Action Item navigates to the Job Plan Workflow.

The Service Officer is required to negotiate a Job Plan.

8

Job Seeker has disagreed with the contents of a Job Plan in their online account. A Job Plan must be agreed with the job seeker + Read more ...

This action item will be displayed when there is an outstanding WAP/MFU activity with a 'NOJOBPLN' keyword on the Assessment List (AL) screen.
