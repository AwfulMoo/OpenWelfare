Determination 2020 - DRA and ex-gratia DRA average weekly income

Based on the Rate Calculator Determination 2020.

Average weekly income is based on the Average Weekly Ordinary Time Earnings (AWOTE) for a full-time adult according to the Australian Bureau of Statistics. This figure is updated in May each year for use from 1 January of the next year, for the duration of that calendar year:

  • From 1 January 2019 the figure is: $1,586.20
  • From 1 January 2020 the figure is: $1,633.80

To annualise the AWOTE, multiple the weekly amount by 52:

  • From 1 January 2019 the annualised figure is: $82,482.40
  • From 1 January 2020 the annualised figure is: $84.957.60
