ABSTUDY Standard Hostel Agreement + Read more ...

Note: the ABSTUDY Standard Hostels Agreement is currently under review by Services Australia and the Department of Social Services. Payments to hostels that are existing signatories to the ABSTUDY Standard Hostels Agreement will continue unaffected for the period of the review. Any enquiries from new hostels seeking to be approved to receive ABSTUDY payments must be referred to the ABSTUDY Portfolio Operations Team.

The ABSTUDY Standard Hostels Agreement determines the conditions for payment of ABSTUDY away from home rate of Living Allowance, Rent Assistance and Remote Area Allowance for students residing in the hostel.

From 1 July 2015, a hostel which is a signatory to the Agreement is also qualified.

Hostels which are signatories to the Agreement can be paid ABSTUDY as an ABSTUDY boarding instalment. If a:

3

Services Australia and AHL National Agreement + Read more ...

A number of Aboriginal Hostels Limited (AHL) hostels provide dedicated student accommodation for secondary school students living away from home. Services Australia and AHL have signed a national Agreement governing the conditions of payment of ABSTUDY away from home allowances. These hostels are not required to sign individual Agreements with Services Australia.

Note: hostels covered by the national Agreement with AHL are listed in the Resources page and are identified on the individual hostel record via a DOC.

Is the hostel a member hostel covered by a separate national Agreement with AHL and therefore not subject to an individual ABSTUDY Standard Hostels Agreement?

  • Yes, go to Step 6
  • No, go to Step 4

4

ABSTUDY Standard Hostels Agreement + Read more ...

The hostel must enter into an ABSTUDY Standard Hostels Agreement for the current 3 year period in order to receive ABSTUDY away from home allowances paid as an ABSTUDY boarding instalment.

Has the hostel been a signatory to an Agreement previously?

  • Yes, go to Step 6
  • No, go to Step 5

5

New ABSTUDY Standard Hostel Agreement + Read more ...

To enter into an ABSTUDY Standard Hostel Agreement an organisation must first have met all local and State/Territory government requirements to operate as a hostel. This includes local councillor approval for 'business use of the premises' as a hostel or lodging house, as well as meeting local building and facility requirements. Similarly State/Territory governments have various requirements to operate as an accommodation facility for children.

Contact the hostel via phone to explain the necessity of the hostel having all the necessary government approval to operate as a hostel before entering into an Agreement in order to access ABSTUDY away from home allowances via an ABSTUDY boarding instalment payment method. Note: Hostels seeking guidance as to appropriate approval process and requirements should be referred to their local council to initiate the approval procedure to operate as a hostel.

Explain that:

  • the hostel must ascertain all necessary local and State/Territory approvals to operate as a hostel
  • unless an Agreement is entered, boarding payments will be made for each qualified student via the fortnight-in-arrears payment method
  • Payments made via the fortnight-in-arrears method does not provide access to transfer unused Group 2 School Fees to top-up any board shortfall
  • no board payments will commence until the hostel has advised whether it will be a signatory to the ABSTUDY Standard Hostels Agreement

The contact should also offer the hostel the opportunity to discuss in person, or via the phone, the conditions of the Agreement where necessary.

Q999 and printable agreement

Two copies of the ABSTUDY Standard Hostels Agreement (see the Resources page for a link to the printable Agreement) must be sent to the hostel with a Q999 covering letter, and request both Agreements be returned fully completed within 14 days with all necessary documentation as required at clause 3.1 of the Agreement. Each page of both contracts must be initialled by the Services Australia delegate (which is normally the ABSTUDY Smart Centre Processing Manager) prior to sending to the Hostel.

The Resources page contains a Hostel Agreement Checklist to support the Agreement process.

Create a DOC on the hostel's organisation record detailing the action undertaken.

Note: the Q999 letter must be generated from the hostel's organisation record in order to maintain a record of all correspondence with the hostel.

Go to Step 8.

6

Renew the ABSTUDY Standard Hostel Agreement + Read more ...

If the Hostel Agreement is for AHL Hostels:

  • contact AHL National Office directly in relation to renewing the single Agreement governing all AHL hostels. See Resources for AHL contact details
  • advise that a new Hostel Agreement is required to continue ABSTUDY boarding instalment payments
  • explain that:
    • unless an Agreement is entered, payments will be made for each qualified student via the fortnight-in-arrears payment method
    • payments made via the fortnight-in-arrears method does not provide access to transfer unused Group 2 School Fees to top-up any board shortfall
    • no board payments will commence until the hostel has advised whether it will be a signatory to the ABSTUDY Standard Hostels Agreement

If the Hostel Agreement is for an individual Hostel (not AHL):

  • contact the hostel via phone to explain that the Agreement has, or shortly will expire and must be renewed in order to access ABSTUDY away from home allowances via the ABSTUDY boarding instalment payment method
  • explain that:
    • unless an Agreement is entered, payments will be made for each qualified student via the fortnight-in-arrears payment method
    • payments made via the fortnight-in-arrears method does not provide access to transfer unused Group 2 School Fees to top-up any board shortfall
    • no board payments will commence until the hostel has advised whether it will be a signatory to the ABSTUDY Standard Hostels Agreement

Go to Step 7.

7

Documents to be sent to the hostel (or AHL) + Read more ...

Send the hostel:

  • 2 copies of the ABSTUDY Standard Hostels Agreement. Each page of both contacts must be initialled by Services Australia delegate (which is the National Manager, Remote and ABSTUDY Smart Centre Branch for AHL Agreements, or the ABSTUDY Smart Centre Processing Manager for individual hostels) prior to sending to the Hostel
  • Q999 Standard Hostels Agreement renewal cover letter which will notify the hostel that the previous Agreement has expired and that a new Agreement is required for the current 3 year period in order for ABSTUDY away from home allowances to be payable via the lump sum payment method. See Resources to access the Q999 standard Hostels agreement renewal cover letter

The Q999 Standard Hostels Agreement renewal cover letter needs to request both Agreements be returned fully completed within 14 days and DOC the hostel’s or AHL organisation record of action undertaken.

No payments will commence until the hostel has advised whether it will be a signatory to the ABSTUDY Standard Hostels Agreement.

Note: the Q999 letter must be generated from the hostel’s organisation record in order to maintain a record of all correspondence with the hostel. AHL has an organisation record and should be used for this purpose.

Go to Step 8.

Payment of arrears and recording outcome

Table 2: This table describes the process involved when the Agreement has been returned, payment of arrears and recording the outcome.

Step

Action

1

When forms returned + Read more ...

Check the 2 returned ABSTUDY Standard Hostels Agreement to ensure that:

  • terms of the agreement have not been altered
  • no pages have been replaced
  • all relevant certificates and approvals have been provided as per clause 3.1 of the Agreement
  • each page has been initialled
  • both copies have been signed by the Chairperson or Manager of the hostel, or an AHL representative, and their signature has been witnessed

Has the Agreement been returned satisfactorily completed?

  • Yes, go to Step 4
  • No, go to Step 2

2

Agreement has not been satisfactorily completed + Read more ...

Contact the hostel to advise that an aspect of the Agreement has not been satisfactorily completed. Return the Agreement for satisfactory completion, and advise that payments of ABSTUDY away from home allowances will not be payable via any method until the Agreement is returned satisfactorily completed.

Note: payments should not be made via the fortnight-in-arrears method until the Agreement is returned completed, as it will affect the capacity to pay ABSTUDY boarding instalment payments once the Agreement is completed.

Once the agreement is returned satisfactorily completed, go to Step 4.

If the hostel advises it will not sign the Agreement, go to Step 3.

3

Fortnight in arrears payment method + Read more ...

Hostels not seeking payment via ABSTUDY boarding instalments, or not willing to accept the conditions of the Agreement must have payment of ABSTUDY allowances directed to the hostel via the normal fortnight-in-arrears payment method.

See Coding away from home rate for the correct coding of a student's record to ensure ABSTUDY away from home allowances are directed to the hostel fortnight-in-arrears.

Create a DOC on the hostel's organisation record detailing the contact with the hostel and that an Agreement will not be entered into by the hostel for the current 3 year period.

If required, request update (via Operational Blueprint feedback) to remove the hostel from the Hostel Agreement list in Resources.

Procedure ends here.

4

Advising the hostel + Read more ...

Advise the hostel via a Q999 letter that payment of ABSTUDY away from home allowances will be made via the ABSTUDY boarding instalment method, commencing at the beginning of the school term immediately after the ABSTUDY Standard Hostels Agreement's acceptance.

Advise the hostel of the Payment on Advice payment method for delivery of entitlements to the hostel.

5

Recording outcome and review process + Read more ...

Record details of the acceptance of the Agreement for the relevant 3 year period on a Display on Access DOC on the hostel's or AHL organisation record:

  • the Services Australia Delegate, and witness, must sign and date both agreements
  • one is then returned to the Hostel (via registered post if posting) for their records
  • scan the other ABSTUDY Standard Hostels Agreement onto the organisation record (using the AHL organisation record for the single Agreement covering all AHL hostels)
  • create a Review on the hostel's or AHL organisation record for 6 weeks prior to the end of the current 3 year period, to contact the hostel in relation to signing another Agreement for the following period
  • request update (via Operational Blueprint feedback) of the ABSTUDY Standard Hostel Agreement list in Resources to reflect the new expiry date of the Agreement
  • update the hostel organisation record where required with any changes to contact person, address, telephone or email, etc.
