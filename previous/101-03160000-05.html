Acceptable evidence for year 12 level of study (or equivalent) obtained overseas

Country

Qualification

Bangladesh
  • Higher Secondary Certificate/vocational higher secondary certificate (Minimum CGPA is 3.5 or 60% in HSC/First Division pass)
  • GCE A-Level (2 passes)

Bhutan
  • Bhutan Higher Secondary Education Certificate awarded by the Bhutan Board of Examinations
  • Bhutan Higher Secondary School Certificate or Middle Secondary School Certificate plus 2 year relevant vocational diploma

Bosnia-Herzegovina
  • Diploma o položenom maturskom ispitu (RS) / Diploma o završenoj srednjoj školi (FiBH) (Secondary School Leaving Diploma)
  • Secondary School Leaving Diploma (diploma o završenoj srednjoj stručnoj školi) awarded by a vocational secondary school
  • Technician (Tehnicar) Awarded by a vocational secondary school

Brazil
  • Certificado /diploma de Ensino Medio (Year 12 equivalent)

Burundi
  • Candidature; Diplôme d'Etudes Supérieures

Cambodia
  • Associate degree
  • Diploma of Upper Secondary
  • Education/Baccalaurate

Canada
  • High School Diploma

Alberta:

  • Alberta High School Diploma
  • Alberta High School Diploma when at least five grade 12 courses have been passed
  • General High School Diploma when at least five grade 12 courses have been passed and awarded between 1985 and 1997

British Columbia:

  • British Columbia Senior Secondary School Graduation Diploma
  • British Columbia Certificate of Graduation or ‘Dogwood Diploma’ when at least five grade 12 courses have been passed
  • British Columbia Adult Graduation Diploma when all five courses are at grade 12 level

Manitoba:

  • Manitoba High School Graduation Diploma
  • Manitoba Graduation Diploma when at least five grade 12 courses have been passed
  • Mature Student High School Diploma when at least five grade 12 courses have been passed

North West Territories:

  • North West Territories Secondary School Graduation Diploma / Northwest Territories High School Diploma

Nunavut:

  • Nunavut Senior Secondary School Graduation Diploma when at least five grade 12 courses have been passed

Newfoundland and Labrador:

  • Newfoundland Provincial High School Graduation Certificate
  • General High School Graduation Diploma when at least five grade 12 courses have been passed

New Brunswick:

  • New Brunswick High School Graduation Certificate
  • New Brunswick High School Diploma/Diplôme de fin d’études secondaires when at least five grade 12 courses have been passed
  • Adult High School Diploma when at least five grade 12 courses have been passed

Nova Scotia:

  • Nova Scotia High School Completion Certificate
  • Nova Scotia High School Graduation Diploma when at least five grade 12 courses have been passed
  • High School Diploma for Adults when at least five grade 12 courses have been passed

Ontario:

  • Ontario Secondary School Diploma with Ontario Academic Courses
  • Ontario Secondary School Diploma with University Preparation Courses or University/College Preparation Courses
  • Ontario Secondary School Honour Graduation Diploma (1989 and earlier)
  • Ontario Secondary School Diploma when at least six grade 12 courses have been passed
  • Ontario Academic Courses (OACs) when awarded up to and including 2003
  • Secondary School Honours Graduation Diploma Awarded between 1977 and 1986

Prince Edward Island:

  • Senior High School Graduation Certificate when at least five grade 12 courses have been passed

Québec:

  • Quebec Diploma of Collegial Studies or comparable qualification
  • Secondary School Diploma/Diplôme d’Etudes Secondaires
  • Diplôme d'Études Collégiales (DEC)

Saskatchewan:

  • Saskatchewan Complete Division IV or Grade XII Standing

Grade 12 Standing

  • Saskatchewan Transcript of Secondary Level Achievement when at least five grade 12 courses have been passed

Yukon:

  • Yukon Certificate of Graduation / Yukon Secondary School Education Certificate when at least five grade 12 courses have been passed

Chile
  • Grado de Bachiller when studied for more than 2 years but less than 3
  • Licencia de Educación Media (Certificate of Secondary Education Certificate) Tecnico-Profesional or Tecnico

China
  • China Peoples Republic National Entrance Examination (Gaokao)
  • National College Entrance Examination (NCEE) (Gaokao)
  • Certificate of Graduation from a Specialised secondary school 3-4 years full-time after 9 years school, or 2 years full-time after 12 years of school
  • General Senior Secondary Unified Graduation (Certificate of Graduation)

Congo, Dem Rep
  • Graduat / Grade de Gradué

Egypt
  • General Secondary Education Certificate
  • Al-Azhar Secondary Education Certificate
  • Technical Secondary Education Certificate
  • Commercial Secondary Certificate
  • Agriculture Secondary Certificate

Fiji
  • Fiji Seventh Form Certificate
  • Fiji Form 7 Examination
  • Fiji School Leaving Certificate (FSLC)

Germany
  • German Abitur
  • Abitur or Reifezeugnis
  • Fachhochschulreife
  • Zeugnis der Allgemeine Hochschulreife
  • Achgebundene Hochschulreife/FachgebundeneHochschulreife (Leaving Certificate) or Zeugnis der Reifeor Reifezeugnis or Abitur or Fachhochschulreife

Hong Kong
  • Hong Kong ALE
  • Hong Kong Advanced Level Examination with at least 2 Advanced Level passes and 3 Hong Kong Certificate of Education Examination (HKCEE) passes (Grade E or above)
  • Hong Kong Higher Level Examination (awarded until 1993 by Chinese-medium schools)
  • Hong Kong Diploma of Secondary Education
  • Hong Kong Diploma of Secondary Education (HKDSE) or Hong Kong Advanced Level Examination (HKALE)

India
  • All India Senior School Certificate
  • All India Senior School Certificate awarded by the Central Board of Secondary Education
  • Indian School Certificate (Year XII/12) awarded by the Council for the Indian School Certificate Examinations
  • Senior Secondary Certificate awarded by the National Institute of Open Schooling
  • Certificate of Vocational Education awarded by the council for the Indian School Certificate Examinations
  • All India Senior School Certificate
  • Indian School Certificate or Certificate of Vocational Education or Senior Secondary Certificate
  • State Boards of Secondary/Senior Secondary Education certificates
  • GCE A-Levels (2 passes)

Indonesia
  • Certificate of Completion of Senior Secondary Education (Ijazah/STTB SMA/STTB SMK)
  • Certificate of Graduation (SKHUN)

Iran
  • High School Diploma / Certificate of Completion of Secondary Education when studied for 4 years or less than 5
  • Pre-University Certificate

Ireland
  • Republic of Ireland Leaving Certificate or equivalent
  • Leaving Certificate when Higher Level grades have been awarded
  • Leaving Certificate/GCE A Levels (2 passes)

Japan
  • Kotogakko Sotsugyo Shosho (Upper Secondary School Certificate of Graduation)
  • Upper Secondary School Certificate of Graduation (Kotogakko Sotsugyo Shosho)

Jordon
  • Twajihi (General Secondary Education Certificate)

Kenya
  • Cambridge Overseas Higher School Certificate awarded until 1973
  • East African Advanced Certificate of Education when awarded between 1967 and 1979
  • Kenya Advanced Certificate of Education when awarded between 1980 and 1988
  • Kenya Certificate of Secondary Education (KCSE) or GCE A level – 2 passes

Korea (South)
  • Korean Republic College Scholastic Ability Test (1993 onwards)
    Note: not clear if this is specific to South Korea
  • Senior High School Certificate (Immumgye Kodung Hakkyo Chorupjang)
    Note: clearly stated as being for South Korea
  • General/Academic Senior High School
  • Certificate/Diploma (Immumgye Kodung Hakkyo Choeupchang)

Kuwait
  • General Secondary School Certificate (Shahadat al-Thanawiya al-A’ama)

Lebanon
  • Baccalauréat Libanais
  • Baccalauréat Technique
  • Baccalauréat II
  • Baccalauréat Technique I & II
  • Attestation of Candidacy awarded in 1978, 1985, 1987, 1988, 1989 or 2014
  • Baccalaureat Libanaise (Baccalaureat 2)
  • Baccalaureat Technique/Technicien or Certificat Professione/de Maltrise/Technicien

Macedonia
  • Диплома за положен завршен испит (Secondary School Leaving Diploma)
  • Државната матура (Matura)
  • Svidetelstvo za zavreno (school leaving certificate) awarded by a vocational secondary School
  • Tehnicar (Technician) awarded by a vocational secondary school

Malaysia
  • Malaysian Matriculation Certificate (Matrikulasi)
  • Malaysian Sijil Tinggi Pelajaran Malaysia
  • Sijil Tinggi Pelajaran Malaysia (STPM)
  • Matrikulasi (Matriculation Certificate)
  • STPM Senior Secondary Certificate of Education or GCE A Levels - 2 passes UEC

Myanmar
  • Matriculation/Basic Education High School (BEHS) plus minimum completed 2 years at Professional College or Secondary Technical College
  • GCE A- Level (2 subject passes) or A level (1 A and 2 AS level passes)

Nepal
  • Higher Secondary Education Board Certificate (Grade 12)

New Zealand
  • New Zealand NCEA Level 3
  • New Zealand Universities Bursaries and Scholarships Examination
  • National Certificate of Education Achievement with a minimum of 80 credits, and 60 credits at Level 3
  • Higher School Certificate
  • New Zealand University Bursaries and Scholarships Examination
  • National Certificate of Educational Achievement (NCEA) – Level 3, minimum 60 credits
  • Higher School Certificate

Papua New Guinea
  • Higher School Certificate

Peru
  • Certificado de Educación Secundaria Común Completa(Secondary Common Education Graduation Certificate) plus a Technical Diploma or entrance to University

Philippines
  • High School Diploma
  • Certificate of Graduation + two years of bachelor degree
  • Grade 12 Senior High School Diploma (2018 onwards)

Poland
  • Polish Matriculation Certificate
  • Swiadectwo Dojrzalosci Liceum Oglnoksztalcacego (Certificate of Matriculation of the General Lyceum)
  • Swiadectwo Dojrzałości (Certificate of Maturity)
  • Matura Swiadectwo Dojrzalosci LiceumOgolnoksztalcacego (Certificate of Matriculation of the General Lyceum)

Russian Federation
  • Attestat o Srednem (Polnom) Obschem Obrazovanii (Certificate of Secondary [Complete] General Education)
  • Attestat o Srednem (polnom) Obshchem Obrazovanii (Certificate of Secondary (Complete) General Education)

Samoa
  • Completion of Form 7
  • Completion of the University of the South Pacific Foundation Year

Note: website directs user to refer to Kiribati. Information above is for Kiribati.

Saudia Arabia
  • Shahadat al-thanawiyyah al-'aama or shahadat al-marhalat al-thanawiyyat (General Secondary Education Certificate) Form 7 or University Foundation Year

Sierra Leone
  • A combination of the West African School Certificate and at least two passes in the West African General Certificate of Education at the Advanced Level (GCE A-level)

South Africa
  • South African National Senior Certificate
  • South African Senior Certificate or Matriculation Certificate of the Joint Matriculation Board (2008 and earlier)
  • National Senior Certificate or Senior Certificate awarded by Umalusi at NQF level 4
  • National Senior Certificate (Year12) Senior Certificate (Year 12) GCE A-Levels 2 Passes
  • International Baccalaureate Diploma

Sri Lanka
  • Sri Lankan GCE Advanced Level
  • General Certificate of Education Advanced Level with a minimum of three passes
  • GCE A-levels – 2 passes

Taiwan
  • Senior High School Diploma
  • Senior Vocational School Diploma
  • Senior High School Diploma or Senior Vocational School Diploma

Thailand
  • Certificate of Secondary Education
  • Matayom 6 or MS 6. (Certificate of Secondary Education)

Turkey
  • Devlet Teknik Lisesi Diplomasi (State Technical High School Diploma)
  • Teknik Lise Diplomasi (Technical High School Diploma) awarded by a technical high school (Teknik lise)
  • Lise Diplomasi (Secondary School Diploma) Technical
  • Vocational School Diploma (Teknik or Meslek Lise Diplomasi)

United Kingdom
  • Great Britain GCE Advanced Level or comparable qualifications
    Note: GB excludes Northern Ireland while the UK includes it
  • General Certificate of Education with one of the following conditions:
    • 3 Advanced Level passes (2 if awarded before 2001) and 3 passes in other subjects
    • Cambridge Pre-U diploma with three principal subjects and the completion of an independent research project
    • Scottish Qualifications Certificate with 3 Higher passes
    • GCE A-Levels (2 subject passes)

USA
  • United States SAT Reasoning Test (Like Australia's ATAR)
    Note: a person can complete the SAT earlier than Year 12, for example, Year 11. Exercise caution
  • High School Diploma

Vietnam
  • Bang Tot nghiep Trung hoc Pho thong (Upper Secondary Education Graduation Diploma)

Zimbabwe
  • South African National Senior Certificate
  • South African Senior Certificate or Matriculation Certificate of the Joint Matriculation Board (2008 and earlier)
  • The Zimbabwe General Certificate of Education with a minimum of two subject passes at Advanced Level and a minimum of five passes at Ordinary Level (grade E or above)
  • Zimbabwe General Certificate of Education at Advanced Levels (A Levels) - 2 passes, or International Baccalaureate Diploma
