Privacy

Protect the customer's personal information during customer location activities when third parties are contacted to assist in locating a customer. Do not reveal or volunteer any information about the customer to any sources. Accidentally revealing personal information may constitute a privacy breach and must be reported to a Team Leader.

When making contact by telephone:

  • verify who is speaking
  • do not reveal any personal information to the other person, and
  • confirm any information obtained

For more information see the Information Privacy Principles.

Websites to assist with customer location searches

This table provides country specific websites where public searches can be conducted to locate a customer.

Note: if any further suitable country specific websites are available, contact International Child Support for inclusion in the table.

Item

Country/Jurisdiction

1

All

https://www.numberway.com/

Numberway is an international directory of white pages and yellow pages phone books, and online directory enquiries. This also provides links to some people search and public records, which utilise multiple sources to find addresses and phone numbers. Some of these sites will only provide limited data for free, however this may be sufficient to determine what further searches are required.
