Check eligibility for allied health assessment services

Table 1: this table describes the process for checking patient eligibility for allied health assessment services 82000, 82005, 82010, 82030, 93032, 93033, 93040 and 93041.

Step

Action

1

Advise caller of online options + Read more ...

Advise the caller of their options to check a patient’s eligibility:

  • Health professionals can use the MBS items online checker in HPOS to see if their patient is eligible for an MBS item
  • All patients can access their Medicare claims history through their Medicare online account or Express Plus Medicare app
  • Remind the caller that the information provided is based on the patient’s Medicare claims history at the time of enquiry

Patients and health professionals can follow instructions on the Services Australia website.

If they want to continue with the call, go to Step 2.

2

Complete security check + Read more ...

For health professionals, see Health professional or their staff call about a claim

For consumers, see Perform security check to authenticate Medicare record

Note: proceed only if the caller meets the security check.

Advise the caller that the eligibility information provided is based on the patient’s Medicare claiming history at the time of the enquiry and is subject to change if there are claims pending.

3

Check patient's age + Read more ...

Ask the caller if the patient is under 13 years of age, alternatively check the patient’s age on CDMS.

Is the patient under 13 years of age?

  • Yes, go to Step 4
  • No, advise the caller the patient is not eligible for the program. Procedure ends here

4

Retrieve the patient’s history + Read more ...

  • Key NHSI,CARDNUMBER,PATIENT and press [Enter]
  • Press [PF9]
    • the patient PIN is displayed

Note: check history by PIN in all instances to ensure the complete claiming history is returned, not just the history associated with one card.

5

Check for pre-requisite associated item numbers on patient’s history + Read more ...

If the referral is from a GP:

  • Key NHSI,PIN,ITEM/3-51,SORT/DOS and press [Enter]
  • Key NHSI,PIN,ITEM/91790,SORT/DOS and press [Enter]
  • Key NHSI,PIN,ITEM/91795,SORT/DOS and press [Enter]
  • Key NHSI,PIN,ITEM/91800-91802,SORT/DOS and press [Enter]
  • Key NHSI,PIN,ITEM/91809-91811,SORT/DOS and press [Enter]

If the referral is from a Specialist or Consultant Physician (including a Paediatrician):

  • Key NHSI,PIN,ITEM/104-131,SORT/DOS and press [Enter]
  • Key NHSI,PIN,ITEM/91822-91826,SORT/DOS and press [Enter]
  • Key NHSI,PIN,ITEM/91832-91836,SORT/DOS and press [Enter]

If the referral is from a Psychiatrist:

  • Key NHSI,PIN,ITEM/296-370,SORT/DOS and press [Enter]
  • Key NHSI,PIN,ITEM/91827-91831,SORT/DOS and press [Enter]
  • Key NHSI,PIN,ITEM/91837-91841,SORT/DOS and press [Enter]
  • Key NHSI,PIN,ITEM/92437,SORT/DOS and press [Enter]
  • Key NHSI,PIN,ITEM/92455-91860,SORT/DOS and press [Enter]
  • Key NHSI,PIN,ITEM/92477,SORT/DOS and press [Enter]
  • Key NHSI,PIN,ITEM/92495-92500,SORT/DOS and press [Enter]

Note: exclude results for item 359.

Is a paid claim displayed?

  • Yes, go to Step 6
  • No, advise the caller that they will need to contact the referring practitioner to advise the referral is not valid until the pre-requisite item number has been claimed. Procedure ends here

6

Check for allied health items on the patient’s history + Read more ...

Key NHSI,PIN,ITEM/82000-82035,SORT/DOS and press [Enter]

Key NHSI,PIN,ITEM/93032-93044,SORT/DOS and press [Enter]

Is a paid claim displayed?

  • Yes, go to Step 7
  • No, advise the caller that that the patient is eligible for the assessment item with the appropriate referral.

If requested, advise the caller 4 assessment services are currently available. Procedure ends here.

7

Advise caller of the number of services available + Read more ...

Does patient history display less than:

  • 4 items in the ranges of 82000 to 82035 and 93032 to 93044 with same date of service, and
  • 4 services for items 82000, 82005, 82010, 82030, 93032, 93033, 93040 and 93041 for any date?

  • Yes, advise the caller that the patient is eligible for the assessment item
    • If requested advise the caller the number of remaining services from the total of 4 services in the child’s lifetime
  • No, advise the caller that the patient is not eligible for the assessment item
