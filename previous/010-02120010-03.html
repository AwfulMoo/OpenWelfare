For ABSTUDY Smart Centre Processing staff only

This page contains information on the steps to be followed during the completion of the ABSTUDY Change of Circumstances/Enquiries Sheet (SY053).

On this page:

Change of Circumstances/Enquiries Sheet (SY053) document completion

Changes to student information

Updating tax and other details

Change of Circumstances/Enquiries Sheet (SY053) document completion

Table 1

Step

Action

1

Document completion + Read more ...

For Service Centre or

ABSTUDY Smart Centre Processing staff

Ensure that the ABSTUDY Change in Circumstances form (SY053) has been checked when received, and that the customer has signed the declaration on the form.

Although it is compulsory for the customer to have completed questions 1 and 2, the remaining questions are optional depending on what the change in the student's circumstances has been.

2

Check which of the questions have been answered: + Read more ...

  • Change of address, go to Step 3
  • Change of bank account details, go to Step 4
  • Change of student income, see Step 1 in Table 2
  • Change of educational institution, see Step 2 in Table 2
  • Discontinuation of full-time studies, see Step 3 in Table 2
  • Change of Australian Apprenticeship (apprentice or trainee) details, see Step 5 in Table 2
  • Tax details, see Step 1 in Table 3
  • Other, see Step 4 in Table 3

3

Change of address + Read more ...

Update the customer's address details on the Address Details (AD) screen, and complete the Accommodation Details (AC) screen if required.

Note: if the customer is paying rent and eligible for Rent Assistance (RA), RA will be paid as a result of the above coding instructions. A Rent Certificate, if required, will also be issued. The assessor must ensure a correct Rent Certificate is issued. The customer will need to return the completed Rent Certificate with any required supporting documents for RA to continue. If the customer does not have a formal written rental agreement, a Rent Certificate will need to be signed by a landlord, agent or person to whom they pay rent.

  • If no further questions were answered, see Step 5 in Table 3
  • If other questions were answered, select the appropriate step below:
  • Change of bank account details, go to Step 4
  • Change of student income, see Step 1 in Table 2
  • Change of educational institution, see Step 2 in Table 2
  • Discontinuation of full-time studies, see Step 3 in Table 2
  • Change of Australian Apprenticeship (apprentice or trainee) details, see Step 5 in Table 2
  • Tax details, see Step 1 in Table 3
  • Other, see Step 4 in Table 3

4

Change of bank account details + Read more ...

Update the payment destination for the customer on the Payment Destination Details (PAS) screen.

There is no restriction on making changes to payment destination for customers who are under 18 and independent.

Note: if the customer is under 18, see the following procedures to ensure the requested changes can be made see Payments for students under 18 years paid to student, parent or a third party.

5

Check the rest of the form + Read more ...

  • If no further questions were answered, see Step 5 in Table 3
  • If further questions were answered, select the appropriate step below:
  • Change of student income, see Step 1 in Table 2
  • Change of educational institution, see Step 2 in Table 2
  • Discontinuation of full-time studies, see Step 3 in Table 2
  • Change of Australian Apprenticeship (apprentice or trainee) details, see Step 5 in Table 2
  • Tax details, see Step 1 in Table 3
  • Other, see Step 4 in Table 3

Changes to student information

Table 2

Step

Action

1

Change of student income + Read more ...

For Service Centre or

ABSTUDY Smart Centre Processing staff

Record the details of the customer's income on the Earned Income Details (EAN) screen in Process Direct or Earned Income Details (EAN) screen in Customer First.

  • If no further questions were answered, see Step 5 in Table 3
  • If further questions were answered, select on the appropriate step below:
  • Change of educational institution, go to Step 2
  • Discontinuation of full-time studies, go to Step 3
  • Change of Australian Apprenticeship (apprentice or trainee) details, go to Step 5
  • Tax details, see Step 1 in Table 3
  • Other, see Step 4 in Table 3

2

Change of educational institution + Read more ...

Update the student's details on the Customer Study Details (EDC) screen in Process Direct or the Education Course Details (EDC) screen in Customer First to reflect the change of educational institution. If the customer is a secondary, non-secondary or tertiary student studying at a TAFE or private provider institution, advise them that proof of enrolment (POE) needs to be provided. Secondary students studying at university will also need to provide POE.

If the customer is studying at an education institution stated above and POE has not been provided, send a letter to request POE. See Requesting information (CLK).

Note: secondary students studying at school and students undertaking a higher education course at university do not have to provide proof of enrolment (POE).

  • If no further questions were answered, see Step 5 in Table 3
  • If further questions were answered, select the appropriate step below:
  • Discontinuation of full-time studies, go to Step 3
  • Change of Australian Apprenticeship (apprentice or trainee) details, go to Step 5
  • Tax details, see Step 1 in Table 3
  • Other, see Step 4 in Table 3

3

Discontinuation of full-time studies + Read more ...

  • If the student has ceased studies (that is, provided last date of full-time studies), their ABSTUDY will need to be cancelled and entitlement to any other income support or Services Australia services investigated. See Payment of ABSTUDY when a student is deferring or discontinuing study before the end of the course. Procedure ends here
  • If the student has dropped from full-time to part-time study, go to Step 4

4

Full time to part time + Read more ...

It will need to be determined whether the course is approved as a full-time or part-time course for ABSTUDY.

  • If the course is a full-time course, investigate whether the student qualifies for a study load concession. See:
  • Assessing the two-thirds study load concession for ABSTUDY
  • Assessing the disability study load concession for ABSTUDY and ABSTUDY Pensioner Education Supplement (PES)
  • Assessing the pensioner study load concession for ABSTUDY Pensioner Education Supplement (PES)
  • If the course is a part-time course, it should be investigated as to whether the student will be entitled to part-time entitlements under the ABSTUDY Part-time Award
  • Go to Step 6

5

Change of Australian Apprenticeship details + Read more ...

Update the Australian Apprenticeship details in either:

Process Direct:

  • Go to the Apprentice Details (NAPD) screen
  • Select Add
  • Key the Australian Apprenticeship details
  • Select Save
  • Key the Receipt Date and Channel. Select Save
  • Select Assess and address any errors/warnings
  • Select Assess again, then select Finish
  • Record details of the update
  • Select Finalise

Customer First

  • Go to the Apprenticeship Details (NAPD) screen
  • Key the Australian Apprenticeship details

6

Check the rest of the form + Read more ...

  • If no further questions were answered, see Step 5 in Table 3
  • If further questions were answered, select the appropriate step below:
  • Tax details, see Step 1 in Table 3
  • Other, see Step 4 in Table 3

Updating tax and other details

Table 3

Step

Action

1

Tax details + Read more ...

For Service Centre or

ABSTUDY Smart Centre Processing staff

Has the customer completed this question and indicated that they do, or do not want tax taken out of their fortnightly payments?

  • Yes:
    • they do not want tax taken out, go to Step 2
    • they do want tax taken out, go to Step 3
  • No, the customer has not answered this question, go to Step 4

2

Payment instruction summary + Read more ...

Update the Payment Instruction Summary (PINS) screen to cease tax deductions.

Has the customer provided any new information under Other?

  • Yes, go to Step 4
  • No, go to Step 5

3

Complete the following tasks: + Read more ...

  • If the customer or their correspondence nominee has not signed the form, contact the customer by phone (where a number is provided) to confirm the tax deduction request and ask them what amount they want deducted each fortnight
  • When advice of tax amount to be deducted has been provided, update the Payment Instruction Summary (PINS) screen to arrange tax deductions
  • If the customer cannot be contacted via phone, issue a Tax Deduction Authority form (SU066) so that the customer can fill this in and post it back. See the Resources page for a link to the SU066 form

Has the customer provided any new information under Other?

  • Yes, go to Step 4
  • No, go to Step 5

4

Other + Read more ...

  • If more information is required to update the change of circumstance advised, send out a Request for Information letter to request documents from the customer. See Requesting information (CLK)
  • Determine if the change of circumstances affects the customer's rate of payment or their qualification for ABSTUDY, and update as appropriate

5

The Form + Read more ...

Record details of any action taken, including updates made or updates requested but not made, on a DOC.
