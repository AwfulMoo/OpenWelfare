Alternative Identity assessment

Step

Action

1

Considering Alternative Identity processes + Read more ...

Check the Identity Confirmation Dashboard for a non-reviewable alternative identity code.

When a person has a non-reviewable alternative identity their identity status will display as not confirmed.

Note: special rules apply in exceptional circumstances for completing Alternative Identity assessments. This includes completing assessments by phone. See Supporting Identity Confirmation for customers unable to attend in person.

Help customers having genuine difficulty obtaining identity documents to get more documents.

If a customer:

  • Attends an Agent site with insufficient identity documents, go to Step 7
  • Contacts about their Alternative Identity Review, go to Step 2
  • Returns a completed RA010 form, go to Step 8

There are special Alternative Identity processes that staff must apply to Care receivers and Partners who are not required to complete identity.

  • Care receivers who are an Income Support Payment (ISP) or Health Care Card (LIC) customer are not required to complete identity requirements, go to Step 6
  • Care receivers who are a Department of Veteran Affairs (DVA) customer (confirmed via DVAS screen), or included in a Child to Adult transfer (CTAT) are not required to complete identity requirements, go to Step 6
  • Partners of Parenting Payment Partnered (PPP) or Health Care Card (LIC) who are an Income Support Payment (ISP) or Health Care Card (LIC) customer are not required to complete identity requirements, go to Step 6

Care receivers and partners who are not on ISP, LIC, DVA or CTAT need to provide COI, PUI and SUIC documents and do not need to complete linkage. Go to Step 5

The customer's Identity Status is under the Snapshot icon in Process Direct.

To access the Identity Confirmation Dashboard:

  • Key START into the Super Key field
  • Select Identity Confirmation Dashboard from the Task Selector

The Process Direct Identity Confirmation Dashboard displays the customer's:

  • Identity Status
  • Identity Linkage tick box
  • RA010 tick box
  • Identity documents:
    • Commencement of Identity (CoI)
    • Primary Use in Community (PUiC)
    • Secondary Use in Community (SUiC)

If the customer:

  • Can provide all required identity documents and complete linkage, see Coding identity documents. The customer can Confirm their Identity and does not need an Alternative Identity assessment. Procedure ends here
  • Can provide approved identity documents within 28 or 56 days, see Identity Review Period. Procedure ends here
  • Has a non-reviewable alternative identity category, procedure ends here
  • Can provide all required identity documents but cannot complete linkage, go to Step 3
  • Cannot provide all required identity documents, go to Step 3

2

Customer contacts about the Alternative Identity review + Read more ...

Has the customer provided all of the identity documents?

  • Yes,
    • code documents and complete linkage if required using the Identity Confirmation Dashboard in Process Direct
    • select FINISH
    • procedure ends here
  • No,
    • ask the customer:
      - is there a reason they are unable to get the documents. For example, they may not have a document, it is misplaced or there are exceptional circumstances
      - can they give the documents before the review expires, or
      - do they need more time to get the documents. If so, extend the review period or see Alternative Identity for a more appropriate alternative identity outcome. This may result in updating an identity category to 'cannot Provide' (ZZ) and completing more questions with the customer using the Identity confirmation dashboard
    • go to Step 3
    • see Alternative Identity Reviews for further information

3

Customer does not have all required documents + Read more ...

Alternative Identity assessments help the agency understand the circumstances of customers having difficulty proving who they are. If a customer attends in person or contacts by phone, staff MUST review their current identity status and discuss with the customer:

  • their ability to provide outstanding identity documents during the assessment or at a later time, and
  • available review periods, and
  • if the Alternative identity assessment can be completed by phone or in person, by staff considering if the customer has exceptional circumstances, and
  • if the customer requires a myGov linking code issued by phone or in person, so customers can upload outstanding identity documents

On the Identity Confirmation Dashboard:

In each category staff must assess if a customer can or cannot give an approved identity document.

Can provide:

  • code and verify the identity documents the customer has given

Can provide later:

  • select YY - will provide later: this will result in a review period. This may be a reasonable option where you have assessed that an Alternative Identity assessment is required because the customer does not have a photo identity document, but has provided a verified PUiC and SUiC document and has indicated that they can provide a CoI within a review period.

Cannot provide:

  • select ZZ - Cannot provide: the customer can never give a verified identity document
    Note: if the customer can give documents for each category but cannot complete linkage:
    • code and verify CoI & PUiC identity documents and
    • select ZZ - Cannot provide for the SUiC document
    • complete an Alternative identity assessment
  • select Next

The Alternative Identity assessment workflow will display. Go to Step 4.

4

Alternative Identity assessment + Read more ...

Based on the customer's circumstances, the Alternative Identity assessment workflow will determine:

  • the questions to ask, and
  • the most appropriate Alternative Identity category and review period

The workflow will prompt Service Officers to verify details on the customer's record. For example, previous addresses, other names etc.

  • select Next to continue
  • complete the full question set

Note: Service Officers must ask all questions in the workflow and accurately record the customer's answers.

Once the customer has answered all questions, an overview screen displays their responses.

  • check all responses are accurate before continuing
  • select Previous and return to the workflow to amend any response, if required
  • select Next and FINISH to finalise the workflow. A pre-filled Fast Note will generate and the appropriate review period will auto apply to the customer's record

The Identity Confirmation Dashboard will show the Alternative Identity category.

  • tell the customer the assessment outcome and review period

For claims processed outside Process Direct:

  • manually code RF on the POI screen in Customer First when finalising the claim
  • go to Step 9

Centrelink International Services

  • alternative Identity coding for customers granted under International Social Security Agreements is completed through the POI screen (by Centrelink International (CIS) staff only)
  • see Proof of Identity documents for overseas customers. (Centrelink International (CIS) staff only)
  • procedure ends here

5

Update the Care receiver or Partner record + Read more ...

This process does NOT require contact with the care receiver or partner.

Open the Identity Confirmation Dashboard:

  • select Identity Confirmation from the Task Selector
  • select Start

Review the care receiver or partner identity status:

Does the care receiver or partner have:

  • a no linkage identity status, (3 identity documents) or
  • a confirmed identity status
    • yes, procedure ends here
    • no, select No Linkage Required from the Identity Linkage dropdown box
  • complete all required fields on the Document details screen. This will include coding and verifying available documents given voluntarily when consent is provided
  • some documents may already be coded and verified
  • based on identity documents that are already coded, apply at least one YY will provide later. If required apply 2 other ZZ cannot provide in other categories where no documents are recorded
  • continue to select Next and continue following this Step

Time to provide documents:

  • select Yes
  • can you provide documents within 56 days
  • select Next

Review and finalise:

  • review category code will show, a Gathering Document open review for a period of 8 weeks
  • select Next and FINISH to create a review and save the customer interaction
  • the open review in the Identity Confirmation Dashboard will auto complete when the claim is finalised. Record the decision on a DOC

Process ends here.

6

Identity status + Read more ...

Does the care receiver have a:

  • no linkage identity status, (3 identity documents), or
  • confirmed identity status
    • Yes, procedure ends here
    • No, code Known to Centrelink (KC) in Process Direct; see Resources for Coding Known to Centrelink (KC)
      - this process does NOT require contact with the care receiver
      - process ends here

7

Alternative Identity assessment at an Agent site + Read more ...

When a customer attends an Agent site with insufficient identity documents, the Agent will:

  • contact Silver Service to advise of the identity documents the customer has presented. The Silver Service Officer must authenticate Specified Personnel at Rural and Remote Agent sites and complete proof of record ownership. Refer to Silver Service phone line for Specified Personnel at Agent sites
  • the Silver Service Officer can complete an Alternative Identity assessment with the agent and customer, go to Step 2
  • important: if the customer does not have their CoI document, Services Australia may be able to help customers with Verification of birth, Verification of Arrival or Aboriginal Population record, see Commencement of Identity
  • complete the Alternative Identity workflow
  • based on the customer's circumstances, the workflow will determine:
    • the questions to ask, and
    • the most appropriate Alternative Identity category and review period
  • refer the case to a Subject Matter Expert (SME) for investigation as soon as possible when:
    • the customer may not be who they are claiming to be, or
    • a document may be fraudulent
  • completes this step again if the customer attends with more documents

If linkage has been established, the customer can upload identity documents using the Upload documents service.

8

Completed RA010 form is returned + Read more ...

When the customer returns the completed RA010 form:

  • scan the RA010 to the customer's record
  • contact the referee to verify their responses by phone. When the referee is not available, contact their employer or community/organisation
    Note: in very remote locations a known community stamp and referee declaration can validate the RA010
  • go to the Identity Confirmation Dashboard in Process Direct and review the identity status. For example:
    • have documents been previously coded and verified, and
    • are there other documents available on the record, or
    • are there documents the customer can provide
  • where the customer is not in a service centre, staff must attempt to complete this discussion by phone with at least one attempt

Based on the discussion with the customer, code and verify available identity documents:

  • where documents cannot be provided, select ZZ - cannot provide
  • tick the RA010 check box
  • select Next
  • select reason for RA010:
    • customer does not currently have identity documents but plans to obtain, or
    • customer does not have required identity documents and is unable to obtain
      Note: if there is no contact with the customer select customer does not have required identity documents and is unable to obtain
  • select Next
  • review and finalise
  • check the review category and review period is correct
  • select Next
  • annotate note with referee's responses
  • select FINISH to finalise

Note: if still unsure about the customer's identity, refer to the local Indigenous Services Officer (ISO) for help.

9

Code Alternative Identity in Customer First for claims processed outside Process Direct + Read more ...

To code Alternative Identity within the claim activity in Customer First (for claims processed outside Process Direct):

  • Go to the POI screen in Customer First
  • Identity Verified may have the option to select 'yes' or 'no'. If so, select 'no'
    Note: this field may not always be available
  • From the Reason for insufficient POI drop down box, select RF. This is a mandatory selection
  • From the Verifying Documents drop down box, select SS
  • In the Date POI Documents requested field, enter the date. This is optional depending on first selection
  • It is not mandatory to code the Service Reason Code field
  • Select the Continue button
  • Annotate the Identity Review Transaction DOC to state that RF has been applied manually as the claim was processed outside of Process Direct
