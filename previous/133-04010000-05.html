Troubleshooting access to online advance options

Item

Description

1

Customer is unable to use this service

The customer is not eligible to use this service.

The Advance payment service (apply, make repayment or adjust repayment) is only available to customers who:

  • have a Centrelink online account linked to their myGov account and
  • get an income support payment, Mobility Allowance (MOB) or Family Tax Benefit (FTB) payment

Tell the customer why they are not eligible.

2

Existing activities

Customers will not be able to process their advance online if there is a started advance (AVC) activity on their record. The system will advise the customer to contact Services Australia to process their advance request.

3

Further help with advance payments digital services

For help with other issues customers may experience using the advance payments digital services, see Troubleshooting Centrelink self service.
