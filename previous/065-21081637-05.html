My Aged Care: Learning Glossary

Word or term

Understanding between Services Australia and the Department of Health and Aged Care

My Aged Care Contact Centre

Owned and managed by Department of Health and Aged Care.

Aged Care Contact Centre

Aged Care SMART Centres

Aged Care Allied Health (ACAH) – Services Australia Provider line

Owned and managed by Services Australia.

Customer – could contact SMARTS and receive the GSO offer and be booked into an ACSO.

Customer – could be warm transferred to SMARTS from My Aged Care (Department of Health and Aged Care) for an ACSO appointment to be booked.

Provider – could be warm transferred to My Aged Care from ACAH or from My Aged Care to ACAH.

My Aged Care Industry Phone line

Owned and managed by Department of Health and Aged Care.

My Aged Care website

Owned and managed by Department of Health and Aged Care.

Customers

Term used by Services Australia for consumers.

Consumer

Term used by Department of Health and Aged Care for someone who has not registered with My Aged Care.

Client

Term used by Department of Health and Aged Care for someone who has registered with My Aged Care.

Carer

Person caring for customer/consumer may or may not be a family member.

Representative

Department of Health and Aged Care term for a person or organisation acting on the customers/consumers behalf.

Nominee

Services Australia term for a person or organisation acting on the customer’s/consumer’s behalf.

Aged care ID

Department of Health and Aged Care uses to identify its clients and their record with Department of Health and Aged Care.

Customer Reference Number (CRN)

Services Australia uses CRNs to identify its customers.

Regional Assessment Service

Provides short-term case management for vulnerable customers with complex or multiple needs. Regional Assessment Service delivered by the Department of Health and Aged Care.

Aged Care Assessment Team

Complex aged care assessment service delivered by Department of Health and Aged Care.

National Screening and assessment form (NSAF)

Department of Health and Aged Care issued form used for comprehensive age care assessments.

The Aged Care Assessment (ACAT)

Comprehensive aged care assessment using National Screening and assessment form.

Aged Care Specialist officer

Services Australia staff providing end-to-end aged care access and navigation of services.

Mobile Service Centres

Services Australia owned and managed mobile access points for rural and remote customers.
