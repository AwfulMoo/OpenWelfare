Notification of a deceased person

Table 1

Step

Action

1

Identify the notifier + Read more ...

At a service centre:

  • ask the notifier for their name and Medicare card number
  • do a POI/security check
  • check the Personal Tab on the Consumer Directory Maintenance System (CDMS)

Does the notifier identify as Indigenous? If yes:

  • ask permission before mentioning the deceased by name
  • establish their relationship to the deceased or authority to inform Medicare

If the security check was:

  • successful, go to Step 2
  • unsuccessful, tell the notifier to:
    • come back with sufficient ID, or
    • complete Advice of death form (SA116) and return it via post, fax or to the service centre

By Phone:

  • ask the caller their name and Medicare card number
  • do a security check using CDMS Inquire screen
  • check the Personal Tab on CDMS

Does the caller identify as Indigenous? If yes, ask permission before mentioning the deceased by name.

2

Did the deceased person have a Centrelink benefit or payment? + Read more ...

Ask the notifier if the deceased person (using their name if appropriate) was receiving a Centrelink benefit or payment?

  • Yes, go to Step 3
  • No, go to Step 7

3

Notify other areas of Services Australia + Read more ...

Ask the notifier, 'has any other area of Services Australia been notified?'

  • Yes, go to Step 7
  • No,
    • tell the notifier that more information is needed to update the Centrelink record
    • go to Step 4

4

Is the deceased a child or adult? + Read more ...

Ask the notifier if it is about the death of a child or adult.

  • For an adult, go to Step 5
  • For a child, go to Step 6

5

Adult Centrelink recipient + Read more ...

For an adult Centrelink recipient:

  • Print and complete an Advice of death form (SA116) while the notifier is still on the phone
  • Go to Step 7

6

Death of a child + Read more ...

Tell the notifier that the agency has a team that helps customers when making first contact about the death of a child. Refer them to the bereavement team.

Over the phone:

Warm transfer to the Bereavement/Double Orphan Pension Helpdesk.

At a service centre:

Call the Child Bereavement Line on their behalf. When the call is answered, tell them that the call is from Medicare and a person would like to notify of a bereavement. Give the phone to the notifier.

Go to Step 7.

7

Amend the deceased persons Medicare record + Read more ...

Amend the deceased person’s Medicare record in CDMS with the date of death.

See table 2 Decide if a person is the Group contact.

8

Reissue a Medicare card + Read more ...

Is the notifier listed on the same Medicare card as the deceased?

  • Yes, issue a replacement Medicare card, if the notifier agrees
  • No, advise the notifier that a new card can be issued at the request of person over the age of 15 listed on that Medicare card

Procedure ends here.

Decide if a person is the Group contact

Table 2

Step

Action

1

Inquire consumer details + Read more ...

On the CDMS Home page:

  • select Inquire on Consumer Details
  • key Medicare card number in ID Number field
  • select Group
  • select Members Details tab

This will display all people enrolled on the Medicare card. It will identify who is the Group contact by showing Y in the Grp Con column.

  • If the deceased is the Group contact with other people on the card, see table 3
  • If the deceased is not the Group contact and on a card with other people, see table 4
  • If the person is the only person on the card, see table 5

Deceased is Group contact with other people on the card

Table 3

Step

Action

1

Deceased is the Group contact with other people on the Medicare card + Read more ...

Where the Group contact is deceased, change the group contact to another person on the Medicare card who is over the age of 15 years.

On the CDMS Home page:

  • select Amend Consumer Details
  • key the Medicare card number in the ID Number field
  • select Group

The Amend Medicare Card Details screen will display.

Select the Name/Address tab:

  • check the Group Association check box
  • key the reference number of the person who will be the new Group contact
  • select Get Details

The Contact name field will populate with the person’s name.

  • select Validate, Validate, Update

The Amend Medicare Card Details screen will display

Note: if the people who remain on the Medicare card are under the age of 15 years of age, you must check if they are on another Medicare card.

  • If they are not enrolled on another card they will become the group contact
  • If they are on another card, end the association for the child on this enrolment

2

Add date of death + Read more ...

On the Amend Medicare Card details screen:

  • select Personal tab

The Amend Personal Details screen displays.

  • select the radio button to select the customer to be amended
  • key the date of death in the Date of Death field
  • select the source in the Death Notification Source field
  • select Comments tab and record the notifying person’s:
    • name
    • relationship
    • Medicare number (if card details are known)
  • Select Validate, Validate, Update

3

Finalise changes + Read more ...

The Family Safety Net registration must be updated where the deceased is registered as the Family Contact (FC).

The Service Officer is to change:

  • FC to the Spouse (SP), and
  • SP to FC

Note: do not remove the deceased person from the Family Safety Net registration. They are automatically removed at the end of the calendar year.

For more information see Family registration and confirmation for Medicare Safety Net.

Deceased is the only person on the card

Table 5

Step

Action

1

Amend consumer details + Read more ...

Where the deceased is the only person on the Medicare card the Group contact must be updated before a date of death can be entered in Personal. The Group contact’s name is changed to EST OF followed by the person’s name.

On the CDMS Home page:

  • select Amend Consumer Details
  • key the Medicare card number in the ID Number field
  • select Group

The Amend Personal Details screen displays.

  • select Name/Address tab
  • untick the Group Association button
  • remove the Contact Reference Number
  • select Est of

This will change the Group contact to Est Of and an Information Message will appear.

  • OK the Message and Validate, Validate, Update

The Amend Medicare Card Details screen will display.

2

Add date of death in Personal Details tab + Read more ...

On the Amend Medicare Card details screen:

  • select Personal tab

The Amend Personal Details screen displays.

  • key the date of death in the Date of Death field
  • select the source in the Death Notification Source field
  • select Comments tab and record the notifying person’s name and Medicare number (if known)
  • select Validate, Validate, Update

Notify other areas of Services Australia

Table 6

Step

Action

1

Was the SA116 form completed? + Read more ...

Was the Advice of death form (SA116) completed or mostly completed during this contact?

  • Yes, go to Step 2
  • No, go to Step 3

2

Submit the form + Read more ...

There are 2 ways of submitting the form:

  • Scan on an MFD, or
  • Fax the form to the Centrelink National Scanning Fax number. The form is received by staff in the National Business Gateway. They will attach it to the Centrelink record and send to the relevant team for processing

3

Notifier to give information later + Read more ...

If the notifier wants to fill in the SA116 later mention they can get more information and support on the Services Australia website What to do when someone dies.

Give them any other relevant service offer.
