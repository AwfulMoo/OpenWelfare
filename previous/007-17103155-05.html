Examples of eligible grandparent relationships and entitlements for ACCS (Grandparent)

Table 1

Example

Description

1

Natural, adoptive or step grandparent of the child

Nick and Bonnie are the biological parents of their child Milton. Nick and Bonnie separate and Nick marries Marika. Marika is Milton’s step parent. In later years Milton marries Sarah and they have a child Nathaniel.

Marika is the step grandparent of Nathaniel.

Milton and Sarah eventually separate and Milton marries Helga.

Helga is Nathaniel’s step parent and Helga’s parents are Nathaniel’s step grandparents.

2

Natural, adoptive or step great-grandparent of the child

Bill and Ping are the biological parents of Jessica. Jessica and partner Bjorn are the biological parents of Theo. Bill and Ping are the natural grandparents of Theo. In later years Theo adopts a child Phoebe.

Bill and Ping are the adoptive great-grandparents of Phoebe.

3

Parent or grandparent of a relationship parent of the child

Neville and Connie are the biological parents of Crystal. Crystal has a child Nicolas born through a surrogate Bianca.

Neville and Connie are the grandparents of Nicolas.

4

Former partner of natural, adoptive or relationship grandparent or great-grandparent

Riccardo and Jean are the biological parents of Ben. Ben is the biological parent of Alison. Riccardo and Jean are the grandparents of Alison. Riccardo and Jean separate and Jean has a new partner Brian. Jean and Brian are the grandparents of Alison (along with Riccardo). In later years Jean and Brian separate.

Brian is the former partner of Jean and, as Brian has primary care of Alison, is a grandparent.

5

Foster parent

Silvio raised Cindy from the age of 6 years as a foster child. In later years Cindy has a child Roy.

Silvio is not considered the grandparent of Roy as Silvio is not the natural, adoptive or step grandparent of Roy.

6

Other relative

Louisa is the biological parent of Petra. Louisa dies and Louisa's cousin Graciella takes on the care of Petra when Petra is 3 years old. In later years Petra has a child Bernie.

Graciella is not considered the grandparent of Bernie because Graciella is not the natural, adoptive or step grandparent of Bernie.

7

Cultural reasons

Sabrina is the biological parent of Jarama. Ruby is the aunt of Sabrina and the great aunt of Jarama. In Ruby’s culture, Ruby has always been known as Jarama’s grandmother due to their strong cultural background and beliefs.

Ruby is not considered the grandparent of Jarama because Ruby is not the natural, adoptive or step grandparent of Jarama.

8

Migration

Marialla is the mother of Luka. Marialla migrated to Australia with her Aunt Francesca. Francesca takes Luka into her care.

Francesca is not considered the grandparent of Luka as Francesca is not the natural, adoptive or step grandparent of Luka.

9

Income Support Payment

Malcolm is caring for Malcolm's natural grandchild Peter.

Malcolm is in receipt of a Service Pension paid by the Department of Veterans' Affairs. As this payment is an ACCS (Grandparent) eligible income support payment.

Malcolm meets the income support requirements for ACCS Grandparent.

10

Income Support Payment

Padma is caring for Padma's adopted grandchild Sarita and claims CCS. Padma does not receive an ACCS (Grandparent) eligible income support payment and was ineligible for ACCS Grandparent. Padma has instead been assessed for an exemption from the CCS activity test and is eligible for 100 hours approved care.

Padma becomes partnered to Santos who is receiving a Disability Support Payment. As Padma's partner is in receipt of an ACCS (Grandparent) eligible income support payment, Padma is now eligible to receive ACCS Grandparent for Sarita. This will increase Padma's rate of subsidy entitlement to 100% of child care fees where the fees charged are equal to or below the hourly rate cap, or up to 120% of the hourly rate cap where the fees charged are above the hourly rate cap.
