Permanent offshore Contributory Parent visa (subclass 143)

Table 2: This table describes the applicable entitlement period to be recorded under the Entitlement tab in CDMS for a person who has applied for a permanent offshore Contributory Parent visa (subclass 143).

Note: where the applicant does not hold the temporary Contributory Parent visa (subclass 173), they are not entitled to Medicare until the permanent Contributory Parent visa (subclass 143) is granted. However, they may be eligible under a RHCA if they meet the specific criteria, see Reciprocal Health Care Agreements (RHCA) eligibility for Medicare and Pharmaceutical Benefits Scheme (PBS).

Expired temporary offshore Contributory Parent visa (subclass 173) or temporary onshore Contributory Parent visa (subclass 884)

Table 5: Where an applicant’s temporary offshore Contributory Parent visa (subclass 173) or temporary onshore Contributory Parent visa (subclass 884) has expired, they can remain eligible for Medicare providing they meet the criteria.

This table details the required criteria for extending eligibility when the applicant’s temporary offshore Contributory Parent visa (subclass 173) or temporary onshore Contributory Parent visa (subclass 884) has expired.

Document

Action

Hold a bridging visa 'A' (BVA), and:

  • have applied for permanent Contributory Parent visa (subclass 143), or
have applied for permanent Contributory Parent visa (subclass 864)

Applicant’s entitlement can be extended.

  • add a new entitlement period and extend for the duration of the BVA or for 12 months from the grant date of the BVA - whichever comes first

Note: notate details of documents sighted in the Document tab in CDMS

Permanent offshore Contributory Parent visa (subclass 143) or permanent onshore Contributory Parent visa (subclass 864) who has not held temporary offshore Contributory Parent visa (subclass 173) or temporary onshore Contributory Parent visa (subclass 884)

Table 6: This table describes the entitlement type for the applicant of permanent offshore Contributory Parent visa (subclass 143) or permanent onshore Contributory Parent visa (subclass 864) who has not held a temporary offshore Contributory Parent visa (subclass 173) or temporary onshore Contributory Parent visa (subclass 884).

Prior place of residency

Action

RHCA country - and the applicant meets the specific RHCA criteria

Where the applicant resided in a RHCA country - eligible for enrolment under that specific RHCA, where the relevant criteria is met.
