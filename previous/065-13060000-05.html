Aged Care Calculation of your cost of care forms

SA486

This digital form:

  • is used to provide a care recipient’s income and/or assets details for an aged care means assessment
  • will only ask questions relevant to the care recipient’s circumstances and will shorten the time taken to complete the form

The digital form includes the Authorising a person or organisation to enquire or act on your behalf (SS313). This is used if the care recipient wants to nominate another person to deal with Services Australia for Centrelink payments and services including aged care.

Home Care Package Calculation of your cost of care form

SA456

This form is used:

  • to provide a care recipient’s income and/or assets details for an aged care means assessment
  • when a care recipient does not receive a means tested payment from Centrelink or the Department of Veterans’ Affairs

The form includes the Authorising a person or organisation to enquire or act on your behalf (SS313). This is used if the care recipient wants to nominate another person to deal with Services Australia for Centrelink payments and services including aged care.

Residential Aged Care Property details for Centrelink and DVA customers form

SA485

This form is used:

  • to provide a care recipient’s income and/or assets details for an aged care means assessment
  • when a care recipient receives a means tested payment from Centrelink or the Department of Veterans’ Affairs and owns or part owns their home including a retirement village

The form includes the Authorising a person or organisation to enquire or act on your behalf (SS313). This is used if the care recipient wants to nominate another person to deal with Services Australia for Centrelink payments and services including aged care.
