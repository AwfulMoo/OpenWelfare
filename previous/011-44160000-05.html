Enrolment flags

Enrolment flags used prior to introduction of CDMS

This table lists the enrolment flags that were used on the Enrolment Flag Pin (EFPI) screen prior to the introduction of the Consumer Directory Maintenance System (CDMS).

Flag

Description

Eligibility and visas

A

Australian citizens.

Australian citizens eligibility for Medicare

E

Migrants (includes New Zealand citizens who reside in Australia).

New Zealand citizens eligibility for Medicare

F

Persons entering Australia (excluding dependants) when visits are financed from funds made available to the Australian/American Educational Foundation, known as Fulbright Scholars.

Ministerial orders and Medicare eligibility

G

Holders of visas according to Ministerial Orders:

  • Temporary protection visa (visa subclass 444, 451, 447 and 785)
  • Humanitarian concern visa (visa subclass 786)

Ministerial orders and Medicare eligibility

H

Holder of temporary Contributory Parent visa subclass 173 (offshore).

Ministerial orders and Medicare eligibility

K

Applicant for permanent Contributory Parent visa subclass 143 (offshore).

Ministerial orders and Medicare eligibility

M

Holder of temporary Contributory Parent visa subclass 884 (onshore).

Ministerial orders and Medicare eligibility

P

Provisional residents visa (temporary visa). Applicants for permanent resident status for duration of temporary visa.

Applicant for permanent residency eligibility for Medicare

R

Reciprocal Health Care Agreements:

  • Belgium
  • Finland
  • Italy
  • Malta
  • The Netherlands
  • Norway
  • Sweden
  • United Kingdom

Reciprocal Health Care Agreements (RHCA) for Medicare and Pharmaceutical Benefits Scheme (PBS)

V

Applicant for permanent Contributory Parent visa subclass 864 (onshore).

Ministerial orders and Medicare eligibility

W

Applicant for permanent resident status. Aged parent category not eligible.

Applicant for permanent residency eligibility for Medicare

X

Holder of visa subclass 785 or 786 who has applied for permanent resident status.

Note: to extend eligibility beyond the current 3 year period the applicants' travel document and approval letter from Department of Home Affairs must be included with enrolment application.

Ministerial orders and Medicare eligibility

Applicant for permanent residency eligibility for Medicare

Z

Applicants for a permanent protection visa (includes an application for permanent resident status).

Applicants for permanent residency eligibility for Medicare

Enrolment control lines

List of Medicare enrolment control lines

This table lists current Medicare enrolment control lines as well as their topics and functions. You can search the list using [Ctrl+F].

Control lines

Topic

Function

EPCI

Postcode enquiry

Postcode locality enquiry screen:

  • EPCI,locality,state code - used to obtain the postcode for the locality and State keyed.
  • EPCI,postcode - used to obtain localities for the postcode keyed.

EPGI

Postcode enquiry

Postgroup locality enquiry screen:

  • EPGI,postgroup,p - Postgroup locality enquiry screen
  • EPCI,statscode,s - Statscode enquiry screen

EREI

Enrolment enquiry

Current enrolment enquiry screen.

Topic

Function

Name and address enquiry

Address search screen.

EFAI

Medicare Safety Net family

Family Safety Net registration screen.

EFRI

Medicare Safety Net family

Family audit trail screen.

EFSI

Medicare Safety Net family

Family Safety Net confirmation screen.

EFPI

PIN enquiry

Flag PIN screen for the PIN keyed.

EMGI

PIN merging

Used to merge two PINs.

ENSI

Name and address enquiry

Blank name search screen.

MHGI

Gap processing transaction

Used to transfer information from Medicare to Medibank Private to enable gap benefits to be paid.
