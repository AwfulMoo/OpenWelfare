Professional registration, manual or auto

Under the 90 Day Pay Doctor Cheque Scheme, Medicare will automatically cancel cheques made payable to eligible medical practitioners through the claimant when the cheque has not been banked after 90 days. The amount is then paid directly into the medical practitioner nominated bank account.

Payment on enquiry screens

Always check for a CLID line. If a CLID line is present, the cheque will be automatically cancelled and paid into the health professional’s bank account on the 91st day.
