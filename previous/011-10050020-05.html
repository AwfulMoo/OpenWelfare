Table 3: for information updated over the phone, AIR Service Officers must get the provider number and confirm the caller's details as well as details of the changes and record notes. See Change requests in the AIR.

Requested change

Action

ABN (ownership change)

Change of ownership requires a new IM004 form to be completed.

Bank details

Australian Immunisation Register – Bank account details for vaccination providers (IM005) form must be completed or new bank details provided on company letterhead with the authorised contact person’s signature.

Business name

The request must be made on a company letterhead with the authorised contact person’s signature.

Cancel registration

The request must be made on a company letterhead with the authorised contact person’s signature.

Contact name details

The authorised contact can update over the phone or on a company letterhead with the authorised contact person’s signature. This is for the AIR program registration only.

Postal address

The authorised contact can update over the phone, by using the IM004 form or on a company letterhead with the authorised contact person’s signature. This is for the AIR program registration only.

Practice/Business address

The request must be made on a company letterhead with the authorised contact person’s signature or email with the state or territory signature block, Adobe Sign or DocuSign, electronic signature from state or territory authorised person.

Registration start date

The request must be made in writing from the applicable state/territory health department with the authorised contact person’s signature or official stamp.

Telephone number

Can be done over the phone, using forms IM004 and IM005 or on company letterhead with the authorised contact person’s signature. This is for the AIR program registration only.

Bank account name on form does not match details in PDS

Check details

If the bank account name on the form does not match any bank account details listed in PDS for the applicant, check the BSB and account numbers.

If they are:

  • the same, continue to link the bank details and process form
  • different, record the new bank details and continue to process the form

If unsure of bank account details, confirm information with the applicant.

  • incorrect

Escalate form

  • contact the provider to confirm the provider type
record a comment on PDS and WLM confirming the name of the person contacted and the correct provider type

Signature date not supplied

Use date form received

Use date form received as start date for provider registration or bank details.

State or territory health department approval not supplied

Escalate form

The form must have either a signature or be stamped by the state or territory health department, and have the authorised representative’s full name.

If missing:

  • Try to contact the provider to resend the signed or stamped registration application with the full name of the authorised representative
  • If unsuccessful, send the application back to the applicant with the standard letter

Note: L type - Medical practice applicants do not need state or territory health department approval.

Troubleshooting error messages in PDS

Table 5

Message

Action

Green – information

An information message displays to advise that the action has been successful. An information message may also appear to advise that data has/has not been found, or advise of other data entry issues.

Orange – warning

A warning message displays to advise there may be a problem with the information entered. The message will indicate what the warning relates to. Check the data, make any necessary changes and then select Save to continue processing if the information is correct.

Red – error

An error message displays to advise there has been a problem processing the information entered. The message will indicate what the error relates to. The error will need to be fixed before the data can be saved.

Pop-up

Pop-ups alert Service Officers to important information for example, navigating away from a page without saving. Service Officers must acknowledge the pop-up by selecting OK or Cancel to continue.
