Independence category

Independent rate of ABY for RSS

Table 1: This table describes the documents required by Random Sample Surveys (RSS) to verify entitlement to the independent rate of ABSTUDY.

Item

Description

1

22 years of age or older + Read more ...

If a customer is 22 years of age or over, they are considered to be independent. This independence criterion is not a reviewable independence category.

Check customer's record:

  • Proof of age should already have been provided as part of the Identity confirmation requirements
  • There is no need to request additional documentation to prove independence on the basis of age

2

Has or previously had a dependent child + Read more ...

A customer is considered independent where they have, or have had, a dependent child in their care. This independence criterion is not a reviewable independence category.

  • If the customer received Centrelink payments in respect of the child, the CHS screen will provide proof of this
  • If Centrelink payments were never received for the child, obtain proof via child's birth certificate or adoption papers. Note: proof can be obtained from the paper file

If verification documentation is required, see ABSTUDY customer has, or has had, a dependent child.

3

Member of a couple or previously was a member of a couple + Read more ...

A customer who is a member of a couple or previously was a member of a couple are considered independent. This independence criterion is not a reviewable independence category.

  • Check the partner is recorded, or was previously recorded on the Marital Status (MS) screen and the customer is receiving or has received the member of a couple rate of payment
  • If customer has not been paid at the member of a couple rate of payment, then proof of being a member of a couple or recognition of the relationship under Aboriginal or Torres Strait Islander lore is acceptable evidence

If verification documentation is required, see ABSTUDY customer currently or previously married or in a registered relationship.

4

De facto relationship for at least 6 months + Read more ...

A reviewable independence category:

  • Check that the partner is recorded as a partner for 6 months or more on the Marital Status (MS) screen
  • Where evidence of the customer's circumstances is not available from Centrelink records, or doubt exists about the customer's circumstances, documentation may be requested from the customer to support the claim for Independence
  • Where documentation is required, applicants must provide a statutory declaration signed by both parties, plus other documentation indicating cohabitation such as rental agreement, electricity, water or telephone accounts

If verification documentation is required, see ABSTUDY customer in de facto relationship.

5

Working full-time or registered as unemployed for at least 3 of the past 4 years + Read more ...

A customer who worked full-time or registered as unemployed for at least 3 of the past 4 years may be considered independent. This independence criterion is not a reviewable independence category.

  • Check for a copy of the DOC on the customer's record with original decision to grant independent status for ABY
  • If there is no DOC on the customer's record, a copy of documents from file which verify grant of independent status is sufficient
  • If neither of these are available, then the customer must provide verification again

If verification documentation is required, see ABSTUDY customer is independent through workforce participation.

6

Orphan and 15 years of age or older + Read more ...

Not a reviewable independence category:

  • Check the customer's Centrelink record to determine if Double Orphan Pension (DOP) has been approved for a carer on behalf of the customer
  • Where there is doubt about a customer's orphanhood, students/Australian Apprentices must provide a death certificate or documentation from an Aboriginal/Torres Strait Islander organisation to the effect that a parent is deceased, or legally missing. Proof can be obtained from the customer's file

If verification documentation is required, see ABSTUDY customer is an orphan.

Independence category

Table 2: This table contains information relating to the recommendation for verification to each of the independence category.

Item

Description

1

Parents cannot care for customer + Read more ...

A reviewable independence category.

Obtain verification from an appropriate person of authority:

  • if the parent is incarcerated, confirm with the relevant authority
  • if the parent is confined to a medical institution or aged care home, confirm with institution/home
  • if the parent is missing, evidence is required of attempts to locate the parent

If verification documentation is required, see ABSTUDY customer's parents cannot exercise their parental responsibilities.

2

Caring for another person's dependent child + Read more ...

A reviewable independence category:

  • Check the customer is receiving Family Tax Benefit (FTB) for the child
  • If the customer is not receiving FTB, documents need to be provided to support the claim

If verification documentation is required, see ABSTUDY customer who has the care of another person's child.

3

Be of school leaving age in the particular state or territory or at least 16 years of age, and meet the unreasonable to live at home criteria + Read more ...

A reviewable independence category:

  • Attach a copy of the Social Worker DOC containing recommendation for the grant of independent status
  • If the reason for independence is the same as when originally granted, no further action is required. If the reason has changed, investigate and record on a DOC whether or not the independent status is to continue

If verification documentation is required, see ABSTUDY unreasonable to live at home (UTLAH).

4

15 years of age or older and has adult status in a traditional community + Read more ...

Not a reviewable independence category:

The Random Sample Surveys Officer (RSSO) is to:

  • check for a DOC on the record confirming the original grant of independence
  • copy confirmation documents from file, or
  • if neither of these are available, then obtain a written statement signed by a tribal elder who has authority in their home community for the observance of cultural practice. It must confirm the completion of a traditional initiation ceremony and a written statement from the student confirming they are not supported financially by their family and live independently in the community

If verification documentation is required, see ABSTUDY customer who has Special Adult Status in a traditional community.

5

18 years of age or over and been in lawful custody + Read more ...

18 years of age or over and previously been in lawful custody for a total of at least 6 months.

Not a reviewable independence category.

Request evidence from the customer:

  • Provide a statement from Corrective Services giving details of imprisonment
  • If the customer cannot provide such a statement, they must provide details of periods in custody and the name of the Corrective Institution to allow details to be verified

If verification documentation is required, see ABSTUDY customer is independent due to previous lawful custody.

6

16 years of age or over and was adopted or fostered by a non-indigenous family + Read more ...

16 years of age or over and was adopted or fostered by a non-indigenous family for more than 2 years and now lives in an aboriginal or Torres Strait Islander community.

A reviewable independence category.

The customer will be required to provide a statement from:

  • an Indigenous community organisation
  • a well-known and respected person of the Indigenous community, or
  • a relevant state or territory authority

If verification documentation is required, see ABSTUDY customer who has returned to live in an Indigenous Australian community.

7

Been out of secondary school for at least 14 months, have worked and earned in a 14 month period 75% or more of the Wage Level A of the National Training Wage schedule included in a modern award + Read more ...

A customer who has earned 75% or more of the Wage Level A of the National Training Wage Schedule since leaving secondary school may be considered independent. This independence criterion is not a reviewable independence category.

  • Check for a copy of the DOC on the customer's record with the original decision to grant independent status
  • If no DOC is on record, check for documents from the file which verify grant of independent status
  • If neither of these are available, then the customer must provide verification again

If verification documentation is required, see:

  • Assessing independence for full time or concessional study load students from regional and remote areas under the self-supporting through employment criterion
  • ABSTUDY customer is independent through workforce participation

8

Worked for on average 30 hours a week for a total of 18 months within a 2 year period + Read more ...

A customer who has worked an average of 30 hours a week for a total of 18 months within a 2 year period may be considered independent. This independence criterion is not a reviewable independence category.

  • Check for a copy of the DOC on the customer record with the original decision to grant independent status
  • If there is no DOC on the record, check for documents from file which verify grant of independent status
  • If neither of these are available, then customer must provide verification again

If verification documentation is required, see:

  • Assessing independence when a customer is self-supporting through full-time paid employment
  • ABSTUDY customer is independent through workforce participation

9

Worked continuously for at least 15 hours a week for at least 2 years since they last left secondary school + Read more ...

A customer who has worked continuously for at least 15 hours per week for at least 2 years since leaving secondary school may be considered independent. This independence criterion is not a reviewable independence category.

  • Check for a copy of the DOC on the customer record with the original decision to grant independent status
  • If there is no DOC on the record, check for documents from file which verify the grant of independent status
  • If neither of these are available, then the customer must provide verification again

If verification documentation is required, see:

  • Assessing independence for full time or concessional study load students from regional and remote areas under the self-supporting through employment criterion
  • ABSTUDY customer is independent through workforce participation

10

Earned $15,000 gross income in approved agricultural work anytime during the period 30 November 2020 to 31 December 2021 + Read more ...

A customer who has earned at least $15,000 gross income in approved agricultural work anytime between 30 November 2020 and 31 December 2021 may be considered independent. This independence criterion is not a reviewable independence category.

  • Check for a copy of the DOC on the customer's record with original decision to grant independent status
  • If there is no DOC on record, check for a copy of documents from which verify the grant of independent status
  • If neither of these are available, then the customer must provide verification again

If verification documentation is required, see:

  • Assessing independence when a student has undertaken approved agricultural work
  • ABSTUDY customer is independent through workforce participation

11

Unreasonable to live at home (UTLAH) + Read more ...

A reviewable independence category.

If the customer is identifying for the first time that it is unreasonable for them to live at home and they are:

  • under 18 years of age, book a Social Work Unreasonable to Live at Home (UTLAH) appointment for a UTLAH assessment
  • 18 - 22 years of age inclusive:
    • issue Youth Allowance: Unreasonable to live at home Statement by Young Person (SY015) and Youth Allowance: Unreasonable to live at home Statement for Parent(s) / Guardian(s) (SY016) forms, and
    • refer to Student Network - UTLAH Over 18 Team (O18)

If the customer has previously been granted UTLAH status and they are:

  • under 18 years of age, and:
    • there has been no change of address notified since they were granted UTLAH, note the Social Work DOC containing the recommendation for grant of independent status in the RSS results DOC
    • there has been a change of address notified since they were granted UTLAH, book a Social Work Unreasonable to Live at Home (UTLAH) appointment for a review of the UTLAH assessment, no further action is required
  • 18 - 22 years of age inclusive:
    • issue an SY015 - Youth Allowance: Unreasonable to live at home Statement by Young Person form
    • if the customer's stated reason why it is unreasonable for them to live at home on the SY015 matches what is shown on their record, no further action is required
    • if the customer's stated reason why it is unreasonable for them to live at home on the SY015 has changed, investigate and record on a DOC whether or not the independent status is to continue and refer to the relevant UTLAH assessment team

The Resources page contains a link to Office Locator. See Unreasonable to live at home (UTLAH) initial contact for Youth Allowance (YA), Disability Support Pension (DSP), Special Benefit (SpB) or Tertiary Access Payment (TAP).

If verification documentation is required, see:

  • Assessing unreasonable to live at home (UTLAH) for customers claiming or receiving Youth Allowance (YA), Disability Support Pension (DSP), Special Benefit (SpB) or Tertiary Access Payment (TAP)
  • Reviews of independence for Youth Allowance (YA)
