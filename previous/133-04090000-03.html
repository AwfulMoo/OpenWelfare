Customer access to the Manage income and assets service + Read more ...

Online guide

See Centrelink online account help - Manage your income and assets details.

For eligible customers, their income and assets details will display. A tile will show a summary of their income and asset details currently on record for each category.

Selecting this tile will display a detailed view of each category of income and assets they have on record.

  • Income figures will display as a fortnightly amount
  • All figures will display to 2 decimal points

If the customer thinks the value of the asset seems low or is not correct, see Item 4.

Customer online updates may not occur if either:

  • the payment type or the type of asset update needs manual processing, or
  • the customer needs to supply documents

When there is a request for documents, the following will display:

  • a list of documents to provide within 28 days
  • an option to upload their supporting documents online
  • an online task on the customer's Centrelink online account homepage until all required documents are uploaded or submitted

Customers can also provide these documents:

  • by post to Services Australia, or
  • in person at a service centre

The Receipt page will also advise if any staff processing is needed for the update to be finalised.

Electronic Messaging

Customers who have subscribed to Electronic Messaging will get a message reminder via email or SMS at day 23 to complete all outstanding, required tasks.

Manual Processing

Some updates from some customer groups will be processed manually. Updates made by RCA/CUR customers, who are self-funded retirees and not in receipt of any other payments, will allocate to service officers for manual processing.

See Coding income and assets for Centrelink payments and services for who can action and code income and asset changes. Customers may not need to contact for small changes to asset values.

Individual Nominees

Nominees can make updates on a customer's behalf.

Nominees can provide verification documents using the Upload documents service in the Manage income and assets service.

Nominees cannot use the standalone Upload documents service if they cannot provide verification documents in the Manage income and assets service. They will need to provide the documents by post or in person at a service centre.

Help online

Customers can access information about this service by reading the help provided by the Digital Assistant.

The Resources page contains:

  • the payment eligibility for online accounts
  • a breakdown of the online items. It advises what customers can add, edit or delete, and if they need to provide verification documents to support their updates
  • Item and Type keywords

Organisation Nominees

Organisation nominees can only make updates for customers in receipt of the following:

  • ABSTUDY
  • Age Pension
  • Austudy
  • Carer Payment
  • Disability Support Pension
  • JobSeeker Payment
  • Partner Allowance
  • Parenting Payment
  • Widow Allowance
  • Youth Allowance

Asset threshold limits that apply to online updates + Read more ...

Online changes to asset values that exceed the limits below are not automatically applied. These updates will require manual processing.

Single customers

  • Decreased by -$500,000
  • Increased by +$500,000

Partnered customers

  • Decreased by -$500,000
  • Increased by +$500,000

Note: the threshold limits do not apply to customers whose payment (and their partner’s payment, if partnered) is a loan-only arrangement under the Home Equity Access Scheme. For example, they do not get a payable rate of a qualifying payment.

The Resources page includes examples of when updates made by the customer will or will not be automatically applied.

3

Updating income and assets online + Read more ...

When a customer updates their income and assets:

  • a maximum of 3 updates are allowed within a current reporting period
  • when they exceed this limit, the update is referred for manual action
  • the maximum update limit resets each fortnight

Updating income and assets online - partnered customers and permission to enquire (PPE)

Customer updates income and assets online and PPE = Y

When a customer is updating jointly owned income and assets and PPE is Yes:

  • the update will allow them to add or edit income and assets
  • if another user (staff or partner) has a lock on the record, updates to income and assets will not be applied

Customer updates income and assets online and PPE = N

When a customer is updating jointly owned or individually owned income and assets, PPE is No, and their partner is currently on income support:

  • the customer will only be allowed to view and update income and assets they fully or partly own
  • the partner will only be allowed to view and update income and assets they fully own or partly own

Common issues + Read more ...

The value of the asset seems low

  • The customer may have a loan or encumbrance against the asset
  • Advise the customer that the asset value is the net asset value. That is, when the security for the loan is against the same asset:
    • Asset value - Asset loan = Net asset value
  • Confirm the current market value and the value of any loan used for the purchase of the asset

The details shown are not correct

  • Customer has not updated their details
  • Centrelink has not updated the customer's details when advised
  • Enter customer's correct details in ISIS or Process Direct
  • Social security assessment requirements and results may differ from the customer's forecast

Sole traders, partnerships, private trusts and private companies + Read more ...

This category displays details of all self-employment:

  • Sole trader and partnerships can be added, edited or deleted
  • Private trusts, Private companies and foreign business details are view only

Sole trader and partnerships

Customers can view:

  • name of the business
  • financial year (currently being assessed)
  • business type
  • registered address
  • total income
  • net asset
  • net asset (partner)
  • their (and their partner's) ownership percentages

Customers can:

  • add a new business
  • update a business address
  • lodge the business income tax return
  • record when the business has ceased operating
  • record a significant change to business income or assets

All updates will create a work item that will allocate to Service Officers for manual action.

Note: Farm Household Allowance (FHA) customers can view details but cannot make changes online.

Trusts and companies

The following details display under this category for each private trust and private company record linked to the customer. Note: linked Special Disability Trusts will not display.

Customers can view these details but cannot make changes online.

If the amount in the field is zero, $0.00 displays. If blank, a field will not display unless their partner has the item recorded.

Customers can view:

  • name of the business
  • financial year (currently being assessed)
  • business type
  • attributed income
  • distribution income
  • total assessable income
  • primary/non-primary production asset
  • assessed primary/non-primary production asset
  • less home property adjustment amount

Foreign business

Customers can view the following details of their overseas business under the Sole traders, partnerships, private trusts and private companies category:

  • business name
  • country
  • income
  • assets

Customers can make updates to foreign business income by selecting the Other income category.

Customers who complete an update online do not need to provide a Mod F. The answers they provide will determine which verification documents are required. Process Direct displays all requested documents under the Task icon. To understand why a document is requested, refer to the Update income and assets online tables on the Resources page.

The Resources page contains a list of items customers can add, edit or delete, and if they need to provide a verification document to support their update.
