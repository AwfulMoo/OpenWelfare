This page contains information for Service Officers on how to identify and action multiple Centrelink Customer Reference Numbers (CRN).

Centrelink customer has multiple CRNs

This table describes the initial actions required when a Centrelink customer appears to have multiple CRNs.

Step

Action

1

Determining if the customer has a potential multiple CRN + Read more ...

Compare the CRNs to determine whether they belong to the same person or different people with similar details.

Screens to check include, but are not limited to:

  • Customer Person Details (CPDS)
  • Address History (ADH)
  • Telephone Summary (TDS)
  • Links Summary (LS)
  • Marital Status (MS)
  • Child Selection (CHS)
  • Child in Care (CHC)
  • Email Address (EMA)
  • Tax File Number Summary (TFS)

For immediate assistance with making a same person determination, contact the DQU Multiples Helpdesk. See Resources for a link to the helpdesk.

Note: for child (CHI) CRNs, the parent/guardian’s record may need to be accessed to assist with comparison.

Do the CRNs belong to the same person?

  • Yes, go to Step 2
  • Unsure. If unable to confirm that the records belong to the same person or different people, go to Step 2
  • No. If the details on CRNs clearly confirm records belong to different people, no further action is required. Procedure ends here

2

Is there an outstanding (not finalised) potential multiple CRNs Fast Note referral to DQU? + Read more ...

Check the Activity List (AL) screen to determine if there are any outstanding activities requiring action.

  • Yes, customer has an outstanding (not finalised) potential multiple CRN Referral , and an undetermined claim requiring assessment, go to Step 6
  • Yes, customer has an outstanding (not finalised) potential multiple CRN Referral, and an outstanding Change of Circumstance updates, go to Step 7
  • No, go to Step 3

3

Refer potential multiple CRNs to DQU + Read more ...

Does the potential multiple CRN relate to an Adult Record?

  • Yes, go to Step 4
  • No, go to Step 5

4

Multiple Adult CRN + Read more ...

Multiple Adult CRNs are referred to DQU using the Multiple CRN Referral Fast Note.in Customer First on either the Multiple or Primary CRN.

In Customer First:

  • Select the Fast Note workflow from the Documents and Correspondence section within Workspace.
  • Enter Multiple CRN Referral in the search field
  • Select the Multiple CRN referral – Customer option from the Template drop down menus
  • Complete any of the following information as required:
    • *The following CRNs are associated:
    • CRN: <enter associated CRN>
    • CRN: <enter associated CRN>
    • CRN: <enter associated CRN>
    • *Other relevant information:
    • <insert relevant information>
  • Select confirm to complete this workflow

Do not make any updates to the records until DQU has determined which record is to be used as the primary CRN.

When urgent assistance is required outside helpdesk hours, for example, the customer is experiencing financial hardship, the Multiple CRN Referral Fast Note has an ‘Urgent’ option available. These will be completed within one business day.

To assist DQU to make a determination, note any additional details in the ‘Other relevant information:’ section of the Fast Note.

All action to delete or link multiple records will be undertaken by DQU.

When the MLC link has been completed the multiple record will be locked and view only.

If the Multiple CRN has:

  • an undetermined claim requiring assessment, go to Step 6
  • any outstanding change of circumstance updates, go to Step 7

Otherwise, procedure ends here.

5

Multiple Child CRN + Read more ...

Multiple Child CRNs are referred to DQU using the Multiple CRN Referral Fast Note.in Customer First on the parent or guardian CRN.

In Customer First:

  • Select the Fast Note workflow from the Documents and Correspondence section within Workspace.
  • Enter Multiple CRN Referral in the search field
  • Select the Multiple CRN referral – Child option from the Template drop down menus
  • Complete any of the following information as required:
    • This Child has multiple CRNs
    • *The following CRNs are associated:
    • CRN: <enter associated CRN>
    • CRN: <enter associated CRN>
    • CRN: <enter associated CRN>
    • *Other relevant information:
    • <insert relevant information>
  • Select confirm to complete this workflow

Do not make any updates to the records until DQU has determined which record is to be used as the primary CRN.

When urgent assistance is required outside helpdesk hours, for example, the customer is experiencing financial hardship, the Multiple CRN Referral Fast Note has an ‘Urgent’ option available. These will be completed within one business day.

To assist DQU to make a determination, note any additional details in the ‘Other relevant information:’ section of the Fast Note.

All action to delete or link multiple records will be undertaken by DQU.

When the MLC link has been completed the multiple record will be locked and view only.

If the Multiple CRN has:

  • an undetermined claim requiring assessment, go to Step 6
  • any outstanding change of circumstance updates, go to Step 7

Otherwise, procedure ends here.

6

Potential multiple CRN Referral has an undetermined claim requiring assessment + Read more ...

Place the claim On Hold.

In Process Direct with the claim in context:

  • Select the Status Icon
  • Select the Edit Icon
  • Select On Hold from the Status drop down menu
  • Select Third Party to Provide Information from the On Hold Reason drop down menu
  • Enter On Hold Expiry Date of 14 days from date of referral
  • Save

In Customer Record:

  • Navigate to the Activity List (AL) screen
  • Either enter H or S next to the STArted claim activity and navigate to the Activity Details (AY) screen
  • Enter a Resubmit Date of 14 days from date of referral
  • In the Notes section - record information that the activity was placed on hold due to Potential Multiple CRN referral
  • Select Enter
  • Repeat process for Progress of claim DOC with a started status

In Customer First:

  • Navigate to the Activity List (AL) screen
  • Select the started claim activity
  • Select the (H) Hold / Resubmit for action on a future date from the Selection Options field
  • Select Continue
  • Enter a Resubmit Date of 14 days from date of referral
  • Select the Notes Twisty menu
  • Record information that the activity was placed on hold due to Potential Multiple CRN referral
  • Select Continue
  • Repeat process for Progress of claim DOC with a started status

For Disability Support Pension new claim activities:

In Customer Record:

  • Navigate to the AL screen
  • Open the Script Selector and run the Streaming & Progress DOC script
  • In the Claim on hold options select > Policy
  • In the Resubmit date field > Enter On Hold Expiry Date of 14 days from the date of referral
  • In the Free text field > Add text > Claim on hold pending outcome of Multiple CRN referral to DQU
  • Select Next, the script will then hold the NCL activity
  • Enter H in the Progress Doc activity from AL
  • Enter a Resubmit Date of 7 days from the date of referral
  • Select Enter to save the update

Are there any outstanding change of circumstance updates?

  • Yes, go to Step 7
  • No, procedure ends here

7

Potential multiple CRN Referral has outstanding Change of Circumstance updates + Read more ...

Change the change of circumstance update status to Held.

In Process Direct with the change of circumstance update in context:

  • Select the Status Icon
  • Select the Edit Icon
  • Select On Hold from the Status drop down menu
  • Select Third Party to Provide Information from the On Hold Reason drop down menu
  • Enter On Hold Expiry Date of 14 days from date of referral
  • Save

In Customer Record:

  • Navigate to the Activity List (AL) screen
  • Either enter H or S next to the STArted activity and navigate to the Activity Details (AY) screen
  • Enter a Resubmit Date of 14 days from date of referral
  • In the Notes section - record information that the activity was placed on hold due to Potential Multiple CRN referral
  • Select Enter
  • Repeat process for all change of circumstance updates with a started status

In Customer First:

  • Navigate to the Activity List (AL) screen
  • Select any outstanding change of circumstances activities with a started status
  • Select the (H) Hold / Resubmit for action on a future date from the Selection Options field
  • Select Continue
  • Enter a Resubmit Date of 14 days from date of referral
  • Select the Notes Twisty menu
  • Record information that the activity was placed on hold due to Potential Multiple CRN referral
  • Select Continue
  • Repeat process for all change of circumstance updates with a started status

Procedure ends here.
