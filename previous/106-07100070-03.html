For Service Officers in Service Centres and Smart Centre Processing sites only

This page contains the process of obtaining information from a customer regarding their income and assets and coding this information on the system.

On this page:

Identifying customers income and assets

Identifying other sources of income

Identifying customers income and assets

Table 1

Step

Action

1

Identify income and assets details required + Read more ...

The customer advises they have changes to their income and assets, including multiple changes, significant changes, or changes where they are unable to provide all of the required details.

Use the guidelines in small change to asset values to determine whether the customer is required to notify.

If they are required to notify:

  • Issue an Income and Assets Mod iA
  • Issue a Request for Information (RFI) letter to be provided within 14 days

Important: customers must be advised that they can provide copies of the supporting documentation to confirm details about their income and investments when they complete and lodge the Mod iA.

Go to Step 2.

2

Mod iA returned + Read more ...

Upon return of Mod iA, cancel the request for review on the Activity List (AL) screen and assess to see if any of the questions indicate further investigation or action regarding the customer's income and investments.

3

Bonds, debentures, money on loan or unsecured notes + Read more ...

Does the customer have any bonds, debentures, money on loan or unsecured notes?

  • Yes, for coding of these products, see Coding income and assets for Centrelink payments and services. These assets will have a deemed income. For information on this, see Deeming provisions. Go to Step 4
  • No, go to Step 4

4

Boarders, lodgers or tenants + Read more ...

Does the customer receive money from any boarders/lodgers/tenants?

  • Yes, see Verifying and recording other income on the Other Income (OIN) screen. See Income from boarders and lodgers for further information. Go to Step 5
  • No, go to Step 5

5

Department of Veterans' Affairs (DVA) pension + Read more ...

Does the customer get a pension paid by the DVA?

  • Yes, for more information on DVA payments, see Income from Department of Veterans' Affairs (DVA), go to Step 6
  • No, go to Step 6

6

Life Insurance policies with a surrender value + Read more ...

Does the customer have any life insurance policies with a surrender value?

  • Yes, and collecting this information verbally
    • if in Customer Record this asset needs to be coded as part of the Other Assets category in the Income/Assets Update and Reviews script. If outside this workflow, on the Other Assets (OAS) screen
    • If in Process Direct, this asset needs to be coded as part of the Other Assets (OASS/OAS), using the Task Selector (START) Income and Assets update workflow
    • See Coding income and assets for Centrelink payments and services, code 'LIF' in the Type: field. Go to Step 7
  • No, go to Step 7

7

Income protection policy + Read more ...

Does the customer have an income protection policy?

  • Yes, the customer may be entitled to an income from this policy due to illness, accident or injury that has caused them to cease work:
    • Ask the customer to bring in the paper work for the policy to assess the income from this policy. Issue an RFI letter giving the customer 14 days to return the information
    • If the customer does not bring the forms in on time, suspend payments (FRC)
    • When the details are returned, assess if the customer is entitled to a payment under the income test. See Income Test for single allowance customers. Cancel the RFI request on AL screen. Go to Step 8
  • No, go to Step 8

8

Gifted cash, assets, property or income + Read more ...

In the last 5 years has the customer gifted any cash, assets, property or income to anyone?

  • Yes, the customer may be affected by deprivation, see Assessing deprivation/gifting - see Step 1 in the Identifying other sources of income table
  • No, see Step 1 in the Identifying other sources of income table

Identifying other sources of income

Table 2

Step

Action

1

Foreign pensions/payments + Read more ...

Has the customer or partner ever paid social security contributions in another country or does the customer get a foreign pension, allowance or other payment from overseas?

  • Yes, this information will need to be followed up as the customer may have an entitlement to payments from a foreign country. Request this information via a Request for Information (RFI) letter giving the customer 14 days to provide. If the customer does not provide the information on time, reject the claim or suspend payments (FRC)
    • When information is provided, cancel the review from AL screen. To update/code foreign payments on the system, see Foreign income and assets. Go to Step 3
  • No, go to Step 2

2

Superannuation + Read more ...

Does the customer or their partner have any money invested in superannuation?

Note: for customers under Age Pension age, superannuation is exempt under the income and assets test and therefore, information relating to superannuation is not to be obtained or recorded.

  • Yes, for assessment see Assessing superannuation, go to Step 3
  • No, go to Step 3

3

Australian income stream + Read more ...

Does the customer receive income from an Australian income stream?

Note: income stream includes allocated income stream or account based pension, market-linked income stream or term allocated pension, lifetime, life expectancy, pooled lifetime income stream, term income stream and defined benefit income stream.

  • Yes, an income stream schedule is required for all types of income stream products including defined benefit income streams. If not provided with Mod iA, issue Details of Income Stream product (SA330) form. Request this information via a Request for Information (RFI) letter, to give the customer 14 days to provide the information. If the customer does not provide the information on time, reject the claim or suspend payments (FRC)
    • When the schedule is returned, cancel the review activity on the AL screen. The income stream will need to be coded on the system. See Income Streams. Go to Step 4
  • No, go to Step 4

4

Managed investments, shares and stocks + Read more ...

Does the customer have any managed investments, shares and stock on an Australian stock exchange?

  • Yes, see:
    • Managed investments - adding a new investment
    • Adding shares and securities
    • go to Step 5
  • No, go to Step 5

5

Shares in private company + Read more ...

Does the customer have shares in a private company?

  • Yes, see Assessing and coding the Private Company details from the MOD PC, go to Step 6
  • No, go to Step 6

6

Other income + Read more ...

Does the customer have any other income?

  • Yes, if income is from:
    • Sole Trading or Partnerships, see Assessing and coding the Business details for sole traders and partnerships Mod F
    • Private Trusts see Assessing and coding the Private Trust Mod PT
    • other Income, see Verifying and recording other income on the Other Income (OIN) screen, go to Step 7
  • No, go to Step 7

7

Recording details + Read more ...

Record all the details on a DOC.
