Changing course of study or educational institution for ABSTUDY students

Table 1

Step

Action

1

Student changing courses + Read more ...

If the student is starting a new course that will be undertaken simultaneously to their current course, see Coding new study details.

A student who receives ABSTUDY or ABSTUDY PES advises they are changing courses or they did not get into their first preference course and so will be studying a different course.

Is the student receiving JobSeeker Payment (JSP) or Youth Allowance (YA) and ABSTUDY Pensioner Education Supplement (PES) and either:

  • in the Disability Support Pension (DSP) transition group with a partial capacity to work, or
  • stopped receiving Parenting Payment Single (PPS) due to their youngest child turning 8 years of age?
    • Yes, they will no longer qualify for ABSTUDY PES on completion of their current course. Procedure ends here
    • No, go to Step 2

2

Approved educational institution + Read more ...

Check for course approval and obtain the institution reference number. See Approved courses of study for ABSTUDY, ABSTUDY Pensioner Education Supplement (PES) and Assistance for Isolated Children (AIC).

Is the new course an approved course at an approved educational institution or an  Indigenous special course?

  • Yes, go to Step 4
  • No, go to Step 3

3

The student will not be eligible for ABSTUDY or ABSTUDY PES + Read more ...

Further assistance will depend upon which ABSTUDY Award the customer is claiming.

The student will need to test their eligibility for other payments.

Cancel the student's payments by ending the current course on the Education Details (EDC) screen.

Procedure ends here.

4

Full-time study load + Read more ...

Will the student still be undertaking a full-time study load?

  • Yes, go to Step 6
  • No, go to Step 5

5

Concessional study load and Part-time Award + Read more ...

Will the student now be undertaking:

  • a concessional study load? (Note: most students on ABSTUDY PES qualify for concessional study load automatically)
  • part time study and qualify for the ABSTUDY Part-time Award?
    • Yes, see Changing study load from full-time to part-time or concessional study load for ABSTUDY or ABSTUDY Pensioner Education Supplement (PES)
    • No, the student will no longer be eligible for ABSTUDY. End the course of study on the Education Details (EDC) screen. Only end the Student End Date field. Do not end the Course End Date field, as this will cause the system to incorrectly calculate a debt for Incidentals Allowance. A student is eligible for the full Incidentals Allowance for their course of study, even if they leave the course early. For further information, see ABSTUDY Incidentals Allowance. The student will need to test their eligibility for other payments. See First Contact Concessions, Carers and Seniors (FCCCS)

Changing details of the course including Student Start or Student End date on the Education Course History (EDCH) screen can also affect entitlement for Relocation Scholarship (RS). See Scholarship study requirements for instructions on potential debts, and appropriate action for these cases.

Procedure ends here.

6

Level of new course + Read more ...

If the new course to be undertaken is:

  • tertiary or secondary non-schooling level course, see Step 1 in Table 3 table
  • secondary at school, go to Step 1 in Table 2 table

Changing course details for ABSTUDY secondary school students

Table 2

Step

Action

1

Secondary school student circumstances + Read more ...

If the student receives Schooling B Award and:

  • they are changing institutions, and the change to the new institution requires a transfer from ABSTUDY Schooling B Allowance to Schooling A (because the student is aged less than 16 years and is no longer qualified as a boarding student), go to Step 2
  • they discontinued studies at 1 school and then recommenced at the same school or a different school:
    • within 28 days, go to Step 4 in Table 3
    • with a gap greater than 28 days, go to Step 5 in Table 3
  • they started studies at the beginning of the year at a different school to the one currently recorded on the Education Details (EDC) screen, go to Step 9

If the student receives Schooling A Award, go to Step 3.

2

Schooling B transfer to Schooling A + Read more ...

Student receives Schooling B and a change of school requires a transfer to Schooling A.

The parent/guardian needs to provide:

  • name of new institution
  • year of study
  • start date, and
  • the address of the student

A new claim will not be needed for the change in study circumstances. The applicant can notify verbally.

Assess their entitlement for ABSTUDY Schooling A.

For staff who are not trained to make the updates:

  • send an ABSTUDY action request Fast Note to the ABSTUDY Smart Centre Processing team, ensuring all relevant details listed above are included
  • select Auto Text > Students, ABSTUDY & Apprentices > Update > ABSTUDY action request > Confirm

Update the changes to transfer the student from Schooling B to Schooling A award:

  • end the course of study
  • add a new Education Course Details (EDC) screen to record the new school details
  • end the student's entitlement to the Away from Home rate on the Away From Home Details (NIH) screen, see Cessation of the ABSTUDY away from home rate
  • end the boarding details on the School Fees and Boarding Details (EYSB) and Accommodation Details (AC) screen

Note: if a student leaves a boarding school prior to the end of term, the school/hostel is only eligible to ABSTUDY payments up until the last date of attendance (LDA). See Cessation of the ABSTUDY away from home rate before transferring the student to the Schooling A Award.

Once it has been determined what date the Schooling A Award is to be granted from, re-index the student for Schooling A entitlements. See Assessing the Claim for ABSTUDY Schooling A.

Procedure ends here.

3

Away from home to study + Read more ...

Is the Schooling A Award student changing to a school in which they will now be living away from home to study?

  • Yes, go to Step 4
  • No, go to Step 6

4

Change of school and living away from home + Read more ...

Student who receives ABSTUDY Schooling A Award advises of a change of school and will now be living away from home.

The student may be eligible for living away from home allowances under the Schooling B Award. They will need to be assessed as to whether they meet the requirements for the additional allowances. Students already assessed for Schooling A Award are not required to lodge a new ABSTUDY claim. Information for the reassessment can be obtained verbally, or by issuing the ABSTUDY Additional Information (SY023) form. Note: a full claim form will be accepted if submitted).

In all cases where a student under 16 years of age has had a change in parent/guardian, a new ABSTUDY claim is required to be lodged by the new applicant, see Claiming ABSTUDY.

Additional supporting information may be required includes:

  • documentation for assessment and coding of the living away from home allowances
  • additional information for Parental Means Test

See Away from home rate for ABSTUDY.

5

ABSTUDY Schooling B Award and assessment of living away from home rate + Read more ...

Student has applied for ABSTUDY living away from home allowances under the Schooling B Award.

The ABSTUDY Schooling A should remain current until the student's eligibility to living away from home allowances is determined.

See Away from home rate for ABSTUDY.

Therefore, once it has been determined that the student:

  • does not meet the requirements for Schooling B Award and away from home allowances, go to Step 6
  • meets the requirements for Schooling B Award and away from home allowances, go to Step 8

6

Change of school and still living at home or does not meet away from home provisions + Read more ...

Students who choose to live away from home to study but do not meet the requirements for grant of living away from home allowances under ABSTUDY can still remain eligible for 'at home' allowances, for example, Schooling A Award. The applicant for the Schooling A Award must still remain as the person they 'normal reside with', for example, parent.

Has the parent/guardian changed for the Schooling A Award?

  • Yes, go to Step 7
  • No, ABSTUDY Schooling A may need action to be taken before EDC screen updates. If the School Fee Allowance has been issued to a school at which the student did not start studies, this will need to be returned to the agency. See ABSTUDY School Fees Allowance (SFA) at home rate (Group 1)

To key the new school on the EDC screen for ABSTUDY Schooling A student that has started a new school year at a different school to the one currently recorded on the EDC screen:

  • 'S'elect the current course with the old education details on the Education Course History (EDCH) screen
  • change the course details
  • in the Action field, key 'C' for correct

To key a new school on the EDC screen for an ABSTUDY Schooling A student that has changed schools after the first day in Term 1 in the relevant year:

  • end the course of study
  • add a new Education Course Details (EDC) screen to record the new school details
  • select the Supplementary Education Payments (EPSP) screen and check that the new school details are showing. See ABSTUDY School Fees Allowance (SFA) at home rate (Group 1)
  • update the Payment Instructions (PIPI) screen to ensure that any future School Fees Allowance payments are sent to the correct school.
  • record details on a DOC
  • send manual attendance check to the new school where the SAS extraction date has passed for One Time Access Codes (OTAC). The Manual Attendance request and SAS extraction dates for each term can be found in the Resources tab of Processing ABSTUDY School Term Allowance payments

Note: if School Fees Allowance have already been paid for the year and the student commenced at the previous school, no further School Fees Allowance can be issued to cover any fees at the new school. See ABSTUDY School Fees Allowance (SFA) at home rate (Group 1).

Procedure ends here.

7

Parent or guardian has changed for Schooling A Award + Read more ...

When the parent/guardian changes for Schooling A Award, the new parent/guardian must lodge a new claim to be assessed for eligibility.

The ABSTUDY record must be cancelled from the date they left the previous parent's/guardian's care:

  • go to the Benefit Action (BA) screen
  • cancel the ABT reason 'ANQ' (Applicant Not Qualified) from the date they left their care
  • record details on a DOC

The STA link to the previous parent/guardian's record may need to be end dated.

The Schooling A Award will then be assessed under the new parent/guardian once they submit a new claim.

Procedure ends here.

8

Cancelling Schooling A Award + Read more ...

Student who receives ABSTUDY Schooling A Award advises of a change of school and will be living away from home and meet the requirements for Schooling B Award.

The Schooling A record (ABT) will need to be cancelled before processing the ABSTUDY Schooling B claim.

If the change is in the current calendar year:

  • go to the Education Course History (EDCH) screen and select the current course
  • key the date that the student last attended studies at the local school in the Student End Date field. Press [Enter]
  • navigate to the Assessment Results (AR) screen and ensure the ABT record has cancelled. Finalise activity
  • record details on a DOC

If the change is in the next calendar year and the Education Course History (EDCH) screen is keyed with the school data for ABSTUDY Schooling A Award:

  • go to the Supplementary Education Payments (EPSP) screen and delete the associated SFA Group 1 for the next calendar year. In the Action field, key 'D' for delete. See ABSTUDY School Fees Allowance (SFA) at home rate (Group 1)
  • 'S'elect the course with the next calendar years incorrect education details on the EDCH screen
  • in the Action field, key 'D' for delete
  • navigate to the Assessment Results (AR) screen and ensure the ABT record has cancelled. Finalise activity
  • record details on a DOC

See Coding away from home rate for ABSTUDY.

Note: if student is granted to board at a boarding school or hostel, the desktop fax for granted boarders must be sent.

Procedure ends here.

9

Student starts studies at a different school + Read more ...

A student receiving ABSTUDY Schooling B has started the new school year at a different school to the one currently recorded on the EDC screen.

To key new school on the EDC screen:

  • 'S'elect the current course with the old course details on the Education Course History (EDCH) screen
  • change the course details
  • in the Action field, key 'C' for correct

If a student is:

  • living away from home, see Cessation of the ABSTUDY away from home rate and Coding away from home rate for ABSTUDY if they continue living away from home
  • granted to board at a boarding school or hostel, the desktop fax for granted boarders must be sent

Record the reason for the change on a DOC and finalise the activity on the Assessment Results (AR) screen.

Proof of enrolment is not required for secondary school students.

Assessing maximum reasonable time/limits of assistance

Table 3

Step

Action

1

Maximum reasonable time/limits of assistance + Read more ...

To calculate reasonable time, see Determining allowable time/reasonable time for a course of study.

To calculate the limit of assistance for a particular level of study, see Determining progress and duration of assistance for ABSTUDY

Has the student reached their maximum reasonable time or limits of assistance for the course of study they are changing to?

  • Yes, go to Step 2
  • No, go to Step 3

2

Student has reached their maximum reasonable time limit or limits of assistance + Read more ...

If the student has reached or exceeded their maximum reasonable time or limits of assistance for their current level of study, they will no longer be eligible for Services Australia administered payments for students:

  • identify if the student is eligible for other income support
  • ensure the student's payment cancels appropriately. See Cancellation of ABSTUDY payments
  • the student should be told about other study possibilities that may qualify for a student payment. This includes:
    • undertaking study at a different course level or
    • accessing ABSTUDY Allowances and Part-time Award

Note: changing details of the course including Student Start or Student End date on the EDCH screen can also affect entitlement for Relocation Scholarship (RS). See Scholarship study requirements for instructions on potential debts, and appropriate action for these cases.

Procedure ends here.

3

Student has not reached their maximum reasonable time limit or limit of assistance + Read more ...

Was the student receiving ABSTUDY Living Allowance, has ceased their previous course, and is intending or commenced another course within 28 days?

Note: do not include the vacation break period when determining the 28 day period. There is no entitlement to Living Allowance over the long vacation period where the approved course ended before 15 September and/or where the approved course immediately after the vacation period starts after 31 March.

  • Yes, go to Step 4
  • No, end the course of study. Go to Step 5

4

ABSTUDY student commencing new course within 28 days + Read more ...

Students who discontinue and then subsequently starts full-time or concessional study-load study in either the same course or a different course, remain entitled to Living Allowance if the period between is no greater than 28 days.

To ensure that there is no gap in ABSTUDY entitlement, key the following on the EDCH screen:

  • 'S'elect the previous course on the Education Course Details (EDC) screen with the old course details
  • change the date in the Student End Date field and the Allowable Time End date field to 1 day before the student start date of the new course (so there is no gap in payments)
  • in the Action field, key 'C' for correct

Coding for Secondary Boarding students

Different coding is required where a secondary boarding student discontinued studies at 1 school and recommences at the same school or a different school within 28 days.

Scenario 1: Confirmation of the Last Day of Attendance (LDA) at previous school and First Day of Attendance (FDA) at new school is provided. Key the following:

  • select the current EDC and update the Student End Date (SED) with day before the student commenced at the new school
  • in the Action field, key 'C' for correct and enter
  • navigate to EDCH screen, type EDC in NXT field and press [Enter] to generate a new EDC page
  • key the Participation Start Date (PSD) and Student Start Date (SSD) as the Actual date the student commenced at the new school and key all other relevant fields
  • navigate to the EYSB screen, update the Date of Event with the day after the student actually ceased at the previous school, remove all boarding /tuition amounts and key NPB
  • in the Action field, key 'I' for insert and [Enter]
  • key a new EYSB with the Actual date the student commenced at the new school and key all relevant fields
  • in the Action field, key 'I' for insert and press [Enter], manually navigate to the AR screen, do not complete. This action, will prompt a system reassessment, which will generate adjustments to display on the Payment Instructions (PIPI) screen. The PIPI screen will now display adjusted (ARO) and an (OTP) payment lines
  • update to ACS screen to match new boarding details
  • manually navigate to the PIPI screen from the AR screen
  • allocate the future OTP PIPI payments to the new school

Note: depending on when the change occurs within the term, determine if any arrears payment is owed to the previous payee. Outcomes could result in an overpayment for the previous payee and/or arrears for the previous school /new school /student.

Scenario 2: Confirmation of the LDA at the previous school has only been provided (and FDA at new school is not provided) updates will result in ABSTUDY being CAN SCS. Key the following:

  • select the current EDC and update the SED of the Actual date the student ceased studies
  • navigate to the EYSB screen and update the date of event with the day after the student actually ceased studies at the previous school, remove all boarding/ tuition amounts and key 'NBP'
  • in the Action field, key 'I' for insert and press [Enter]
  • complete the activity via AR, resulting in ABSTUDY CANcel Ceased Studies (SCS)

Scenario 3: The LDA is already keyed and FDA is provided for a new school or recommences at old school within 28 days. Key the following:

  • select the previous EDC, correct the SED to the day prior to the new student start date of the new school prior to coding a new EDC
  • key 'EDC' in NXT and press [Enter] to generate a new EDC page to record the new school details key:
    • participation Status Event date as the day after the previous student end date
    • student start date as the actual date the student commenced
  • select the EYSB and overtype the DOV as the actual student start date of the new school. Complete all relevant fields
  • update to ACS screen to match new boarding details
  • restore the ABSTUDY via BA screen
  • manually navigate to:
    • AR to ensure the system completes the reassessment
    • the PIPI screen to ensure that all payments are being directed to the correct payee, update to new payee if required
  • finalise the activity via AR and check that all payments are correctly calculated and are directed to the correct payee

For Schooling A Secondary students changing schools, update the PIPI screen to ensure that any future School Fees Allowance payments are sent to the correct school.

For Schooling B Secondary students living away from home, see Cessation of the ABSTUDY away from home rate and Coding away from home rate for ABSTUDY if the student is to continue living away from home.

Note: if student is granted to board at a boarding school or hostel, the desktop fax for granted boarders must be sent.

Record details in a DOC.

Procedure ends here.

5

Adding the new course for a student in receipt of ABSTUDY or ABSTUDY PES + Read more ...

ABSTUDY students who stopped studies and then re-claimed ABSTUDY with a gap of more than 28 days may need to re-claim ABSTUDY depending on the length of the gap. See the Resources page in Claiming ABSTUDY to determine if a new claim is needed.

To record new study details, see Step 2 in the Coding education course details table in the Coding new study details procedure.

If a secondary school student:

  • stops living away from home, see Cessation of the ABSTUDY away from home rate
  • continues living away from home, see Coding away from home rate for ABSTUDY

If a student is granted to board at a boarding school or hostel, the desktop fax for granted boarders must be sent.

Record the reason for the decision and action on a DOC and finalise the activity on the Assessment Results (AR) screen.

Note: students who stop study in 1 course and then start a new course, only have an entitlement for another Incidentals Allowance for the new course in the current academic year if the student did not receive a full year Incidentals Allowance for the previous course, and the new Course End Date is later than the previous course's Course End Date.

In addition, if the new Course End Date is before the previous Course End Date, the system may calculate a debt for a portion of the Incidentals Allowance (the amount of Incidentals Allowance entitlement is based upon the total of the period that the student undertook the first course plus the length of the new course). See ABSTUDY Incidentals Allowance.

Advise the student if Proof of enrolment is required.

Was the student in receipt of the ABSTUDY Living Away from Home Allowance to attend the previous education institution?

  • Yes, reassess the student's entitlement to the Living Away from Home Allowance to attend the new education institution plus changes may need to occur to their address, boarding fee, etc. See:
    • Away from home rate for ABSTUDY and
    • Assessing continuity of study for ABSTUDY away from home rate. Procedure ends here
  • No, record details of changes to study detail on a DOC

Procedure ends here.
