Tax features within the Centrelink system 103-08020000

Expand all


This following provides information about the tax features within the Centrelink system. The main facility supplied by this system is to produce Payment Summaries (previously known as Group Certificates).

Tax features

The main tax features of the Centrelink system includes the automatic calculation and creation of customer Payment Summaries and the electronic transmission of payment summary and amended payment summary details to the Australian Taxation Office (ATO). It is also possible to:

  • view a customer’s payment summary
  • issue or Reissue a printed copy of a customer’s payment summaries
  • make manual adjustments to a customer’s payment summary record.

Note: customers can access a printed copy of their Payment Summary through a range of self service options or by contacting Services Australia. See Centrelink Payment Summaries

The system also includes a facility to:

  • automatically transfer Centrelink payment summary data to the ATO
  • automatically transfer Centrelink payment summary data to the Child Support Programme (CSP) for mutual customers
  • automatically adjust payment summary details due to debt processing, benefit transfers and returned/rejected payments that affect a previous financial year period(s)
  • retrieve payment summary details for the tax year-to-date facility
  • allow tax detail adjustments for Bereavement Payments in the current financial year period, and
  • recovery of tax instalments.

The Resources page contains details of the tax features within the Centrelink system.
