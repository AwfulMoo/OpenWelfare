This page contains a summary of the process for amending a child support
assessment retrospectively when new income information is obtained or
determined in circumstances where the customer was prevented from
providing necessary income information at the time a child support
assessment is being made, or did not seek an amended tax assessment in a
prompt way in certain circumstances.
