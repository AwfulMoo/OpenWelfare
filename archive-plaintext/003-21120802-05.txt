DRA and NZ DRA average weekly income cut-off points

Based on the Rate Calculator Determination 2020.

Average weekly income is the Average Weekly Ordinary Time Earnings
(AWOTE) for a full-time adult in Australia according to the Australian
Bureau of Statistics.

AWOTE (average weekly income)

Table 2: Customers are initially eligible for DRA or NZ DRA if their
disaster affected income is less than the fortnightly rate of the AWOTE
(average weekly income).

Map of affected areas

To assess a customer’s LGA:

Open the NSW Live Feed App

Place the address given by the customer in the search box

Select the correct address that fills in the drop down

In the Layers menu on the right:

-   select Declared LGAs - DRA - Floods (Nov 2021)

If the address given by the customer is:

-   in a declared LGA
    - it will be shaded in green
    - the LGA name will show in the middle of the LGA. Note: staff may
    need to zoom out until the LGA name shows
-   not in a declared LGA, to identify the LGA name, scroll down the
    Layers menu on the right and select LGA 2020

Continue to assess the claim:

-   Eligibility for DRA and NZ DRA claims
