Links on this page may be unavailable to external audiences.

Transmittal process

Transmittal describes the process used by Child Support when requesting
overseas jurisdictions to recognise and enforce child and spousal
maintenance liabilities.

This document describes the processes actions in preparing a case for
transmittal:

-   prepare case for transmittal
-   translate transmittal package
-   copy package
-   process transmittal
-   send transmittal package to Central Authority
-   notify payee
-   storage/filing

Note: this document does not include the process to follow when
requesting New Zealand to recognise and enforce child and spousal
maintenance liabilities.
