For National Business Gateway staff only

This document outlines a listing of system codes used in Rent Deduction
Scheme (RDS) transactions.

Codes and values

The listing contains the following types of codes:

-   direct deduction transaction types
-   output transaction values
-   other types of codes

Codes and values for the RDS can be found in the RDS technical
specifications. Housing Authorities (HA) are provided with user
specifications relevant to their service.

Service Officers that receive technical enquiries should direct the HA
to the National Business Gateway Conformation Services helpdesk for
assistance.

The Resources page contains a link to the National Business Gateway
Confirmation Services helpdesk.
