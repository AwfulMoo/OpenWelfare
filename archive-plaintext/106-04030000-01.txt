This document outlines procedures to assist in assessing how the date a
claim for ABSTUDY or Assistance for Isolated Children (AIC) Scheme is
lodged will affect the payment start date.

Claim lodgement

Certain student and Australian Apprentice claims have differing rules
regarding claim lodgement than other Services Australia administered
payments. These are ABSTUDY, AIC, and ABSTUDY or AIC Pensioner Education
Supplement (PES). They are not covered by the 'deemed claim' provisions
of the Social Security (Administration) Act 1999, and each has different
rules regarding when the form needs to be lodged to claim payment.
