Derived fees example 1

In example 1, the patient billed was 1 of 5 patients seen at the
hospital. The schedule fee is calculated by:

-   adding the base fee of $8.50 plus
-   the fee for item 58 calculated by dividing the amount of $15.50 by 5
    patients = $3.10
-   total schedule fee = $8.50 + $3.10 = $11.60

Derived fees example 2

In example 2, the patient was given 4 fields of superficial
radiotherapy. The schedule fee is calculated by:

-   adding the fee for item 15000 (1 field) of $43.25 plus
-   the fee for item 15003 calculated at 3 additional fields, i.e., 3 x
    $17.35 = $52.05
-   total schedule fee = $43.25 + $52.05 = $95.30

Derived fees example 3

In example 3, the patient's injury related to a foreign body in the
shoulder. The schedule fee is determined by:

-   adding the fee for item 57703 (shoulder x-ray) of $54.00 plus
-   the amount for the report as stated in item 59103 of $21.30
-   total schedule fee = $54.00 + $21.30 = $75.30
