
Parenting, family assistance and Parental Leave Pay (PPL) reviews 007-13000000

-   Background

[Expand/Collapse all sections]Expand all

This document outlines information on Parenting, family assistance and
Parental Leave Pay (PPL) payment and benefit reviews that may be set up
on a customer's record, either automatically or manually, depending on
the circumstances.

Government intent

Services Australia conducts reviews for a variety of reasons. Reviews
are necessary to ensure that customers are receiving the correct
entitlement and payment amount based on their current circumstances.

Types of reviews

It includes:

-   child reviews
-   child support reviews
-   Alignment of Care processes for data exchanged between Centrelink
    and Child Support
-   reviews for Family Tax Benefit (FTB) and Child Care Subsidy (CCS)
-   recording estimate details of a New Financial Year Assessment (NFYA)
    for FTB and CCS
-   Mutual Customer Identification (MCI) process between Services
    Australia and the Australian Tax Office (ATO) to ensure customers
    receive the correct entitlement of FTB and CCS
-   mutual customer matching process between Child Support and
    Centrelink
-   an employer assisted dispute resolution process for PPL
