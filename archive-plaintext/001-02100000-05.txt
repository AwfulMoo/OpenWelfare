Services Australia links

The Department of Employment and Workplace Relations (DEWR) Workforce
Australia helpline will assist with general Self-Employment Assistance
enquiries and advise the job seeker of any local Self-Employment
Assistance providers near them. This phone number is located in
Department of Employment and Workplace Relations.

Self-Employment Assistance Helpdesk: where conflicting information about
Self-Employment Assistance participation or Allowance is received,
contact the Participation Programmes Team.

Job seekers wishing to participate in Self-Employment Assistance are to
be referred by Workforce Australia, Disability Employment Services or
ParentsNext providers. Services Australia staff are not to refer
customers directly to a Self-Employment Assistance provider.
