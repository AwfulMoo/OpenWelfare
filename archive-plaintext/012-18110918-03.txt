This page contains the processes by which eligible medical
practitioner’s additional training in non-directive pregnancy support
counselling (PSC) is registered in the Provider Directory System (PDS),
after the associated request has been opened through the current
workflow arrangements.

On this page:

Assessing a request to recognise additional training in non-directive
PSC

Processing a medical practitioner’s additional training in non-directive
PSC – non vocationally registered - notification from RACGP (662)

Processing a medical practitioner’s additional training in non-directive
PSC –vocationally registered - notification from RACGP (647)

Processing a medical practitioner's additional training in non-directive
PSC - non vocationally registered - notification from ACRRM (663)
