Family Tax Benefit (FTB) Part A is limited to the base rate for a child
if the customer or their partner is entitled to apply for maintenance
for the child but does not take reasonable action. See A New Tax System
(Family Assistance) Act 1999, schedule 1, clause 10, Effect of
maintenance rights.
