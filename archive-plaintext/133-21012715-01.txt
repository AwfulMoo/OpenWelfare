This document outlines information for staff to help customers update
their relationship status from partnered to single using their
Centrelink online account.

Update relationship details service

Eligible customers can use the Update relationship details service
through their Centrelink online account to advise of a separation from
their partner.

Eligible customers

Customers are eligible to use the [A self service option is available
for customers] Update relationship details service if they are currently
on:

-   JobSeeker Payment (JSP)
-   Youth Allowance (YA)
-   Age Pension (AGE)
-   Carer Payment (CP)
-   Austudy, or
-   Disability Support Pension (DSP)

The customer must meet the following:

-   has a Centrelink online account linked to myGov
-   does not have a nominee arrangement
-   is not income managed (unless in an income management exemption
    period)
-   does not receive payments via a group payment schedule
-   does not receive payments to an international payment
