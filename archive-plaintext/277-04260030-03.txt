This page contains information to support decision making about
processing a refund to a customer or third party and preparing a
submission to create a consolidated revenue debt in situations when a
customer has received funds they were not entitled to (for example,
mistaken disbursements). It also describes the process involved in
finalising a consolidated revenue debt and consideration of waiver
provisions.
