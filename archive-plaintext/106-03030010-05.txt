Drought Concessional Loans Scheme

The Drought Concessional Loans Scheme closed for applications on 30 June
2018. The scheme delivered three types of loans – Drought Assistance,
Dairy Recovery and Business Improvement Concessional Loans. Loans were
delivered by state delivery agencies on behalf of the Commonwealth.

Commonwealth Farm Business Concessional Loans

From 1 July 2018, farmers may apply for to the Regional Investment
Corporation (RIC) for the Commonwealth’s farm business concessional
loans. They offer a number of different loan types including:

-   Drought Loan
-   AgBiz Drought Loan
-   Farm Investment Loan
-   Water Infrastructure Loan and
-   AgriSTarter loan

For more information about the Commonwealth’s farm business concessional
loans and eligibility requirements, please contact the RIC on 1800 875
675 or view their website Regional Investment Corporation.
