Delegations and authorisations

Service Manager, Intensive Collection Services

-   issues DPO, issues a Departure Authorisation Certificate (DAC) and
    revokes DPO where child support debt/overpayment (not including
    penalties) is under $50,000

National Manager, Intensive Collection Services

-   issues DPO, issues a DAC and revokes DPO where child support
    debt/overpayment (not including penalties) is $50,000 and above

DPO Contact Officer

-   issues DAC out of office hours where child support debt/overpayment
    is under $50,000
