This page contains the process between Part A (Healthcare Provider
Identifier - Organisation (HPI-O)) and B (My Health Record) registration
when a combined Application to Register a Seed Organisation is received
(HW018).

Combined application processing

Processing a combined Application to register a Seed Organisation

Organisation is processed and made ready for Service Officers to
complete the My Health Record registration (Part B).
