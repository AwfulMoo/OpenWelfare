This document outlines information about linking or unlinking a
Contracted Service Provider (CSP) Officer record to a CSP organisation
in the Healthcare Identifiers (HI) Service.

Link or unlink request

A CSP Officer can request to add or remove a link between themselves and
the CSP organisation they represent.

To link or unlink a CSP Officer from a CSP organisation, the officer
must provide the following to a Service Officer:

-   a completed 'Application to add, replace or remove a Contracted
    Service Provider officer' form
-   documentary evidence that the officer has authority to act on behalf
    of the organisation when registering with the HI Service

The request to remove an officer from an organisation can also be made
by an individual who has provided sufficient evidence to prove that they
will become the new CSP Officer of the CSP organisation.

A CSP organisation must have at least one CSP Officer and up to a
maximum of 3 linked at any one time.

CSP Officers can be linked to multiple CSP organisations.

For every transaction request in the eHealth Program (EHP application
via a Service Officer, details of the person making the request must be
entered. In addition to the Service Officer ID details, the details of
the requestor are also recorded.
