This document outlines how Service Officers can view a history of
changes and access to a Provider Digital Access (PRODA) record.

Account history

After searching for the correct PRODA account:

Go to the Manage Account screen

On bottom left side of screen, select the Account History button

The Account History screen displays the following information:

-   date the transaction occurred
-   the username that performed the transaction
-   the function that was performed on the record
-   the comments relating to the transaction

The account history includes information about the evidence of identity
and change of name verification processes.
