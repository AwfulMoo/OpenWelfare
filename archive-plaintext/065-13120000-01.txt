This document outlines information about residential care reports
contained in the Aged Care Staff Portal (ACSP).

Historical SPARC process

The System for the Payment of Aged Residential Care (SPARC) is the
legacy aged care payment system for residential care. SPARC is a view
only system from 20 August 2022.

To view the legacy SPARC process, see the historical version.

AN-ACC/ACFI report

Lists care recipients who have

-   a changed Aged Care Funding Instrument (ACFI) score
-   entered permanent residential care during the report range dates,
    even if they have an ACFI classification or not

Provides additional information for Service Officers to check
eligibility for the Continence Aids Payment Scheme (CAPS)

Can be run on an ad hoc basis and applies to residential care recipients
only

Annual and Lifetime Cap report

-   Displays a list of care recipients showing their Annual and/or
    Lifetime Cap accruals, and any refund amounts that have been paid to
    care recipients who have reached either cap
-   Displays an aggregate summary of all care recipients who have, or
    require, a refund along with total amount shown
-   Is generated as required and applies to residential care recipients
    only

Non-advances report

Displays a list of all

-   instances where a residential care service’s advance payment was
    stopped or held, and
-   unverified manual adjustments

Provides information for Service Officers to investigate the non-payment
of advances, including the reason of the non-payment

Is generated on a monthly cycle and applies to residential care services
only

Quarterly Review Dry run report

-   Displays the results of Quarterly Review dry run calculations
-   Allows the Aged Care Programme Branch (ACBP) and Smart Centres (SC)
    to undertake a quality check to ensure the rates (changed fee
    amounts) have been applied as expected, and to ensure refunds have
    been calculated correctly
-   Can be generated multiple times for a specified period and applies
    to residential care services only

Quarterly Review report

-   Displays the results of Quarterly Review production run
-   Allows the Aged Care Programme Branch (ACBP) and Smart Centres (SC)
    a summary view of updated care recipients’ fees and/or refunds
    calculated as a result of the quarterly review
-   Is generated on a quarterly basis and applies to residential care
    services only

Viability Supplement report

-   Displays the viability supplement details for a residential care
    service
-   Allows Service Officers to view the viability supplement details at
    a daily level or for a claim month
-   Can be run on an ad-hoc basis and provides information for Service
    Officers to investigate and resolve queries about viability
    supplements
-   Applies to residential care services only

Accessing reports through the Aged Care Staff Portal (ACSP)

Service/Programme Officers:

can access residential aged care reports in the ACSP, see the Process
page for how to do this

are redirected to a Tableau display:

-   this populates/displays the selected report, and allows for various
    customisation and filtering options

must have:

-   the correct Essentials business role, and
-   the Tableau Viewer Access resource to access reports
-   see the Resources page for the User permission roles required to
    access reports

The Resources page contains a list of the User permission roles required
to access reports and a link to contact details for Aged Care.
