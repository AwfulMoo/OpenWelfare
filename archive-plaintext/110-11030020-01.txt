For Intelligence and Investigations Branch only

This document outlines how to request information for Intelligence and
Investigations Branch criminal investigations and administrative
investigations, and how to record details on the case management system.

Written requests required

The Australian Government Investigations Standards (AGIS) requires
agencies to have written procedures for liaison with other agencies and
for ensuring this liaison is appropriately recorded.

Agencies include but are not limited to:

-   Australia Post
-   Australian Electoral Commission (AEC)
-   Australian Securities and Investment Commission (ASIC)
-   Australian Taxation Office (ATO)
-   Australian Transaction Reports and Analysis Centre (AUSTRAC)
-   Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade (DFAT)
-   Department of Home Affairs
-   Department of Education, Skills and Employment
-   Department of Health and Aged Care

Request for information via email

The request for information can be made via email if the agency is
affiliated with GovLink or PGP Secure Email. The email should be
categorised as ‘Official’ or as per the security level indicated on the
GovLink website.
