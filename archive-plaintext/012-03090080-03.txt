Identify and flag possible duplicate EHP records

A duplicate record is where two or more identifiers have been created
for the same individual.

Only Tier 3 Service Officers can complete this procedure.

Resolve possible duplicate EHP records

Where at least one of the records identified as a possible duplicate is
registered by a Data Source, Tier 3 staff complete the resolution
process. Tier 3 staff have the relevant access to edit these records.

Only Tier 3 Service Officers can complete this procedure.

Remove duplicate flag from EHP records (Tier 3)

eHealth Program (EHP) records have been previously flagged as potential
duplicates by a data source (for example Australian Health Practitioner
Regulation Agency (Ahpra)), automatic matching report or other Tier 3
staff. Only Tier 3 staff have the relevant access to edit these records.
