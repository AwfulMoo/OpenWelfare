This page contains a description of the circumstances in which nurse
practitioners, midwives and medical practitioners can use Pharmaceutical
Benefits Schedule (PBS) prescription pads to prescribe PBS medicines,
links to the Services Australia nurse practitioner and midwives page,
the Department of Health and Aged Care, the Nursing and Midwifery Board
of Australia and Australian Health Practitioner Regulation Agency
(Ahpra) Register of Practitioners.

On this Page:

Pharmaceutical Benefits Schedule (PBS) prescription pads

Working for hospital means working in a public capacity within the
hospital - hospital prescription forms must be used.

Outreach means working at a public hospital location but conducts
outreach for State Health Department.
