This document outlines information available to help customers who
contact about claiming a payment or service from Services Australia due
to being self-employed or a farmer.

Making a claim

Appropriate payments and services should be identified for customers to
claim based on their circumstances.

Customers should be invited to lodge claims for payments even if there
is doubt about their eligibility to that payment.
