Policy

The Use of data matching in Commonwealth Administration Guidelines are
issued by the Information Commissioner under section 27(1) (e) of the
Privacy Act. Although the guidelines are not legally binding, the
Information Commissioner has sought agreement from agencies to adopt the
guidelines in relation to the data matching programs in which they
participate. Centrelink has voluntarily agreed to adopt these
requirements.
