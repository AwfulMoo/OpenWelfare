This document explains the Agreement including the process of making a
claim and coding for Australian payment under the agreement of Croatian
payments.

Start date

The Agreement with Croatia started on 1 July 2004.

Claiming and benefits covered

The Agreement generally allows customers to lodge a claim for payment
from either country. It also allows customers to add together periods of
residence in Australia and periods of social security coverage in
Croatia, to meet the minimum requirements for payment.

Australian payments covered:

-   Age Pension
-   Disability Support Pension (DSP) for the severely disabled

Customers can claim Australian payments under the Agreement by
contacting Services Australia or the Croatian authorities.

Croatian payments covered:

-   Old Age Pension
-   Disability Pension
-   Survivor Pension

Customers can claim a foreign pension by contacting Services Australia
or the Croatian authorities.
