This document outlines information about the PIP After Hours Incentive.

Aim of the Incentive

The PIP After Hours Incentive supports general practices to give
patients suitable access to after-hours care.

Apply for and update details for the After Hours Incentive

A practice can apply and/or update their details for the After Hours
Incentive using:

-   Health Professional Online Services (HPOS), or
-   The Practice Incentives Program After Hours Incentive application
    (IP030) form

Withdraw from the After Hours Incentive

A practice can withdraw from the After Hours Incentive via:

-   Health Professional Online Services (HPOS), or
-   Submitting a written correspondence

The correspondence must include the:

-   Practice ID
-   Practice name
-   Full practice address
-   Consent to withdraw from the After Hours Incentive, and
-   Signature by at least one registered owner or authorised contact
    person

A practice can reapply at any time.

The Resources page contains information about eligibility for the 5
payment levels of the Incentive.
