This document outlines the process involved for transmittal of Child
Support information (excluding NZ) - transmittal referrals and decisions
to transmit.

Transmittal process

Transmittal describes the process used by Child Support when requesting
overseas jurisdictions to recognise and enforce child and spousal
maintenance liabilities.

This document describes the processes for a transmittal referral:

-   possible transmittal referral (International Operations Service
    Officer)
-   decision on referral (Central Authority team Service Officer)

Note: this document does not include the process to follow when
requesting New Zealand to recognise and enforce child and spousal
maintenance liabilities.
