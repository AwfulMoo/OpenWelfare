
Cancellation, rejection and Current Zero Rate codes for Family Tax Benefit (FTB) 007-07090010

-   Background
-   Process
-   References

[Expand/Collapse all sections]Expand all

This page contains information about new claim rejections associated
with changes to the claim lodgement period for Family Tax Benefit (FTB).
It does not cover information regarding not effective claims or
withdrawal of claims for FTB or Stillborn Baby Payment (SBP).
