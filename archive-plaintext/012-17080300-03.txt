This page contains information on how an Assessing Officer (AO) is to
complete assessment of a Medical Indemnity Online Claiming (MIOC) High
Cost Claim Indemnity Scheme (HCCS) initial or subsequent payment
application or a Section 38 response to determine if the HCCS payment
application meets the relevant eligibility requirements under the HCCS
scheme.
