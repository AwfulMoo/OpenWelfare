Information exchanged with state and territory housing authorities

Table 2: This table describes specific information, which can be
released verbally to state and territory housing authorities by Income
Management Contact Officers (IMCO). This is only provided in response to
a vulnerable referral and information is restricted to that permitted
under the Public Interest Certificate (PIC). Information is not provided
in written or electronic/email form as it is not considered a secure
channel for exchanging information.
