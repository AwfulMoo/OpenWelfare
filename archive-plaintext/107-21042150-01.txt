Compensation Recovery

The Medicare Compensation Recovery team manage the recovery of Medicare
compensation debts. These include:

-   Medicare benefits
-   nursing home benefits
-   residential care subsidies
-   home care subsidies

The Medicare Compensation team raise insurer and claimant debts. Debts
remaining outstanding after 28 days are referred to Medicare Recovery.
