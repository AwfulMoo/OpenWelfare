For Debt Management Officers (DMO) only.

This document outlines the procedure to assess a customer's financial
circumstances for debt recovery purposes.

Financial assessment

Financial information should only be obtained:

-   when an offer of repayment is not within acceptable guidelines
-   from the customer over the phone where possible, and
-   must be documented on the customer's record

Interest Charge (IC)

The Interest Charge (IC) only applies to debtors who:

-   are no longer current or receiving payment/s
-   do not have, or are not honouring, an acceptable payment arrangement

See Interest Charge (IC) for Centrelink debtors no longer receiving
payments

National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS) bank accounts

Do not garnishee bank accounts held exclusively for the purposes of
self-managing NDIS funds. They are not assessed as income or assets.
