This document outlines the role of the Services Australia Team Leader in
the disaster recovery centres created to provide information or services
to meet the short, medium and longer-term assistance needs of people
affected by a disaster.

Team Leader responsibilities

Responsibilities of the Team Leader include:

-   Supporting the team on site
-   Assisting with public enquiries
-   Acting as the Services Australia liaison point with the
    Evacuation/Recovery Centre coordinators
-   Attendance at the Evacuation/Recovery Centre
