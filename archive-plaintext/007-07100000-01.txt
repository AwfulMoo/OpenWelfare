
Enquiry, rate and assessment explanation screens for family assistance and Paid Parental Leave scheme payments 007-07100000

This document outlines the screens that provide assessment information
for family assistance and Paid Parental Leave scheme payments.

On this Page:

Family Payments

These payments include:

-   Family Tax Benefit (FTB) including Newborn Supplement (NBS)
-   Newborn Upfront Payment (NBU)
-   Child Care Subsidy (CCS) and Additional Child Care Subsidy (ACCS)
-   Parental Leave Pay (PPL)
-   Dad and Partner Pay (DAP)
-   Double Orphan Pension (DOP)
-   Stillborn Baby Payment (SBP)
-   Baby Bonus (BBY), and
-   Maternity Immunisation Allowance (MIA) (discontinued on 1 July
    2012).

FAO system screens

Enquiry screens for FAO system payments are listed on the FAO/FAM
Enquiry (FENQ) menu.

Enquiry screens include:

The FAO/FAM General Information (FGI) screen provides a summary of a
customer's assessment information for FTB

Rate explanation (REX) screens for each service reason (payment type)
provide details of how the customer's rate was calculated. REX screens
allow navigation to a number of screens for further assessment details

Assessment Explanation (AX) screens for each service reason show child
assessment and eligibility details. The MIT Explanation Summary (MNEX)
screen shows maintenance income assessment details used to calculate FTB
Part A

Adjusted taxable income details can be viewed for the relevant financial
year from the FAO Income Summary (FINS) and FAO Income Summary Details
(FISD) screens.

The Event Summary (ES) screen displays a one line summary for each
activity completed on the customer's record. Lines can be selected to
view further details for the activity

The FTB Income Support Payment Explanation (FTBISPX) screen provides
information relating to:

-   whether a customer is/was in receipt of an income support payment
    for the purposes of FTB Part A assessment
-   income used in the calculation of the secondary and/or primary
    earner income tests for FTB Part B

Screen help [F1] and field help [Shift/?] provide information about the
details recorded on these screens.

Maintenance screens used for FTB purposes can be accessed from the
Maintenance Entitlement Group Summary (MNGS) screen.

CCS screens (Process Direct)

Information about Child Care Subsidy (CCS) can only be viewed in Process
Direct.

Selecting the Child Care icon displays the information (excluding
income) used to determine a customer’s CCS/Additional Child Care Subsidy
(ACCS) entitlement, including:

-   Entitlement summary
-   Activity Test (extracted from the ATD table)
-   Exceptional Circumstances (extracted from the EXC table)
-   Weekly Subsidy
-   ACCS details (for Temporary Financial Hardship, Transition to Work,
    Grandparent and Child Wellbeing) – information is extracted from the
    TFH table, TTW table and CHGA table. Child Wellbeing information is
    extracted from the provider record who submitted the application)
-   Child Immunisation Details (extracted from the CIM table in the
    child’s record)
-   Child School Details (extracted from the CED table in the child’s
    record)

Selecting the Family Assistance icon will display:

-   Most recent family estimated and actual (where available) income
    (extracted from the FTI table). This is used to determine a
    customer’s CCS percentage
-   Care details of children
-   Mutual Customer Identification Progress Stage, where details of any
    TFN mismatches can be viewed

[] The Online Estimators can be used to estimate a family's potential
FTB and CCS entitlement.
