This document outlines the process which applies to income processing
for the period 1 July 2008 to 30 June 2011 (21 November 2012 for WA
ex-nuptial cases). It details the different types of income that may be
used in an assessment and when to update a customer's income.

Overview

There are a number of income types that can be used in an assessment.
The Income Hierarchy on the Process page indicates the income types that
can be used in an assessment in order of preference.

For income processing from 1 July 2011 (22 November 2012 for WA
ex-nuptial cases) see Income processing for Child Support customers.
