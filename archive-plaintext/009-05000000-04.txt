Legislation

Links to the Comlaw site now point to a 'Series' page which lists all
available historical versions. Select 'Current' and then navigate to the
appropriate legislative reference(s) listed below.

For details regarding the method used for rating the behaviour and
special care needs of a person aged under 16, see Social Security Act
1991, section 38D, Child disability assessment tool.

For details regarding the method used for rating the disability,
emotional state, behaviour and special care needs of a person aged 16 or
more, see Social Security Act 1991, section 38C, Adult Disability
Assessment Tool.

For details regarding the method for rating the functional ability,
behaviour and special care needs of a person aged under 16, see Social
Security Act 1991, section 38E, Disability Care Load Assessment (Child)
Determination.

Agreements and arrangements

The department delivers payments and services on behalf of other
government agencies. A range of bilateral agreements and Bilateral
Management Arrangements (BMA) governs our relationships with those
agencies.

For more information, contact the Department of Social Services (DSS)
Relationships Team.
