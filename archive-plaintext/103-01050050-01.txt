This document outlines the Community Code Search (PQIMS) screen which
will enable Service Officers to search and sort a service component and
Community Codes for income managed customers.

PQIMS screen

This screen is restricted to searching for Income Management service
components only. Remote Servicing Teams should print a list of customers
requiring service, prior to the visit, as case connectivity options may
be limited.

The Resources page contains a list of Income Management service
components.
