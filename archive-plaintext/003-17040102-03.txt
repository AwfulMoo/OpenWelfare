This page contains information on assessing eligibility for Ex-gratia
Disaster Recovery Allowance (DRA) assistance for New Zealand
non-protected Special Category visa (SVC) holders affected by Tropical
Cyclone Debbie - March 2017. Claims are processed by selected Smart
Centres
