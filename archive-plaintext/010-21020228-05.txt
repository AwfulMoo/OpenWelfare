Text for Q999 suspension letter

After careful consideration, your Assistance for Isolated Children (AIC)
for <student's name> has been suspended for Term <term and year> as we
have received information from <school> that they were not enrolled from
<date>.

Please supply confirmation from the school that <student's name> is
enrolled there and studying full time. This should be on the school's
letterhead.

If this information is not supplied within 28 days, AIC will be
cancelled and a debt may be raised. You can upload the information to
your myGov account or call us on 13 23 18.
