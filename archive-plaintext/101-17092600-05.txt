Table 1: This table contains a list of Veterans' Information Services
(VIS) sites at Services Australia service centres and includes the
address of each site, the number of VIS Officers required in each site
and the site category.

VIS service centres are categorised as:

-   Small - 1 hour DVA services per week (52 hours per annum)
-   Medium - 3 hours DVA services per week (156 hours per annum)
-   Large - 7 hours DVA services per week (364 hours per annum)
