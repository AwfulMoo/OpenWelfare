This document outlines how to transfer a Pensioner Education Supplement
(PES) payment to an ABSTUDY PES payment to enable a customer to access
additional payments under the ABSTUDY scheme, such as:

-   Incidentals Allowance
-   Away from Base assistance
-   Fares Allowance
-   Residential Cost Option

Entitlement to transfer

Customers are legally entitled to transfer from PES to ABSTUDY PES in
order to receive additional payments under the ABSTUDY Scheme, such as
ABSTUDY Incidentals Allowance, even if they already received EdEP during
the year.
