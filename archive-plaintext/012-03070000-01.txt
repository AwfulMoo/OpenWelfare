This document outlines information about Contracted Service Providers
(CSP) Officers and Contracted Service Providers (CSP) organisations and
their connection in relation to the Healthcare Identifiers (HI) Service.

CSP Organisation

The CSP organisation is a legal entity that is registered with the HI
Service by the individual who will become the CSP officer of the CSP
organisation. A CSP organisation can access the suite of Healthcare
Identifiers Service functionality once they are registered and linked to
a healthcare organisation also registered with the HI Service.
