This page contains a summary of the process for the External
Complaints - Child Support (ExCCS) team to manage complaints from the
Commonwealth Ombudsman. When required ExCCS Service Officers consult
with Service Support Managers (SSM) and Service Managers (SM).

On this page:

Ombudsman complaint or Ombudsman Complaint Transfer received

Respond to an Ombudsman complaint or Ombudsman Complaint Transfer

Seek approval for written response to an Ombudsman Complaint

Finalise an Ombudsman complaint and provide feedback
