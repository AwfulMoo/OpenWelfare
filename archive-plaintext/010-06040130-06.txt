A student undertaking approved overseas study may receive Rent
Assistance (RA) for their accommodation in Australia, if they continue
to pay rent while they are overseas. They are not eligible for RA for
their overseas accommodation. The following training in ESSentials
provides information about RA:

-   CLK01042 - Rent Assistance - Personal Circumstances
-   CLK01043 - Rent Assistance - Verification
-   CLK01044 - Rent Assistance - Coding (this includes workbooks 1 to 4)
