This document outlines a list of the cancellation, rejection and zero
rate codes for Family Tax Benefit (FTB) and indicates those that can be
restored. The Centrelink system requires a specific code when a decision
to cancel or suspend a payment, or to reject a claim, is recorded. A
description of the code is used in correspondence with customers.

Not effective claims

Where a claim is deemed not effective it cannot be restored as there has
been no decision made about the customer's entitlement that is the claim
has neither been rejected nor cancelled. For further information see Not
effective claims or withdrawal of claim for Family Tax Benefit or
Stillborn Baby Payment.

The Process page contains a list of cancellation and rejection codes for
FTB.
