This document explains specific information about the Agreement
including the process of making a claim for Australian payment under the
agreement and coding of Hungarian payments.

Start date

The Agreement with Hungary started on 1 October 2012.

Claiming and benefits covered

The Agreement generally allows customers to lodge a claim for payment
from either country. It also allows customers to add together periods of
residence in Australia and periods of social security coverage in
Hungary, to meet the minimum requirements for payment.

Australian payment covered

-   Age Pension

Customers can claim Australian payments under the Agreement by
contacting Services Australia or the Hungarian authorities.

Hungarian payments covered

-   Old Age Pension
-   Survivor's Pension

Customers may receive Old-Age and Survivors' Pensions concurrently.

Customers can claim a foreign pension by contacting Services Australia
or the Hungarian authorities.
