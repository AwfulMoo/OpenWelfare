This document outlines information about claims and reviews for Mobility
Allowance (MOB) and claims for Advance Payment.

Check the customer completed the form correctly

When a MOB claim, review form or MOB Advance Payment claim is lodged in
person, staff must check the form is completed and signed, while the
customer is present.

If all information has been provided, scan the form and any other
documents provided by the customer.

The Mobility Allowance National Team (AMW) process mobility claims and
reviews.

The Resources page contains links to Payment and Service Finder,
Centrelink Online Accounts, forms and publications for MOB.
