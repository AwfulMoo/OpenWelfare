This document outlines the Vocational and Educational Guidance
Assistance Scheme (VEGAS) which provides grants to sponsoring
organisations to conduct projects for Indigenous students, their parents
and prisoners in lawful custody.

Aim of the scheme

These grants aim to foster positive attitudes towards participation in
education and provide information to assist students to consider their
options for further study and careers.

Administration

VEGAS is administered by the Department of Education as part of the
Indigenous Education (Targeted Assistance) Act 2000 (IETA). Further
information is available from the Indigenous Education Units (IEU) in
the Department of Education offices, or by going to the Department of
Education website.

Services Australia does not handle enquiries regarding VEGAS. Customers
with questions about VEGAS should contact the Department of Education.
For the contact details for the Department of Education in each
State/Territory see Indigenous Tutorial Assistance Scheme (ITAS).
