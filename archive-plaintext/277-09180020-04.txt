Delegations and authorisations

Service Officers APS3 and above

Prepare documents for the review of objection decisions and decisions on
extension of time applications, under section 37 of the Administrative
Appeals Tribunal Act 1975 and section 95c of the Child Support
(Registration and Collection) ACT 1988.

Service Officers APS5

Apply a direction for non-disclosure concerning certain documents.

Identified EL1 positions

Make written:

-   submissions to the AAT
-   requests for permission to make oral submissions to the AAT
