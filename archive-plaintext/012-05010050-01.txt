This document outlines how eBusiness Service Centre Service Officers
perform electronic Medicare claim deletions in Mainframe.

Claim types not deleted by eBusiness Service Centre

The following claims cannot be deleted by Service Officers

-   Claiming Medicare Benefits Online (CMBO) claims via myGov
-   ECLIPSE IMC patient claims
-   payee code 8 Simplified Billing and ECLIPSE claims
-   payee code 7 Patient claims
-   Department of Veterans' Affairs (DVA)
-   In-patient Medical claims
-   Online Bulk Bill and Patient claims processed on a previous day
-   Online Bulk Bill and Patient claims that have already made the
    payment run

See Handle claim that cannot be deleted table on the Resources page for
more information.

Control lines

The following control lines can be used to verify if the claim or
transmission has been received by Services Australia:

-   bulk bill: DQRI, provider number, claim number
-   patient claim: NQAI, Medicare number
-   Easyclaim: NHSI, Medicare number, IRN

The Resources page contains claim deletion rules, information
requirements and contact details to handle claims that cannot be
deleted.
