
This document outlines information relating to Austudy.

Government Intent

Austudy provides income support for full time students and Australian
Apprentices aged 25 years and older.

Eligibility for Austudy

The applicant must be 25 years or older and be either undertaking
full-time study in an approved course at an approved educational
institution or be a full-time Australian Apprentice.

Austudy is subject to an income and assets test and the applicant must
meet residence requirements.
