For staff processing International Disbursements only.

This document outlines information about the process of sending bulk
payments electronically to overseas child support administrations.

Schedules currently prepared

A schedule is a spreadsheet lodged with CSA Cash Management & Reporting
to facilitate payments to either:

-   An overseas authority
-   An individual customer or overseas authority by cheque
-   An individual customer by direct debit to bank account

Schedules are created to send bulk payments out of Australia. The
following schedules are currently prepared:

-   WIRE/EFT Transfers
-   Payments Overseas (Cheque payment)
-   Austria
-   Belgium
-   Canada, British Columbia and Ontario
-   Finland
-   Germany
-   Netherlands
-   New Zealand
-   Norway
-   Slovakia
-   Sweden
-   Switzerland
