This document outlines information regarding the processes to follow
when customers have a change of address or rent details.

Government Intent - Requirement to notify

Centrelink customers are required to notify of any changes to their
address details to ensure that Centrelink have complete and correct
contact information, and that customers are receiving correct
entitlements and communications.

Confirm customer details

Every customer's address (including postal address) and telephone
details (including mobile phone numbers) must be checked at every
contact, and updated if required.

Note: if the customer has authenticated themselves via IVR, address
details do not need to be confirmed.
