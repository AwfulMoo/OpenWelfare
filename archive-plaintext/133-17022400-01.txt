This document outlines how to help customers find out about payments
they might get or services they might use.

About Payment and Service Finder

Payment and Service Finder is a service that combines:

-   Payment Finder
-   Service Finder, and
-   the online rate estimator

Customers will answer questions about their circumstances. The service
will provide possible payments and assistance from Services Australia.
This may include assistance from community service providers.

The service consists of three parts:

-   payment discovery
-   service discovery
-   estimation of payment rates

Customers can use the online estimator options to:

-   calculate potential payment amounts, or
-   see how a change in their circumstances will affect their Centrelink
    payments

The following estimators are not available in Payment and Service
Finder:

-   Compensation impacts
-   Child support estimator
-   Child support care estimator

Payment and Service Finder is available to use in Simplified Chinese.

The Resources page contains a link to the Payment and Service Finder,
Frequently Asked Questions about the service and a Learning Byte
training resource.
