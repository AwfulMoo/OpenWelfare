This document outlines different information concerning payment rates
and thresholds.

Information to determine rate of payment

Information provided includes:

-   ready reckoners
-   coding manual rates of payment
-   maximum rates of payment
-   thresholds
-   rate charts
-   add-on payments

Centrelink staff assess a customer's eligibility to receive these
payments according to legislative requirements.

A different Income Test may apply for customers receiving income from an
agreement country if they are granted via an agreement.

Historical rates of payment can be found in the Guide to Social Security
Law by using the link in Policy.
