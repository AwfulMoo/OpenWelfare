This document outlines information about Manual Follow-up (MFU)
activities relating to payment summaries calculations.

MFU tax activities

If a manual follow-up (MFU) tax activity displays on the customer
record, the payment summary will not issue.

The negative amount on a customer’s payment summary must be corrected.

Once the MFU activity is actioned, the payment summary can be issued to
the customer.

Identifying the problem with the payment summary

See General information on Manual Follow-up (MFU) tax activities for
information on how to identify issues with the payment summary
