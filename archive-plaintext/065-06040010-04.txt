Payment specific

For the qualification provisions for Commonwealth Seniors Health Card,
see Social Security Act 1991, section 1061ZG, Qualification rules.

For the NARWP provisions for a Commonwealth Seniors Health Card, see
Social Security Act 1991, section 1061ZH, Newly arrived resident's
waiting period.

Social Security Act 1991, section 1061ZI, Duration of the newly arrived
resident's waiting period.
