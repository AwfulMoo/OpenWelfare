This document outlines information about the Pharmaceutical Benefits
Scheme (PBS).

Government intent

The PBS is an Australian Government program that subsidises a wide range
of prescription medicines, making them more affordable to eligible
customers.

The Resources page contains links to Australian government departments
and health organisations relevant to Pharmaceutical Benefits Scheme
(PBS).
