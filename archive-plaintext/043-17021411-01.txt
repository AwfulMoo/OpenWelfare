This document contains processes for recovering funds once an
overpayment has occurred and a debt raised against the claimant for the
Tasmanian Freight Equalisation Scheme (TFES).

Recovering an overpayment or debt

The Tasmanian Transport Program Team has the following options as per
Schedule 4 of the Public Governance, Performance and Accountability Act
2013 (PGPA) and Clause 5.8 of the Ministerial Directions to recover a
debt:

-   offset against a current claim or claims in part or in whole
-   offset against future claims in part or whole
-   invoice raised by Services Australia Accounts Receivable for payment
    in full or by instalments via BPAY or cheque
-   request a waiver or write off from Department of Infrastructure and
    Regional Development
-   potential non recovery

The Resources page contains links to letters, the Services Australia
website, internal documents and external websites for more information
