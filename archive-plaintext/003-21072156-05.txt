Geospatial tool

To assess if a customer lives or works in a relevant area:

Open the Victoria Live Feed App

Place the address given by the customer in the search box. Residential
and employer addresses may need to be checked

Select Search (magnifying glass icon)

Select COVID-19 Disaster Payment - VIC July 2021 in the Layers menu on
the right hand side. Do not select the COVID-19 Disaster Payment - VIC
August 2021 map for this event

If claiming for a relevant area in

-   Greater Melbourne/Mildura, the address must lie in the blue shaded
    area to be eligible to receive a payment
-   All Other Areas in Victoria, the address must lie in the red shaded
    area to be eligible to receive a payment

Continue to assess the claim
