Examples of known customers

List of known customers

This table describes the role types which can be known customers to
Services Australia and the list of numbers they can give to prove this.

Healthcare providers can do all different types of business with
Services Australia, to do certain types of business they have to
identify who they are. Service Officers can verify the numbers they
supply have been used previously with Medicare to do with other business
and therefore are known to Services Australia.
