Definitions

The definitions of 'approved course of education or study' and
'undertaking full-time study' have the same meaning as in the Social
Security Act, see:

-   A New Tax System (Family Assistance) Act 1999, Part 2, Division 1,
    section 3, Definitions
-   Social Security Act 1991, Part 2.11, Division 1, Subdivision B,
    section 541B, Undertaking full-time study
-   Student Assistance Act 1973, Part 1, section 5D, Minister may
    determine secondary and tertiary courses etc.
