This document outlines how to complete and assess the SY068.

The SY068 form

The applicant must make an initial claim for Basic Boarding Allowance
(BA) using the Claim for Assistance for Isolated Children (AIC) (SY040).
They use the SY068 to apply for this allowance for any additional
periods of short term boarding in the same year of study.

The applicant who completed the initial SY040 for the first period of
short term boarding lodges the SY068.

Lodging the form

The applicant must lodge the initial SY040 on or before 1 March in the
year following which the board relates or no allowance is payable.

An applicant experiencing vulnerable circumstances may be given an
additional 14 days to submit their claim, which can be extended to 13
weeks in some circumstances.

The applicant lodges the SY068:

-   at any service centre
-   via the Express Plus mobile app
-   by [A self service option is available for customers: Placed under
    section headings or general content where self service options are
    available.]uploading documents online, or
-   by posting to the reply paid address on the form

The applicant needs to:

-   complete and sign the form
-   add any further details to a separate sheet of paper and attach it
    to the form
