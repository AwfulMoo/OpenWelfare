This document outlines the list of forms in the Aged Care program for
service providers. Aged care forms that are submitted electronically
(via email or fax) must contain a digital copy or an image of a
signature to be deemed as acceptable. This is known as an electronic
signature.

The Reference page contains a link to the Electronic Transaction Act
1999 for information on electronic signatures.

The Resources page contains a list of aged care forms for service
providers.
