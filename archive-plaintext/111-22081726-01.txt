This document outlines the video chat service. It instructs how to
create, reschedule and complete/cancel video chat appointments in
Process Direct.

Video Chat appointments

For some interactions, the agency is using video chat as an alternative
to an in person face-to-face interview.

The staff selected to undertake video chat appointments are using a
specific version of Microsoft Teams to:

-   conduct video chat with customers, and
-   manage the video chat appointments

Service delivery staff standards

-   Be aware that the agency is using video chat for several appointment
    types
-   Know how to create, reschedule, cancel/complete video chat
    appointments in Process Direct

Video chat staff refer to specific training and resource materials when
conducting video chat appointments.

The Resources page contains links to the Service Australia website,
mySupport and a link to reporting customer aggression.
