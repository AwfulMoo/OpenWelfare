This document explains the process for transmitting a case to the Inland
Revenue, New Zealand (NZIR).

Transmittal from Child Support to NZIR

Use this process to send Australian Child Support cases for registration
and collection by Inland Revenue, New Zealand (NZIR). This is
appropriate when Child Support has received information from NZIR
confirming the payer resides/lives in New Zealand.
