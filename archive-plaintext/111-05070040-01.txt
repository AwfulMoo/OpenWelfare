This document outlines the role and responsibilities of Smart Centre
Team Leaders in the Technical Peer Support (TPS) process.

Team Leader role

Team Leaders provide assistance to TPS by:

-   dealing with customers who have requested to speak to a supervisor
    regarding customer service issues, and
-   assisting Service Officers to stop incorrectly generated arrears
    from being issued

The Resources page contains a link to mySupport that has instructions on
how to request to stop an incorrect immediate payment from being issued.
