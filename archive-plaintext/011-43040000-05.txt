DHSC screen

Fields on a restated line can be corrected at any time throughout the
DHSC stage. The [Enter] key must be pressed to accept any changes.

Note: whenever a line is protected the system removes the dashes in all
fields that are empty and the colour changes to blue.

Adjustment action codes

Adjustments can be actioned on lines that have:

-   not already been adjusted (the A fields or these lines contain '-')
-   restated lines (the A fields contain R)
-   new lines (the A fields contain N)
-   I may be entered (to insert a line) in the A field if over keying an
    R or N
