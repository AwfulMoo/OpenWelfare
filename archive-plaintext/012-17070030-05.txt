This page contains MT items payable to service providers, MT50 pathology
tests abbreviations for non-Medicare rebatable pathology tests, and
links to the Medicare Benefits Schedule (MBS) and Veterans' Affairs
Processing (VAP) contact details.

MT items payable to service providers

Process MT items not listed in the MBS or DVA Allied Health Fee Schedules but payable to service providers

This table describes how to process items not listed in the MBS or DVA
Allied Health Fee Schedules, but payable to service providers.

Note:

when the service is not itemised as an MT item it must be rejected with
RSN code S 578 'Item number cannot be determined from information
supplied'

when 3-DVA ITM? is displayed, check item rules in table below. Use PI18
if MT item is payable according the business rules
