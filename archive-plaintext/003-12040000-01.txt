This document outlines how Services Australia social workers conduct
assessments for family assistance payments.

Social worker assessments

Social workers conduct assessments for:

-   Child Support non-parent carer assessments
-   situations where a child may be at risk of harm

Services Australia staff will refer customers to social workers for
informal assessments, with referrals for non-parent carer assessments
made by Child Support.
