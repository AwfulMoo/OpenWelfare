This page contains information to assist Service Officers with amending
a notice of past benefits (NOPB) or notice of charge (NOCH) and to
delete valid NOPB - all services taken and issue new Medicare history
statement (MHS).

Service Officers must review the References and Resources pages before
issuing an amended NOPB or NOCH.
