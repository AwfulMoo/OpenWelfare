Verbal claim

For every phone claim taken:

Right click on the Verbal claim template link below

Select 'Save link as'

Staff must save the template to their desktop

Open the template

Complete a new template for each period of self-isolation, quarantine or
caring a customer is applying for

Do not delete any questions in this template

Always access the version in Resources for future claims

-   Content is updated regularly. Using the current version prevents
    accidental use of customer data from a previous claim

Complete all questions as per your discussion with the customer by:

-   Answering 'Yes' or 'No' as needed, and
-   Documenting relevant information

Any information in blue that is not relevant to the customer's claim
should be deleted once actioned otherwise the template may exceed the
character limit for Fast Notes

Use the hyperlinks to progress through the template

Copy and paste the completed template into the Pandemic Leave Disaster
Payment Verbal Claim Fast Note

When completing the template, follow Operational Blueprint

Use this template and Operational Blueprint together

Once completed, staff must:
- copy and paste content into the Pandemic Leave Disaster Payment Verbal
Claim Fast Note in Customer First
- delete the saved template
- start a new template from the below link for any new claim
