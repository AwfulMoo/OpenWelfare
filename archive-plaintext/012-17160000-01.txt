Veterans’ Affairs processing (VAP) codes

A range of VAP codes exist to assist Service Officers with processing
claims for the Department of Veterans’ Affairs (DVA). The codes are used
for a variety of functions including:

-   Allied Health
-   Claim type indicators and auto-operator pay codes
-   EFT and Cheque Recovery
-   Electronic transmission
-   Function Keys (PF) for manual data entry - claims
-   Hospital accommodation items schedule
-   Hospital contracts enquiry system (VAMM)
-   Hospital online data enquiry (VQME)
-   International Classification of Diseases (ICD) codes
-   Late lodgement retention requirements
-   Medical terms
-   Patient eligibility
-   Processing indicator (PI) codes
-   Processing reason (RSN) codes
-   Provider speciality codes
-   Repatriation medical fee schedule summary
-   Source office codes
-   VCEI reason codes
-   White card treatment reason codes
