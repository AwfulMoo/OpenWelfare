This page contains information on how an Assessing Officer (AO)
completes an assessment of a Medical Indemnity Online Claiming (MIOC)
Incurred But Not Reported (IBNR) or IBNR/High Cost Claim Indemnity
Scheme (HCCS) initial or subsequent payment application, or a Section 38
response, to determine if the payment application meets the eligibility
requirements under the IBNR or IBNR/HCCS scheme/s.
