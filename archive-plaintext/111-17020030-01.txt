This document outlines the storage and handling of compensation recovery
future preclusion paper files.

Documents relating to future preclusion cases

All documents relating to compensation recovery future preclusion cases
are scanned so physical files are no longer created.

Records for older cases can be made up of paper file(s), including:

-   on and off file (batch) documents
-   an electronic record
-   magnetic media

The Resources page contains a link to the Records Management service
request webform.
