If the timeframe is not met under:

Section 24(4) or section 25(4)

-   a notice of charge cannot be issued and no amount is recoverable
    under the HOSC Act
-   it will be considered lost revenue by the Australian government

Section 33D

-   a notice of charge cannot be issued and no amount is recoverable
    under the HOSC Act
-   s33K applies and the whole advance payment is payable to the injured
    person or claimant
-   the injured person’s liability to pay any amounts owed in relation
    to the claim is discharged
-   it will be considered lost revenue by the Australian government

In either case a Z2812 Past benefits and subsidies will not be recovered
advice is issued.
