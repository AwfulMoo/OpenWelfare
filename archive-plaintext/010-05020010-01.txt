This document outlines the completion of the Information you need to
know about your claim for Pensioner Education Supplement form (SY004).

When to use this claim

The Information you need to know about your claim for Pensioner
Education Supplement (PES) form (SY004) is used to claim PES. This form
should only be used if the customer cannot, or does not want to claim
PES online, or claim PES verbally using Assisted Customer Claim (ACC),
as per the digital first standard.

Reclaiming within 52 weeks

If a Pensioner Education Supplement (PES) customer is reclaiming within
52 weeks of last receiving their PES, they can complete the continuation
form, Information required for continuation of PES or ABSTUDY (SY075).

PES claims for ABSTUDY customers

Australian Aboriginals and Torres Strait Islanders wishing to claim
ABSTUDY Pensioner Education Supplement need to complete Form c (SY020)
for the ABSTUDY Pensioner Education Supplement claim.

How to lodge this claim

Claims and forms may be lodged via the customer's [] online account or
using the [] Express Plus mobile app, in person at the nearest service
centre, or posted using the appropriate reply paid envelope.

Documents supporting their claim may also be lodged via:

-   Upload documents,or
-   Express Plus mobile app, or
-   in person at the nearest service centre, or
-   posted using the appropriate reply paid envelope
