This page contains information on how to assess and process
notifications and requests for general practitioners; medical
practitioners who are registered or previously registered as a
specialist in the specialty of General practice (including FRACGP and
FACRRM who have registered or previously registered as a specialist with
Ahpra) and medical practitioners included, or previously included on the
VR.

On this page:

Assess a general practitioner notification

Process recognition as a specialist general practitioner subject to
Section 19AA

Process recognition as a specialist general medical practitioner subject
to Section 19AB

Process removal as a general practitioner

Process a request to confirm if a medical practitioner is on the
Vocational Register

Escalate GP specialist recognition enquiries

PSO receives escalated GP specialist recognition enquiry
