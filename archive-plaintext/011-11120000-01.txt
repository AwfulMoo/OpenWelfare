This document outlines processing for returned AODR mail marked ' Not at
this address' or 'Return to sender'.

AODR Return to Sender mail

All return to sender mail for the AODR is scanned into and processed via
PaNDA.

Where mail is returned and marked as either:

-   'Not at this address'
-   'Returned to sender'

Note: damaged mail is to be escalated via PSO Referral Form.

The Resources page contains contact details for AODR and AODR Programme
Support Officer (PSO).
