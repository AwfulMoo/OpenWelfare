This document outlines what happens when parents or guardians of ABSTUDY
customers who are subject to the parental income test, commence or
return to work.

Parental income test

The following ABSTUDY customers are subject to a parental income test:

-   dependent students and Australian Apprentices under 16 who are
    eligible for the Away from Home Rate (AFH)
-   all dependent students and Australian Apprentices 16 or over

Income

The parental income test is based on the income of the relevant
parents/guardians irrespective of whether the ABSTUDY customer lives at
home or away from home.
