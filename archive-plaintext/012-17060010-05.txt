This page contains patient episodes initiations (PEI) items that are
payable when associated with the correct pathology items, pathology
incentive items payable when associated with the correct PEI and patient
episode initiation fees that can be raised by the first approved
pathology authority (APA), fees that cannot be raised by the second APA
and the specimen referred fees that can be raised by the second APA.
This page also contains MT50 pathology test abbreviations.
