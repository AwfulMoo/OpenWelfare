This document outlines information on performing telephone security
checks for aged care services. This process applies to Services Officers
from the Aged Care Payments Team (Aged Care and Allied Health Branch).

Enquiries about aged care

Services Australia can receive calls for information about aged care
from:

-   aged care providers
-   nominees
-   Power of Attorneys (POA), and
-   care recipients

Service Officers must first ask questions to authenticate the caller’s
identity.
