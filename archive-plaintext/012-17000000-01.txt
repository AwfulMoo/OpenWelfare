This document outlines the various policies and services related to
Veterans' Affairs Processing (VAP).

Government intent

Services Australia processes claims for veterans in collaboration with
the Department of Veterans' Affairs (DVA), which develops and manages
policy for veterans. Services Australia assesses claims and makes
payments to medical, hospital and allied health providers who treat
eligible veterans, their spouses and dependants. DVA assesses veterans'
eligibility.

The Resources page contains a link to the Restriction lift request form
for VAP and a link to contact details for VAP.
