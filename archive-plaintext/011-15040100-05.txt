Medicare Compensation Recovery Notice of Judgment or Settlement (MO022)

Email templates

Email templates available via this link are endorsed for use by Services
Australia and are the latest versions. Staff must not use locally
produced email templates.

The full list of available Medicare Compensation Recovery email
templates are available from the Letters and electronic messaging
sub-site.

Letter templates

The Standard Letter Templates (SLT) available via this link are endorsed
for use by the agency and are the latest versions. Do not use locally
produced letters.

The full list of available Medicare Compensation Recovery letters is
available from the Letters and electronic messaging sub-site. For
instructions on how to use the SLT, see Using Standard Letter Templates
(SLT).
