This document outlines the specific codes used when a decision is
recorded on a customer's record to assess their claim as not effective,
or reject a claim for Dad and Partner Pay (DAP). This procedure contains
a list of the codes used for DAP.

Note: DAP is not payable for children born or adopted on or after 1 July
2023. Fathers and partners will need to claim PPL.

DAP codes

In most cases, a Dad and Partner Pay (DAP) claim will be automatically
finalised as not effective or rejected based on details coded when
processing the claim. A description of the code is used in letters to
the customer.

The Process page contains a list of rejection and not effective codes
for DAP.

The Resources page contains a link to a DAP claim status and Act
reference table.
