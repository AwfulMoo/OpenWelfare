Services Australia website

Claim for Pandemic Leave Disaster Payment – Tasmania

Claim forms

Period of self-isolation, quarantine or caring that starts:

-   between 9 December 2021 and 17 January (inclusive), use the Claim
    for Pandemic Leave Disaster Payment (EM252) form
-   before 9 December 2021, (that is, up to and including 8 December
    2021), take a claim for this period over the phone using the verbal
    claim template. If the customer is not able to submit a claim via
    phone, use the Claim for Pandemic Leave Disaster Payment – Tasmania
    (EM224) form
