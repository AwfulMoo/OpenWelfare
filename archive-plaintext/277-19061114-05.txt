History of section 29 - Presumption of parentage

Third Party Carer Application updates in Cuba

This table contains information to be updated in the Third Party
Application Details dialogue box in the Relationship window when changes
are made to non-parent carer case after 3 March 2008. The changes to a
non-parent case can include:

-   terminating events
-   changes in residency status
-   special circumstances applications
-   new applications
-   updating an existing non-parent case

Once the information has been input, Cuba automatically selects the
appropriate post 1 July 2008 formula to apply to the case.
