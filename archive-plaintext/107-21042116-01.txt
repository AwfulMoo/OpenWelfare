This document outlines information about how to create and adjust
invoices for private health funds.

For Medicare Debt Management Officers (DMO) only

Private Health Fund debts

Private health fund debts arise from incorrect claims submitted through
the Simplified Billing (SB) claim channel. Private health insurers make
claims on behalf of the servicing Medical Practitioner. Debts may be
identified by the:

-   Health Insurer
-   Medical Practitioner, or
-   Services Australia

Invoices raised from processed overpayments

Medicare Recovery generates the weekly Report Management Distribution
System (RMDS) report which:

-   displays all adjustments resulting in overpayments for Simplified
    Billing transactions
-   provides information to raise invoices for later day adjustment
    (LDA)

The Resources page contains contact details and mainframe system
parameters.
