This document outlines the responsibilities of Services Australia staff
when an asylum seeker contacts for information about the Status
Resolution Support Services payment, or if a customer in receipt of the
Status Resolution Support Services payment contacts with an enquiry
about their payment.

SRSS new claims

Asylum seekers are required to initiate a SRSS new claim by contacting
their local SRSS service provider in their state. For this information,
refer the person to the Department of Home Affairs external website. See
Resources.

All other enquiries

Services Australia will:

-   advise SRSS customers about their payment including the rates and
    when it will be paid
-   contact SRSS customers in band 5 or 6 to complete their compulsory 6
    monthly SRSS review
-   complete Medicare enrolments for SRSS customers

Services Australia cannot provide advice to SRSS customers on
immigration or visa status.

Customers with enquiries about eligibility for the SRSS program and
immigration or visa status to the Department of Home Affairs.

Types of SRSS enquiries

SRSS customers may contact Services Australia if they:

-   have not been paid
-   commenced working
-   request an income statement
-   want to query the amount paid
-   want to advise a change of circumstances
-   want to reschedule an appointment
-   want to update their bank account details
-   disagree with a Services Australia decision
-   want information about an SRSS debt

If a customer requires further assistance, refer them to their
Department of Home Affairs contracted service provider.
