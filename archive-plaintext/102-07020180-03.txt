This page contains information about customers who will not commence
full time studies and they are receiving ABSTUDY, ABSTUDY Pensioner
Education Supplement (PES) and Assistance for Isolated Children (AIC) or
AIC PES.

On this page:

Identifying payment and circumstances after not commencing full-time
study

Assessing and coding suspension, rejection or cancellation
