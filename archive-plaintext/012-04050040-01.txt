This document outlines the steps between processing Part A (HI Service)
and Part B (My Health Record) of an Application to Register a Seed
Organisation (HW018).

When to use these procedures

If Part A and Part B of an Application to Register a Seed Organisation
(HW018) have both been completed and submitted, follow the processes in
the process tab.

If either part has been submitted without the other being completed,
then handle as per the Related links.

The Resources page contains the Application to Register a Seed
Organisation process workflow and instructions relating to PDFs.
