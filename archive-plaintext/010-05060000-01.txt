This document outlines the qualification requirements for customers
claiming Pensioner Education Supplement (PES).

Qualification

Pensioner Education Supplement (PES) is paid in addition to the primary
'qualifying' payment to help with the ongoing costs associated with
study.

To qualify for PES a student must meet all of the following:

-   be receiving one of the qualifying payments for Pensioner Education
    Supplement (PES)
-   satisfy the residence requirements
-   be of PES age (there is no maximum age limit for PES)
-   be undertaking qualifying study
-   have not completed a Masters or Doctorate degree or qualification

Note: there are extra criteria to consider when determining whether a
customer will be eligible to receive PES for a 25% concessional study
load.

Consolidated processing of PES activities

PES processing has been consolidated nationally and claims are processed
by the Student Network - PES Processing Service Centres. Search PES in
Office Locator for details.

Pensioner Education Supplement (PES) including ABSTUDY PES is a
supplementary payment paid in addition to the customers regular income
support payment and as a result there is no urgent claim processing
arrangement.
