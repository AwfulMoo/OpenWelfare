External websites

Infoxchange Service Seeker

An extensive electronic health, welfare and community services
directory. It has up-to-date information on housing, counselling,
education and training, youth, health and financial services.

Ask Izzy

A location based online directory. It links customers with services such
as housing, food, material items, health services, emergency funds,
counselling, legal advice and life skills and education.

Rome2rio

For details of cost and time taken for different methods of transport
between locations. Care needs to be taken when determining travel by car
costs, as Rome2Rio does not show the amount per litre used for petrol.
Rome2Rio should only be used as a guide to determine if the customer’s
requested amount is realistic for the travel proposed.

Whereis

Maps of Australia, street directory, driving directory and aerial views.
