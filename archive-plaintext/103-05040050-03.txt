This process does not apply to Advance payments for Home Equity Access
Scheme loans.

Select self managed to help a customer make a lump sum repayment (either
via their own self service accounts or via online services staff
assisted view

Select the Assisted tab to assist customers with queries about their
lump sum repayments or to update their advance correctly.
