Using the Aged Care Staff Portal (ACSP) – Service in Context

Service Officers can use the Aged Care Staff Portal (ACSP) via Service
in Context view. This view provides information from a service’s
perspective. Service Officers can use the Service in Context view to:

-   process events and claims for all aged care services
-   search for services within all care types
-   register invoice amounts, report Commonwealth (CW) unspent amounts
    and view a care recipient’s home care account for home care purposes
    only
-   approve aged care claims
-   manage and request registration of service user details, and
-   manage and register service contact details

Note:

A flexible care service can either be a short-term restorative care or a
transition care service.
