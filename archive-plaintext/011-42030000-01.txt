This document outlines information on archived assessing bulletins for
the Medicare Benefits Schedule (MBS).

MBS changes

From 1 December 2020 assessing bulletins will no longer be provided for
MBS changes. For any information relating to new releases or interim
changes refer to operational messages and MBS Online.

Note: assessing information in QITI cannot be released externally. If a
health professional needs assistance with MBS interpretation, tell them
to email the AskMBS email advice service.
