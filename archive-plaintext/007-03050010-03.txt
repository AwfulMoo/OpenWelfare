This page contains information relating to payment choices for Family
Tax Benefit (FTB).

[] Select the Self-managed tab to help the customer update their payment
choice online.

Select the Assisted tab to advise about FTB payment choices and to
record a person's choice or to view details of an Interactive Voice
Response (IVR) phone self service interaction.

Self managed

The following individuals are not able to update their payment choice
online:

-   those on a manual rate or with a current nil legislative rate of FTB
-   payment nominees who are not also correspondence nominees for
    eligible customers

Customers who are able to access and are eligible to use the online
option, see Payment Choices online service.

See the Resources page for:

-   a link to Payment choices for Family Tax Benefit information on
    Services Australia website, and
-   further information about payment choices

If a person is unable to use the service, select the Assisted tab for
Service Officers to record details.
