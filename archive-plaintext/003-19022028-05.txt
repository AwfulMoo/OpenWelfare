The claiming period has now closed, unless special circumstances apply.
For more details see Australian Government Disaster Recovery Payment
(AGDRP) and Disaster Recovery Allowance (DRA) and their ex-gratia
equivalent claims lodged outside of the time limit.

This page contains tables with the relevant DRA and DRA ex-gratia income
cut-off points for Newstart Allowance (NSA) and Youth Allowance (YA).
There are also links to Operational Messaging, Payment and Service
Finder, forms, maps and the Emergency Management Teams' contact details.
