Agreements and arrangements

Services Australia deliver payments and services on behalf of other
government agencies. A range of bilateral agreements and Bilateral
Management Arrangements (BMA) governs our relationships with those
agencies.

The Family Programmes and Strategic Partnerships Branch, in the
Programme Design Group, provide strategic support for the Executive to
help manage the relationships with the Department of Social Services
(DSS), as well as those relationships with other departments as
required.

For more information on the relationship with:

-   DSS see Department of Social Services (DSS) Relationship Team page
    or contact the DSS Relationship Team
-   other departments contact the Government Partnerships and Families
    Management Information Team
-   Department of Health and Aged Care, email The Health Strategic
    Relationship Team
