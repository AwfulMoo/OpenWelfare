Geospatial tool

To assess if a customer lives, works or was present in a relevant area:

Open the QLD Live Feed App

Place the address given by the customer in the search box. Residential
and employer addresses may need to be checked

Select Search (magnifying glass icon)

Select COVID-19 Disaster Payment QLD in the Layers menu on the right

For relevant areas in:

-   South East Queensland, the address must lie in the blue shaded area
    to be eligible to receive a payment
-   Far North Queensland, the address must lie in the red shaded area to
    be eligible

Continue to assess the claim
