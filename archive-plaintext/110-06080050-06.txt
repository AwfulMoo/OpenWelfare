Purpose:

This program is designed for Business Integrity and Compliance Risk
staff and explains the basic searches and functions of the Integrated
Review System (IRS) including attribution.

The program consists of six courses:

-   BUS00182 - Integrated Review System (IRS)
-   BUS00183 - IRS - Finding Reviews
-   BUS00184 - IRS - Viewing Reviews
-   BUS00185 - IRS - Actioning Reviews
-   BUS00188 - Compliance and Fraud Attribution
-   BUS00189 - IRS - Finalising reviews
