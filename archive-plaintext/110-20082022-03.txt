This page contains steps to investigate a customer’s record before
processing a remediation activity. This includes when limiting dates or
remediation are documented on the record. Complete the following
processes in sequence to gain an understanding of the customer’s
circumstances, and to identify any subsequent impacts. After completing
this initial investigation, work through the step-by-step processes in
Income compliance remediation processing.
