This document explains how to assess and process the ABSTUDY additional
information form (SY023).

When to use this form

Applicants will need to complete and lodge an ABSTUDY Student Additional
Information Form (SY023) if they have lodged an incomplete or incorrect
claim for ABSTUDY.

Service Officers may also request the customer completes an SY023 if
they advise of a major change to their circumstances that may affect
their rate of or eligibility for ABSTUDY, such as, moving from
Incidentals only to ABSTUDY Living Allowance.

If the customer is only advising changes to their study details, they
can [A self service option is available for customers: Placed under
section headings or general content where self service options are
available.] view and update the study details online if they are
registered online and are authenticated.

Processing

ABSTUDY Smart Centre Processing Teams process ABSTUDY claims. See
Processing arrangements for ABSTUDY claims and enquiries.
