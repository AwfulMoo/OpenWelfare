This page contains information for Service Officers in Service Centres
and Student/ABSTUDY/AIC Smart Centre Processing centres only.

This page contains information relating to determining a dependent child
for inclusion in the family pool for PIT for Youth Allowance (YA)
ABSTUDY (ABY) and Assistance for Isolated Children (AIC) Scheme
Additional Boarding Allowance (ABA).

Dependent child determination for the purpose of PIT

This image provides a summary of information to determine a dependent
child for inclusion in the family pool for PIT.

-   For YA job seekers, processing is done by Service Officers in the
    Service Centre
-   For YA students, processing is done by Student Smart Centre
    Processing centre staff only. Look up Processing Service details
    for: Austudy/YAL Student Claims
-   For ABSTUDY, processing is undertaken by ABSTUDY Smart Centre
    Processing centre staff only. Look up Processing details for:
    ABSTUDY and ABSTUDY PES
-   For AIC, processing is undertaken by AIC Smart Centre Processing
    staff only. Look up Processing details for: Assistance for Isolated
    Children (AIC)
