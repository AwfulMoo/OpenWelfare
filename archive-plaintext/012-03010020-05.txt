Naming convention and storage location

At the beginning of each calendar year the Health Professional (HP)
records management files that are used to file large quantities of files
each year should have new folders created. As HP Records Manager
electronic files only have a certain capacity they can hold. For
example, Register a network organisation, Joint HI and My Health Record
application.
