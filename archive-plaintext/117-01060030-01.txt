This document outlines how to reactivate a compensation claim.

Reactivate a compensation claim with a status of finalised

Staff in the Compensation Recovery Team (CRT) are able to reactivate the
claim. All other users have browse access only.

A claim is reactivated to allow updates. It will revert to finalised
when the updates are complete.
