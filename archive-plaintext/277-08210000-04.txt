Delegations and Authorisations

International Service Officer 3 and above

-   determine if the case meets the criteria for a General Withdrawal
-   determine if the case meets the criteria for a Return

Non Compliance Service Officer 3 and above

-   determine if the case meets the criteria for a Return

Deceased Customer Management Service Officer 3/4 and above

-   determine if the case meets the criteria for a Return Customer
    Deceased

Specialist Residency Team Service Officer 3 and above

-   determine if the case meets the criteria for a Return No Longer
    Habitually Resident

International Central Authority Service Officer 3 and above

-   notify NZIR of a withdrawal or return
-   process the withdrawal or return by ending the case
