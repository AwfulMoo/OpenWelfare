This document outlines information on processing amended certificates
for New Zealand ‘In’ cases at the request of Inland Revenue, New Zealand
(NZIR). These are cases where the payer resides in Australia and the
payee in New Zealand (or the payee has left New Zealand but there are
still arrears owing).

The Resources page contains a link to mySupport, contact details,
macros, letters, user guides email template.
