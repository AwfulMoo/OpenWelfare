This page has the process for assessing urgent payment requests due to
Centrelink delay. The decision to approve or deny an urgent payment is
made by a Service Officer at the APS4 level or above, Service Delivery
Partner Service Officers or specific Smart Centre staff. These are staff
fully trained in urgent payments.
