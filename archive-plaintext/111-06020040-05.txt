Note: use the above document as a guide only. Staff should use available
tools such as Office Locator and the relevant Operational Blueprint
procedure for relevant business processes.

Only specialist staff should use the specialist service reasons. To book
a specialist appointment, refer to the relevant Operational Blueprint
procedure and select the appropriate Appointment Type.

Certain appointment types have different actions available for staff and
customers:

-   Staff complete actions through Customer First using the Centrelink
    Appointment System
-   Customers complete actions through myGov, Centrelink linked member
    services
