This document outlines how a customer or a Service Officer can change
how the customer gets their Medicare letters. Customers can view letters
in their myGov Inbox or have them posted.

Managing letters

[] A customer can manage how they get their Medicare letters using their
Medicare online account. They can also call.

Service Officers can change this in the Consumer Directory Maintenance
System (CDMS). Staff can also view a history of updates.

Customers can view most Medicare letters in their myGov Inbox. Some
letters still need to be posted.
