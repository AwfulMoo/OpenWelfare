This document outlines how to navigate and use common screens and
generic functions in Process Direct. For payment and process specific
information, see the relevant Operational Blueprint procedure.

Features

Process Direct is a web based platform used to process customer claims
and transactions. In Process Direct, staff can:

-   Process most major income support payment claims
-   Process Income and Asset updates, Medical Certificates, Advance
    Payments, Youth Allowance (YA) and ABSTUDY annual Parental Income
    Test (PIT) reviews
-   Reissue Rent Certificates
-   Make referrals and request documents for new claims and change of
    circumstance updates
-   Create, reindex and regenerate most claim types
-   Create and annotate DOCs and Notes
-   View digital images
-   Seek help or raise issues via the digital assistant (Roxy)

Common Template Versions (CPTv)

Process Direct is available in 2 versions - CPTv3 and CPTv5.

Most transactions are processed in CPTv5, however, the following
processes are still completed in CPTv3:

-   Child Care Subsidy claim processing
-   Status Resolution Support Service (SRSS)

The Resources page contains information on how to identify Process
Direct versions.
