This document outlines information for Service Officers about Child
Support estimates when a customer initially makes contact to elect to
make an estimate, or to discuss their situation when an estimate may be
an appropriate option.

The Service Officer role at this initial contact is to determine if an
estimate or other administrative option is appropriate.
