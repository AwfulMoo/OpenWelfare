This page contains information relating to financial investments that
are liable to deeming provisions. Some investments may be exempt from
deeming provisions in special circumstances.

Deeming exemptions

Deeming Exemption Register

This table describes information relating to the Deeming Exemption
Register that contains information about the income test and assets test
treatment of investments with companies and financial institutions in
financial difficulty. The register has information about these
investments where an application for exemption has been granted.
