If a child is in immediate danger or risk of harm you must act, and a
delegate must call 000. If you have concerns about a child’s safety,
conduct the risk and referral process.

Information needed for a social worker referral

This table describes how to refer an individual linked to the National
Redress Scheme to a social worker. Do not use the Social Work guided
procedure.
