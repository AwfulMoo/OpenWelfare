Centrelink Appointment System appointment types and parameters

Note: the above document is to be used as a guide only. Staff should
utilise available tools such as Office Locator and the relevant
Operational Blueprint procedure for relevant business processes.

Only specialist staff should use the specialist service reasons. To book
a specialist appointment, refer to the relevant Operational Blueprint
procedure and select the appropriate Appointment Type.

Certain appointment types have different actions available for staff and
customers. Staff actions are completed through Customer First using the
Centrelink Appointment System. Customer actions are completed through
myGov, Centrelink linked member services.
