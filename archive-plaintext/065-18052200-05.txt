Investigation process

Service Officer must undertake an investigation as part of their process
to accept or reject an event outside the 70 day and/or 2 year limit.

Investigation may include:

checking for duplicate delegate activities pending approval

reviewing care recipients’ profiles in the aged care payment systems

checking previous event submission

checking claims and payment statements

checking system notes (that is, care recipient notes, service notes,
provider notes etc.)

checking previous correspondence from the aged care service

checking the relevant Aged Care form, for example:

-   Aged Care Entry Records (ACER)
-   Application for the Oxygen and/or Enteral Feeding Supplement
-   Aged Care Home Packages transfer level of care for Aged Care
    recipients
-   Aged Care Application for the Dementia and Cognition Supplement in
    Home Care

receiving the documentary evidence from the aged care service to support
the requests to change an event outside the 70 day and/or 2 year limit
