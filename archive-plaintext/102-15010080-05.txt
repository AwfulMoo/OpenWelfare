Student Payments Helpdesk

If a customer provides proof that the information Services Australia has
about their loan is incorrect, staff must email the evidence to the
Student Payments Helpdesk.

Note: the Student Payments Helpdesk is for internal use only. Contact
details must not be given to customers.
