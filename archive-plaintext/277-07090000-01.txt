This document outlines the procedure to follow for the reconciliation of
income estimates.

Overview

This document describes the manner in which Child Support must reconcile
an estimate when Child Support decides the actual income is greater than
the original customer estimate.

For details on reconciling an estimate taken on or after 1 July 2010,
see Child support estimates - Reconcile (from 1 July 2011).

For details on accepting a new estimate, see Child support estimates
(post 1 July 2010). For details on reviewing an estimate, see Child
support estimates review (post 1 July 2010).

The Resources page contains a link to the Estimate reconciliation
checklist macro.
