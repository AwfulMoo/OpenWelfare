This document outlines how Specialised Provider Services (SPS) and
Program Management report privacy incidents for the Practice Incentives
Program (PIP) and Workforce Incentive Program (WIP) - Practice Stream

Privacy Information

A privacy incident occurs when Services Australia inappropriately
collects, stores, allows access to, uses, or discloses the personal
information of customers and or staff. PIP and WIP – Practice Stream
privacy incidents are reported as they happen.

Possible privacy incidents

Examples of possible privacy incidents include:

-   information sent to incorrect practice
-   documents uploaded to incorrect practice profile in PIP/WIP –
    Practice Stream Online
-   incorrect practice details entered (such as bank account details)
-   patient personal information disclosed
-   practice documents/information disclosed
