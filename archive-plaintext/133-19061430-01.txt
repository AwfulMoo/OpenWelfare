This document outlines self service options for Medicare customers.

Online services

Point of service claiming (by the health professional) is the quickest
and easiest way for customers to claim their Medicare benefit. The
Medicare benefit is paid into their nominated bank account.

If this service is unavailable, customers can submit their claim using
their Medicare online account or the Express Plus Medicare mobile app.

Customers have a range of online service options including:

-   make a Medicare claim
-   make a Thalidomide claim
-   enrol a newborn or add additional newborn documents
-   view their Medicare claim history
-   view a digital copy of their Medicare card
-   manage their Medicare card and the people on it (eligible customers
    only)
-   view Medicare Safety Net balance
-   confirm Medicare Safety Net family
-   check their child’s eligibility for the Child Dental Benefits
    Schedule
-   view, download and save their immunisation history
-   view, save, share and print their COVID-19 digital certificate
-   view and update their personal information
-   view their Healthcare Identifier
-   view and update organ donor details

The Resources page has links to more information, support and online
guides.
