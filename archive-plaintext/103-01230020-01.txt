This document outlines how to change the Personal Identification Number
(PIN) for a customer's BasicsCard.

Changing a BasicsCard PIN

A customer, or their payment nominee (if the BasicsCard was issued to
the payment nominee on behalf of the customer), can request to change
the PIN for their BasicsCard. This can be actioned by Service Officers
at:

-   Centrelink Service Centres
-   Remote Service Centres
-   Remote Servicing Team with Centrelink system connectivity, or
-   by contacting the Income Management Line

BasicsCard blocked due to incorrect PIN

A customer's BasicsCard will be automatically blocked by the Card
Transaction Provider (CTP) if the customer makes six incorrect PIN
attempts in a row.

The BasicsCard will remain blocked until the customer contacts Services
Australia.
