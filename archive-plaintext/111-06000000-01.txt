Customer First is an integrated ICT solution used by Services Australia
staff to support a range of functions across the agency. These include
workload management and appointments.

Access

To access and use Customer First, staff must have:

-   the relevant security role in ESSentials
-   all resources for the service delivery brand system, e.g. Medicare
    mainframe, eBusiness Portal, or Centrelink Income Security
    Integrated System (ISIS)

The Resources page describes the Customer First roles and contains
mySupport links for access to system resources.
