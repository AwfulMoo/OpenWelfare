Example text for Q999 letter

You were recently granted an exemption from your mutual obligation
requirements for personal reasons. The exemption period will finish on
{variable: exemption end date} and you will need to undertake compulsory
activities, in order to continue to receive your {Variable: Parenting
Payment, JobSeeker Payment, Youth Allowance, Special Benefit}.

If you would like to discuss any difficulties you may still have meeting
your agreed Job Plan requirements, please contact the agency to speak
with a Service Officer. The Service Officer can also arrange for you to
speak with a Services Australia Social Worker about supports and options
available for you. You may be eligible for an extension to your current
exemption for a further period of time.

If you would like to discuss your situation please contact us as soon as
possible on XXX.
