For staff in the Tasmanian Transport Programs (TTP) Team only

This page contains detailed information of the steps required for a
request of extension of time (EOT) for the lodgement of a claim or other
timeframes for Tasmanian Freight Equalisation Scheme (TFES) assistance.

Extension of time (EOT) to lodge TFES claim

Claimant process for extending time to lodge a claim

This table demonstrates the process for a claimant to request an
extension of time.
