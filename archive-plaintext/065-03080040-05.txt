Online task inviting customers to claim the CSHC

An online task will appear on a customer’s Centrelink online account
homepage inviting them to claim a Commonwealth Seniors Health Card
(CSHC) if their Age Pension cancelled due to any of the following
reasons:

-   INC - Excess income
-   ASS - Assets over limit
-   CMP - Precluded due to Compensation direct deductions or
-   COM - Compensation preclusion period
-   CFP – Failed to claim a foreign payment
-   NPB – Not permanently blind
-   E6F – Earning Preclude from 6 consecutive fortnights
-   E6P – Partner earnings preclude for 6 consecutive fortnights
-   EXR – Exceeds income/assets thresholds due to foreign exchange rate
    processing
-   FPD – Foreign pension direct deductions applied
-   VST – Voluntary surrender trust and company

The online task will not appear for those customers who:

-   already have a CSHC
-   have submitted a claim for CSHC
-   have had a CSHC claim rejected in the previous 13 weeks
