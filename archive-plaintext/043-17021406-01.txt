This document outlines how to update a claimant record for the Tasmanian
Freight Equalisation Scheme (TFES).

Change in business details

Claimants can update their details by:

-   accessing TFES online services, or
-   lodging a Registration and Change of Details (TAS020) form

Note: updates made online, may result in a record becoming ineligible.
If this occurs, the Tasmanian Transport Programs (TTP) team will action
the change.

Examples of updated include:

-   Claimant or company name or trading name
-   Australian Business Number (ABN)
-   Postal or physical address
-   Contact person or signatories
-   Bank Account details

The Resources page contains links to various websites for additional
information on Tasmanian Freight Equalisation Scheme (TFES) and links to
the TFES forms.
