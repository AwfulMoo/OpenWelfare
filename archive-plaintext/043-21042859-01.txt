For staff in the Tasmanian Transport Schemes (TTS) only.

This document explains the steps for Services Australia staff to
complete Tasmanian Freight Equalisation Scheme (TFES) claims using the
Express Process (EP).

EP is a risk-based approach for the processing of TFES claims.

The Resources page contains a link to the user guides, Services
Australia website and external websites.
