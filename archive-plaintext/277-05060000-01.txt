This document outlines the processing of registrable maintenance
liabilities which arise under court orders or court-registered
agreements.

Overview

Child Support can register and collect a child maintenance, spousal
maintenance or de facto maintenance entitlement arising from a court
order or a court-registered agreement. These are commonly referred to as
'stage 1 cases'.

Stage 1 court orders can be made under the Family Law Act 1975, the
Family Court Act 1997 (WA), the Matrimonial Causes Act 1959 or another
law of an Australian State or Territory.

Roles and responsibilities

Service Officer

-   Deal with Applications for Collection of Maintenance Payments, when
    received by telephone or written application (Form 1) and
-   Determine if an application is eligible and process the application

Service Officer (SO2)

-   Register or refuse to register a maintenance liability

Service Support Officer (SSO)

Provide advice on complex issues or when Service Officer requires
assistance.
