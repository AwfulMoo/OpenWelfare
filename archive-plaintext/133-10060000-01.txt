This document outlines how customers can register, remove and amend
their Australian Organ Donor Register (AODR) details online. They can do
this using their Medicare online account.

AODR services online

[] Customers over 18 years of age can use their Medicare online account
to:

-   view their registration status
-   register their organ and tissue donation decision
-   amend their organ and tissue donation decision
-   remove their organ and tissue donation decision
-   register their decision not to be an organ or tissue donor
-   opt out of getting invitations to register

Age restrictions

-   Customers must be over 18 to record their consent on the register
-   Customers 16 to 17 years can register their intent. Parental consent
    is required
-   Children under 16 years cannot register their consent on the
    register
