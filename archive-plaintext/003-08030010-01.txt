This document outlines how to identify the category under which
customers who claim Special Benefit (SpB) may be eligible.

Identifying the correct category of SpB

Identifying the correct category of SpB and applying it to the customer
is a very important aspect of SpB claim assessment.

The SpB category controls:

-   the maximum time the customer may remain on payments
-   the maximum rate the customer can be paid
