Removal of tumour, cyst, ulcer or scar

Removal of tumour, cyst, ulcer or scar (items 31206 to 31225)

Table 1: This table describes the MBS items and their descriptors for
removal by surgical excision (other than shave excision) of tumour,
cyst, ulcer or scar (other than a scar removed during the surgical
approach at an operation).

The descriptors for these items outline different lesion sizes, and the
amount of lesions removed. These items do not restrict with each other
as the items are specifically for different lesion sizes and the amount
of lesions removed Items 31220 and 31225 are not for excision of viral
verrucae (common warts) and seborrheic keratoses.

These items must be sent for histological examination.
