From 17 March 2021, Income Management (IM) customers based in the
Northern Territory (NT) can permanently transition to the Cashless Debit
Card (CDC) program. Customers on an income support payment who are
living in the NT and are not currently on IM, will be able to volunteer
for the Cashless Debit Card (CDC) from this date.
For more information on this process or to initiate an IM to CDC
transition, see Income Management (IM) to Cashless Debit Card (CDC)
Transition in the Northern Territory, which also contains staff
resources and links to the transition overview Intranet page for
additional information.

This document outlines information on eligibility for Supporting People
at Risk (SPaR) Income Management.

Eligibility criteria

To be eligible for SPaR Income Management the customer or their partner
must be in receipt of a category H welfare payment and:

-   Not subject to a measure that is higher in the Overview and
    hierarchy of Income Management measures
-   Not subject to a payment nominee arrangement with a Public Trustee
    of any state/territory
-   Not subject to a payment nominee arrangement where the nominee is an
    excluded payment nominee for Income Management purposes
-   Have been referred for Income Management by an approved referring
    authority in a designated SPaR area applicable for the
    state/territory
-   A valid notice has been received by the department regarding the
    customer's referral

Determining eligibility

The Services Australia Income Management Contact Officer (IMCO) will
determine if a person referred for Income Management by an approved
referring authority contact person is a mutual customer and is eligible
for Income Management.

Payments income managed under SPaR Income Management

Payments that can be income managed under SPaR are:

-   category H welfare payment
-   category I welfare payment

Category H welfare payments include some Department of Veterans' Affairs
(DVA) payments. This means that Services Australia may be income
managing a customer's DVA payments on behalf of DVA. There are certain
payments not subject to Income Management.
