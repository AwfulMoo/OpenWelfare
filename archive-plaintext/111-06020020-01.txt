This document outlines how to view, reschedule or cancel an appointment
in the Centrelink Appointment System using the Customer First workflow.

Finalising appointments

A separate procedure explains how to finalise a customer appointment.

Rescheduling an appointment

In some cases it is necessary to reschedule an appointment, and the
customer (or third party) may have to provide proof of why the
appointment was rescheduled. For example, a Medical certificate,
payslip.

[A self service option is available for customers] Customers can
reschedule most appointment types using their Centrelink online account
through myGov or the Express Plus Centrelink mobile app. Advise
customers to use this service if appropriate.

See the Resources page for a full list of appointments and parameters
for rescheduling.

Cancelling appointments

Customers who fail to attend some set appointments could incur a
penalty.

Reminders or advices about appointments

[A self service option is available for customers]Customers can view and
manage most of their appointments using their Centrelink online account
through myGov or the Express Plus Centrelink mobile app.

Contact about a claim

If a customer intends claiming an income support payment, make sure all
their contact details are current. See Contact in relation to an
intended claim (CLK).
