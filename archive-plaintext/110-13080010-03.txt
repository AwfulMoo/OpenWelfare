Risks to a customer's privacy have been identified. See Separating
Safely - protecting personal details to make sure the customer's
personal details are safe before progressing.

For staff conducting Relationship Status Interventions only

This page contains the actions for Compliance Officers (CO) to find the
match data and information to discuss, and what to do when the customer
does or does not respond to the initial letter.
