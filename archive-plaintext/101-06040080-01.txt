This document outlines information relating to the restoration of Low
Income Health Care Card (LIC).

LIC restoration qualification criteria

A customer's Low Income Health Care Card (LIC) can be restored, provided
the underlying cancellation reason was incorrect and the customer meets
the qualification criteria for LIC.

If a LIC has expired, then it cannot be restored. However, a renewal
activity can be completed up to 13 weeks from the expiry date of the
LIC.
