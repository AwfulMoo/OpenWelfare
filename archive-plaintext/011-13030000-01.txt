This document outlines claiming information and procedures for the
External Breast Prostheses Reimbursement Program (EBPRP).

On this Page:

Claimable items

The EBPRP provides a reimbursement of up to $400 for each new or
replacement external breast prostheses for women who have had breast
surgery as a result of breast cancer.

Non-claimable items

The EBPRP does not cover purchases for products such as:

-   breast prostheses purchased before 1 July 2008
-   bras and other foundation products
-   post-surgery swimwear
-   internal prostheses
-   nipple inserts
-   external breast prostheses covers
-   damaged or lost prostheses
-   faulty prostheses needing replacement within the 2 year period.
    These should be covered by the supplier's warranty, the claimant
    should contact the supplier to seek a replacement
-   prostheses as a result of Poland's Syndrome
-   prostheses as a result of surgery not related to breast cancer.
