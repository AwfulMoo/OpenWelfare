This document outlines information for Service Officers helping users
update their logon details or change their personal details or account
status in their Provider Digital Access (PRODA) account.

Types of updates

Users may need help with the following updates to their PRODA account:

-   forgotten username or password
-   reset their password
-   update their security questions
-   set up the mobile app
-   mobile app settings
-   change their preference for the delivery method of their
    verification code
-   look up Registration Authority (RA) number
-   cancel their account
-   recover their cancelled account
-   update their contact details
-   change their name
-   add Medicare identifier numbers (Health Professional Online Services
    (HPOS) only)
-   session time out

The Resources page contains PRODA screenshots for these processes, a
list of identifiers for Healthcare providers and administrators, and
links to frequently asked questions and contact us information for PRODA
users.
