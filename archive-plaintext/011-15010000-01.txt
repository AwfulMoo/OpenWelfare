This document outlines information about case management in Medicare
Compensation Recovery claims and includes links to relevant procedures.

Background

Case management in Medicare Compensation Recovery covers the handling of
Medicare Compensation Recovery claims. Before releasing any information
about a claim, customers must be authenticated. There are many steps
involved in the processing of claims with claims being either notifiable
and not notifiable.
