This document outlines the procedure for receiving and transferring
claims for Essential Medical Equipment Payment (EMEP) received at
Medicare service centres.

EMEP

EMEP is an annual payment issued to eligible customers who have
additional increased home energy costs from the use of essential medical
equipment required to manage a disability or for heating and cooling of
a home required to manage a medical condition.
