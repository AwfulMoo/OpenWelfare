This document outlines a summary of Employment Services Assessment
(ESAt) automatic activities, including their purpose and action required
by staff.

Automatic activities

There are a number of different automatic activities created to support
the ESAt referral process. The Process page contains specific keywords,
activity codes and activity text which will assist staff to identify
ESAt automatic activities and action these appropriately. This includes
activities to flag:

-   customers who require an ESAt
-   the progress of started ESAts
-   customers whose payments can be restored after failing to attend
    their ESAt appointment
-   submitted reports awaiting acceptance
