This page contains list of codes shown against rejected electronic funds
transfer (EFT) payments on Medicare Government Direct Entry Services
(GDES) reports and useful source codes to help with locating forms.

Useful source codes

eServices

Locate the original form in PaNDA under folder 'Miscellaneous- eServices
Provider Banking Details’ for source codes:

-   CYES, AYES, GAPA, ATIQ, CDHI, CDBD, EMEB, IATB

Provider Registration

Locate the original form in PaNDA under folder ‘Provider Registration -
Application’ or ‘Provider Registration - Health’ for source codes:

-   AJOC, EMCC, MNWA, CMAC, CDPR, GAPA, IATB, IATC, IATD

Search in the folders 'Miscellaneous' and 'Provider Registration' for:

-   GAPA and IATB

                                   

-   Services Australia ABN 90 794 605 008
