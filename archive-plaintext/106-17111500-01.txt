This document explains specific information about the International
Social Security Agreement with Estonia. It includes the process of
making a claim for Australian payment under the Agreement and coding of
Estonian payments.

Start date

The Agreement with Estonia started on 1 January 2018.

Claiming and benefits covered

The Estonian Agreement generally allows customers to lodge a claim for
payment from either country. It also allows customers to add together
periods of residence in Australia during their working life and periods
of social security coverage in Estonia, to meet the minimum requirements
for payment.

Australian payments covered:

-   Age Pension

Customers can claim for Australian payments under Agreements. See Claims
for Australian payments under International Agreements.

Estonian payments covered:

-   Old Age Pension (AGE)
-   Survivor Pension (SUR)

Customers can claim a foreign pension by contacting Services Australia
or the Estonian authorities.
