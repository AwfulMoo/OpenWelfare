This document outlines information on debt recovery processes for
External Breast Prostheses Reimbursement Program (EBPRP).

Overpayments

Debt recovery is undertaken after it has been identified that a claimant
has been overpaid and is required to return the outstanding funds to
Services Australia.

There can be various reasons that a claimant may be incorrectly paid,
these can include:

-   The claimant has contacted Services Australia and enquired as to why
    they have received the payment
-   The overpayment was identified during the Quality Control process or
    other assurance
-   There is an identified intertwined record
-   The claimant has not had breast surgery as a result of breast cancer
    related reasons
-   The Service Officer processed the claim incorrectly or the records
    were accidentally mixed during processing
