Standard withholding rates for FTB payments

Table 4

If a customer has a Family Assistance reconciliation debt in additional
to another type of debt, the following standard withholding rates apply
to their FTB payment if the customer is not receiving an income support
payment.

+-----------------------------------+-----------------------------------+
| If the customer receives          | Offer amount                      |
+-----------------------------------+-----------------------------------+
| More than base rate FTB Part A    | 25 percent of total FTB           |
|                                   | entitlement (may include FTB Part |
|                                   | B)                                |
+-----------------------------------+-----------------------------------+
| Base rate FTB Part A              | 95 percent of total FTB           |
|                                   | entitlement (may include FTB Part |
|                                   | B)                                |
+-----------------------------------+-----------------------------------+
| Base rate FTB Part B              | 95 percent of payment             |
+-----------------------------------+-----------------------------------+
