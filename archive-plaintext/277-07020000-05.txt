This page contains links and tools relating to income processing for
Child Support customers 1 July 2008 to 29 June 2011 (21 November 2012
for WA ex-nuptial cases).

Income amendment date of effect

This table describes when an income amendment to an assessment will take
effect from.

  ------------------------- -------------------------------------------- ----------------------------------------------------------------------------
  The supplied income is?   Can parent lodge their tax return on time?   When is the default income replaced?
  Higher than default       N/A                                          For the entire Child Support Period
  Lower than default        Yes                                          For the entire Child Support Period
  Lower than default        No                                           From the day Child Support was notified unless special circumstances apply
  ------------------------- -------------------------------------------- ----------------------------------------------------------------------------

Note: a new assessment will be generated when an income issues before
the 15th of the month. If an income issues after the 15th of the month,
the new assessment will generate the following month.

For more information, see the Child Support Guide 2.3: Child Support
Periods.
