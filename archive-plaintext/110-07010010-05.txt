How to identify a DSLD online review activity

Service Officers using Workload Management may be allocated a general
Work Item (for example: scanned document, earnings update), for a
student selected for the DSLD online review. While in the student’s
record, Service Officers are encouraged to complete all outstanding
business within their scope, except for the following DSLD review
activities:

Reassess Benefit (REA) activity on the Activity List (AL) screen:

-   the Keywords (KWD) screen contains #ZCOC and DSLDCOC

Integrated Review in the Interaction History:

-   Description is DSLD
-   Activity Reason is Students Digital Study Load Declaration

Change of Circumstances Interaction in the Interaction History. Select:

-   Categories tab
-   Categorisation 1 - Compliance
-   Categorisation 2 - Student Digital Study Load Declaration

Failed CRM activity on the Activity List (AL) screen where the Keywords
(KWD) screen contains at least 1 of the following:

-   RESDDI
-   SUSDDI
-   CANDDI
-   DDIERR

Debt activity on the Activity List (AL) screen:

-   the Keywords (KWD) screen contains either DQRDDI or DSLD
