This page contains links to guides and documentation that will support
Compliance Officers (CO) in the processing of Manual Income Confirmation
(MIC) interventions, including links to Assessing Additional Risk,
Assessing and Coding Income and Assets, Source Document Options,
Documenting requirements for MIC Interventions, MIC Intervention text
for customer and third party verification, the ATO information request
form, deceased customer, contact details and a link to the Customer
Compliance online Service desk. Links on this page may be unavailable to
external audiences. It also contains examples of examples to assist COs
in determining if the evidence is sufficient and if Verbal updates are
reasonable.
