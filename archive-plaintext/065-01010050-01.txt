This document outlines information on claiming the Commonwealth Senior
Health Card (CSHC).

On this Page:

Eligibility for the CSHC

To be eligible for the CSHC a customer must meet all eligibility
criteria. See Eligibility for a Commonwealth Seniors Health Card (CSHC).

Claiming Commonwealth Seniors Health Card (CSHC)

Customers must be encouraged to complete a claim for a Commonwealth
Seniors Health Card (CSHC) via a digital channel. See Claiming
Commonwealth Seniors Health Card.

Entitlement on the CSHC

The CSHC may entitle the cardholder to:

-   discounts on prescription medicines listed under the Pharmaceutical
    Benefits Scheme (PBS)
-   bulk-billed General Practitioner (GP) appointments at the discretion
    of the GP
-   reduced out-of-hospital medical expenses through the Medicare Safety
    Net

In some instances, additional health, household, transport, education
and recreation concessions may be offered by state or territory and
local governments and private providers. However, these providers offer
these concessions at their own discretion, and the availability of these
concessions may vary from state to state.

See Commonwealth Seniors Health Card (CSHC) concessions for further
information.

Queries on the CSHC

Often customers enquiring about eligibility for CSHC do not qualify for
an Age Pension due to income or assets being too high, however Service
Officers should check Qualification for Age Pension.
