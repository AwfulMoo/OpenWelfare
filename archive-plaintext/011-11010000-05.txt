Organ Donor registration via Medicare Claim form (MS014)

This table contains information for Service Officers when an organ donor
form is received via a Medicare Claim form (MS014). Before processing a
MS014 form, Service Officers must check the Medicare claim or organ
donor registration sections contain all the required information and
adheres to AODR business rules. See Processing rules for Australian
Organ Donor Register (AODR).
