This document contains the instructions for processing and maintaining
care records.

Types of notifications covered in this document

-   Disputed care notifications on or after 23 May 2018
-   General care notifications on or after 1 July 2018
-   WA Ex-nuptial case care notifications on or after 15 May 2019,
    effecting only Child Support Periods (CSPs) with a WA Ex-nuptial
    status of No

For all other situations, see:

-   Change in child support care levels before 1 July 2018 (15 May 2019
    for WA-Ex-nuptial cases),and
-   Care Cuba Process Help - Care levels notified before 1 July 2018 and
    in WA Ex-nuptial child support periods.
