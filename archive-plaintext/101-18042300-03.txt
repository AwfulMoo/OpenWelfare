Pre-claim processing

Note: for LIC claims more than 10 months old where error E0031 presents,
navigate to the Customer First Tab, Table 5 for processing steps needed.
After the claim has been finalised in Customer First/Customer Record,
the claim activity in Process Direct will need to be set to ’not
effective’.
