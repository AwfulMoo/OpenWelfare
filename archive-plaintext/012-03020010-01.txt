This document outlines the initial security check that is needed when
taking a call from a Responsible Officer (RO), Organisation Maintenance
Officer (OMO) or Contracted Service Provider (CSP) officer calls to make
a request on behalf of an organisation, as well as what information can
be provided.

The Resources page contains information on what details may be provided
in response to such a phone call including links to forms and contact
details for Tier 2 Service support.
