For information on instances where the amount of Child Care Subsidy
(CCS) received is more than the amount than they should have been
received, see:

A New Tax System (Family Assistance) (Administration) Act 1999

-   section 71B Debts in respect of CCS or ACCS - no entitlement
-   section 71C Debts in respect of CCS or ACCS - overpayments

For information on the different methods of account recovery available
for CCS debts, according to whether the debtor is an individual or an
approved child care service provider see:

A New Tax System (Family Assistance) (Administration) Act 1999

-   section 82 Methods of recovery
-   section 87A Setting off debts of an approved provider against child
    care service payments
-   section 69 Special provisions relating to approved providers
