This page contains information about how the card type and entitlement
are displayed in the Department of Veterans’ Affairs (DVA) authority
system and a link to contact details for Veterans' Affairs
Pharmaceutical Advisory Centre (VAPAC).

On this Page:

Card type display and veteran entitlement

In the DVA authority system, the Repatriation Benefits card (RPBC) card
type is treated the same as for PTEC (gold card) veterans who have
eligibility to all items, including authority items, within PBS and
Repatriation Pharmaceutical Benefits Scheme (RPBS).

The card type displayed on all DVA authority screens reflects the
highest level of entitlement at the current date.

Entitlement display on RPQE screen

The Patient Enquiries - Eligibility Details screen (RPQE) displays RPBC
in the ENT DESC field to denote entitlement under the orange card.

Note: queries about more information of DVA entitlements are to be
referred to VAPAC.
