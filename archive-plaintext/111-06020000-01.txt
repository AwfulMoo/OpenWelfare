System functionality

The Centrelink Appointment System:

-   is a scalable agency-wide appointment system
-   streamlines staff interactions when managing customer's appointments
    and appointment profiles across the Centrelink service brand
-   has been developed to provide an innovative, intuitive and effective
    service that encourages responsibility and self-sufficiency for
    customers allowing staff to support those most in need
-   allows appointments to be booked and managed by staff, and with most
    appointment types able to be rescheduled by customers
-   works with the Personal Communications channel hierarchy to notify
    and remind customers of their appointments using electronic channels
    or physical letters where the customer does not use Electronic
    Messaging
