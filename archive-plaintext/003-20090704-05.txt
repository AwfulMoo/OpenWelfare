Pandemic Leave Disaster Payment - South Australia

Claim forms

Period of self-isolation, quarantine or caring that starts:

-   between 9 December 2021 and 17 January 2022 (inclusive), use the
    Claim for Pandemic Leave Disaster Payment (EM252) form
-   before 9 December 2021, (that is, up to and including 8 December
    2021), take a claim for this period over the phone using the verbal
    claim template. If the customer is not able to submit a claim via
    phone, use the Claim for Pandemic Leave Disaster Payment – South
    Australia (EM227) form

Scenarios - general

-   between 9 December 2021 and 17 January 2022 (inclusive), see
    Scenarios - from 9 December 2021
-   before 9 December 2021 (that is, up to and including 8 December
    2021), see Scenarios - before 9 December 2021

If a South Australian resident tries to claim for the Victoria Pandemic
Leave Disaster Payment, see Scenarios - eligibility for either South
Australia or Victoria Pandemic Leave Disaster Payment.
