Employment Income Confirmation (EIC) and Check and Update Past Income
(CUPI) Operational Blueprint pages are under review. Compliance Officers
must seek advice from the Policy Engagement Team before actioning an EIC
or CUPI. Contact the team by the CADO Service Desk page.

This page contains the process for Compliance Officers (CO) who are
actioning returned correspondence for compliance interventions. This
includes the check and update past income (CUPI) service, Employment
Income Confirmation (EIC), Manual Income Confirmation (MIC), Business
Income and Bank Interest (BIBI) and Business and other income types
(BOIT).

On this page:

Initial contact letter returned or tracking expired for CUPI or BOIT

Initial contact request where the letter is unmapped for CUPI or BOIT

Initial contact letter returned or tracking expired for EIC, MIC or BIBI

Initial contact request where the letter is unmapped for EIC, MIC or BIB

Record the letter as delivered

Processing required when initial contact letter undeliverable

Processing for non-current customers where initial contact letter
undeliverable

Reissue the initial contact letter

Partner notification letter for CUPI or BOIT service has been returned
or tracking status has expired

Partner notification letter request for CUPI or BOIT service has reason
of Unmapped Aus Post Delivery status
