Managed investments

Managed investments may include:

-   Life office and friendly society insurance bonds
-   Public unit trusts such as cash management, mortgage, shares
    (equity) and bond trusts
-   Unlisted property trusts
-   Superannuation and rollover investments of customers who are Age
    Pension age, such as investments in approved deposit funds, deferred
    annuities and superannuation funds
-   Margin loan (which is a loan that uses shares and managed
    investments as security for borrowing)
-   Timeshare investments from December 2015

Note: From 6 March 2010, all listed property trusts on an Australian
stock exchange must be updated in the shares and securities.
