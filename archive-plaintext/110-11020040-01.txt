For Intelligence and Investigations Branch only

This document outlines how to record details of the progress of a
prosecution after acceptance of the prosecution referral by the
Commonwealth Director of Public Prosecutions (CDPP).

Details to be recorded

The following details are to be recorded on the investigation record as
the case progresses:

-   charges as notified by the CDPP
-   summons details
-   court proceedings before and after scheduled court hearings
-   penalties after the final court proceeding, if penalties were
    imposed
-   appeal details if the customer or Services Australia appeals the
    verdict

The Resources page contains a link the CDPP website.
