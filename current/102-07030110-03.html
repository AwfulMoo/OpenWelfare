Commencing or returning to work for Pensioner Education Supplement (PES), ABSTUDY PES and Assistance for Isolated Children (AIC) PES 102-07030110

This page contains information on how to reassess a student's Pensioner Education Supplement (PES) ABSTUDY PES or Assistance for Isolated Children (AIC) PES entitlement when they have commenced or returned to work.

Assessing return to work

PES, ABSTUDY PES or AIC PES student commencing or returning to work

This table describes the steps to follow when a customer in receipt of PES, ABSTUDY PES or AIC PES commences or returns to work.

Step

Action

1

Request details from customer + Read more ...

Obtain the following details from the student, or the AIC applicant in the case of the AIC PES student:

  • Date of commencement of employment
  • If applicable, the last date that full-time or 25% concessional study load study was undertaken or enrolled (whichever is the earlier)

2

Effect of work on PES, ABSTUDY PES or AIC PES + Read more ...

Check whether the customer or student's work or level of income will affect their eligibility for their PES or ABSTUDY PES qualifying payment.

For information about return to work procedures for the various payments paid under the Social Security Act 1991 that can attract PES, ABSTUDY PES or AIC PES, see:

  • Commencing or returning to work Disability Support Pension (DSP)
  • Commencing or returning to work for Carer Allowance (CA)
  • Commencing or returning to work Carer Payment (CP)
  • Commencing or returning to work or self-employment Parenting Payment Single (PPS)
  • Commencing or returning to work or self-employment Parenting Payment Partnered (PPP)
  • Commencing or returning to work Special Benefit (SpB)
  • Commencing or returning to work for JobSeeker Payment (JSP) and Youth Allowance (job seeker)
  • Commencement of full-time work for Assistance for Isolated Children (AIC) Scheme students
  • Commencing or returning to self employment Disability Support Pension (DSP)
  • Commencing or returning to self-employment for JobSeeker Payment (JSP), Youth Allowance (YA) and Austudy customers

For payments paid under the Veteran's Entitlement Act, the customer will need to provide evidence of their continuing entitlement to their PES or ABSTUDY PES qualifying payment (e.g. letter from the Department of Veterans' Affairs (DVA).

Note: Parenting Payment Single and Disability Support Pension are the only qualifying payments for AIC PES.

3

Eligibility for PES, ABSTUDY PES or AIC PES + Read more ...

Is the customer still eligible for their PES, ABSTUDY PES or AIC PES qualifying payment?

  • Yes, go to Step 4
  • No, go to Step 7

4

Check if the customer is still undertaking PES, ABSTUDY PES or AIC PES qualifying study + Read more ...

For information about the qualifying study rules for ABSTUDY PES and AIC PES and PES, see:

  • Determining approval of mainstream or Indigenous courses for ABSTUDY
  • Assistance for Isolated Children (AIC) Scheme Pensioner Education Supplement (PES) qualifications
  • Assessing qualifying study for Pensioner Education Scheme (PES)

5

PES, ABSTUDY PES or AIC PES qualifying study + Read more ...

Is the customer still undertaking PES, ABSTUDY PES or AIC PES qualifying study?

  • Yes, go to Step 6
  • No, go to Step 8

6

Student is still eligible for PES, ABSTUDY PES or AIC PES + Read more ...

  • Code the customer's income from earnings on the Employment Income Details (EAN) screen. See Recording and correcting employment income details and choose the procedure which is appropriate the type of benefit, pension or allowance and the type of earnings.
  • Advise them to contact within 14 days if there is any change in their circumstances
  • Record detail of employment and earnings on a DOC

Procedure ends here.

7

Customer is no longer eligible for PES, ABSTUDY PES or AIC PES + Read more ...

When the student's PES, ABSTUDY PES or AIC PES qualifying payment is cancelled, PES, ASTUDY PES or AIC PES payments should cancel automatically. Note: if for any reason this does not occur, cancel the customer's PES payment manually.

8

Customer is no longer eligible for PES, ABSTUDY PES or AIC PES + Read more ...

Cancel the customer's PES payment.
