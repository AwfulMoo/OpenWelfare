This page explains most of the questions on the (SY017) form.

Questions on the Unreasonable to live at home (UTLAH) statement by independent third party

This table expands upon questions on the Unreasonable to live at home statement by independent third party (SY017). Not all questions have been included.

Question

Description

Question 3

What is your relationship with the young person and their family? + Read more ...

A brief description of how they know the young person.

Question 12-15

How do you know the young person and their family? + Read more ...

  • A brief description of the length of your relationship and what contact have you had with them
  • Describe any contact they have had with the parent(s)

Question 24

Do the young person's parents live together? + Read more ...

If no, need to indicate if the young person has had contact with their 'other parent' (the parent the young person was not living with) in the past 2 years. If so, they also need to describe the relationship between the young person and the other parent.

Question 8-9

Is the young person currently living at home with their parents? + Read more ...

No, need to indicate when the young person left their parent's home, with an exact date if known.

Questions 19-24

Problems between the young person and their parent(s) + Read more ...

Need to state (to the best of their knowledge) what sort of problems there are, including:

  • how long the problems have been going on
  • any way the young person or their parent(s) have tried to sort out the difficulties (including what has been tried and any names of people or organisations they contacted) and whether this may have helped
  • if the young person is allowed to return home
    • No, state why not
    • Yes, describe any conditions that may apply

They should include as much detail or examples as possible and extra paper can be attached if there is not enough room on the form.

Question 23

Do you think it is unreasonable for the young person to live at home? + Read more ...

Need to state yes or no and give details according to the guidelines in the covering information page, for example extreme family breakdown, serious risk to physical or mental well-being, parents unable to provide a suitable home.

Question 30

Are the young person's parent(s) or anyone else providing money or other support to the young person? + Read more ...

If yes they need to provide details including who is providing this, how often and what type of support it is (for example, food, paying bills, subsidising rent, school fees, regular money).

Question 31

Is there any other information you can provide that will help Services Australia decide if it is unreasonable for the young person to live at home? + Read more ...

Any additional information to go here, extra pages can be attached if needed or attach any other documents or reports which they feel may be helpful.
