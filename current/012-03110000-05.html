Description of organisation name details

Description of HPI-O name details for the HI Service

This table lists and describes various features of HPI-O names for the HI Service.

Organisation detail

Description

Organisation Name

Commonly the name of the business entity, for example, Robert J. Green Unit Trust.

Trading Name

Commonly the name of the surgery, for example, Greens Medical Centre.

Australian Standard

Australian Standard (AS4846) definitions for recording of organisation names:

  • "Business name - Business name used only for trading purposes".
  • "Enterprise name - generally, the complete organisation name should be used to avoid any ambiguity in identification. This should usually be the same as company registration name".

Other

Used when the organisation name does not fit into any one of the categories listed above.

Examples of HPI-O names, EHP options and help notes

This table provides examples of HPI-O names, suggestions for Service Officers to enter details in the eHealth Program (EHP) and help notes.

Example of information provided

Enter into the EHP

Notes

Organisation name: Medical Practice Services

Trading name: City Medical Centre

Australian Business Number (ABN) search: Medical Practice Services Pty Ltd
  • Medical Practice Services (select 'Enterprise Name')
  • City Medical Centre (select 'Business Name')
  • ABN name matches (without Pty Ltd) the organisation name provided; we can confirm that the ABN belongs to this business and the business is a legal entity. Enter this name into EHP and select Enterprise name.
  • Trading name should be added as provided and select business name.
  • Ignore the tick box provided by the applicant.
  • Where the organisation is not a known customer one of the 10 acceptable documents required for the seed organisation must still be supplied.

    Organisation name: Brisbane Seven Day Medical Centre

    Trading name: Not provided (left blank)

    ABN search: Medicines R us Pty Ltd

    Trading Name: Brisbane Seven Day Medical Centre

    Brisbane Seven Day Medical Centre (select 'Business Name')
    • Only one name is required to be entered, as only one was supplied.
    • ABN trading name matches organisation name provided by applicant; we can confirm that the ABN belongs to this business and the business is a legal entity. Enter this name and select Business name.
    • Ignore the tick box provided by the applicant.
    • Where the organisation is not a known customer one of the 10 acceptable documents required for the seed organisation must still be supplied.

    Organisation name: XYZ NSW Pty Ltd

    Trading name: City South Medical Centre

    ABN search: The Trustee for City South Medical Centre Trust
    • City South Medical Centre (select 'Enterprise Name')
    • XYZ NSW PTY Ltd (select 'Other name')
  • ABN name matches closely the trading name provided; we can confirm that ABN belongs to this business and the business is a legal entity. Enter this name into EHP and select Enterprise name.
  • Organisation name should be added and select 'other name'.
  • Ignore the tick box provided by the applicant.
  • Where the organisation is not a known customer one of the 10 acceptable documents required for the seed organisation must still be supplied.
