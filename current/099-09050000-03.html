This page contains the process to follow when a Widow Allowance (WA) customer advises they are leaving Australia.

System coding

Step

Action

1

Widow Allowance (WA) customer advises they are leaving Australia + Read more ...

Run the Portability Script - Departures and Returns. Use the script when available.

The customer may contact after using the online Travelling outside of Australia service. If the online service has already coded the absence, details are recorded in a DOC. The online service may refer the customer to contact to have their absence coded, or explore further portability for their situation. In either case, the Service Officer is to run the script.

Is the script working?

  • Yes, see Coding absence using the Portability Script (departures and returns) for customer leaving Australia. Procedure ends here
  • No, and the interview cannot be delayed, go to Step 2
  • No, and the interview can be delayed:
    • Book an appointment to be completed by the customer's local Service Centre
    • Ensure customer contact details are correct
    • Record details on a DOC
    • Procedure ends here

2

Length of absence + Read more ...

Will the overseas absence be temporary?

  • Yes, go to Step 5
  • No, go to Step 3

3

Customer leaving Australia to live in another country + Read more ...

  • The customer's payment will cancel from date of departure. Any add-ons will also cease on departure from Australia. Advise the customer they will not be able to reclaim WA if they return to live in Australia again in the future. If they require income support on their return they must claim another payment as Widow Allowance is closed to new claims
  • Check if the customer has employment income or is a 2WE/variable lodgement reporter. If they are, see Recording and correcting employment income details
  • Explain the decision to the customer
  • Record all details of the interview in a DOC including any overseas contact details
  • Give the customer the contact details for Centrelink International Services (CIS)
  • Check if the customer has any further queries and, if not, the interview can be terminated
  • If requested by the customer, issue a Pre Departure Interview - Portability Decision (XOB101) letter

4

Recording that the customer is leaving Australia to live in another country + Read more ...

  • Record the change of address on Address Details (AD) screen if the customer has provided new address details. Note: it is not mandatory for the customer to provide an address outside Australia
  • Record the overseas telephone number if available
  • Go to the Customer Advised Travel Details (RSCD) screen
    • Code the date of departure in the Event date: field
    • Code the destination country in the Country: field. Code 'XT' if the customer is travelling to multiple destinations or the destination country is unknown
    • Leave the Reason: End date: and Ext Rsn: fields blank
    • Code Source: and DOR: fields if required
    • Press [Enter]
  • Update the Country of Residence (CRES) screen
  • Go to the Assessment Results (AR) screen and check payment will cease from the date of departure for a residence related reason
  • Finalise the activity. Do not suppress the auto advice

Procedure ends here.

5

Studying overseas + Read more ...

Is the customer leaving Australia to complete full time study as part of an Australian course?

  • Yes, payment can continue until the end of the units of study overseas as long as the customer stays enrolled in full time study as part of their approved Australian course. For CIS referral process, see Student studying outside Australia. Procedure ends here.
  • No, go to Step 6

6

Complete the portability interview + Read more ...

  • Advise the customer payment is payable for maximum portability period of up to 6 weeks. If the customer remains outside Australia after their payment has ceased to be portable, their payment will be suspended
  • Advise customer about the continuation of payment on their return to Australia. If they remain outside Australia more than 13 weeks after their payment has suspended, their payment may be cancelled. As WA closed to new claims from 1 July 2018, they will not be able to reclaim WA on their return
  • Ask the customer for any contact address or phone number while overseas
  • Ask the customer if they wish to nominate an agent to act on their behalf while they are overseas. If so, record the details in a DOC
  • Determine if the customer on return has to contact when they have returned to Australia and discuss this before the customer's departure to correctly assess their ongoing entitlements

Check if the customer has employment income is a stimulus customer

  • Check if the customer has employment income or is a 2WE/ variable lodgement reporter. If yes, see Recording and correcting employment income details

Finalise interview

  • Check if the customer has any further queries
  • If requested by the customer, issue a Pre Departure Interview - Portability Decision (XOB101) letter

7

Recording the temporary overseas absence + Read more ...

  • Record the change of address on Address Details (AD) screen if the customer has provided new address details. Note: it is not mandatory for the customer to provide an address outside Australia
  • Record the overseas telephone number if available
  • Update any change in rent details or accommodation. See Completing the Accommodation Details (AC) screen and assessing Rent Assistance (RA)

8

Record the departure + Read more ...

  • Go to the Customer Advised Travel Details (RSCD) screen
    • Code the date of departure in the Event date: field
    • Code the destination country in the Country: field. Code 'XT' if the customer is travelling to multiple destinations, the destination country is unknown, or they are travelling outside Australia on a cruise
    • Leave the Reason: End date: and Ext Rsn: fields blank
    • Code Source: and DOR: fields if required
    • Press [Enter]
  • If the customer knows their exact return date, return to the RSCD screen. Record a second line with the date of return in the Event date: field and Australia in the Country: field. Leave the Reason: End date: and Ext Rsn: fields blank.
  • Go to the Assessment Results (AR) screen and finalise the activity. Do not inhibit the advice
