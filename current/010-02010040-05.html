ABSTUDY request for private board provider details form

If a secondary student is boarding privately, the ABSTUDY request for private board provider details form must be issued to the person responsible for making payment decisions.

The person responsible for redirecting payments to a private board provider is either a:

  • parent/guardian for a dependant student under 18 years of age
  • student 18 years of age or over
  • student claiming independence

ABSTUDY request for private board provider details form

This form cannot be used to verify rent for the purposes of Rent Assistance (RA). A rent certificate will need to be issued if the customer is claiming RA.

ABSTUDY Claiming Tool (ACT) FAQ's

Table 1: This table provides information on the ABSTUDY Claiming Tool (ACT) processes and handy hints and tips.

Question

Description

1

What happens to the ACT once the claim is finalised?

The ACT retains the answers provided. The processing copy is uploaded to the student's record, except for Schooling A claims. These claims are uploaded to the applicant's record. The processing copy can be located via Document Tools in Customer First menu.

A NCL activity will be indexed and the details recorded in a NCL progress DOCument. The activity is then transferred (O) to the ABSTUDY Smart Centre teams for finalisation.

2

Can there be more than one claim at a time?

Yes - multiple claims can now be completed.

Schooling A claims for more than one student can be recorded within the same ACT workflow.

For all other ACT claims, the instructions in Table 4 must be completed before commencing another ACT claim.

3

How do I re-print the Customer Declaration Form (CDF)?

The CDF can be re-printed from the History Summary (HS) screen.

To re-print and identify the CDF, use the letter code:

  • ABY - Student claiming - ABYYBALA
  • ABY - Parent claiming - ABYYBALP
  • ABT - Applicant claiming - ABYYBALS
  • PES - Student claiming - ABYYBALA

Select the twisty next to the letter required.

Select Print Local.

On the Letter Print (LPA) screen:

  • Copy: field - code 'Y'
  • Printer: field - code the required printer number
  • select Continue

4

How do I access a Processing copy after the NCL has been cancelled?

If the NCL activity has been cancelled, the processing copy can be accessed via Customer First - Document Tools menu. Staff can view the information recorded in the processing copy prior to re-indexing the NCL activity.

Documents required to support the ABSTUDY claim process

Table 2

Item

Description

1

Documents requested to support the claim before it can be assessed

Customer Declaration Form (CDF)

The applicant's acceptance of the verbal declaration will be recorded in a DOC on the student's record. Applicants who do not provide their acceptance to the verbal declaration must return a signed CDF.

Study details

The applicant must provide the student's study.

Secondary student unable to provide school details

A secondary boarding facility may conduct an interview or selection exercise prior to accepting a student. Therefore, at the time of claiming, confirmation of the student's school they will be attending may not be possible. The School Details is a mandatory field and must be completed. For these students in the School Details field, record the text 'To be advised'. This allows the ACT to be completed and the claim to be assessed. The school details must be advised as soon as possible.

Parental income

If parents/guardians cannot complete these questions or would prefer to provide their details separately, a MOD JY must be issued. The link to the MOD JY is above.

Where a student does not meet the independent criteria, the parental income test must be applied regardless of receiving parental support or not. In these cases issue the MOD JY.

Away from home

See Away from home rate for ABSTUDY for specific information or documentation required based on the student's circumstances.

Unreasonable to live at home

Students who identify as being unreasonable to live at home, may need to provide documentation to support their claim. See ABSTUDY unreasonable to live at home (UTLAH).

Independence

Students who state they will meet one of the Independence criteria, may be required to provide evidence before their claim can be assessed. See Independence for ABSTUDY customers.

Identity Confirmation

For information when Identify Confirmation is required, see Additional procedures exclusive to ABSTUDY apply in some circumstances.

Tax File Number (TFN)

Students who do not have, or know their TFN, must be given a Tax file number application or enquiry for Aboriginal or Torres Strait Islander (NAT1589) form for completion and lodgement within 14 days of lodging their claim.

See Tax File Number (TFN) for ABSTUDY for further information.

Proof of Enrolment (POE)

Students may be required to provide POE. See Proof of enrolment (POE) in a course of study.

Note: proof of acceptance is not required for boarding school students. Payment on Advice will determine if a student has been accepted or not.

Even though a proof of acceptance letter is not required to prove a student has been accepted, the letter may be accepted when the student is providing information for their away from home rate assessment, such as information relating to their scholarship or enrolment in a special program.

The progress DOC must be specific about the information required rather than just requesting proof of acceptance.
