Reassessing eligibility when a student is deferring or discontinuing study

Step

Action

Student on Austudy or YA has ceased or intends ceasing study, or will not continue same course in next study period + Read more ...

If the customer:

  • had an intention to commence a course and will not, or did not, commence their course. See Customer will not commence full-time study for Youth Allowance (YA), Austudy and Pension Education Supplement (PES)
  • is currently recorded as being in an 'Intention to Study' period (check EDC screen coding) or they have been Suspended - Failed to Provide Course Details (SUS-PCD). See Processing end of course reviews for Austudy and Youth Allowance (YA)

For all other customers, go to Step 2.

2

Ceased study due to a medical condition + Read more ...

Has the customer ceased full-time study due to a medical condition?

  • Yes, see the appropriate procedure below:
    • Austudy student or Australian Apprentice becomes incapacitated
    • Youth Allowance (YA) student or Australian Apprentice becomes incapacitated
  • No, go to Step 3

Has the customer started an Australian Apprenticeship?

  • Yes, investigate entitlement to ABSTUDY, Austudy or YA as an Australian Apprentice, see Existing customer starts an Australian Apprenticeship. Procedure ends here
  • No, go to Step 4

Part-time study + Read more ...

Is the customer now studying part-time?

  • Yes, see:
    • Changing study load from full-time to part-time or concessional study load for Youth Allowance (YA), Austudy or PES
    • Go to Step 1 in Changing study details to cancel payment when ceasing studies table
  • No, see Step 1 in Changing study details to cancel payment when ceasing studies table

Changing study details to cancel payment when ceasing studies

Table 2

Step

Action

1

Notification requirements + Read more ...

Students must advise they have ceased study within their notification period.

Statement reporters must notify changes on or before the Entitlement Period End Date (EPED) in which the change occurred.

Notification reporters must notify changes within 14 days.

Has the customer advised within their notification provisions?

  • Yes, go to Step 3
  • No, go to Step 2

Note: customers who contact the agency early and wish to advise that they are ceasing studies on a future date, will be always considered to have met their notification provisions.

2

Notification: customer has not advised in accordance with their notification period that they have ceased study + Read more ...

As the customer has failed to notify a change in circumstances within their notification period, they are no longer eligible for payment. Update the customer record as follows:

  • navigate to Education Course History (EDCH) screen and correct the Student End Date: field, to:
    • the date last studied, or
    • the date the customer ceased to have an intention to return to study
  • insert a Newstart Education Status (NES) entry:
    • Event Date: field, code the student end date plus 1 day
    • Student Status: field, code 'NST' (not a full-time student)
    • Education Level Attained: field, update if necessary

For customers under 22 years:

  • Navigate to the Benefit Action (BA) screen
  • Service Reason: field, select the customers payment type
  • Action: field, CAN - Cancel
  • Reason: field 'NST' - not a full-time student
  • Effect Date: field, new study end date plus 1 day.

Note: customers 22 years of age or over will not require manual cancellation via the BA screen. These customers will have their payment automatically cancelled from the updates made to NES and EDCH.

Customers wishing to receive further financial assistance must lodge a new claim.

Record the details of the decision to cancel on a DOC.

Go to Step 8.

3

Notification: is in the same or an earlier entitlement period + Read more ...

Is the change being actioned before, or in, the same entitlement period in which the change occurred?

  • Yes,
    • end the course of study on the Education Course History (EDCH) screen, correct the Student End Date: field to:
      the date last studied, or
      the date the customer ceased to have an intention to return to study
    • if the customer does not wish to receive further income support, payments will cancel from date coded on EDC + 1 day
    • go to Step 6
  • No, go to Step 4

4

Notification: change is being actioned in the entitlement period following the entitlement period in which the customer ceased study + Read more ...

Has the customer notified in accordance with their notification period, but the change is being actioned in the entitlement period following the entitlement period in which the customer finished the course, or ceased to have an intention to return to study?

  • Yes:
    • End the course of study on the Education Course History (EDCH) screen. Correct the Student End Date: field to the EPED of the entitlement period in which the change occurred
    • If the customer does not wish to receive further financial assistance, payments will cancel from EPED date + 1 day
    • Go to Step 6
  • No, go to Step 5

5

Notification: change is being actioned after the entitlement period following the entitlement period in which customer ceased study + Read more ...

Customer has notified in accordance with their notification period, but the change is being actioned after the entitlement period following the entitlement period in which the customer finished the course, or ceased to have an intention to return to study.

End the course of study on the Education Course History (EDCH) screen. Correct the Student End Date: field to the last day of the notification period. If the customer does not wish to receive further financial assistance, payments will cancel from the last day of the notification period +1 day.

6

Update Newstart Education Status (NES) screen + Read more ...

Update the NES screen:

  • Event Date: field, code the day after the Student End Date as recorded on the EDC screen
  • Student Status: field, code 'NST' (not a student)
  • Education Level Attained: field, update if necessary

Record the details of the decision to cancel on a DOC.

7

Does the customer want to continue to receive an income support payment? + Read more ...

  • Yes,
    • for a job seeker payment, see Student to Job seeker transfers
    • for an alternative income support, see Contact in relation to an intended claim (CLK)
  • No, and the customer is:
    • Under 22 years of age, cancel their YA payment with a reason of 'NST' (not a full-time student) via the Benefit Action (BA) screen with Event Date of study end date plus 1 day
    • 22 years of age or over, finalise the activity via Assessment Results (AR) to automatically cancel the customers payment

Record the details of the decision on a DOC.

Procedure ends here.

8

Debt incurred for failing to notify + Read more ...

Determine if the customer has incurred a debt for failing to notify of ceasing studies, see:

  • Raising debts resulting from a failure to report or notify and
  • Raising debts for Centrelink administered payments for students.

Record details of the decision to cancel and any other action taken on a DOC.
