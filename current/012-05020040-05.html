Revoke and reissue PKI Individual or Site certificates

Documents required for changes to be made to PKI Individual or Site certificates

This table describes the documents required for changes to be made to Public Key Infrastructure (PKI) Individual or Site certificates.

Note: a new PKI site certificate application is required when the Australian Business Number (ABN) has changed.

Type of change

Documents required

Change of Certificate Manager/Authoriser
  • Request to Revoke or Reissue a Public Key Infrastructure Certificate form (HW003)

Note: ensure that the correct signatures are applied on the relevant sections of the form, for instance, Authoriser’s signature on Q.17 and Certificate Manager signature on Q.18.

Is the Certificate Manager/Authoriser known to Services Australia?

  • No, provide full Evidence of Identity (EOI) and Acceptable Referee Declaration Public Key Infrastructure Certificate applications form (HW004)
  • Yes, include provider number and signature. Note: known customers can also be individuals with existing PKI certificates. They must supply their PKI Registration Authority (RA) number on the form

Note: in questions 11 of the Request Form, if certificate is not required, applicant must state ‘Change of Certificate Manager only, no CD required’.

Site moving to new location in a different suburb (no other changes to practice)
  • Request to Revoke or Reissue a Public Key Infrastructure Certificate form (HW003)
  • State on the Reason for requested action (Q.11) the date the new PKI site certificate (CD) is required
  • Ensure that the correct signatures are applied on the relevant sections of the form. Note: only 1 signature is required from either Certificate Manager, or Certificate Authoriser, providing details are the same as recorded in CIMS. If Certificate Manager has changed then EOI documentation, HW004 form and Certificate Authoriser signature are required
  • No other documents are required for this change
  • A new PKI site certificate (CD) will be sent to practice

Site moving to new location in the same suburb (no other changes to practice)
  • Request to Revoke or Reissue a Public Key Infrastructure Certificate form (HW003)
  • Advise change of address including the Registration Authority (RA) number signed by the Certificate Manager
  • State on the Reason for requested action (Q.11) Location move in same suburb
  • No PKI site certificate (CD) will be sent to the practice, information is updated by the eCertificates team for record purposes in the instance where a replacement CD is required

Change of practice name (no other changes to practice)
  • Request to Revoke or Reissue a Public Key Infrastructure Certificate form (HW003) with the affected RA number
  • State on the Reason for requested action (Q.11) the date the new PKI site certificate (CD) is required
  • Ensure that the correct signatures are applied on the relevant sections of the form, for instance, Authoriser’s signature on Q.17 and Certificate Manager signature on Q.18
  • A new PKI site certificate (CD) will be sent to practice

Renewal of Individual Certificate
  • Request to Revoke or Reissue a Public Key Infrastructure Certificate form (HW003) with the individual RA number
  • State on the Reason for requested action (Q.11) the request for Individual Certificate is for an approved programme
  • To continue to access HPOS will need to apply for a Provider Digital Access (PRODA) account

Note: individual PKI certificates may be issued in circumstances where they are unable to access the approved programme with PRODA.
