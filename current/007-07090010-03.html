Cancellation, rejection and Current Zero Rate codes for Family Tax Benefit (FTB) 007-07090010

This page contains information about new claim rejections associated with changes to the claim lodgement period for Family Tax Benefit (FTB). It does not cover information regarding not effective claims or withdrawal of claims for FTB or Stillborn Baby Payment (SBP).

Current Zero Rate (CZR) codes for Family Tax Benefit (FTB)

Table 2: This table contains a list of the FTB Current Zero Rate (CZR) codes.

Code

Description

Initiation

IEU

Income exceeds upper limit

Where an instalment FTB customer has provided a revised family income estimate reducing Part B to a nil rate due to exceeding the primary earner income limit.

Note: cancellation will apply if the source of the estimate is an uplift.

INR

Income Nil Rate

Instalment FTB has reduced to nil rate due to

  • FTB Part A rate reduction due to immunisation or Healthy Start for School requirement, or
  • FTB customer is in an ISP employment nil rate period and has provided an income estimate that is over the threshold to receive payments.

IPO

Instalment Payments Prohibited (OVR)

Instalment FTB was overridden, resulting in PIP being applied.

IPP

Instalment Payments Prohibited

Instalment FTB Prohibited.

IPQ

Instalment Prohibited (RCC)

Zero rate Regular Care customer’s FTB prohibited.

IPS

Instalment Prohibited (OVR RCC)

Zero rate Regular Care customer’s FTB was overridden resulting in PIP being applied.

ITH

Income Too High

Where an instalment FTB customer has provided a revised family income estimate that exceeds the allowable limit (reduces the legislative rate of payment to nil for FTB Part A or the secondary earner income test for FTB Part B).

Note: cancellation will apply if:

  • the source of the estimate is an uplift, or
  • the income threshold changes and the existing income estimate exceeds the new income threshold

MCA

Mandatory Continuous Adjustment

Where an instalment FTB customer has Mandatory Continuous Adjustment in place resulting in a zero rate.

MCO

Nil Pay More Choice Option

Where an instalment FTB customer has a More Choice For Families Payment Choice in place resulting in a zero rate.

PLA

Customer gets Parental Leave Pay or assessed for either reason EIR or EIA

FTB(B) is zero rate as the customer receives PPL or assessed for either reason EIR (Employer registration required) or EIA (Employer acceptance required).

PLB

Partner gets Parental Leave Pay or assessed for either reason EIR or EIA

FTB(B) is CZR as the customer’s partner receives PPL or assessed for either reason EIR (Employer registration required) or EIA (Employer acceptance required).

PLC

Elected on parental Leave Pay Claim

FTB(B) is CZR via option on the customer's PPL claim.

PLD

Elect on Parental Leave Pay Claim

FTB(B) is CZR via option on the partner's PPL claim.

RCC

Regular Care Children

Regular Care Child customers may be eligible for Rent Assistance (RA), Health Care Card (HCC), lower threshold for the Medicare Safety Net, Remote Area Allowance paid with income support payments (for example, JobSeeker Payment) and may also be eligible for Child Care Subsidy (CCS).
