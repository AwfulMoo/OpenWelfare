This document outlines the process to be followed to generate and issue a Centrelink payment summary on the system. The page also identifies where customers can go to access their Centrelink payment summary.

Eligibility to issue/access a payment summary

A payment summary is generated for customers who receive a reportable payment. This can include:

  • Customers who have received a taxable payment from Centrelink within a financial year
  • Customers who have received a reportable tax exempt payment from Centrelink within a financial year
  • Customers whose reportable payment is cancelled within a financial year. Note: the Centrelink payment summary is generated at the end of the financial year following the cancellation

Note: Centrelink payment summaries are no longer automatically issued by surface mail at the beginning of each financial year. However, when adjustments are made on a Centrelink payment summary, an amended Centrelink payment summary will be automatically sent to the customer's preferred mail channel.

Inability to issue/access a payment summary

A Centrelink payment summary is unable to be issued to or accessed by a customer even though the customer has received a reportable payment in the following circumstances:

  • Where the taxable entitlement, non-taxable entitlement, Remote Area Allowance (RAA) or tax deducted amount is less than zero
  • Where an error has occurred during the calculation of a customers' Centrelink payment summary

A Debt Raising ACTion DOC Request Fast Note is to be used in Customer First to refer these cases to the specialist debt processing team advising that the payment summary cannot be issued. See Resources for information on locating the Fast Note.

For more information, see General information on Manual Follow-up (MFU) tax activities.

Accessing a Centrelink payment summary

Centrelink payment summaries are available for customers to access on request through a range of self service channels, including:

  • their online account though myGov
  • the Express Plus Applications
  • the Self service line for customers
  • Self Service terminals at service centres

Note: Centrelink payment summaries can be requested/accessed through the self service channels for the last seven financial years.

The Resources page contains links to Australian Taxation Office (ATO) Legislation and Online Services and information about access the Fast Note.
