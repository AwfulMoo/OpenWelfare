Separated parents calculators

Care calculator

Care calculator.

Service Officers can use this calculator to determine the care percentage.

Note: the care calculator times out at 15 minutes. Before opening the care calculator, interpret written care arrangements and establish the pattern of care. Refresh [F5] the care calculator before keying information if it has been open for a period of time .

                        

Child Support estimator

Child Support estimator.

Use this estimator to:

  • estimate the child support payable for a child support period
  • perform estimations that are:
    • simple - based only on income details, or
    • complex - by adding variables such as levels of care, age of child(ren) and other case related information

The estimator can also be used for:

  • combined Child Support/Family Tax Benefit (FTB) estimation, or
  • FTB estimation only

Change of assessment (COA) income and expenses calculator

COA income and expenses calculator

Use this calculator to calculate income and expenses to support COA Decision Makers (DMs).

Presumption of Parentage (POP) calculator

Presumption of Parentage calculator

Use this calculator to calculate the date 44 and 20 weeks prior to the child's date of birth.

Child support formula calculators

Use these calculators to determine child support payments for parents with single or multiple cases. The number of relevant dependent children and children in other cases is not restricted.

The calculators cannot:

  • work out child support if a non-parent has care of any of the children (even if they do not have a child support assessment)
  • recognise relevant dependent children that are not in a parent's 100% care


Specialist staff including Decision Makers (DMs), Part 6A Objections and Change of Assessment (CoA) staff can use the Specialised Child Support formula calculator.

Service Officers can use the Child Support formula calculator.


Extension of Time (EOT) calculator

Extension of Time (EOT) calculator

Use this calculator to calculate if a customer needs to apply for an EOT for their objection.​
