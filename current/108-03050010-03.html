Checking DVA income coding and referring work to the DVA Clearance Team

Step

Action

1

DVA payment/benefit notified by the customer or during a new claim + Read more ...

The customer has notified that they received a DVA payment or benefit. This may be notified as part of a new claim.

  • Customer notifies of a DVA grant, a change to existing DVA payment, or they have lodged an application for an Aged Care means assessment and declare a DVA payment, go to Step 2
  • Customer advises of a DVA payment as part of a Services Australia new claim, and/or a DVA error presents during new claim processing, go to Step 4
  • Customer is advising of a new Family Assistance estimate that includes a change to DVA income, go to Step 5
  • A parent/guardian advising they get a DVA payment when providing income details for the Parental Income Test (PIT) for Assistance for Isolated Children (AIC), Youth Allowance (YA), or ABSTUDY Living Allowance, go to Step 6
  • For all other DVA payment enquires, go to Step 3

2

Is there a DVA payment coded on the customer record? + Read more ...

Check the Department of Veterans' Affairs Summary (DVAS) screen for DVA coding.

Is there a DVA payment/benefit coded on the DVAS screen?

  • Yes, and:
    • the information needs to be updated, go to Step 7
    • this information does not need to be updated, however the customer has questions about the impact to their rate of payment, go to Step 3
  • No, and the customer needs to declare a new grant or the payment information needs to be coded on the DVAS screen, go to Step 7

3

Customer has questions about their rate of payment which is being impacted by DVA income + Read more ...

Check the DVAS screen for DVA payment type. The Service Officer should attempt to help the customer with their enquiry.

If the customer has further questions that the Service Officer cannot answer, refer the enquiry to the DVA Clearance Team.

Go to Step 7.

4

DVA income or concession is declared in a new claim and/or an edit/error or warning presents during new claim processing + Read more ...

The DVA Clearance Team check and code all DVA income declared as part of a new claim . Edits and warnings that may present during new claim processing:

  • E001OG: DVA Reverse Clearance required
  • E002OG: Web Service error
  • SR247: DVA Clearance Required - new customer income
  • SR307: DVA Clearance Required - new partner income
  • SR253: DVA Clearance - review customer payments
  • SR308: DVA Clearance - review partner payments

Before referring work to the DVA Clearance Team, make sure:

  • all coding has been completed within the new claim
  • the customer has provided all requested information
  • all applicable specialist referrals have been completed
  • all other errors have been cleared and the claim is ready to be finalised

Refer to the DVA clearance team, go to Step 7.

5

DVA income declared for family assistance purposes + Read more ...

When a customer is updating their income estimate for Family Assistance purposes, Service Officers must make sure the estimate is correctly recorded. See the Background page for further details.

See Helping families provide a reasonable annual income estimate for family assistance payments to help the customer provide an accurate estimate.

Check the Department of Veterans' Affairs Summary (DVAS) screen for DVA payment details.

Note: incorrect coding of DVA payments may result in the customer receiving the incorrect rate of family assistance payments.

Is the correct DVA payment information recorded on the DVAS screen?

  • Yes, no further action is needed, continue with estimate update. Procedure ends here
  • No, or the Service Officer is unsure the correct payment information is recorded on the DVAS screen, go to Step 7

6

DVA income declared for YA, ABSTUDY or AIC Parental Income Test (PIT) exemption + Read more ...

When a parent or guardian is in receipt of certain DVA payments, the child's YA, ABSTUDY or AIC payment may be exempt from the PIT.

DVA payments attracting a PIT exemption are recorded on the parent's Department of Veterans' Affairs Summary (DVAS) screen.

The system will automatically transfer the PIT exemption to the young person's Household Income and Assets (NHI) screen. The PIT Exempt Rsn field will have reason as Dept of Vet Affairs Pen.

The transfer of the PIT exemption happens only if the young person is correctly PIT linked to the parent. See Creating, updating and ending Parental Income Test (PIT) links with dependent student, Australian Apprentice and job seeker recipients or claimants.

If the parent has declared parental income (for example online or paper MOD JY, SY012 or verbally), and:

  • advised they get a payment from DVA, and
  • that payment is not recorded on the DVAS screen
  • go to Step 7

7

Refer to the DVA Clearance Team + Read more ...

The DVA Clearance Team must complete coding of DVA payments .

Note: customers in receipt of Veteran Payment from DVA must not have claims rejected due to multiple entitlement exclusion without consultation with DVA. Veteran Payment is a temporary payment and customers may claim at Services Australia while in receipt of this payment.

The following must also be referred:

  • A DVA manual follow-up (MFU) on Activity List (AL) screen is preventing completion of other work
  • A debt explanation is needed, for example, if there is a residual Services Australia debt amount after the debt has been partially recovered from DVA arrears

In Customer First, to refer the case to the DVA Clearance Team:

  • In the record of the customer in receipt of the DVA payment create an open work item using the Fast Note workflow:
    • Select Auto Text, use Generic > Enquiry > DVA Clearance/Advice Request
    • Text: include details of what the referral is for. Include the payment type, frequency, amounts and dates payments have been received from DVA as relevant
    • Select Confirm

If applicable, tell the customer a referral has been made to the DVA Clearance Team for action.

If this is a hand off, procedure ends here.

Place the activity on hold, as further work is needed after the DVA Clearance Team check or action the record.

8

Holding work until DVA Clearance Team actions have been completed + Read more ...

Place claim or activity on hold to user for 5 business days.

DVA Clearance Team will:

  • close the Fast Note
  • code DVA income as appropriate, and
  • DOC actions taken and the outcome

The claim or activity will be taken off Hold in Customer First or changed to In Process for Process Direct.

When the work item is returned, complete the activity.

Procedure ends here.
