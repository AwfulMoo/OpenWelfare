Output calculations for extracting Service Update and Review activity data

Item

Description

1

Review activity + Read more ...

Finalised Reviews (Gross Reviews)

Number of Reviews which have or have had a Review Status of 'FIN' (Finalised), and the Date Review Finalised is in the period.

Completed Reviews (Net Reviews)

Number of Reviews which have a current Review Status of 'COM' (Completed), and the Date Review Completed is in the period.

Avg Days to FIN (Average days to finalise)

Total number of days from Date Review Registered to Date Review Finalised, where the Date Review Finalised is in the period, divided by the number of reviews finalised in the period.

Annual Savings ($000) (Total annualised savings)

Fortnightly $ Amount multiplied by 26 for reviews where the current Review Status is 'COM' (Completed), the variation amount is not zero, the Date Review Completed is in the period, and the:

  • Rule 1: Review Result is 'DVR' (Downward Variation), 'TDV' (Transfer Downward Variation), or
  • Rule 2: Review Result is 'CAN' (Cancelled) or 'SUS' (Suspended) and the Review Result Reason is not 'TFR' (Transferred) or 'RST' (Restored)

Plus

Amount of Debts (Raised) which are greater than zero where the Debt Status is not 'UND' for reviews with a current Review Status of 'COM' (Completed), and:

  • Rule 1: Date Review Completed is in the period and the Date Debt Raised is prior to the end of the period or
  • Rule 2: The Date Debt Raised is in the period and the Date Review Completed is prior to the start of the period

Note: for allocation of debt amounts at the monthly breakdown level, use Date Review Completed unless Date Debt Raised is later than Date Review Completed, in which case use Date Debt Raised.

Amount is displayed in Thousands ($000).

2

Cancellations/Suspensions + Read more ...

Number FIN (Gross) (Finalised Reviews with a 'CAN' or 'SUS' result)

Number of reviews which have or have had a Review Status of 'FIN' (Finalised), the variation amount is not zero, the Date Review Finalised is in the period, the Review Result at the time of finalisation is 'CAN' (Cancelled) or 'SUS' (Suspended), and the Review Result Reason is not 'TFR' (Transferred).

Number COM (Net) (Completed reviews with a 'CAN' or 'SUS' result)

Number of reviews where the current Review Status is 'COM' (Completed), the variation amount is not zero, the Date Review Completed is in the period, the Review Result is 'CAN' (Cancelled) or 'SUS' (Suspended) and the Review Result Reason is not 'TFR' (Transferred) or 'RST' (Restored).

Percent Gross (Percentage of finalised reviews with a 'CAN' or 'SUS' result)

Number of reviews which have or have had a Review Status of 'FIN' (Finalised), the variation amount is not zero, the Date Review Finalised is in the period, the Review Result at the time of finalisation is 'CAN' (Cancelled) or 'SUS' (Suspended) and the Review Result Reason is not 'TFR' (Transferred)

Divided by:

The total number of reviews which have or have had a Review Status of 'FIN' (Finalised) during the period.

Percent Net (Percentage of completed reviews with a 'CAN' or 'SUS' result)

Number of reviews where the current Review Status is 'COM' (Completed), the variation amount is not zero, the Date Review Completed is in the period, the Review Result is 'CAN' (Cancelled) or 'SUS' (Suspended) and the Review Result Reason is not 'TFR' (Transferred) or 'RST' (Restored)

Divided by:

The total number of reviews which currently have a Review Status of 'COM' (Completed) during the period.

Amt ($000) Gross (Finalised annual $ amount for 'CAN' and 'SUS' results)

Fortnightly $ Variation Amount multiplied by 26 from reviews which have or have had a Review Status of 'FIN' (Finalised), the variation amount is not zero, the Date Review Finalised is in the period, the Review Result at the time of finalisation is 'CAN' (Cancelled) or 'SUS' (Suspended), and the Review Result Reason is not 'TFR' (Transferred).

Amount is displayed in Thousands ($000).

Amt ($000) Net (Completed annual $ amount for 'CAN' and 'SUS' results

Fortnightly $ Variation Amount multiplied by 26 from reviews where the current Review Status is 'COM' (Completed), the variation amount is not zero, the Date Review Completed is in the period, the Review Result is 'CAN' (Cancelled) or 'SUS' (Suspended), and the Review Result Reason is not 'TFR' (Transferred) or 'RST' (Restored).

Amount is displayed in Thousands ($000).
