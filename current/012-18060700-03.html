Identifying a 3GA placement + Read more ...

Depending on the approved 3GA placement received, different documentation and processing requirements can apply.

Approved Medical Deputising Service (AMDS) placements

Check the document includes:

  • medical practitioner’s name
  • approved from and to dates
  • specifies the 3GA program the medical practitioner is enrolled in
  • a physical practice address (not a PO box)
  • Department of Health and Aged Care approval and signature
  • approved 3GA placement instrument is 1 page
  • new placement requests require an application for a provider number
  • extensions may be processed without an application for a provider number form

Approved Private Emergency Department (APED) placements

Check the document includes:

  • medical practitioner’s name
  • approved from and to dates
  • specifies 3GA program the medical practitioner is enrolled in
  • a physical practice address (not a PO box)
  • Department of Health and Aged Care approval and signature
  • approved 3GA placement instrument is 1 page
  • new placement requests require an application for a provider number
  • extensions may be processed without an application for a provider number form

Australian General Practice Training (AGPT) placements (pre 1 January 2019)

Check the document includes:

  • medical practitioner’s name
  • approved from and to dates
  • consortium name and 3 digit code is specified
  • a physical practice address (not a PO box)
  • approval and signatures from:
    • the registrar, and
    • Consortium senior or delegated medical educator, and
    • the Department of Health and Aged Care
  • approved 3GA placement instrument is 3 pages
  • may be processed without an application for a provider number form for new location requests and extensions

Australian General Practice Training (AGPT) placements (post 1 January 2019)

Check the document includes:

  • medical practitioner’s name
  • approved from and to dates
  • specifies 3GA program the medical practitioner is enrolled in
  • a physical practice address (not a PO box)
  • Department of Health and Aged Care approval and signature
  • approved 3GA placement instrument is 1 page
  • new placement requests require an application for a provider number
  • extensions may be processed without an application for a provider number form

Queensland Country Relieving Program (QCRP) placements

Check the document includes:

  • medical practitioner’s name
  • the medical practitioner is:
    • ‘authorised to undertake a rural locum placement, under the approved Queensland Country Relieving Doctors program as specified in Schedule 5, Part 2, of the Commonwealth Health Insurance Regulations 1975’ or 'authorised to undertake an approved rural locum placement, a program specified in section 26 of the Commonwealth Health Insurance Regulations 2018'
  • approved from and to dates
  • specifies 3GA program the medical practitioner is undertaking
  • a physical practice address (not a PO box)
  • signature from authorised signatory for Queensland Health
  • approved 3GA placement instrument is 1 to 2 pages
  • new placement requests require an application for a provider number
  • extensions may be processed without an application for a provider number form

Remote Vocational Training Scheme (RVTS) placements

Check the document includes:

  • medical practitioner’s name
  • approved from and to dates
  • specifies 3GA program the medical practitioner is approved for
  • a physical practice address (not a PO Box)
  • approval and signatures from:
    • the registrar, and
    • RVTS senior medical educator (or authorised delegate), and
    • RVTS
  • approved 3GA placement instrument is 1 page
  • may be processed without an application for a provider number form for new location requests and extensions - application may be in full or location and signature pages may only be received which is acceptable for processing

Rural Locum Relief Program (RLRP) placements

Check the document includes:

  • medical practitioner’s name
  • approved from and to dates
  • specifies 3GA program the medical practitioner is enrolled in
  • a physical practice address (not a PO box)
  • authorised signatory for Rural Workforce Agency’s signature block
  • approved 3GA placement instrument is 1 page
  • new location requests require an application for a provider number
  • extensions may be processed without an application for a provider number form

Special Approved Placement Program (SAPP) placements

Check the document includes:

  • medical practitioner’s name
  • approved from and to dates
  • specifies 3GA program the medical practitioner is enrolled in
  • a physical practice address (not a PO box)
  • Department of Health and Aged Care approval and signature
  • approved 3GA placement instrument is 1 page
  • new placement requests require an application for a provider number
  • extensions may be processed without an application for a provider number form

Specialist College Trainee placements

Check the document includes:

  • medical practitioner’s name
  • approved from and to dates
  • specifies 3GA program the medical practitioner is enrolled in
  • a physical practice address (not a PO box)
  • signature of trainee
  • name of college and name of training program
  • Chief Executive Officer name and signature
  • College stamp
  • Approved 3GA placement instrument is 2 pages
  • New placement requests require an application for a provider number
  • Extensions may be processed without an application for a provider number form

Temporary Resident Other Medical Practitioner (TROMP) placement

Check the document includes instrument from the Department of Health and Aged Care:

  • medical practitioner's name
  • date granted from
  • specifies 3GA program the medical practitioner is enrolled in
  • approved 3GA instrument is 1 page
  • Department of Health and Aged Care approval and signature
  • approved 3GA instrument is only issued once and is non-location specific
  • a TROMP is indicated on PDS with a 198 specialty code as stem level only

Application for a provider number:

  • specialty code 198 exists on the stem, an application for a provider number form is required to issue a provider number

Note: where a removal request lists a specific location/s to be removed, Service Officers should only remove the 3GA placement from this location - any other placements should remain as is. A request to close a location does not remove a 3GA placement from that location.

Step

Action

1

Assess the notification + Read more ...

Notification of removal from a 3GA placement may be in the form of:

  • a new approved 3GA placement for an existing location that has a current 3GA placement
  • notification from the medical practitioner:
    • signed letter to close the 3GA placement recorded against a provider number
    • phone call from the medical practitioner requesting to close the 3GA placement recorded against a provider number, see Perform telephone security check for Medicare health professionals
    • removal from the Department of Health and Aged Care or from the Registered Training Organisation (RTO) which advises the relevant medical practitioner is to be removed from the specified program

Note: if the request is only to close the provider location and does not specifically request to close the 3GA placement, do not amend and 3GA or OMPs specialty codes. A request to close a location does not remove a 3GA placement from that location

Notification from the Department of Health and Aged Care or the RTO specifies:

  • the program which the medical practitioner is to be removed from
  • the medical practitioner/s required to be removed
  • if applicable, the reason/s for the removal, for example, no longer working at location/s or obtained specialist registration
  • the specific location/s this notice applies to (may not cover all locations)

Note: Australian General Practice Training Program (AGPT) placements may not contain a specific location for removal.

A notice may contain the names of multiple medical practitioners, multiple reasons for removal, and multiple locations. Make sure the removal for the medical practitioner indicated in the naming convention of the application is processed. The remaining medical practitioners specified on the request will have a file saved under their name.
