Retrieving an archived claim

Table 1

Item

Details

1

Claim data from the customer’s record in Process Direct record is no longer available and has been archived + Read more ...

Was the customer’s claim finalised at least 2 years ago and can no longer be viewed from the Transactions icon tab bar in Process Direct?

  • Yes, go to Step 2
  • No, procedure ends here

2

Accessing the Archive Search Display + Read more ...

From the Process Direct Landing page, select the Archive Search Display tile.

3

Requesting the retrieval of an archived claim + Read more ...

To retrieve an archived claim, 1 of the 2 mandatory criteria of CRN or Transaction ID must be entered. The Transaction ID field is used to input the Claim ID. The Date From and Date To are optional fields and may be entered to refine the retrieved results.

Press Enter or select the ‘Search’ icon at the top right of the application.

4

Retrieved claim display + Read more ...

The below details for the Claim are displayed upon successful search:

  • Customer (CRN) Header Summary
  • Claims Details (Attachments)
  • Claim Status Change Documents
  • Follow on Activities (if any)

5

Restricted Access (RA) customers + Read more ...

The archived claim records for Restricted Access (RA) customers will not be available for search and view in Process Direct unless the user accessing the archive for RA customers has one of the below roles:

  • ZC.XX.REST.ACC.ADMIN
  • ZC.XX.REST.ACC.MGR
  • ZC.XX.REST.ACC.OFF.L1

When there is a need to retrieve an archived claim for a RA customer and the required resource is not held, contact the National Restricted Access line on 1800 413 955 or NATIONAL.RESTRICTED.ACCESS mailbox.
