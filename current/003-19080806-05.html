Payment query template

Received a payment for travel period one and did not travel because of COVID-19

Table 1

Circumstance

Approach to apply

Received a payment, not booked flights, accommodation and/or other incidentals.

Customer should use the financial help received for travel during travel period one the next time they travel for the purpose of observing or participating in the Dutch national prosecution.

Received a payment, booked flights, accommodation and/or other incidentals and are able to get a credit for future travel.

Customer should use the financial help received for travel during travel period one the next time they travel for the purpose of observing or participating in the Dutch national prosecution.

Received a payment and booked flights, accommodation and/or other incidentals that can be fully refunded.

Customer should use the financial help received for travel during travel period one the next time they travel for the purpose of observing or participating in the Dutch national prosecution.

Received a payment, booked flights, accommodation and/or other incidentals and can only get a partial refund.

Customers should use the partial refund received for travel during travel period one the next time they travel for the purpose of observing or participating in the Dutch national prosecution.

Customers may apply under special circumstances to receive the balance not refunded or credited, in addition to the difference between funding received for travel period 1 and the grant funding for travel period 4.

Received a payment, booked flights, accommodation and/or other incidentals and cannot get a credit or refund.

The financial help received is considered spent for the purposes the payment was made.

If the payment can be recovered through travel insurance, this should be treated the same as ‘Received a payment and booked flights, accommodation and/or other incidentals that can be fully refunded’ above.
