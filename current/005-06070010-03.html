Responding to SRSS customer enquiries

This table describes the process for responding to enquiries from SRSS customers.

Step

Action

1

Customer asks how much they have been paid + Read more ...

In the Customer First record:

  • Go to Workspace
  • In the search field enter Payment List and search
  • Select Payment List from the Financial Customer Care block
  • Select the payment amount to view payment components, including deductions

Payments are listed including the date, amount and the bank account the payment was sent to.

For more information, see Viewing payment details for native Customer First payments.

Customer asks for details of next payment

To view Planned Payments in Customer First:

  • Go to Workspace
  • Select CRN hyperlink
  • Scroll down to Cases tab
  • In the Type tab select Status Resolution Support Services
  • In the Social Services tab select the latest SSP ID
  • Select Planned Payments

Note: for provisional or future payments, advise the customer that the amount is an estimate of the future payment.

To view payments due to be delivered overnight:

  • Go to Workspace
  • Select Financial Customer Care
  • Select Open Item List

This will list payments that are queued and ready to issue.

2

Customer did not receive payment + Read more ...

Check:

  • If the non-payment is due to the 6 monthly review not completed, action the review.
    See Actioning Status Resolution Support Services (SRSS) payment reviews
  • If SRSS status is CAN, see Suspending, cancelling, or restoring Status Resolution Support Services (SRSS) payments
  • If there has been a change in circumstances, see Change of circumstances for Status Resolution Support Services (SRSS) payment customer
  • If customer’s payment is returned. See Payment list with text Return reason. Update the bank account details in Process Direct. Payment automatically issues to the new bank account the following business day.
    See Changing payment destination
  • If payment destination details are correct, check the SSP has been approved. See Status Resolution Support Services (SRSS) Social Service Plan

To view current bank account details, in Customer First

  • Go to Workspace
  • Select CRN hyperlink
  • Select Bank Detail Edit Tab
  • Procedure ends here

3

Change payment destination + Read more ...

To view current bank account in:

  • Customer First
    • Select CRN hyperlink
    • Select Bank Account Detail Tab
  • Process Direct
    • Key PASB in the SuperKey

Is the customer requesting to change the payment destination?

  • Yes. See Changing payment destination. DOC the record with the updated bank account details
  • No. Procedure ends here

Note: the updated bank account will apply from the date of the change.

4

Rescheduling an appointment + Read more ...

Contact the customer's relevant Refugee and asylum seeker teams to reschedule the appointment. That is, New Claim or 6 monthly review appointment.

5

Change of circumstance + Read more ...

If the customer is contacting about a change of circumstance, see Change of circumstances for Status Resolution Support Services (SRSS) payment customer.

6

Request for an income statement + Read more ...

In Customer First, access the latest Social Service Plan (SSP)

  • Go to Workspace
  • Select the CRN hyperlink
  • Scroll to the customer's Cases tab
  • In the Type tab, select Status Resolution Support Services
  • Under customer name and address, hover over the word More, select the drop down tab and select either:
    • Print Income Statement Local, or
    • Print Income Statement Central

7

Services and assistance + Read more ...

SRSS customers are not eligible for other services or referrals, including advance payments, bereavement, social work referrals except where immediate crisis exists, health care cards, concession cards or referrals to other agencies.

Refer these customers to their Department of Home Affairs contracted service provider. See Status Resolution Support Services (SRSS) payment customer is in hardship.

8

Queries about immigration status, transition of payments or other further assistance + Read more ...

Services Australia will administer payments under SRSS and can only provide information about payment rates, composition and delivery of the payment once current.

If a customer's query is about:

  • obtaining advice on their immigration status, refer them to the Department of Home Affairs
  • the transition of SRSS payments to Services Australia, refer them to their Department of Home Affairs contracted service provider
  • any other assistance, refer them back to their Department of Home Affairs contracted service provider
