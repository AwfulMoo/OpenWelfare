Eligibility assessment

Table 1

Step

Action

1

No longer a full time Australian Apprentice + Read more ...

A customer is still considered to be an Australian Apprentice if they:

  • have a current Commonwealth Registration Number, and
  • are a full-time Australian Apprenticeship

TYIMS automatically updates a customer's Australian Apprenticeship status on the Apprenticeship Details (NAPD) screen. However, customers are still required to advise Services Australia of any change to the status of their Australian Apprenticeship within 14 days. There is no entitlement to payment as an Australian Apprentice if the Commonwealth Registration Number is cancelled or suspended in the Access Apprenticeships (powered by TYIMS). See Resources for access to TYIMS.

Where a customer advises they have or will cease their Australian Apprenticeship:

  • they can be transferred to another suitable payment even if their Commonwealth Registration Number is still current
  • the NAPD screen may need to be manually updated to allow the customer to be transferred to another payment
  • if the customer is contacting to advise they will cease an Australian Apprentice for a future date, updates can only be made up to 14 days before the date the customer will cease their apprenticeship
  • Australian Apprentices who will cease their apprenticeship in the future, the Access Apprenticeships database should automatically transfer information to update the NAPD screen when a customer is no longer an Australian Apprentice
    • payments will cancel automatically as a result of this update. The customer will be sent an auto advice of cancellation and will need to contact Services Australia if they wish to seek further income support

Note: if a customer is changing apprentice employers, advise the customer to add their new employer in their next report. NAPD will automatically update with the new apprenticeship employer through the existing Access Apprenticeships link

Has the customer advised they have ceased to be an Australian Apprentice?

  • Yes, go to Step 2
  • No, procedure ends here

2

Customer advises they are no longer an Australian Apprentice + Read more ...

The customer can be registered as a Youth Allowance (job seeker) when they:

  • are receiving Youth Allowance
  • are aged under 22 years of age
  • have notified within 14 days of ceasing their apprenticeship, and
  • want to receive payment as a job seeker

Is the customer eligible to be registered as a Youth Allowance (job seeker)?

  • Yes, see Step 1 in Table 2
  • No, go to Step 3

3

Customer is receiving Youth Allowance, Austudy or ABSTUDY + Read more ...

Is the customer asking for further assistance as a job seeker or a full-time student?

  • Yes, and the customer will be a:
    • Full-time student, go to Step 4
    • Job Seeker, go to Step 5
  • No, payments need to be cancelled. Go to Step 6

4

Customer advises they will be commencing a full-time course of study + Read more ...

If there will be a gap between ceasing the Australian Apprenticeship and commencing as a student of more than 14 days, the customer must register as a job seeker.

Will there be a gap of more than 14 days?

  • Yes, to continue receiving an income support payment without a gap, the customer must register as a jobseeker before commencing study, go to Step 5
  • No, see Step 1 in Table 3

5

Customer is required to register as a job seeker to continue to be paid + Read more ...

The customer can continue to receive payments as a job seeker when they:

  • notify within 14 days of ceasing the apprenticeship
  • want to continue to be registered as a job seeker

Has the customer met both parts of the criteria?

  • Yes, see Step 1 in Table 2
  • No, go to Step 6

6

Cancel payments + Read more ...

Service Support Officers (SSO), Technical Peer Support Officers (TPSO) and authorised Smart Centre Processing staff have access to end date the NAPD screen with the apprenticeship end date. See Service Officer role when seeking Technical Peer Support (TPS) in Smart Centres.

Does the Service Officer have access to Access Apprenticeships and the NAPD screen? See Resources for access to TYIMS.

  • Yes, go to Step 8
  • No, go to Step 7

7

NAPD requires updating + Read more ...

Service Officers without access must create an Open work item using the Customer First (CF) Fast Note workflow:

ABSTUDY

  • Category 1: Students, ABSTUDY and Apprentices
  • Category 2: Update
  • Template: ABSTUDY Apprentice action required
  • Select confirm
  • Add the following text: Customer ceased their apprenticeship on DD/MM/YY. NAPD update is required and payments are to be cancelled

Youth Allowance (YA)/Austudy

  • Category 1: Students, ABSTUDY and Apprentices
  • Category 2: Update
  • Template: Apprentice Employer Update
  • Select confirm
  • Add the following text: Customer ceased their apprenticeship on DD/MM/YY. NAPD update is required and payments are to be cancelled

Tell the customer that their payment will be cancelled, as they are no longer qualified.

If the customer requires further financial assistance:

  • tell them to lodge a claim using online services via myGov
  • refer them to the Payment and Service finder on the Services Australia website. See Resources for a link

Procedure ends here.

8

Cancel payments continued + Read more ...

The Australian Apprenticeship must be end dated on the NAPD screen to cancel their existing payment. The update will need to be done before another payment can be granted.

Go to the NAPD screen by coding NAPD in Next: field.

Code:

  • Apprenticeship End Date: field with the date the Australian Apprenticeship ceased. Use the earlier of the date:
    • Advised by the customer, or
    • Showing in Access Apprenticeships
  • Source: field with the relevant contact channel
  • DOR: field with date of receipt
  • Action: field with 'C' (correct)
  • Press [Enter]

Update any final employment income on Employment Income Summary (EAPP) screen or using the Earnings and Reporting workflow. See Recording and correcting employment income details.

If the customer has no final employment income:

  • correct the last apprentice income entry on the EAPP screen: Empl Sts: field from FTO to PTC

If the customer was receiving:

  • Youth Allowance and is aged under 22, go to Step 9
  • Youth Allowance and is aged 22 years and over, ABSTUDY or Austudy
    • Do not code the Newstart Education Status (NES) screen to update the status as NST (not a student)
    • Finalise the activity for payment to cancel LAT (left apprenticeship)
    • If the customer requires further financial assistance, refer them to the Payment and Service finder on the Services Australia website. See Resources. Advise them to lodge a claim using online services via myGov
    • DOC the record

Procedure ends here

9

Youth Allowance customer under 22 years + Read more ...

For Youth Allowance customers aged under 22, cancel the payment manually via the Benefit Action (BA) screen:

  • Service Reason: field, 'YAL'
  • Action: field, 'CAN'
  • Reason: field, 'CLR'
  • Effect Date: field, the date the customer ceased their Australian Apprenticeship plus one day

Insert a new NES screen:

  • Event Date: field, the date the customer ceased their Australian Apprenticeship +1 day
  • Student Status: field, 'NST'

Finalise the activity to cancel the payment and DOC the record.

If the customer requires further financial assistance:

  • tell them to lodge a claim using online services via myGov
  • refer them to the Payment and Service finder on the Services Australia website. See Resources

Procedure ends here.

3

Customer has discontinued or completed the Australian Apprenticeship + Read more ...

If the Access Apprenticeships link has not worked to end the apprenticeship:

  • The Access Apprenticeships database will need to be checked to determine the current status of the Commonwealth Registration Number
  • Service Officers in the Apprentice Processing Teams have access to Access Apprenticeships

If Access Apprenticeships shows the Commonwealth Registration Number as current, a customer can only transfer from an apprentice payment to a job seeker payment if they ask for the transfer.

Update any final earnings using the Earnings and Reporting workflow. See Recording and correcting employment income details.

Go to the NAPD screen by coding NAPD in Next: field.

Code:

  • Apprenticeship End Date: field with the date the Australian Apprenticeship ceased. Use the earlier of the date:
    • Advised by the customer, or
    • Showing in Access Apprenticeships. Service Officers without access to Access Apprenticeships will use the date advised by the customer
  • Source: field with relevant contact channel
  • DOR: field with date of receipt
  • Action: field with 'C' (correct)
  • Press [Enter]

For YA customers only - navigate to the NES screen by coding 'NES' in the Next: field and pressing [Shift] + [F4] (Workaround). Code:

  • Event Date: field with cessation date + 1
  • Student Status: field with 'NST' (Not a student)
  • Action: field with 'I' (Insert)
  • If the customer is aged 16-17 years and not turning 18 years within 3 months:
    • an exemption will be required to be coded on NES screen
    • the exemption reason should be 'OTH' (other) and coded for a 21 day period
    • the 21 day period commences from cessation date of apprenticeship + 1

Do not update the NES screen for Austudy and ABSTUDY customers.

Go to Step 4.

4

Payment type + Read more ...

What payment is the customer currently receiving?

  • ABSTUDY, go to Step 5
  • Austudy, go to Step 6
  • Youth Allowance and is:
    • aged 22 years and over, go to Step 7
    • aged under 22 years, go to Step 8

5

Customer is receiving ABSTUDY + Read more ...

ABSTUDY payments will auto cancel once the apprenticeship has been end-dated on the NAPD screen.

The customer will need to claim JobSeeker Payment (JSP) or Youth Allowance (job seeker) if they wish to continue to receive assistance. If the customer wishes to claim JSP or YA:

  • record details on a DOC and finalise activity
  • Tell the customer:
    • their payment will be cancelled as they are no longer qualified
    • they need to claim online for JSP or YA (other) using Online Services. See Processing JobSeeker Payment (JSP) claims

Procedure ends here.

6

Customer is receiving Austudy + Read more ...

Austudy payments will auto cancel once the apprenticeship details have been end-dated on the NAPD screen.

The customer will need to claim JobSeeker Payment (JSP) if they wish to continue to receive assistance. If the customer wishes to claim JSP:

  • Record details on a DOC and finalise activity
  • Tell the customer:
    • their payment will be cancelled as they are no longer qualified
    • they need to claim online for JSP using Online Services. See Processing JobSeeker Payment (JSP) claims

Procedure ends here.

7

Customer is receiving Youth Allowance and is 22 years and over + Read more ...

The customer will need to claim JobSeeker Payment (JSP) if they wish to continue to receive assistance. If the customer wishes to claim JSP:

  • record details on a DOC and finalise activity
  • Tell the customer:
    • their payment will be cancelled as they are no longer qualified
    • they need to claim online for JSP using Online Services. See Processing JobSeeker Payment (JSP) claims

Procedure ends here.

8

Customer is under 22 years and receiving Youth Allowance + Read more ...

  • Youth Allowance will remain current
  • Record details on a DOC and finalise activity
  • Book Student to Jobseeker transfer appointment
  • At the appointment:
    • the Service Officer who contacts the customer will run the Looking For Work (LFW) workflow to determine an appropriate referral to an employment services provider
      Note: Looking For Work can only be run the day after the apprenticeship has ended
    • book an initial appointment with the provider of the customer's choosing
    • ensure the customer is aware of their mutual obligation requirements and regular reporting requirements

Procedure ends here.

3

View Access Apprenticeships registration + Read more ...

If Access Apprenticeships shows the Commonwealth Registration Number as current, a customer can only transfer from an apprentice payment to a student payment, if they ask to be transferred.

Where the customer has not asked to be transferred:

  • no further action can be taken until Access Apprenticeships is updated to cancel the Commonwealth registration number
  • set up manual review to regularly check the customer record
  • record the details on a DOC

For an immediate transfer to occur:

  • the customer must provide full details of their course, and
  • the Service Officer must be able to determine that there are no allowable/reasonable time issues

Is all information available and there are no allowable/reasonable time issues?

  • Yes, go to Step 4
  • No, go to Step 5

4

Change the customer's activity type to full-time student + Read more ...

Go to the NAPD screen by coding NAPD in Next: field.

Code:

  • Apprenticeship End Date: field with the date the Australian Apprenticeship ceased. Use the earlier of the date:
    • advised by the customer, or
    • showing in Access Apprenticeships. Service Officers without access to Access Apprenticeships will use the date advised by the customer
  • Source: field with the relevant contact channel
  • DOR: field with date of receipt
  • Action: field with 'C' (correct)
  • Press [Enter]

Service Support Officers (SSO), Technical Peer Support Officers (TPSO) and authorised Smart Centre Processing staff have access to end date the NAPD screen with the apprenticeship end date. See Service Officer role when seeking Technical Peer Support (TPS) in Smart Centres.

For YA and Austudy customers, code 'NES' in the Next: field and press [Shift]+[F4] Workaround. Code:

  • Event Date: field with date completed Australian Apprenticeship + 1
  • Student status: field with 'FTS' (Full time student)
  • Action: field with 'I' (Insert)

Do not update the NES screen for ABSTUDY customers.

Service Officers must:

  • Code the new study details
  • Record details on a DOC
  • When the NAPD screen has been updated, finalise the activity via the Assessment Results (AR) screen.
  • Update any final employment income on EAPP or by using the Earnings and Reporting workflow.

Procedure ends here.

5

Service Officer is unable to determine study details or there are allowable time issues + Read more ...

If customer is still receiving YA, Austudy or ABSTUDY as an apprentice:

  • advise customer that full-time study details are required, and previous academic transcripts (where applicable for allowable time issues)
  • collect and record study details, see Coding new study details

If the customer is unable to supply documentation at the time of the contact, a request for information is to be sent requesting the customer supply documents within 14 days.

Procedure ends here.
