This page details the process for dealing with a request for information about a Child Support customer record.

Information requested

Step

Action

1

Manual information request received  (Centrelink Liaison Officer (CLO)) + Read more

Manual information requests are received in the Centrelink Liaison mailbox and actioned by Centrelink Liaison Officers (CLOs).

2

Document request for information (CLO)   + Read more

CLOs must document requests in the customer’s  Communication window using the following selections from the drop down menus:

  • Type – ELECTRONIC IN
  • Case – select relevant case number
  • Reason – PROVIDE INFORM
  • With – CENTRELINK
  • Category – OTHER
  • Issue – OTHER

3

Review manual information request  (CLO) + Read more

CLOs must review requests to ensure the information can be disclosed as per the Memorandum of understanding - exchange of electronic data.

If the information requested cannot be provided to  Centrelink, the CLO will:

  • update the  Communication window to detail the reason
  • email the officer requesting the information to advise their request cannot be provided, and copy the email to Centrelink Liaison mailbox

Go to Step 4 if the information can be provided.

4

Send information to  Centrelink   + Read more

If the information can be provided to Centrelink, the CLO will  obtain it from Cuba.   If further investigation is necessary, the CLO may need to contact the owning Service Officer or subject matter experts (COA, Care etc).

The information must be sent to the requesting officer, and the email copied to Centrelink Liaison mailbox.

The CLO must update the  Communication window with details of the information  provided to Centrelink.
