Removal of tumour, cyst, ulcer or scar (items 31206 to 31225)

Table 1: This table describes the MBS items and their descriptors for removal by surgical excision (other than shave excision) of tumour, cyst, ulcer or scar (other than a scar removed during the surgical approach at an operation).

These items do not restrict with each other as the items are specifically for different lesion sizes and the amount of lesions removed Items 31220 and 31225 are not for excision of viral verrucae (common warts) and seborrheic keratoses.

Skin flaps

Skin flaps (items 45200, 45201, 45202, 45203, 45206 and 45207)

Table 6: This table sets out the skin flap items and their descriptions.

MBS item

Description

45200

Single stage local flap, simple and small

Must not be in association with any items between 31356 and 31376

45201

Muscle, myocutaneous or skin flap, where clinically indicated to repair one surgical excision made in the removal of a malignant or non-malignant skin lesion.

Must be in association with 31000, 31001, 31002, 31003, 31004, 31005, 31358, 31359, 31360, 31363, 31364, 31369, 31370, 31371, 31373 or 31376

Note: item 45201 can be claimed up to three days after the initial excision service. This is to allow for weekends, public holidays or for patients who live in a rural or remote location. The practitioner other than the practitioner that performed the initial excision service may perform the service under item 45201.

45202

Muscle, myocutaneous or skin flap, to repair one surgical excision made in the removal of a malignant or non-malignant skin lesion,

if the clinical relevance of the procedure is clearly annotated in the patient's record and either:

  • item 45201 applies and additional flap repair is required for the same defect; or
  • item 45201 does not apply (e.g. the excision is not one of the associated items) and either:
    • the patient has severe pre-existing scarring, severe skin atrophy or sclerodermoid changes, or
    • the repair is contiguous with a free margin

45203

Single stage local flap, complicated or large, excluding flap for male pattern baldness and H-flap or double advancement flap.

Must not be in association with any items between 31356 and 31376

45206

Single stage local flap, on eyelid, nose, lip, ear, neck, hand, thumb, finger or genitals

Excluding H-flap or double advancement flap.

Must not be in association with any items between 31356 and 31376

45207

H-flap or double advancement flap if indicated to repair one defect, on eyelid, eyebrow or forehead.

Must not be in association with any items between 31356 and 31376
