This page contains information on how to process Ex-Carer Allowance (child) Health Care Card (EHC) renewal in Customer First.

Ex-Carer Allowance (child) EHC - Renewing the card

Step

Action

1

Selected for review + Read more ...

The renewal process for the EHC can be lodged verbally or customers can lodge a Claim for Ex-Carer Allowance (child) Health Care Card (SS456) as a renewal.

When the customer is automatically selected for review, a renewal letter will be issued to the customer that day. If the customer does not respond to the letter within 28 days then their card will expire and record status will be CAN-EXP.

Is the customer's Ex-Carer Allowance (child) EHC still current and they wish to renew the card?

  • Yes,
    • if the customer is advising cessation of full time study, see Changes in a customer's circumstances and effect on concession card entitlement
    • otherwise, go to Step 4
  • No, if the customer is:
    • lodging a (SS456) form, go to Step 2
    • contacting more than 13 weeks of the card expiry date, go to Step 3
    • contacting within 13 weeks of the card expiry, date go to Step 4

2

Lodging a (SS456) form + Read more ...

Is the customer contacting within 13 weeks of the previous card's expiry date?

  • Yes, go to Step 4
  • No, go to Step 3

3

Contact outside 13 weeks + Read more ...

If the customer is contacting outside 13 weeks of the expiry date of the card, the customer must re-apply for the card.

If the customer is lodging a Claim for Ex-Carer Allowance (child) Health Care Card (SS456) form:

  • scan the form and any supporting documents onto the customers record
  • tell the customer the claim will be sent for processing
  • document the customer’s record explaining why they must re-apply for the card

Otherwise:

  • tell the customer to lodge an online claim
  • run an Assisted Customer Claim (ACC), see Viewing and processing online and assisted customer claims
  • run the First Contact Concessions, Carers and Seniors (FCCCS) workflow. This will issue a customised Claim for Ex-Carer Allowance (child) Health Care Card (SS490), or
  • issue an Application for a Health Care Card for a former recipient of a Carer Allowance Health Care Card (SS456)

Procedure ends here.

4

Process the renewal + Read more ...

In the EHC system, select 'ISS' from the System: field and key 'SVEHC' in the Nxt field.

If the EHC is current, select the renewal activity either from the Future Activity List (FAL) or Activity List (AL) screen, then select the screens that need updating:

  • the Student status (EHCA) screen. All details will prepopulate, only update where Student ID details have changed with Date of Event as date of contact
  • the Benefit Action (BA) screen
    • SVC rsn - 'EHC'
    • Action - 'REN'
    • Effect date - renewal date (new card start date will be auto-populated by system)
    • Source - (usually) 'PHO' or 'PER'
    • DOR - (usually) system date
  • finalise the activity via the Assessment Results (AR) screen
  • record the details on a DOC. Use Fast Note - select Auto text > Concession Cards > Claims > Ex-CDA (child) HCC (EHC) NCL Grant/Reject option

If the EHC has expired, create an activity on the Benefit Action (BA) screen, by completing the following fields:

  • SVC rsn - 'EHC'
  • Action - 'REN'
  • Effect date - renewal date (new card start date is previous card's cancellation date)
  • Source - (usually) 'PHO' or 'PER'
  • DOR - processing date
  • update the Student status (EHCA) screen, if required
  • finalise the activity via the AR screen
  • record the details on a DOC. Use Fast Note - select Auto text > Concession Cards > Claims > Ex-CDA (child) HCC (EHC) NCL Grant/Reject option.

If the customer is qualified for an EHC, a new card will be issued to them on the Health Care Card carrier (SS357).

Scan to store any SS456 form and documents, see Scanning Centrelink documents using an MFD.
