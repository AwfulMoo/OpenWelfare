Community Development Employment Program

  • Automation will run once a month for each staff member
  • Updates are determined by comparing QSS profiles with QOLStat results
  • Line managers will get an email summary of updates that have occurred, and any manual action needed for their staff

Components of the process that are automated:

  • Initial proficiency assessment
  • Advancing proficiency
  • Annual proficiency review
  • Expiring proficiency
  • Identifying manual action required

Components of the process which are not automated:

  • Downgrading proficiency (reducing sample from Proficient 'P' to Learner 'L'
  • QOL checker accreditation/reaccreditation
  • Trigger proficiency assessments
  • Limited payment systems
