This page contains examples of derived fees. These examples may not correspond with current Medicare Benefit Schedule (MBS) information.

On this Page:

Derived fees example 1

In example 1, the patient billed was 1 of 5 patients seen at the hospital. The schedule fee is calculated by:

  • adding the base fee of $8.50 plus
  • the fee for item 58 calculated by dividing the amount of $15.50 by 5 patients = $3.10
  • total schedule fee = $8.50 + $3.10 = $11.60

Example 1 - consultation item 58

This table provides an example only of the derived fee for consultation item 58. Item descriptions and fee amounts used may not correspond with current Medicare Benefit Schedule (MBS) information.

Item

Group A2 - Other non-referred attendances to which no other item applies

Consultation at a place other than consulting rooms or a Residential Aged Care Facility

58

Professional attendance by a medical practitioner (other than a general practitioner) on 1 or more patients on 1 occasion at a place other than consulting rooms or a residential aged care facility. Brief consultation of not more than 5 minutes.

Derived Fee: an amount equal to $8.50, plus $15.50 divided by the number of patients seen, up to a maximum of 6 patients. For 7 or more patients - an amount equal to $8.50 plus $.70 per patient.

Derived fees example 2

In example 2, the patient was given 4 fields of superficial radiotherapy. The schedule fee is calculated by:

  • adding the fee for item 15000 (1 field) of $43.25 plus
  • the fee for item 15003 calculated at 3 additional fields, i.e., 3 x $17.35 = $52.05
  • total schedule fee = $43.25 + $52.05 = $95.30

Example 2 - radiation oncology items 15000 and 15003

This table provides an example only of the derived fee for radiation oncology item 15003. Item descriptions and fee amounts used may not correspond with current Medicare Benefit Schedule (MBS) information.

Item

Group T2 - Radiation oncology, Subgroup - Superficial

15000

(Benefits for administration of general anaesthetic for radiotherapy are payable under item 21980) Group T10

Radiotherapy, superficial, (including treatment with x-rays, radium rays or other radioactive substances), not being a service to which another item in this Group applies, each attendance at which fractionated treatment is given.

Fee: $43.25

Benefit: 75% = $32.45, 85% = $36.80

15003

Two or more fields up to a maximum of 5 additional fields

Derived Fee: the fee for item 15000 plus for each field in excess of 1, an amount of $17.35.

Derived fees example 3

In example 3, the patient's injury related to a foreign body in the shoulder. The schedule fee is determined by:

  • adding the fee for item 57703 (shoulder x-ray) of $54.00 plus
  • the amount for the report as stated in item 59103 of $21.30
  • total schedule fee = $54.00 + $21.30 = $75.30

Example 3 - diagnostic radiology item 59103

This table provides an example only of the derived fee for radiology item 59103. Item descriptions and fee amounts used may not correspond with current Medicare Benefit Schedule (MBS) information.

Item

Group I3 - Diagnostic radiology, Subgroup 9 - Radiographic examination for localisation of foreign bodies and report

59103

Foreign body, localisation of and report, not being a service to which another item in this Group applies (R).

Derived Fee: the fee for radiographic examination of the area and report plus an amount of $21.30.
