Manage payment destination online

Table 1

Step

Action

1

Manage payment destination + Read more ...

See online guide, Centrelink online account help - Manage where your payments are sent.

The customer can view all their current Payment Destinations (unless they have a payment nominee appointed). From this page, they can select Change payment destination to update their payment destination.

Previous payment destination details will not display if the customer is a newly independent customer. This may be the case if they are changing their payment destination from their parent/guardian's bank account to their own.

New or existing payment destination details may apply to one or more of a customer's current payments.

2

Customer selects Change payment destination to update their payment destination + Read more ...

Customers must have a payment status of:

  • Current (CUR)
  • Current Zero Rate (CZR)
  • Pay (PAY)
  • Assessed (ASS)
  • Suspended (SUS), or
  • Cancel Next Pay (CNP)

Is the customer eligible to use this service?

  • Yes, go to Step 3
  • No, the customer will return to the Payment Destination page. Procedure ends here

3

Update Payment Destination + Read more ...

The customer can:

  • use an existing payment destination for their payment/s, or
  • add a new payment destination for their payment/s (see online guide, Centrelink online account help - Manage your payment destination)

From this page, the customer can then select:

  • Begin, go to Step 4
  • Home to cancel the process, and return to the online account homepage, procedure ends here

4

Update Payment Destination - Enter Details page + Read more ...

The customer must update the following information:

  • Account name
  • Account number
  • Branch code (BSB)

Note: the customer must have legal access to the account. The account must be in the customer's name alone or jointly with another person. Customers without legal access to the account will not be able to change the payment destination online.

The customer can then select:

  • Next, go to Step 5
  • Home to cancel the process, procedure ends here

5

Update Payment Destination + Read more ...

The customer must select the payment they would like sent to the nominated payment destination.

Newly independent customers can view their current payment destination details. They will not be able to view previous payment destination details.

After selecting a payment on this page, the customer can select:

  • Next, go to Step 6
  • Home to cancel the process and return to the previous process, procedure ends here

6

Update Payment Destination - more than 1 payment + Read more ...

Does the customer receive more than 1 payment type:

  • Yes, customer:
    • must answer Yes or No to applying the update to all their payments, and
    • select Next, followed by Continue
  • No, customer selects Continue, go to Step 7

7

Update Payment Destination - Review and submit page + Read more ...

The customer must select Begin to review their payment destination.

This page displays the updated payment destination details.

If the customer has a partner, the system will advise that the update will not affect their partner's record.

The customer can select either:

  • Edit payment destination details if the details are wrong
  • Next if the details are correct

The customer is presented with the following declaration:

I declare that:

  • The information I have given is correct
  • Giving false or misleading information is a serious offence
  • I need to advise Centrelink of any changes to my circumstances as soon as the change occurs
  • I have read and accept the privacy statement

The customer must select the checkbox to accept the declaration.

From this page, the customer can select:

  • Continue, go to Step 8
  • Home, to cancel the process and return to the Payment Destination page, procedure ends here

8

Update Payment Destination - Receipt page + Read more ...

This page confirms the update has submitted successfully.

It also provides a receipt number, the date and Australian Eastern Standard/Daylight time.

From this page, the customer can select:

  • Information you provided to view a summary of the updates
  • Save your receipt to keep a record of their update
  • Return to Manage Payment Destination to go back to the Payment Destination page
  • Home to go back to the online account homepage

View payment history online

Table 2

Step

Action

1

View payment history + Read more ...

Customers may go online to view a history of their Centrelink and family assistance payments. This service provides a list of all payments made to the customer during the last 15 months. This will display in date order.

The following payments will not show on the View Payment History page:

  • Farm Household Allowance (FHA)
  • Child Care Subsidy (CSS) including Additional Child Care Subsidy (ACCS), and
  • Status Resolution Support Services (SRSS)

Customers can access the service using:

  • Centrelink online account
    • Select the Next Payment or Last Payment tile on the Centrelink online account homepage
    • Select MENU > Payments and Claims > Manage payments > View payment history
  • Express Plus Centrelink mobile app
    • Select Profile on the Express Plus Centrelink mobile app home screen, then select Financial details

Customers will see the delivery date, gross and net amounts for their next payment. Providing there are no changes in their circumstances, the 'pending payment' will display:

  • the net amount they will receive
  • the delivery date

Customers can see details of individual payments by selecting the Benefit or net amount figure.

The View payments option is also available on the Express Plus Centrelink mobile apps.
