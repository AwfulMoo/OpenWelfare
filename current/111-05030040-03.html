Indigenous Services 1800 136 380 + Read more ...

Opening hours: Monday - Friday, 8 am - 5 pm. Queue opening times are in the customer's local time.

For customers who identify as an Aboriginal and/or Torres Strait Islander person, to call about our payments and services. They may:

  • live in rural and remote parts of Australia
  • do not have direct access to other facilities
  • identify as 'non-Indigenous recipients/claimants and use this line if their enquiry directly relates to their Indigenous dependent, the Indigenous person they care for, or the Indigenous person for whom they are a nominee.'
