Processing travel/KM items for community nursing

Table 3: This table describes how to process payments of community nursing claims for travel/KMs.

Note: From 1st January 2019, Community Nursing travel is paid at $0.76 per kilometre after the first 20 KM. Providers are to include KM travelled and the charge claimed in the service text of online claims. Subtract the first 20km from each visit from the total KM travelled and add the residual amount in the claim. A force pend has been placed on NA10 to allow manual processing of this item. The return message 3-DVA ITM? has updated to remind staff of processing instructions.

Step

Action

1

For online claims: + Read more ...

Check service text for the number of kilometres being claimed:

  • If the distance travelled has not been supplied by the provider or the charge has been calculated incorrectly, reject the NA10 item with RSN S545
  • If the distance travelled is present and the charge has been calculated correctly, reject ALL services on that voucher with RSN S259 and process manually using a different Date of Lodgement than the online claim

For manual claims, go to Step 2

2

Manually assess travel for NA10 + Read more ...

  • Multiply the number of KM’s claimed by $0.76, the result is the total benefit payable
  • Key this amount as the fee for the NA10 and press enter
  • key PI 18 and RSN 288 and press enter
  • The system will add GST to the total and the claim is now OK to pay
