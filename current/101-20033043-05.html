Coronavirus Supplement Payment Rate Change Transition Matrix - January 2021

Eligible customers with a regular fortnightly reporting period that started on or before 31 December 2020 were entitled to receive a Coronavirus Supplement payment of $250 with their regular payment. This means that customers who were due to report on or before 13 January 2021 received $250 with their regular payment.

Customers whose regular reporting day was 14 January 2021 onwards, received $150 per fortnight. Customers continued to receive $150 per fortnight until 31 March 2021 as long as they remained eligible.

Example - Coronavirus Supplement Entitlement Period

Table 7

Item

Description

1

Customers reporting period spans across 2 Coronavirus Entitlement Periods

A customer's reporting period in most cases differed to that of the Coronavirus Entitlement Period. When a customer's payment is granted, their first reporting period may span over 2 Coronavirus Entitlement Periods. If the customer was eligible to receive at least $1 of payment within both of the Coronavirus Supplement Entitlement Periods, the system will pay them 2 payments of the Coronavirus Supplement.

A customer is only eligible to 1 supplement per Coronavirus Supplement Entitlement Period. Check if the customer has already received the supplement after they have reported for their reporting period.

The One Time Payment Main Menu (OTPMM) screen within Customer First gives further information and the explanation to the eligibility dependant on the customers reporting period.

2

Customer paid $1

Customer in receipt of JobSeeker Payment (JSP) is due to report on 18 February for the period 5- 18 February 2021.

The customer declares income from employment. They have no working credit.

The income depletes their basic benefit but the customer still received a partial amount of Rent Assistance (RA) of $50.

As the customer has still received more than $1 of Coronavirus Supplement eligible income support payment, they will receive the Coronavirus Supplement payment for that period.

3

Customer transfers to Age Pension

Customer in receipt of Jobseeker Payment (JSP) with an EPED end date of 20 January 2021.

Customer reported on 20 January 2021 and received a Coronavirus Supplement payment of $150 with their regular JSP on 21 January 2021.

Customer transfers to Age Pension on 26 January 2021.

Customer receives a final partial payment of JSP for the period 21 - 25 January 2021. Because they have received at least $1 of their final partial payment they will receive a final Coronavirus Supplement payment of $150.
