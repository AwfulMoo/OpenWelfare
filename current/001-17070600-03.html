Manually booking Job seeker claim appointment

Table 1

Step

Action

1

Customer needs to book a claim appointment manually + Read more ...

If for any reason a customer is unable to book the job seeker online claim appointment through the claim or when completing an Assisted Customer Claim with a customer, book the Jobseeker Online Claim Appointment via the Appointments tab in Process Direct.

From the Booked Appointments screen, select create new appointment and complete the following fields:

  • Appointment details
    • Appointment Type: Jobseeker Online Claim Appointment
    • Appointment Channel: Phone
    • Service Reason: JobSeeker Payments or Youth Allowance
  • Appointment Booking
    • On this day: select required date
    • Location: Participation Appointments Profile 2
    • Select the appointment time. Note: if the available time is in minus, this means there are no available appointments at this time. Staff are only to select appointments with positive values
  • Select Save button

Procedure ends here.

Customer attends service centre for job seeker online claim appointment

Table 2

Step

Action

1

Customer presents at service centre + Read more ...

When a customer presents at a service centre to attend their scheduled job seeker online claim appointment, explain that the preferred option is for the customer to attend via phone. Determine if there are other reasons why the customer has come into the service centre.

Note: if the customer has a Restricted Access record, see Administration of Restricted Access.

Is the customer unable to attend the scheduled appointment time and wants to reschedule the phone claim appointment?

  • Yes, see Step 9 in Table 3
  • No, go to Step 2

2

Customer requests the service centre to conduct the appointment + Read more ...

If it is identified that the customer requires the appointment to be conducted by the service centre:

  • select the Customer Summary tab from Process Direct
  • key the CRN and select Go
  • from the Search Results click on the CRN
  • select the Appointments icon
  • highlight the appointment line by selecting anywhere on the booked appointment that you are actioning
  • select the Change selected appointment icon
  • click on the drop down menu in the New Status field and update the appointment status to Completed
  • click the Save button

Go to Step 3.

3

Review the Progress of Claim (PoC) DOC + Read more ...

The Progress of Claim DOC provides information on where the claim is at during the assessment process.

In the Super Key field, key DL and press [Enter]. The Display on Access DOC and DL/Notes screens will display.

  • Select the OLC Claim Progress or ACC Claim Progress hyperlink from the DL/Notes screen
  • Review the information in the Text box
  • Streamline claims, see below

Streamline claims

Streamline claims are identified by a Transition Claim DOC on the DL/Notes screen.

The types of transition claims are:

  • STU to JSP Transition Claim DOC
  • PP to JSP Transition Claim DOC

Note: some customers transferring to JobSeeker Payment (JSP) may meet the eligibility criteria for their claim to be progressed for automatic assessment in Process Direct. Where this occurs a benefit status of Current Zero Rate (CZR) with the reason Awaiting Participation Interview (API) will display. At the job seeker claim appointment the Participation Interview must be completed so a RapidConnect determination made on the Job Seeker RapidConnect (JMRC) screen to determine the customer's start date of payments. The Job Seeker Snapshot is completed for Youth Allowance Student to Youth Allowance Job seekers.

See Straight through processing and Participation Interview.

Procedure ends here.

Actioning a job seeker online claim appointment

Table 3

Step

Action

1

Allocate the job seeker online claim appointment + Read more ...

Service Officers can access Workload Management capability and/or Work Items through:

  • Worklist (Work Optimiser)
  • Inbox (Process Direct)

To receive a job seeker online claim appointment, select the Ready button on the top right corner of the Process Direct Inbox.

Service Officers are allocated:

  • Job Seeker New Claim Appointment
  • Participation Appointment for streamline JSK claim (Parti Transfer Appt)
  • Student to Job Seeker Transfer Appointment (Stu to Jsk Appt)

Bundling

At times staff may receive multiple Work Items, referred to as a 'bundle' or 'bundle allocation'. Bundled Work Items display in the Process Direct Inbox with a unique Bundling ID. See Workload Management.

Restricted Access records

If allocated a Restricted Access record:

  • Send an email, using the security marking Official: Sensitive, to Restricted Access. Include in the email:
    • CRN
    • Customer has lodged a JSP or YAL claim
    • Claim/transfer appointment booked for (date) and (time)
  • Do not hold the appointment to inbox in Process Direct or Work Optimiser

2

Check customer record + Read more ...

Check the following on the customer record before starting the Participation Interview workflow:

  • If the previous benefit status shows as Cancelled (CAN). If the benefit status shows the cancellation reason as Cancelled Next Pay (CNP), Service Officers code the following on the Benefit Action (BA) screen. Note: this does not apply to student or parenting transfers:
    • SVC Rsn - previous benefit (with the status of CNP)
    • Act - CAN
    • Rsn - OTH
    • DOE - DPT+1 or start date of payment if no payments were made to the customer. In these cases, payment will update to REJ-OTH
    • Complete Source and Receipt date and Submit Change
    • Annotate the progress of claim DOC with appropriate details of CNP cancellation
  • If the customer was a full-time student prior to claiming, end all Education Details via the EDCH screen
  • If current study details still show on the customer's record, end date the study details. See Effect of end of course on payments for students
  • The Participation Summary has no pending Job Seeker Snapshot indicator(s)
  • If there is an existing ESAt flag, see Actioning a pending Job Seeker Snapshot initiated Employment Services Assessment (ESAt) activity
  • Where a customer requires an Interpreter to undertake the Jobseeker online claim appointment and Participation Interview, see Contacting a telephone interpreter (on-demand)

3

Contact the customer/correspondence nominee + Read more ...

Service Officers are to contact the customer/correspondence nominee at the time the customer has booked for their jobseeker online claim appointment.

A pre-call notification SMS via Desktop Messaging must be sent prior to calling a customer, see Calling a customer or returning a customer's call.

In Process Direct:

  • select the Appointments icon
  • highlight the appointment line by selecting anywhere on the booked appointment that you are actioning
  • select the Display Appointment icon to view the customer's contact details

4

Authenticate the customer + Read more ...

When a call is answered, the Service Officer can advise their name and that they are calling from Services Australia. Service Officers must inform the caller at the start of every outbound call that the call may be recorded for quality assurance and training purposes.

The Service Officer must be satisfied the customer record being accessed belongs to the correct person prior to assisting with a customer enquiry or before commencing the job seeker online appointment. See Authenticating a Centrelink customer.

Note: it is not appropriate for the Service Officer to disclose the reason for the call or to identify the team they work with, as this could reveal the type of business the person being sought has with the agency, and thereby impact on their privacy.

5

Conducting the appointment + Read more ...

The Booked Appointments table will show if the customer has previously booked and attended a Jobseeker Online Claim Appointment.

If the booked appointment outbound call relates to the:

  • first appointment:
    • first call, go to Step 6
    • second call, go to Step 7
  • second appointment:
    • first call, go to Step 8
    • second call, go to Step 9

Note: do not start the Participation Interview until successful contact has been made, the customer/nominee has been authenticated and the customer or correspondence nominee wishes to proceed.

6

First appointment - First call + Read more ...

Service Officers must attempt to contact the customer using all available numbers.

Was the first customer contact call successful?

  • Yes:
    • and the customer/nominee is wanting to conduct the appointment:
      - on the Change Appointment screen, from the First Appointment > First Call drop down box, select Answered. The appointment status will auto-update to Completed
      - see Table 4
    • and the customer/nominee is requesting to reschedule the appointment, go to Step 10
  • No:
    • on the Change Appointment screen, from the First Appointment > First Call drop down box, select Not Answered (either 'message left' or 'no message left')
    • the system will then auto-populate the outcome on the Change Appointment screen, the Notes tab in Process Direct and un-assign the appointment Work Item with the reason SCR - Second Attempt Required
    • Procedure ends here

7

First appointment - Second call + Read more ...

Make a second attempt to contact the customer/correspondence nominee. Service Officers must attempt to contact the customer using all available numbers.

Was the second customer contact call successful?

  • Yes:
    • and the customer/nominee is wanting to conduct the appointment:
      - on the Change Appointment screen, from the First Appointment > Second Call drop down box, select Answered. The appointment status will auto-update to Completed
      - see Table 4
    • and the customer/nominee is requesting to reschedule the appointment, go to Step 10
  • No:
    • on the Change Appointment screen, from the First Appointment > Second Call drop down box, select Not Answered (either 'message left' or 'no message left')
    • the system will then auto-populate the outcome on the Change Appointment screen, the Notes tab in Process Direct and update the appointment status to Cancelled - Did Not Attend (DNA)
    • the job seeker online claim phone appointment will be placed on hold with a reason of Awaiting Appointment
    • a Rebook Appointment task will also generate in the customer's Centrelink online account. Procedure ends here

An immediate notification (SMS or email) will be sent to the customer telling them to sign into their Centrelink online account or Express Plus Centrelink mobile app and complete the online task to rebook their appointment.

The customer online claim tracker will show as 'On hold' until the customer completes the task. The online task will disappear once the appointment has been rebooked.

8

Second appointment - First call + Read more ...

When the customer actions the Rebook Appointment' task in their Centrelink online account, in Process Direct, the Booked Appointments table will show the second (rebooked) appointment as New.

Was the first customer contact call for the second appointment successful?

  • Yes:
    • and the customer/nominee is wanting to conduct the appointment:
      - on the Change Appointment screen, from the Second Appointment > Second Call drop down box, select Answered. The appointment status will auto-update to Completed
      - see Table 4
    • and the customer/nominee is requesting to reschedule the appointment, go to Step 10
  • No:
    • on the Change Appointment screen, from the Second Appointment > First Call drop down box, select Not Answered (either 'message left' or 'no message left')
    • the system will then auto-populate the outcome on the Change Appointment screen, the Notes tab and un-assign the appointment Work Item with the reason SCR - Second Attempt Required
    • Procedure ends here

9

Second appointment - Second call + Read more ...

Make a second attempt to contact the customer/correspondence nominee. Service Officers must attempt to contact the customer using all available numbers.

Was the second customer contact call for the second appointment successful?

  • Yes:
    • and the customer/nominee is wanting to conduct the appointment:
      - on the Change Appointment screen, from the Second Appointment > Second Call drop down box, select Answered. The appointment status will auto-update to Completed
      - see Table 4
    • and the customer/nominee is requesting to reschedule the appointment, go to Step 10
  • No:
    • on the Change Appointment screen, from the Second Appointment > Second Call drop down box, select Not Answered (either 'message left' or 'no message left')
    • the system will then auto-populate the outcome on the Change Appointment screen, the Notes tab in Process Direct and update the appointment status to Cancelled - Did Not Attend (DNA)
    • in Process Direct, the claim will auto-reject with reason Failed to Attend (FAI)
    • Procedure ends here

10

Rescheduling an appointment + Read more ...

  • Select the Reschedule icon
  • Select the new appointment details and save the updates
  • Tell the customer or correspondence nominee they must attend an appointment within 14 days of submitting their claim or it will be rejected
  • Procedure ends here

2

JobSeeker Payment (JSP) or Youth Allowance (YA) Provisional + Read more ...

Customers may be eligible to apply for JSP or YA Provisional when they have an outstanding Disability (DSP) claim. Customers who meet this criteria are exempt from mutual obligation requirements for the period during which the DSP claim is being determined. To determine if the customer should be coded with an exemption:

  • Go to the claim by selecting the Receipt ID hyperlink on the Appointment details line.
  • The Customer and Activity Information is displayed. If the customer is claiming:
    • JSP, go to Step 3
    • YA, go to Step 4

3

Determining if customer is claiming JobSeeker Provisional + Read more ...

To determine if the customer is claiming JobSeeker Provisional and exempt from mutual obligations:

  • Select the Keyword icon. If the keyword JSPPROV is displayed the customer is applying for JobSeeker Provisional, or
  • Select the Review of claim. Under the Circumstance details, the circumstances will display Disability Support Pension - JobSeeker Payment confirming the customer is applying for JobSeeker Provisional

Is the customer applying for JobSeeker Provisional?

  • Yes, from the Review of Claim note the date of the claim submission, go to Step 5
  • No:
    • go to the More Options tab
    • select the Participation Interview
    • complete the Participation Interview. Note: the Resources page contains a script for service officers
    • procedure ends here

4

Determining if customer is claiming Youth Allowance Provisional + Read more ...

To determine if the customer is claiming Youth Allowance Provisional and exempt from mutual obligations:

  • Go to the Document List (DL) by entering 'DL' in the Super Key
  • Check to see if a Progress of Claim (PoC) DOC displays for Disability Support Pension (DSP)
  • Continue to check the DL screen to ensure no DSP outcome DOCs or withdraw DOCs are on the record after the DSP PoC

Has the customer lodged a claim for Youth Allowance and Disability Support Pension?

  • Yes, from the DSP PoC DOC, note the Receipt Date, go to Step 5
  • No:
    • go to the More Options tab
    • select the Participation Interview
    • complete the Participation Interview. Note: the Resources page contains a script for service officers
    • procedure ends here

5

Customer is claiming JobSeeker Provisional or Youth Allowance Provisional + Read more ...

Customers who submit a dual DSP and JSP/YAL JSK claim are eligible for an initial 13-week exemption from mutual obligations while the DSP claim is being determined. The claiming DSP exemption will be coded

Service Officers are to:

  • Annotate the JSP/YAL PoC DOC advising customer is eligible for a DSP - Claiming DSP exemption from the 13 weeks starting from the DSP claim submission date
  • Go to the More Options tab
  • Select Participation Interview
  • Complete the Participation Interview. Note: the Resources page contains a script for service officers

See JobSeeker Payment (JSP) and Youth Allowance (YA) (Provisional).
