IHI general enquiries – frequently asked questions

Table 1

Question/situation

Response

Action

What is an IHI used for and/or do I need an IHI?

You need an IHI if you want:

  • to register for a My Health Record, or
  • view your COVID-19 digital certificate online

People who are not eligible for Medicare or Department of Veterans Affairs (DVA), will need an IHI to view their COVID-19 digital certificate online.

Do I already have an IHI?

If you are enrolled with Medicare or the Department of Veterans Affairs (DVA), you will already have an IHI.

  • Check if the individual has an IHI, see Search for Individual Healthcare Identifier (IHI) in Healthcare Identifiers (HI) Service
  • Do a security check before giving them their IHI over the phone, see Security checks and release of Individual Healthcare Identifier (IHI) information
  • Ask the caller to repeat back the number to confirm they have written it down correctly

How do I apply for an IHI?

Direct the caller to myGov to apply for an IHI online using the IHI service, or the Services Australia website for the MS003 form

Can my child get an IHI?

You can apply for an IHI using the paper application form for your child if they are under 14 years. A child over 14 years must apply for their own IHI.

A parent or guardian will need to complete the MS003 form, send it in with certified copies of EOI documentation for their child.

The online process is not available for individuals under 14 years of age.

Can I view my child’s vaccination history online?

To view a COVID-19 digital certificate for a person under 14, the parent needs to apply for an IHI for their child using the MS003 form and then register the child for a My Health Record.

Direct the caller to the below website:

  • Apply for an IHI via the MS003, and then
  • Register for a My Health Record

How will I know what my IHI is?

If you have applied for an IHI using a paper form, once processing is complete, a letter will be sent to you, which includes your IHI.

If you have applied online, your IHI will be notified to you on the screen.

How do I view electronic proof of vaccination history if I already have an IHI?

If you are eligible for Medicare or DVA you will already have an IHI.

You can view your vaccination history by using Medicare Online Account.

See the Linking Centrelink, Child Support and Medicare online accounts table.

I used to have a Medicare card because I was in Australia on a temporary visa. I am no longer eligible for Medicare. Can I view vaccination information online?

Yes, if there is an IHI associated with the eligibility end dated Medicare record, the customer can view their information in the IHI service.

You can use the IHI service to view your COVID-19 digital certificate.

I applied for an IHI but have since moved.

Can I update my address?

You can only update the address for a person who has an IHI only record in CDMS (not a Medicare or DVA record).

Individuals who have a Medicare or DVA record must update their address via their Medicare online account or by calling the Medicare public enquiry line (see Phone us, Medicare phone numbers, Medicare program).

To update the address for a person who has an IHI only record in CDMS (not a Medicare or DVA record):

  • Do a security check, see Security checks and release of Individual Healthcare Identifier (IHI) information
  • If they identify their previous address stored in the Consumer Directory Maintenance System (CDMS), update with new details, see Amend Individual Healthcare Identifier (IHI) in Healthcare Identifier (HI) Service
  • If needed, escalate to Tier 3 – Digital Health Operations

I applied for an IHI some time ago but I have since moved.

The IHI can be provided over the phone after a security check.
  • Do a security check before giving the caller their IHI over the phone, see Security checks and release of Individual Healthcare Identifier (IHI) information
  • Ask the caller to repeat back the number to confirm they have written it down correctly

I did not receive a letter with my IHI on it.

A new copy of the letter can be requested in CDMS.
  • To have a new letter printed, go to the Notification Details tab and request a new letter, see Step 3 of Amend a IHI

Individual enquires about the progress of their IHI application.

If the application process is complete, the IHI can be provided over the phone after a security check.
  • Do a security check before giving them their IHI over the phone, see Security checks and release of Individual Healthcare Identifier (IHI) information
  • Ask the caller to repeat back the number to confirm they have written it down correctly
  • If the application has not been completed, check PaNDA to confirm the status of the application. If required escalate to Tier 3 – Digital Health Operations to prioritise processing

What do I do with the IHI once I get it and I would like electronic proof of my vaccination?

Once you have your IHI you can:

  • create a myGov account and link the IHI Service to view your COVID-19 digital certificate, or
  • register for My Health Record. See Register for a My Health Record for details on how to register

To view a COVID-19 digital certificate the parent must apply for an IHI for their child using the MS003 form and then register the child for a My Health Record.

See also, Register for a My Health Record

  • Alternatively a paper certificate may be requested from the Australian Immunisation Register (AIR)
  • Go to the AIR enquiry line (see Phone us, Medicare phone numbers, Australian Immunisation Register)

Apply for an IHI via the MS003 and then register for a My Health Record.
