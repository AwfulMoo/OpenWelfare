ICT Service Desk referrals

Locked payments need to be unlocked by ICT for the debt shell activity to be correctly assessed and finalised. Complete the Status Resolution Support Services (SRSS) issues webform, select Debt Incident from the drop down list of issues to report:

  • Go to mySupport
  • Search using key words, error message or application name and select the relevant webform
  • Complete and Submit the required webform taking note of any receipt or reference code produced

SRSS debt reason codes

Reason code

Description

DCS

Dependent children/students

Use when a customer was paid DCA:

  • when the child was no longer in their care
  • at the same time as their partner was paid DCA
  • when their partner received FTB for the child
  • after the child turned 18

DUP

Duplicate payment

Use when a customer:

  • has been paid an SRSS payment for the same period and same component more than once. This includes Living Allowance; Rental Assistance Allowance and Dependent Child Allowance
  • has been incorrectly paid lump sum arrears

Do not use when a customer has been paid an SRSS payment and another Centrelink payment at the same time. Use NEP for this situation.

NEP

Not eligible for payment

Use when a customer has been paid an SRSS payment:

  • and another Centrelink payment at the same time
  • after they were cancelled; suspended or rejected

RAE

Rent incorrectly declared/coded

This is used when the customer was paid Rental Assistance Allowance they were not entitled to as:

  • they did not provide their rent verification
  • partner is receiving Rent Assistance as part of Family Tax Benefit
  • they changed to a Band that does not pay Rental Assistance Allowance

If the Rental Assistance Allowance is on the same Debt Shell as DCA that was paid to the partner then use DCA as the reason code.

ISI

IEP

Income: earnings wrongly declared/coded(self)

Income: earnings wrongly declared/coded(ptnr)

Use these when the customer declared income and later amended the amount earned.

IES

IPN

Income: earnings not declared/coded (self)

Income: earnings not declared/coded (ptnr)

Use these when the:

  • customer did not declare the income at the reporting time
  • Service Officer did not action the declared income

The income was declared in ISIS and the information did not populate into SAP.

UCE

Income: undeclared cash earnings

OIS

OIP

Income: not earnings or investment (self)

Income: not earnings or investment (ptnr)

PAD

Payment made after death
