Insolvency email template

Item

Description

1

The below Insolvency/ Liquidation email text must be sent from the PPLINFO mailbox.

The email must not be modified with the exception of inputting the ABN and name.

Create a new email. Do not reply from an email received.

From: PPLINFO

Subject: Response - Official notification of liquidation

Classification: Unofficial

Hello,

We have received your official notification of liquidation.

There are no employees claiming Parental Leave Pay under:

ABN: XXX XXX XXX XXX

Name: XXXXXXX

Regards,

Paid Parental Leave Employer Processing Team

Services Australia

Phone: 131 158

*If you are calling from overseas, please phone +61 3 6222 3455, and ask to be transferred to the Paid Parental Leave Employer Processing Team.
