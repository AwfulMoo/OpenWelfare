Online Messaging for customers

Where a customer has not provided correct Medicare details for their child or a customer is receiving FTB for a child that is not up to date with immunisation requirements, (Immunisation status of 'N') the following messaging will appear in their online services:

  • Customers where incorrect Medicare information is provided for their child:

'You need to provide (child's name) correct Medicare number, child reference number (IRN) and date of birth. We cannot check (child's name) immunisation status with the Australian Immunisation Register.'

  • Customers receiving FTB for a child with an established link with the AIR and the child is not up to date with immunisation requirements (Immunisation status of 'N'):

'Your payments will be affected as your child does not meet immunisation requirements. Please contact your vaccination provider or general practitioner to update your child's immunisation record.'

Families Override Contact Officers

Staff with appropriate access can be located from the Business Processing and Change Branch homepage > Featured Content > Families Override Contacts Program.

Explanation of messages, issues and possible Service Officer action

Table 1: This table provides an explanation of the various error messages that customers can receive when using online services.

Category

Description

1

Reminder - Your payments will be affected as the child does not meet immunisation requirements. Update details

This reminder will display if the child is not linked with the AIR and a Medicare number is needed. The reminder will hyperlink to an Online Service that allows the customer to provide the Medicare number for the relevant child.

2

Reminder - We have checked the Australian Immunisation Register and the child does not meet the immunisation requirements. Please contact your vaccination provider or general practitioner to request an update for immunisation record

This reminder will display if the child is linked with the AIR and the child's immunisation status is 'Not Met'. The customer is advised to contact their vaccination provider as their payment will be affected if they fail to take action.

3

Message - You are unable to update your child's Medicare number online at this time as: You have already completed an update for this child

An update to the Medicare number has been made recently and is pending. This is to prevent duplication of information.

4

Message - This service is currently unavailable. Please try again later

Customer record is locked by a Service Officer or an outstanding activity. See Access to Centrelink self service - set up, checking, locking and resetting for record locking information.

5

Message - We are unable to process your update. Please contact on 136150

There is a submitted activity on the customer's record – in Customer First, check the Activity List (AL) screen. Action as appropriate.

AIR Mismatch codes

Families Override Staff Note:

When viewing the Medicare portal if individual registration source is listed as ACIR SIN Claim this generally indicates the child received vaccinations but is not yet enrolled in Medicare. For more information see Merge or match individual records and SIN records in Australian Immunisation Register (AIR).

Where a child is not linked and no mismatch code is present, the GP Warning and Suggested Action will display 'Send a link request to AIR by entering the child's Medicare number using the MEI screen'.
