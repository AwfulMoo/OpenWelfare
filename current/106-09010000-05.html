Digital Driver Licences - Security and refresh features

A digital driver licence contains the same information as a physical driver licence. The same information is required for coding in the Identity Confirmation Dashboard in Process Direct and verification for DVS.

The physical card must be provided and scanned to the customer record as per current escalation procedures if:

  • the Service Officer is not satisfied that the licence photo matches the customer
  • the digital drivers licence cannot be refreshed, or
  • the digital drivers licence fails to verify via the DVS
