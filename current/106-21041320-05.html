Country Specific Information

Liaising electronically with the Patronati

Apart from the phone, contact with the Patronati can also be made via email and OOM. However, there are conditions to this usage:

  • OOM – All documents, including foreign pension claims listing/box number, are sent via OOM

Format:

<ES Reference>_<Surname>_<XXX>n

Note: ‘UNKNOWN’ is used where the Spanish reference is not known.

where XXX is:

CLM – claim for Spanish AGE/INV/SUR

INS - request for Spanish periods/providing AWLR

NOG – Notice of Grant of Australian pension

NOR - Notice of Rejection of Australian pension

MED – request/response medical documentation

OTH – any other liaisons

n = to be used if files need to be split due to size limit

11111111111_GARCIA FERNANDEZ_CLM1

UNKNOWN_GARCIA FERNANDEZ_OTH

NOR - Notice of Rejection of Spanish pension

OTH – any other liaisons
