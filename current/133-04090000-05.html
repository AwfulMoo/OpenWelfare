Scenarios - asset threshold limit

Table 18: Scenarios when asset value changes made by the customer will or will not be automatically applied.

Item

Description

1

Online changes to asset values that will be applied automatically

Customer updates the following details:

  • adds savings of $3,000
  • deletes shares valued $5,000
  • total change is a decrease of $2,000 (within the threshold limit)

Automatic updates

If the change occurs, the update will finalise automatically and the details can be viewed in Process Direct or Customer First/Customer Record.

In Process Direct, a Note will show:

  • who made the update
  • that the update was finalised by Straight Through Processing (STP)
  • the system used (WEB)
  • the change of circumstance number
  • the creation and receipt dates
  • what income & assets updates were made

The Note displays on the Notes screen in Process Direct and copies to the DL screen in Customer First/Customer Record.

2

Online changes to asset values that will not be applied automatically

Customer updates the following details:

  • adds savings of $550,000
  • deletes other asset - Vehicle value $20,000
  • total change is an increase of $530,000 (over the threshold limit)*

Factors including payment and asset type may also affect whether the update will require manual processing or apply automatically.

Example: Termination payment update will require manual processing for JSP customers, however update will be automatically applied for AGE pension customers.

Manual processing

Process Direct

If the change needs manual processing or action by a Service Officer, the work item will drop through as a handoff for staff to action. Select the work item to open the transaction on the customer's record.

The Change of Circumstance Overview will provide the reason/s for Manual Action and a Resolution.

Customer First/Customer Record

If the change needs manual processing, the system will create an open DOC. The DOC will advise all the information supplied for staff to action.

Service Officers will need to:

  • manually code the income and asset changes
  • if applicable, verify the update using any documentation provided

These activities will have the keyword OIAMAN.

If a customer updates multiple categories in one transaction, it will have keyword OIACM.

The customer's ability to update income and assets detail will be locked until their income and asset update is completed in Process Direct or Customer First/Customer Record:

A message on the customer's Income and assets summary page advises that their account is locked and no updates are allowed to their income and assets due to changes in progress. They cannot make any changes to their income and assets details until their current update is processed.

If an update results in a change in payment, the customer is notified through a letter sent to their myGov Inbox (if subscribed) or postal mail.

Frequently asked questions

Table 19

Item

FAQs

1

How can I help a customer who started to update their income and assets details online but is stuck on a page?

Staff can access Customer Online View to help the customer navigate through the service.

Access this in Customer First by selecting Customer Online View in the Customer Online Account section.

Customers can also self-manage using online guides on the Services Australia website by Signing in on the Services Australia homepage, then online guides for help.

2

The customer updated their savings account details online. Why can they not submit the update until they update their home and contents details as well?

This occurs if there is nothing on their Centrelink record for their value of their home and contents. The customer is asked to provide home and contents details so their record can be updated.

3

Can the customer print a summary of all their income and assets?

Yes. Customers can select Generate income and asset statement on the Income and assets summary page. The statement is broken into individual income and assets categories.

4

Why has the customer got a message on their Income and assets summary page advising their account is locked? They cannot update their income and assets due to changes in progress. There are no started activities on the customer or partner record to finalise.

Refer to the Task locking information on the Background page for what to check. If there are no open activities that need to be finalised, escalate for investigation. See Troubleshooting Centrelink self service.

5

How does an organisation nominee know if income or assets are affecting their customer's payment?

They can use the Estimator component of the Payment and Service Finder.

6

Can a customer cancel their updates after submitting them?

They cannot do this online. If they need to make changes after submitting their update, they need to contact the agency to make any changes.

7

Can a customer do their Income and asset review online?

If they are sent a review for their income and assets because of a bereavement, they will be able to select the online task on their Centrelink online account homepage to complete the review online. If they are sent an income and assets review not relating to a bereavement, they need to complete and return the form. They will not be able to complete the review using their Centrelink online account.

8

A customer calls to discuss an update they completed online. Why does Process Direct not show the update?

The customer will always see their most recent updates in their online account when an update is finalised. However, if the customer's update completed quickly, it may not be visible in Process Direct. Perform a SAP refresh in Process Direct to align the customer and staff views.
