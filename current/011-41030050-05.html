This page contains field descriptions for tabs in the Consumer Directory Maintenance System (CDMS) for the Child Dental Benefits Schedule (CDBS).

Eligible Persons Details tab

Table 1: This table describes the field descriptions for the Eligible Persons Details tab in CDMS.

Field

Description

Agency ID
  • Customer Reference Number (CRN) - unique number provided by Centrelink
  • Department of Veterans' Affairs (DVA) file number - unique identifier number (UIN) provided by DVA

Agency ID type

CRN, DVA ID or DVA UIN

Name

First name, initial and last name - provided by Centrelink or DVA

Date of birth

Provided by Centrelink or DVA

Gender

Provided by Centrelink or DVA

Consumer ID

The child/teenager's Consumer ID is:

  • displayed where a Centrelink or DVA record has been matched to a Medicare record
  • blank where a Centrelink or DVA record has not been matched to a Medicare record

Matched Medicare status
  • E (Exact match by system)
  • P (Possible match by system)
  • N (Not matched)
  • A (Already matched)
  • M (Manually matched)
  • U (Unmatched)

Address sensitivity code

(indicates when the addressee's address details are valid or available)
  • Blank - the address is valid and has been used by Medicare to issue the notification
  • 1 - Cancelled - the Centrelink entitlement has been cancelled. Note: the child/teenager was entitled to a Centrelink benefit at some time in the calendar year but the entitlement has been cancelled. They remain entitled to Child Dental Benefits Schedule (CDBS) in the calendar year.
  • 2 - Deny Access - the addressee must not be given details about the child/teenager due to restrictions in place by Centrelink
  • 3 - Both Cancelled and Deny Access

Note: only Tier 3 can access code 2 and 3.

Address
  • Address of the eligible child/teenager as they are the person in receipt of the payment from Centrelink or DVA
  • An amended address actioned by Medicare as part of the notification issue/reissue process

Note: when blank, see Guardian/Parents Details tab.

Return mail flag

To be updated by Service Officers if the notification is sent back to Medicare. The return mail flag can only be performed in the Amend Consumer Details tab.

See also: Patient eligibility for Child Dental Benefits Schedule (CDBS) in Medicare

Overseas indicator

Provided by Centrelink or DVA

In prison indicator

Provided by Centrelink or DVA

Medicare deceased date

Date of death from the Medicare system

Agency deceased date

Provided by Centrelink or DVA

Note: where Deceased indicator is:

  • updated with a date, a notification is not issued
  • blank, a notification is issued when all other matching criteria is met

Consumer end date

The End Date indicator is set during the matching process between the Centrelink or DVA record and the Medicare record when an end date is recorded against a consumer record on CDMS.

Note: where a Consumer End Date is:

  • updated with a date, a notification is not issued
  • blank, a notification is issued when all other matching criteria is met

Medicare eligibility indicator

The Medicare Eligibility indicator is set during the matching process between the Centrelink or DVA record and the Medicare record. Where a matching Medicare record is found, the indicator is set to Y.

Note: where Medicare Eligibility indicator is:

  • Y, a notification is issued when all other matching criteria's are met
  • N, a notification is not issued

Entitlement Details tab

Table 3: This table describes the field descriptions for the Entitlement Details tab in CDMS.

Field

Description

Currently Eligible

Yes/No

Date of Inquiry

Displays the current date of inquiry.

Balance of Inquiry

Displays the remaining CDBS balance as at the date of inquiry.

Display ‘Unavailable’ if web service is down.

Current Entitlement Period

Displays the 2 consecutive calendar years of entitlement. The first calendar year is determined by the year the child/teenager receives their first CDBS service.

Example: A child/teenager receives and claims their first dental service in 2021. Therefore, their current entitlement period is 2021-2022

If a child/teenager is eligible for CDBS in 2021 but does not have any dental services in 2021, instead, they have services performed and claimed in 2022 their current entitlement period is 2022-2023

Note: A child/teenager must meet eligibility criteria for each calendar year of entitlement

CAP Amount

Displays the CDBS cap amount.

The cap amount is indexed yearly from 1 January. The increase in cap amount will only apply to a child/teenager who received their first eligible service in that year.

Example: A child/teenager has their first service in 2022 (therefore, year 1 of their 2 year eligibility is 2022). Because the benefit cap amount in 2022 is $1,026 this amount will apply across 2022 and 2023, assuming eligibility is maintained in 2023

If a child/teenager has their first service in 2021 (therefore, year 1 of their 2 year eligibility in 2021) they will receive the 2021 benefit cap amount of $1,013 which will apply across 2021 and 2022, assuming eligibility is maintained in 2022.

Entitlement year

Displays the child/teenager’s current or previous year of CDBS entitlement. Displays up to 2 rows.

Example: If a child/teenager’s Current Entitlement Period is 2022-2023 and they also had entitlement in 2021. Entitlement Year will display 2022 on the first row and 2021 on the second row.

Displays 'Unavailable' when web service is down

Benefit code
  • ABY = ABSTUDY - the person has eligibility because they are an ABY customer in their own right or receiving ABY (Schooling B) on behalf of an applicant
  • ABA = the person has eligibility because they are an ABY (Schooling A) customer on behalf of an applicant
  • YAL = Youth Allowance payment-the person has eligibility because they are a YAL customer in their own right
  • DSP = Disability Support pension - the person has eligibility because they are a DSP customer in their own right
  • CAR = Carer payment - the person has eligibility because they are a CAR customer in their own right
  • SPL = Special Benefit payment - the person has eligibility because they are an SPL customer in their own right
  • FTB = Family Tax benefit - the person has eligibility because they are an FTB Part A customer in their own right
  • F1X = FTB partner - the person has eligibility because their partner is an FTB Part A customer
  • KFY = child of FTB guardian - the person has eligibility because their carer(s) are FTB Part A customers
  • PPS = Parenting Payment Single - the person has eligibility because they are a PPS customer in their own right
  • PST = the person has eligibility because their parent is a PPS customer
  • PPP = Parenting Payment Partnered - the person has eligibility because they are a PPP customer in their own right
  • PPR = the person has eligibility because their partner is a PPP customer
  • PPT = the person has eligibility because their parent is a PPP customer
  • KDH = Double Orphan Pension - the person has eligibility because their career is a DOP customer

Department of Veterans' Affairs (DVA) codes are:

  • DVT - Veterans' Children Education and Training Scheme
  • MDT - Military Rehabilitation and Compensation Act Education and Training Scheme (MRCAETS)

Notification Details tab

Table 4: This table describes the field descriptions for the Notification Details tab in CDMS.

Field

Description

Letter type

Family letter

Individual letter

Approved Care Organisation (ACO)
  • Initial - indicates the initial notification
  • Reissue - indicates that the original notification was reissued
  • Initial Requested - indicates that an initial notification has been requested via the reissue tab, but has not been sent yet
  • Reissue Requested - indicates that a reissue notification has been requested via the reissue tab, but has not been sent yet
  • No Send - The requested notification was not sent due to address sensitivity code

Contact type
  • Mail - indicates the notification was issued to the consumer via physical mail
  • myGov - indicates the notification was issued to the consumer displayed via myGov Inbox

Date sent

Displays the date that the notification was sent to Fuji Xerox or my.gov.au

When there is no date sent then the notification is waiting to be sent in the next processing batch

Addressee name

Name of the addressee displayed in this column

Address

Displays the address the notification was sent to

When the contact type is myGov the address is blank
