Error and warning messages

Table 2

Error

Message text

Action

SR594

Crisis Data is mandatory for Crisis Payment claim

This will display if there is no data on the CRISIS_PRI screen.

Staff to update the screen with Crisis Payment claim data.

SR598

Customer may not be in Australia. Refresh RSIM.

This will display if the customer is not in Australia based on RSIM but claim has come through and the customer has said ‘yes’ they are in Australia.

Staff to check the RSIM screen and call the immigration datalink for such claims to determine if in Australia or not.

SR613

Qualifying payment Claim ( Claim ID ) is currently ‘In Process’

This will display if they system identifies that customer is not current on qualifying benefit but they have a claim for a qualifying payment that is 'In Process', 'On Hold' or 'Submitted'.

Staff to progress the qualifying payment prior to finalising the Crisis Payment or the Crisis Payment will reject. The Crisis Payment should not be finalised until the income support payment has been granted or rejected.

SR624

Cus not SUS or CAN prior to release date. Review entitlement to primary payment.

This will display if the system does not identify a SUS or CAN on the system for the period the customer was in prison or psychiatric confinement.

Staff to check the customer’s entitlements for the period of imprisonment and if not entitled staff should follow the admission process to update imprisonment details followed by debt action and referrals (step 12) before re-establishing primary payment eligibility.

The Crisis Payment should not be finalised until review of entitlement is complete. The claim should be placed on hold while this occurs.

Prison servicing forms

Table 3:

Form

Description

SU508

Claim for Crisis Payment Release from Prison or Psychiatric Confinement

To be issued to prisoners claiming Crisis Payment (CrP) before or after release from custody.

SU508 is only required where Assisted Customer Claim or online claim is not available.

SU517

Release Notification Ledger

This form is to be used by the Correctional Centre/Prison to notify Services Australia of 'due to be released' prisoners.

This form may not be used by service centres that have already established a local procedure for receiving this advice from a Correction Centre/Prison in their locality.

SU619

Prison Release and Anticipated Payment Receipt

This form is to be used when the prison issues CrP and/or Anticipated Payments by EBT card. It should be held at the prison until the payment is issued to the customer by an authorised prison officer. It must be signed by the customer and prison services officer on issue of the payment.
