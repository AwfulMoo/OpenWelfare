Processing a transfer from PES to ABSTUDY PES

Step

Action

1

Customer makes contact to enquire about ABSTUDY PES and additional entitlements under ABSTUDY + Read more ...

Is the Pensioner Education Supplement (PES) customer enquiring about ABSTUDY PES eligibility or receiving the additional entitlements available under ABSTUDY, such as Incidentals Allowance, Additional Incidentals Allowance, Away from Base, Fares Allowance or Residential Costs Option?

  • Yes
    • Advise the customer that a Claim for ABSTUDY PES (SY020) should be lodged by either contacting the ABSTUDY Smart Centre Telephony queue on 1800 132 317 or staff can use a priority transfer number (speed dial) 6008. The ABSTUDY Smart Centre Service Officer will complete the claim using the ABSTUDY Simplified Claiming Tool
    • The tool produces an Information to Support your Claim (ISC) letter and a Customer Declaration Form (CDF) listing the information provided by the customer and any additional information or verification documents the customer needs to provide. The customer will be required to sign the CDF and return it with any additional information requested
    • Customers who wish to complete a paper claim form can print this form via Self Service in a service centre
    • PES claims can be lodged without the return of a signed CDF, with the exception of customers applying for ABSTUDY PES and in receipt of a Department of Veterans' Affairs (DVA) qualifying pension
    • When a customer attends a face to face interview, Service Officers will issue a paper claim form ABSTUDY Pensioner Education Supplement claim (SY020)
  • No, procedure ends here

Note: staff who are not trained in ABSTUDY are required to transfer the customer to the ABSTUDY queue. ABSTUDY staff will complete the ABSTUDY Claiming Tool.

2

ABSTUDY PES claim for SY020 lodged at service centre + Read more ...

  • Access staff accept paper claims and documents required by processing teams

Check the claim to ensure the following:

  • customer name
  • address
  • phone number(s)
  • signature on claim

Check that the customer has completed all questions. If there are gaps, encourage the customer to complete all questions. A thorough check will be done by the processing team. Advise the customer that questions that have not been answered may cause a delay with claim processing.

Scan the claim and supporting document to the customer's record on Day 1.

Procedure ends here.

3

Process the ABSTUDY PES claim in accordance to the claim procedures + Read more ...

When the SY020 has been received, the PES is required to be cancelled so the ABSTUDY PES claim can be processed. The PES cancellation should be processed as follows:

  • Navigate to PES system:
    • In Nxt code SVPES, and
    • Change the system to ISS
  • Cancel the PES, in Nxt code BA and press [Enter]
    • Svc Rsn field code 'EPF'
    • Action field code 'CAN'
    • Reason field code 'OTH'
    • Effect date field code DPT + 1
  • DOC the record:
    • Select Fast Note template: Suspend Cancel Reason
    • Record all relevant details in the Fast Note

The SY020 should then be processed in accordance with the procedure outlined in Assessing the Claim for ABSTUDY Pensioner Education Supplement (SY020).

An appropriate start date for ABSTUDY PES payments and the cancellation date for PES should be coded to ensure there is no gap in entitlement and no overpayment of PES.

4

Transferring back to PES + Read more ...

The customer should be advised that they are free to request their transfer back to PES (without the need to lodge a new claim), after receiving the additional entitlements under ABSTUDY PES.

Note: the customer should not transfer back to PES until all additional entitlements have been received under ABSTUDY PES, such as:

  • Incidentals Allowance
  • Additional Incidentals Allowance
  • Away from Base assistance
  • Residential Costs Option
  • Fares Allowance

If there is any doubt about what additional ABSTUDY assistance may be available for an individual student, advice should be sought from the ABSTUDY Smart Centre Telephony or ABSTUDY Smart Centre Processing team. See Office Locator contact details on the Resources page.

If the customer contacts Services Australia to request a transfer back to PES, record the details in a Fast Note - select Auto Text, use Students, ABSTUDY & Apprentices > Pensioner Education Supplement > PES Study Update Action DOC.
