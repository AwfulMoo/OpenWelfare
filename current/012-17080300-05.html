HCCS overview

Category

Description

Eligibility

(members)

A claim may be eligible if it is made against a person who is 1 of the following:

  • A medical practitioner
  • An allied health professional, if the Date of Incident occurred before 1 July 2020
  • A midwife engaged as an employee to practise as a midwife, if the incident occurred on or after 1 July 2010 and before 1 July 2020

Eligibility

(Applicant)

All participating MIIs and MDOs are eligible

Notification Date

Date of Notification of the claim must be on or after 1 January 2003

Criteria
  • The incident, or series of incidents, must have occurred in the course of, or in connection with:
    • practice by the practitioner of their medical profession
    • practice by the midwife engaged as an employee
  • The incident must have occurred within Australia or one of its external territories
  • The applicant (insurer) must have made a payment, or be liable to pay a payment in relation to the claim
  • The payment must be made in line with the insurer’s contract of insurance/policy schedule issued and within the ordinary course of business
  • The insurer must not have been notified of the occurrence of the incident, nor claim against or by the person in relation to the incident before 1 January 2003
  • The claim amount must exceed the threshold for the relevant threshold date

What is not payable?

The applicant cannot claim an amount if:

  • another party has responsibility to pay it
  • It is an insurer to insurer payment
  • the cost was not incurred in relation to the incident
  • the judgment or settlement amounts are still within the appeal period or have been appealed or stayed
  • it’s a GST component of a defence costs that is not exempt
  • partly or fully within the course of treating a public patient in a public hospital. If it occurs fully, the application is rejected. If partly, the total application payment must be reduced proportionately

Clarification

The HCCS threshold is based on the date the MII or MDO was first notified of the incident or claim. The thresholds are:

  • $2 million for claims notified between 1 January 2003 and 21 October 2003
  • $500,000 for claims notified between 22 October 2003 and 31 December 2003
  • $300,000 for claims notified between 1 January 2004 and 30 June 2018
  • $500,000 for claims notified on or after 1 July 2018
