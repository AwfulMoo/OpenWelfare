AIR staff verbal updates for vaccines on the AIR for incorrect/missing details

Table 1

Note: verbal updates are processed in extremely limited cases, by AIR staff only.

Step

Action

1

Determine caller type + Read more ...

If the caller is:

  • a customer, go to Step 2
  • a vaccination provider, practice or vaccination hub, go to Step 3

2

Perform security check for individual + Read more ...

Confirm the individual's identity by performing an AIR security check.

Did the individual pass the security check?

  • Yes, go to Step 4
  • No, see Unable to verify identity for AIR enquiries. Procedure ends here

3

Perform security check for vaccination provider or their staff + Read more ...

If the caller is:

  • a practice or vaccination hub staff member ask the caller, ‘do you have permission from the vaccination provider to request information about this record?’:
    • Yes, complete a security check to verify their identity. See Authentication and release of information for Australian Immunisation Register (AIR) for more information. Go to Step 8
    • No, tell the caller that they cannot proceed. Procedure ends here
  • a health professional, complete a security check to verify their identity. See Security check and release of information for Australian Immunisation Register (AIR) for more information. Go to Step 8

4

Check vaccination history + Read more ...

Are vaccination details already recorded on the individual's vaccination history?

  • Yes:
    • Do not key information again. Tell the individual that information has already been recorded
    • Confirm that Centrelink links are established and if not, offer to create links or escalate to AIR Centrelink liaison if not trained in linking
    • If vaccination history is up-to-date and the parent, guardian or individual wants vaccination evidence, tell them how to access. See Proof of vaccinations and the Australian Immunisation Register (AIR). Procedure ends here
  • No, go to Step 5

5

Determine need for verbal update + Read more ...

Tell the parent or guardian for a child under 14, or individual that they need to speak to the provider to request the missing vaccination details be sent to the AIR.

If it is urgent and the individual meets one of the following criteria staff may call the provider. The individual:

  • urgently requires proof of vaccination for travel
  • urgently requires proof of vaccination to visit to relative in a nursing home or health facility
  • urgently requires proof of vaccination for employment purposes
  • Family Tax Benefit for the individual has been or will be reduced
  • Child Care Subsidy for the individual has been rejected or cancelled

Does the customer meet the criteria?

  • Yes, staff are able to contact the provider. Go to Step 6
  • No, advise that they will need to contact the provider. Tell them that they have confirmed that they have spoken to AIR and their vaccination is not appearing

6

Collect vaccination provider details + Read more ...

Ask the individual for:

  • their contact number
  • the best time to call them back
  • the vaccination provider’s name and details or (practice name and contact number)

Explain to the customer that they will be put on hold while the provider is now contacted. Go to Step 7.

7

Call vaccination provider on behalf of customer + Read more ...

  • Ring the practice using the details in Provider Directory System (PDS)
  • Service Officers need to use the following script when calling practices and providers:
    • “Hello this is <first name> from Services Australia. Before we proceed with this call, I need to inform you the call may be recorded or listened to for quality and coaching purposes. I am calling in regard to your clarifying a patients record”
  • If vaccination provider is available, complete a security check to verify their identity. See Security check and release of information for Australian Immunisation Register (AIR) for more information. Go to Step 8
  • If the provider is not available:
    • ask when would be the best time to call back, or
    • advise they can update the record in the AIR site. Go to Step 10

Uncontactable Providers

The following providers are not to be contacted via telephone when completing verbal updates for callers:

Aspen, Sonic, Victoria COVID19-Hubs, NT COVID-19 Hubs, Sydney Olympic Park.

  • Complete the escalation form
  • Advise customer this has been escalated and will be looked into

8

Update mainframe + Read more ...

Update the immunisation history. For more information see Process claims in the Australian Immunisation Register (AIR). Go to Step 9.

9

Record comments + Read more ...

A comment must be recorded on the individual's record when a vaccination provider has confirmed an update to an AIR record.

Record comments in mainframe:

  • Key YCUI anywhere on a blank screen. The YCUI – Registration Amendment Search screen displays
  • Key Medicare number or individual’s details and press [Enter]. The YCUA – Amendment Registration screen displays
  • Select [PF3 - COMMENTS]. The YCCO - Comments List screen displays
  • Select [PF3-ADD COMM]. The YCCV - Comments Details screen displays
  • Record the following details in the comments:
    • parent, guardian or individual’s details including contact phone number (in case follow up is required)
    • vaccination provider number
    • missing vaccines or scheduled vaccines (e.g. 4 month immunisations). Note: vaccine brands can be abbreviated but do not use processing codes
    • reason the verbal request has been accepted
    • could not contact provider (if applicable)
  • Go to Step 10

Note: if updating immunisation history on behalf of a vaccination provider. Procedure ends here.

10

Return to individual’s call + Read more ...

  • Return the individual on hold to advise the outcome
  • Tell the caller how to access proof of vaccinations online. See Proof of vaccination and the Australian Immunisation Register (AIR) to advise individuals how to access proof of vaccination online. Procedure ends here

Cold chain breach

Table 2

Step

Action

1

Perform security check + Read more ...

Confirm the identity of the vaccination provider by performing a security check. See Authentication of recognised vaccination providers for AIR enquiries for more details.

Did the vaccination provider pass the security check?

  • Yes, go to Step 2
  • No, see Unable to verify identity for AIR enquiries. Procedure ends here

2

Display vaccination history + Read more ...

Log on to mainframe:

  • Key YCUI anywhere on a blank screen. The YCUI – Registration Amendment Search screen displays
  • Key Medicare number or individual’s details and press [Enter]. The YCUA - Amendment Registration screen displays
  • Press [PF14 – VACC HIST] to go to the YIRD - Episode History-Basic screen

3

Check the vaccination history + Read more ...

Confirm the affected vaccination:

  • Do not remove the vaccine or dose number (as it has been administered)
  • Record a comment in mainframe, including the vaccine affected, the date given and notifying provider

4

Another dose of the same or equivalent vaccine been given after the affected vaccine + Read more ...

If another vaccine has been given:

  • the re-administered vaccine may be in a pend status and should have the dose number updated to the same as the cold chain breach dose. This will complete the same schedule
  • ensure the earlier cold chain breach vaccine is C (clarify) and Y (no possibility of resolution (clarify)
  • record a comment in mainframe, including the vaccine affected, the date given and notifying provider

Note: as per due/overdue rules, sometimes the earlier doses of DTP/Polio will need to be R and Z to prevent the system from showing a dose being required, where it is not. For example, dose 4 of DTP given at or after 3 years and 6 months to prevent a dose 5 DTP showing as required on mainframe.

5

Record comments + Read more ...

A comment must be recorded on the individual’s record when a vaccination provider has confirmed an update to an AIR record:

  • Key YCUI anywhere on a blank screen. The YCUI – Registration Amendment Search screen displays
  • Key Medicare number or individual’s details and press [Enter]. The YCUA – Amendment Registration screen displays
  • Select [PF3 - COMMENTS]. The YCCO - Comments List screen displays
  • Select [PF3-ADD COMM]. The YCCV - Comments Details screen displays
  • Record the comments
  • Press [Enter]
  • Press [F9] to update
