Assessing and finalising MIC interventions 110-18020702

This document outlines the process for assessing and finalising Manual Income Confirmation (MIC) interventions.

Assess

Apply income information provided by the customer to clarify a discrepancy if possible.

If the customer cannot provide a suitable update to clarify a discrepancy:

  • For employment income. apply income information provided by the third party to the customer record
  • For Family Day Care Interventions only, if both the customer and third party have failed to respond, finalise the intervention with the best information available

The Compliance Officer (CO) will calculate any overpayment via an ADEX or MultiCal debt calculation.

Manual partner intervention

To identify if the outcome could affect the current or past entitlement of the partner or ex-partner, create a manual partner intervention on their record.

Updates made to the customer/ partner/ ex-partner records during the intervention will be attributed to the intervention results. The partner must be notified of the intervention.

A manual partner intervention should not be loaded if the partner/ ex-partner:

  • was not in receipt of a Centrelink payment during the period of the intervention
  • has a primary intervention in their own right

Finalise

Use the finalisation script to finalise the intervention. Actions should include any debts raised and documentation.

Request for a further explanation of decision

See Customer contact about compliance intervention decision.
