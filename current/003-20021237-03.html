This page contains information on assessing claims for Emergency payments from customers affected by the Australian Capital Territory Bushfires, December 2019. Emergency payments include Disaster Recovery Allowance (DRA) and their associated ex-gratia payments.

Claiming DRA and ex-gratia DRA + Read more ...

To lodge a claim for DRA and ex-gratia DRA, customers can either:

  • call the Australian Government Emergency Information Line on 180  22  66 to claim over the phone
  • post their form and supporting documents to:
    Emergency Processing Centre
    Reply Paid 7815
    Canberra BC, ACT 2610
  • lodge their form and supporting documents at their nearest service centre, agent, access point or recovery centre (if available). The service centre scans this information onto the system, or faxes it to 1300  727  760
  • fax their completed form and supporting documents to 1300  727  760

See Scanning a document and Storing documents.

For further information on how a customer can claim, how to manage phone and paper claims, and how to action Callback DOCs and ACTDOCs, see Claiming emergency payments.
