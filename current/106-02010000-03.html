This page contains information on timeliness standards for Social Security and welfare claims processing standards.

On this page:

Timeliness standards for claims - A

Table 1: This table contains Social Security and Welfare Claims Processing Standards that are applied to claims starting with A. Note: claims timeliness is calculated in calendar days (including weekends and public holidays) from the date of receipt to the date the claim is completed. The date that a claim is lodged is counted as 'day zero'.

Letter

Terms

A

See also: B-D, E-P, S-Z

ABSTUDY, Additional Child Care Subsidy (ACCS) - Child Wellbeing Application, Additional Child Care Subsidy (ACCS) - Temporary Financial Hardship, Age Pension, Assistance for Isolated Children (AIC) Student, Austudy

Timeliness standards - A

Table 2: This table contains timeliness standards for claim types A.

Claim type

Timeliness standard

ABSTUDY

≥70% of payments processed within 21 days of claim lodgement

Additional Child Care Subsidy (ACCS) - Child Wellbeing Application

100% within 28 days

Additional Child Care Subsidy (ACCS) - Temporary Financial Hardship

100% within 28 days

Austudy

≥85% of payments processed within 42 days of claim lodgement

Age Pension

≥80% of payments within 49 days of claim lodgement

Assistance for Isolated Children (AIC) Student

≥70% of payments processed within 21 days of claim lodgement
