Supporting Documents

This table sets out the supporting documents requested in an Application for a Medicare provider number and/or for a prescriber number for a medical practitioner form (HW019) and the circumstances where these are required.

Supporting document

Circumstances when required

Proof of residency status: Australian citizen document or permanent residency visa
  • Initial application for a provider number, and
  • Medical practitioner was born overseas, or
  • Medical practitioner is changing status from temporary resident to permanent resident

Proof of residency: temporary resident visa
  • Medical practitioner is requesting a 19AB(3) exemption from Health, and/or
  • Medical practitioner is requesting access to Medicare benefits and/or assisting at operations

Proof of New Zealand permanent residency or citizenship at time of enrolment in medical school
  • Initial application for a provider number, and
  • Medical practitioner gained their primary medical qualification at an accredited medical school in Australia or New Zealand

Proof of fellowship, specialty code in PDS or 3GA Placement
  • Initial application, and
  • Medical practitioner is requesting access to Medicare benefits and/or assisting at operations

Evidence of current registration with the medical board
  • Initial or subsequent application for a provider number
  • Medical practitioner is an overseas trained doctor, and
  • Medical practitioner is a temporary resident

Letter of Support
  • Initial or subsequent application for a provider number
  • Medical practitioner is currently subject to 19AB
  • Medical practitioner is requesting access to Medicare benefits and/or assist at operations, and
  • The medical practitioner is applying for either:
    • 19AB(3) exemption from Health
    • after-hours specialist class exemption
    • after-hours temporary resident class exemption
    • academic class exemption

Business Structure and EFT details

Initial or subsequent application for a provider number, and

Medical practitioner is requesting access to Medicare benefits and/or assisting at operations

Training Schedule or Medical registration that states the medical practitioner is training on the Competent Authority Pathway
  • Initial or subsequent application for a provider number, and
  • Medical practitioner is:
    • currently subject to 19AB
    • temporary resident
    • requesting assisting at operations, and
    • does not have a 407 visa
