This page contains a list of rejection/cancellation codes for Essential Medical Equipment Payment (EMEP). The service reason is MEP. Claims and restorations are processed by EMEP Processing Services staff only.

Essential Medical Equipment Payment (EMEP) processing

This table describes a list of rejection/cancellation codes for Essential Medical Equipment Payment (EMEP). The service reason is MEP.

Details

Important information + Read more ...

EMEP may be rejected or cancelled via automatic assessment of details on the customer's record, or manually via the Benefit Action (BA) screen.

Customers can request a review of the decision to reject or cancel EMEP. EMEP is paid under social security law.

EMEP can only be restored if it is cancelled incorrectly.
