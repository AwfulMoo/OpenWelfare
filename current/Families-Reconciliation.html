Families and Child Care Peak and Reconciliation/Payment balancing

Expand all


​​​​​​​​​​​​​FTB and CCS reconciliation is the balancing of customer's payments/entitlements after the e​nd of the financial year. It's the way we compare their actual income against their estimat​ed income to check we paid their FTB and/or CCS correctly. This page provides links to reconciliation network communications, resources and support tools.

Key dates to remember for 2021 reconciliation:

  • 1 July, FTB reconciliation begins
  • 5 July to 15 July, FTB 2020/21 bulk reconciliation process takes place
  • 9 August, CCS reconciliation begins
