Process to action updates to Commonwealth Scholarship Offers

Table 1: This table describes the process to follow when advice has been received regarding Commonwealth Scholarships.

Step

Action

1

Customer Contact + Read more ...

Has the customer contacted to update Commonwealth Education Costs Scholarship (CECS), Commonwealth Accommodation Scholarship (CAS) or Indigenous Commonwealth Reward Scholarship?

  • Yes, go to Step 2
  • No, see Assessing scholarship income, procedure ends here

2

TCSI Scholarship Review + Read more ...

To determine if the customer is contacting in relation to a TCSI Scholarship Review check the following in Process Direct;

  • TCSI Review Doc in Notes
  • TCSI Initiation Letter Communications Out in Snapshot
  • Outstanding Change of Circumstances activity in Transactions

Is the customer contacting in relation to an outstanding TCSI Scholarship Review?

  • Yes, see Student Payment Circumstance Updates (Student Payment Reviews)
  • No, go to Step 3

3

Check the Higher Education Scholarship Summary screen + Read more ...

Process Direct

Commonwealth Scholarships are displayed on the HESD screen.

  • Select Add to Record Status
  • Scholarship Type. select:
    • CEC - Commonwealth Education Costs Scholarship (refers to Indigenous CECS)
    • CAS - Commonwealth Accommodation Scholarship (refers to Indigenous CAS)
    • IAS - Indigenous Access Scholarship refers to the Indigenous Access Scholarship that was previously offered as part of the Indigenous CAS under Commonwealth Scholarship Program Guidelines prior to 1 January 2017 and cannot be offered to new higher education students after this date
  • Start Date and End Date for the period to which the relevant scholarship relates
  • Scholarship "ON" ind - displays whether or not the customer receives the associated Commonwealth Scholarship(s)

Customer First

Commonwealth Scholarships are displayed on the HESS screen.

  • Type: ‘CEC’ refers to Indigenous CECS
  • Type: ‘CAS’ refers to Indigenous CAS
  • Type: ‘IAS’ refers to the Indigenous Access Scholarship that was previously offered as part of the Indigenous CAS under Commonwealth Scholarship Program Guidelines prior to 1 January 2017 and cannot be offered to new higher education students after this date

Rec: field displays whether or not the customer receives the associated Commonwealth Scholarship(s).

Date: field indicates the period to which the relevant scholarship relates.

Is the customer advising of a Commonwealth Scholarship(s) for a period already recorded?

  • Yes, no further action is required. Procedure ends here
  • No, go to Step 4

4

HESS screen is inaccurate + Read more ...

If the customer is advising they:

  • have been offered a CECS or CAS, go to Step 5
  • are no longer in receipt of the Commonwealth Scholarship, go to Step 6
  • have accepted an offer or received CECS, go to Step 7
  • have accepted an offer or received CAS, go to Step 8

5

Commonwealth Scholarship Offered + Read more ...

Customers may be offered a Commonwealth Scholarship from a higher education provider.

Before deciding to accept the Commonwealth scholarship discuss with the customer:

  • Indigenous CECS precludes the payment of SSL. Refer to Student Start-up Loan
  • Indigenous CAS precludes the payment of a Relocation Scholarship (RS) or Residential Costs Option (RCO) for the same relevant scholarship period. For more information regarding Relocation Scholarship, refer to Relocation Scholarship (RS) eligibility. For more information regarding Residential Costs option, refer to Payment of Residential Cost Option (RCO) for ABSTUDY and ABSTUDY PES.
  • IAS does not preclude payment of SSS, SSL or RS, but was only offered to students who met Commonwealth Scholarship Program guidelines prior to 1 January 2017. The student will need to seek further advice from the education provider regarding their offer.

Will the customer accept the offer?

  • Yes, and it relates to a
    • CECS, go to Step 7
    • CAS, go to Step 8
  • No, procedure ends here

6

Ceased receiving Commonwealth Scholarship + Read more ...

Where the customer advises they no longer receive a Commonwealth Scholarship

Customer First/Customer Record or Process Direct:

  • Update the HESD End Date as:
    • CAS 31 December of the calendar year
    • CECS 30 June or 31 December of the calendar year
  • Finalise activity
  • Record a DOC

Procedure ends here.

7

Accepted CECS + Read more ...

Customer advises they did receive or will receive a CECS.

Record the Commonwealth Scholarship(s) on the Higher Education Scholarship Details (HESD) screen in:

Process Direct

  • Select Add

Scholarship Type: select CEC - Commonwealth Education Costs Scholarship

  • Start date:
    • 1 January (appropriate year) for period 1 Jan to June, or
    • 1 July (appropriate year) for period 2 July to December
  • End Date: leave blank
  • Scholarship "ON" ind: select Y - Yes
  • Select Save
  • Assess the activity
  • Record a DOC

Customer First/Customer Record

  • Type: ‘CEC’
  • Receives: Y
  • Start date to be entered as:
    • 1 January (appropriate year) for period 1 Jan to June or
    • 1 July (appropriate year) for period 2 July to December
  • End Date: leave blank
  • Finalise activity
  • Record a DOC

Procedure ends here.

8

Accepted CAS + Read more ...

Customers who have elected to receive ABSTUDY Residential Costs Option (RCO) cannot receive a CAS in the same relevant scholarship period.

If a customer advises they have received a CAS, check the record to determine if they were also receiving ABSTUDY RCO in the same relevant scholarship period.

The Address Details (AD) screen will display the Sharer Status: field with a code of RCO.

Is the customer receiving or has been receiving ABSTUDY RCO payments in the relevant scholarship period?

  • Yes, contact the student, and
    • advise that RCO and CAS cannot be received for the same relevant scholarship period
    • advise the customer they must decide whether receipt of RCO, or receipt of CAS and various Living Allowance entitlements is the most beneficial
    • if the customer accepts RCO, advise them to decline the CAS from the higher education provider. Record the conversation in a DOC. Procedure ends here
    • if the customer accepts CAS, end RCO. Record the conversation in a DOC
  • No, record the Commonwealth Scholarship(s) on the Higher Education Scholarship Details (HESD) screen in:
    • Process Direct, go to Step 9
    • Customer First/Customer Record, go to Step 10

9

Update HESD screen in Process Direct + Read more ...

  • Select Add

Scholarship Type: select CEC - Commonwealth Education Costs Scholarship

  • Start date:
    • 1 January (appropriate year) for period 1 Jan to June, or
    • 1 July (appropriate year) for period 2 July to December
  • End Date: leave blank
  • Scholarship "ON" ind: select Y - Yes
  • Select Save
  • Assess the activity
  • Record a DOC

Procedure ends here.

10

Update HESD screen in Customer First/Customer Record + Read more ...

  • Type: ‘CAS’
  • Receives: Y
  • Start date to be entered as:
    • 1 January (appropriate year) for period 1 Jan to June or
    • 1 July (appropriate year) for period 2 July to December
  • End Date: leave blank
  • Finalise activity
  • Record a DOC
