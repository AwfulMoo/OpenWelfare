Establishing Commencement of Identity (CoI)

Table 1:

Step

Action

1

Verify CoI + Read more ...

Customers must provide documents to establish their identity before a payment or service can be granted.

Identity is confirmed if the customer has provided:

  • a valid CoI document
  • a Primary Use in Community (PUiC) document
  • a Secondary Use in Community (SUiC) document, and
  • linkage is completed

A CoI document is not required when the:

  • Identity Summary page has a status of 'Confirmed', see under the Snapshot icon in Process Direct, or
  • the customer has a CoI document verified in the Identity Confirmation Dashboard in Process Direct

Does the customer have a CoI document verified on their record?

  • Yes, procedure ends here
  • No, go to Step 2

2

CoI documents required + Read more ...

Customers can lodge identity documents in person, by phone or online via Upload documents.

Was the customer born in Australia?

  • Yes, go to Step 3
  • No, go to Step 5

3

Customer born in Australia + Read more ...

Does the customer have a valid CoI document?

  • Yes,
    • to code, go to Step 8
    • if providing an Aboriginal Population Record (APR), go to Step 7
  • No, the customer is or will have difficulty providing an Australian Birth Certificate, go to Step 4

4

Customer has difficulty providing CoI document

Customer needs help to obtain a birth certificate + Read more ...

If the customer only needs help applying for a birth certificate:

  • Download the relevant state's application form from the Births, Deaths and Marriages home page. See the Resources page for a link
  • Help the customer complete the form, copy and certify any identity documents required for the application and advise the customer to send the necessary payment with the application form

See, Coding identity documents for details on how to code 'customer will provide this document later'.

Customer is unable to obtain a birth certificate + Read more ...

For Service Delivery and Random Sample Survey staff to achieve identity confirmation.

There may be circumstances affecting the customer's ability to obtain a birth certificate, such as:

  • financial hardship (customer has liquid assets/funds of less than 1 fortnight's maximum income support payment)
  • limited mobility
  • intellectual impairment

See FAQs for information on appropriate and inappropriate Request for Verification (SS257) referrals.

Request for Verification (SS257)

Complete a Request for Verification (SS257) using the Identity Confirmation Dashboard in Process Direct to confirm the birth of a customer with the relevant Registry of Births, Deaths and Marriages (RBDM) when:

  • the customer is unable to obtain a CoI document,
  • the customer was born in Australia, and
  • the customer's birth is registered in Australia

The Service Officer must:

Access Identity Confirmation Dashboard in Process Direct:

  • Select Identity Confirmation from the Task Selector
  • Select Start
  • Read information in the ‘Read to Customer’ text box to the customer

The customer must give consent to use the following procedure. If the customer refuses consent, do not use the Identity Confirmation Dashboard. See Coding identity documents and email details to the DVS Operations team.

When consent is given:

  • In the Commencement of Identity (CoI) drop down box, select ZZ - Cannot provide
  • Select Next
  • Complete the questions and select the criteria the customer meets. (If the referral is not appropriate, follow up may be required).
  • Select Next
  • Complete details obtained from the customer on the Request for Verification (SS257) screen
  • + Add other names including maiden and/or previous name and/or parent information
  • Select Next when all details have been coded

Note: Service Officers can now proceed with the claim activity. This process will automatically apply an 8 week Gathering Document (GD) review period.

The process will create the SS257 referral Fast Note which will be allocated to the DVS Operations team. Do not complete the Fast Note DOC.

The DVS Operations team:

  • review the SS257
  • contact the Service Officer if more information is required
  • send the SS257 to the relevant RBDM
  • arrange payment for the verification request

Once a verification of birth is received from the RBDM, the DVS Operations team will manually verify the birth certificate details in the Identity Confirmation Dashboard and DOC the outcome.

Note: RBDM does not supply an original birth certificate.

Procedure ends here.

5

Customer born overseas + Read more ...

The customer must provide a CoI document before payment can be granted.

Is the customer able to provide a valid commencement document?

  • Yes, go to Step 8
  • No, go to Step 6

6

Customer born overseas and cannot provide a valid CoI document + Read more ...

Is the customer an Australian Citizen?

  • Yes, code ZZ - Cannot provide in the Commencement of Identity field in the Identity Confirmation dashboard in Process direct and complete an Alternative Identity assessment
  • No, complete the ‘Verification of Australian Visa’ Fast Note in Customer First with customer details for the DVS Operations team

The DVS Operations team will:

  • investigate the Department of Home Affairs Immigration system to determine if the customer has an Australian Visa
  • verify the Australian Visa in the Identity Confirmation Dashboard in Process Direct
  • finalise the Fast Note DOC

Note: inappropriate referrals will be annotated and returned for further action and DVS Operations will email the Service Officer.

See FAQs for information on appropriate/inappropriate Fast Note Visa verification referrals.

Note: this process cannot be used for the COVID-19 Disaster Payment or the Pandemic Leave Disaster Payment.

Procedure ends here.

7

Customer has an APR + Read more ...

Customer provides a Northern Territory APR certificate.

Service Officers must:

  • ensure the APR certificate is scanned to the customers record
  • complete the Aboriginal Population Record Escalation Fast Note within Customer First, and
  • code YY – will provide later in the Commencement of Identity field in the Identity Confirmation Dashboard in Process Direct.

The DVS Operations team will:

  • contact the Service Officer if further information is required or APR is not scanned
  • verify the APR in the Identity Confirmation Dashboard in Process Direct
  • finalise the Fast Note DOC

Note: the DVS Operations team cannot action the request unless the APR certificate has been scanned to the customer’s record.

See Identity documents A to Z for example images of APR’s.

8

Customer can provide a valid CoI document + Read more ...

If the customer has a valid CoI document, record details of the document in the Identity Confirmation Dashboard in Process Direct.

See Coding identity documents.
