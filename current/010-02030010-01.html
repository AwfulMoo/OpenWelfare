This document outlines the age requirements for ABSTUDY.

General criteria

Students and full-time Australian Apprentices are eligible for ABSTUDY assistance if they meet the general ABSTUDY eligibility criteria and the eligibility criteria for the type of ABSTUDY Award for which they are applying. This includes meeting the age criteria for the relevant ABSTUDY Award.

ABSTUDY Awards

A number of ABSTUDY Awards have minimum age requirements as an eligibility criteria, as well as age being a determining factor in the rate of payment applicable to an individual student or apprentice.

Note: there is no maximum age for ABSTUDY.

Each of the seven Awards available to eligible ABSTUDY applicants have specific age criteria. These Awards are:

  • Schooling A - available to students only
  • Schooling B - available to students only
  • Tertiary - available to students and Australian Apprentices
  • Part-time - available to students only
  • Testing and Assessment - available to students only
  • Masters and Doctorate - available to students only
  • Student in Lawful Custody - available to students and Australian Apprentices

This procedure is used to determine the eligibility of students and Australian Apprentices based on age requirements for the relevant ABSTUDY Award, where age is one of the Award eligibility criteria.

The Resources page contains a table showing the age and study requirements for applicable ABSTUDY Awards and the benefits payable.
