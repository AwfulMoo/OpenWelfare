Examples - customer qualifies for payment at a later date

Table 1

Item

Example

1

A 67 year old customer submits an Age Pension new claim on 3 January. This customer is qualified to receive an Age Pension having met the age and residence requirements, but needs to serve a compensation preclusion period that ends on 13 January.

Therefore, the customer's start date will be 14 January, the date after the customer's compensation preclusion period ended.

2

A RapidConnect job seeker attends their RapidConnect appointment within 2 business days. Their JobSeeker Payment (JSP) or Youth Allowance (YA) job seeker payability start date will start from the date they attended their RapidConnect appointment.

Factors that may impact the payability start date include:

  • the timeframe in which the RapidConnect appointment was attended
  • the RapidConnect appointment is:
    • rescheduled and if the reason is acceptable or not, or
    • not attended and if the reason is acceptable or not
  • the customer fails to attend a RapidConnect appointment within 28 days, or
  • the customer is exempt from RapidConnect

These factors may result in payability starting from either:

  • the date they attended their RapidConnect Appointment
  • the date the Online Participation Workflow (OPW) was completed
  • the date the original RapidConnect was booked, or
  • the date the completed claim was submitted

Note: job seekers selected for and referred to Workforce Australia Online for Individuals employment service, will not have an initial RapidConnect appointment booked. These job seekers:

  • meet their RapidConnect obligations by agreeing to their Job Plan online, and
  • are eligible from date of claim, but may not be payable due to waiting periods

3

Some Youth Allowance (YA) customers submit claims in anticipation of becoming independent in the future.

A customer submits a claim for YA on 1 April anticipating reaching the age of independence on 2 May. If the customer qualifies for YA as at 1 April, determine how they want to be assessed. Then customer can be a dependent customer from 1 April or an independent person from 2 May.

If the customer only wants to claim as an independent person, they are payable from 2 May. This is within 13 weeks of submitting the claim.
