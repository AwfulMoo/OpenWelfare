Business Rules for Clinical psychology

Table 6:

Item

Rule

1

The type and number of treatments provided under a referral are based on clinical need. There is no limit on number of consultations claimed in a 12 month referral period.

2

Treatment in residential aged care facilities (RACF) high care and public hospitals requires prior approval.

3

After eight sessions of trauma focussed therapy a Case Review US07 and prior approval are required before any further treatment.

4

Providers are to comply with billing arrangements as outlined in the Notes for Allied Mental Health Care Providers available on DVA’s Allied health professional’s website. See External websites for a link to the DVA Allied health professional site and scroll down to locate this information.
