Relationship Profile naming conventions

Relationship profile names must be unique and adhere to the following:

  • no longer than 50 characters
  • words or abbreviations separated by an underscore
  • only has acronyms approved for use in WLM
  • acronyms must be all uppercase
  • whole words must start with a capital followed by lowercase

See the below WLM Business Rules documents for full details:

  • Workload Management naming conventions details mandated naming conventions for key components of WLM including Relationship Profiles
  • Workload Management naming conventions glossary has a list of acronyms approved for use in WLM
