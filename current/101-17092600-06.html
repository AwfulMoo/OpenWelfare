VIS Officer training

The Face-to-Face Partnerships team coordinates and schedules VIS training with DVA.

Refer to the task card below for the process to schedule training for new VIS Officers.

Do not share this attachment externally. See Freedom of Information - Information Publication Scheme.

Veterans' Information Service (VIS) Scheduling New Officer Training Task card

Refresher training

VIS Officers must complete the following refresher training every 2 years. Email the Face-to-Face Partnerships Team to advise the refresher training has been completed and include:

  • VIS Officer name
  • VIS location
  • completion date

Unit 1: DVA Overview, Veterans' Information Service

Unit 2: Income Support; Rehabilitation and Compensation (including DVA legislation)

Unit 3: DVA Health Care; Bereavement Assistance; Veterans and Veterans Families Counselling Service (VVCS)

Unit 4: Veterans' Information Enquiry Window (VIEW) and MyService

DVA SSO Training

SAU00164 - Introduction to DVA

SAU00208 - MyService and Veteran Recognition

External Training Attendance online form

External Training Attendance online form - VIS Officers must complete this form to ensure training provided by DVA is reflected in the Learning Management System (LMS) on ESSentials.
