Authorisation to certify documents

Item

Description

Who can certify

Certification

Certification is acceptable from:

  • owners, partners, directors, company secretaries and company managers can:
    • certify those forms requiring certification
    • nominate persons as approved TFES signatories
  • owners, partners, directors, company secretaries and company managers and their approved signatories can
    • sign forms not requiring certification

Forms to be certified

Certified forms

The only forms that must be certified by owners, partners, directors, company secretaries and company managers are:

  • TFES Manufacturers' and Miners' application for approval of items form (TAS025)
  • TFES King Island and Furneaux Group intrastate component - Manufacturers' and Miners' application for approval of items form (TAS026), and
  • TFES Registration and change of details form (TAS020)

TAS020 form

The TAS020 form is:

  • a TFES form that must be certified
  • the form used by owners, partners, directors, company secretaries and company owners to nominate other persons as TFES signatories
  • the form to update other details, including changes to the bank account

Other TFES form types

Other forms signatories

All remaining forms can only be signed by:

  • owners, partners, directors, company secretaries, company managers, or
  • their approved signatories

Note: this does not include the TFES - Supplier's Certificate form (TAS005), (TAS027) or the TFES - King Island and Furneaux Group Intrastate Component - Supplier's Certificate form (TAS018) which must be signed by the relevant supplier.

Business owners and partners

Verifiable against ABN

Business owners or partners are not restricted in what position title they use. The key consideration is whether the person certifying the form is an owner or a partner of a business which has an ABN.

Their answers must be verifiable against their ABN.

Directors and company secretaries

Titles verified against ACN

Directors and company secretaries are not restricted in what position title they use.

As those titles are official Australian Securities and Investment Commission (ASIC) terms for officeholders of registered Australian companies they must be verifiable against their Australian Company Number (ACN).

Company manager

Right level of authority

To determine if a company manager has the right level of authority to certify TFES forms, see Australian and New Zealand Standard Classification of Occupations (ANZSCO).
