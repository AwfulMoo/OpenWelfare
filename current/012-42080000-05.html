Examples

Scaling discount examples

Table 3: this table contains scaling discount examples in different scenarios.

No.

Examples

1

Overseas trained doctors and foreign graduates of an accredited medical school subject to s19AB

Example 1

During the month of June, Dr X met the $5,000 threshold, they held an s19AB exemption in a location in an ASGC-RA 5 category. Therefore, Dr X is eligible for the ASGC-RA 5 discount of 30 days for that month.

Example 2

Work undertaken in multiple ASGC-RA categories in the month of March.

ASGC-RA 2, Value of schedule fee: $13,000

ASGC-RA 4, Value of schedule fee: $10,000

ASGC-RA 5, Value of schedule fee: $2,000

Total value of schedule fee: $25,000

Result:

  • During the month of March, Dr Z met the threshold, as the total value of schedule fees for the claims processed was $25,000
  • While the highest value of claims provided in an ASGC-RA 2 category ($13,000), Dr Z will be eligible to receive the ASGC-RA 2 scaling discount for the month of March
