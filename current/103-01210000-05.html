Nominee IM scenarios and suspension rules - hierarchy

This table describes several scenarios relating to nominee Income Management, and nominee Income Management suspension rules.

Item

Scenario

1

John's scenario

John is not currently income managed, but his payment nominee, Suzie, has just commenced Income Management under the Long Term Welfare Payment Recipient (LTWPR) measure. John is switched onto NIM-CIM. John contacts two weeks after being placed on NIM-CIM to transfer to Voluntary Income Management (VIM) as he has heard about the VIM Incentive Payment. John is not eligible for Voluntary Income Management (VIM) as he is already being income managed under another measure.

2

James' scenario

James is currently on Voluntary Income Management (VIM) and decides to appoint a payment nominee, as he will be in hospital for eight weeks. He appoints his sister Julie to be his payment nominee and she is income managed under the Disengaged Youth (DEY) measure. NIM-CIM is manually switched on in the background. James will continue to be income managed under Voluntary Income Management (VIM) as the agreement was in place prior to NIM-CIM being switched on.

3

June's scenario

June is currently income managed under the Vulnerable Welfare Payment Recipient (VWPR) measure, and her payment nominee (partner) is income managed under the Child Protection (CP) measure. NIM-CP is manually switched on in the background. When the Vulnerable notice expires, Vulnerable Welfare Payment Recipient (VWPR) Income Management ends. June will continue to be income managed under NIM-CP, meaning the percentage of her payments being income managed will be 70%.

4

Nominee IM suspension rules

  • If a payment nominee's payments are SUS-INM for failing to attend an Initial Assessment interview, principals income managed in their own right will not have their payments suspended
  • A principal who is eligible for Income Management in their own right and whose payment nominee does not attend the Initial Assessment interview within the relevant engagement period for that measure will have their payments suspended
  • If a principal's payments are income managed under Nominee Income Management and their payment nominee's payments have been SUS-INM for failing to attend their (payment nominee's) Initial Assessment interview, the principal's payments will also be suspended
