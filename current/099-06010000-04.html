Bereavement definitions

Social Security Act 1991, part 2.2, Age pension

Surviving partner was receiving Age Pension

Social Security Act 1991

  • section 82, Death of partner - Qualification for payments under this Subdivision
  • section 84, Lump sum payable in some circumstances
  • Where the surviving partner dies within the bereavement period, see section 86, Effect of death of person entitled to payments under this Subdivision

Surviving partner was receiving Austudy

Social Security Act 1991

  • section 592, Qualification for payments under this Division
  • section 592B, Lump sum payable in some circumstances
  • Where the surviving partner dies within the bereavement period, see section 592D, Effect of death of person entitled to payments under this Division

Social Security Act 1991

  • section 235, Continuation of carer payment for bereavement period where person cared for dies
  • section 239, Lump sum payable in some circumstances

  • Where the surviving partner dies within the bereavement period, see section 146K, Effect of death of person entitled to payments under this Subdivision

  • Where the surviving partner dies within the bereavement period, see section 660LE, Effect of death of person entitled to payments under this Subdivision

Surviving partner was receiving Farm Household Allowance (FHA)

Farm Household Support Act 2014, section 90

This outlines the application and modification of the Social Security Act and the Social Security Administration Act. Where there is a reference in those Acts to JobSeeker Payment or Youth Allowance, it is as if there were also a reference to FHA.
