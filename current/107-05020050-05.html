Cash arrangement confirmation letter

This letter issues when:

  • an arrangement is first entered on to the system, or
  • the frequency or repayment amount changes on the below screens:
    • Regular Cash Payments (OPRC)
    • Irregular Cash Payments (OPIC)

The letter:

  • confirms the arrangement
  • tells the customer to contact Services Australia if their financial circumstances change, or if they are unable to make a payment
  • warns of possible further action if the arrangement is broken and the customer does not contact the agency. This includes garnishee, referral to a collection agent or civil recovery
  • generates at the end of the business day

If the customer has set-up a cash arrangement and withholdings, the letter will show this extra arrangement.

The letter includes the below details to help the customer make payments:

  • A barcode in the footer of the letter to pay at any Australia Post office or outlet
  • A Biller Code and an Australia Post POSTbillpay Reference Number. These are in the footer of the statement style letter to pay via POSTBillpay
  • A BPay Biller Code and Reference Number in the footer to make payments via BPay
