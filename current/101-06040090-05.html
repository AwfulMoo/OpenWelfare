Cancellation and rejection codes for Low Income Health Care Card (LIC) 101-06040090

  • Background
  • Resources
Expand all


This page contains a list of rejection/cancellation codes for Low Income Health Care Cards (LIC). It also advises which codes may be restored and indicates whether they may be restored at a Smart Centre Call or only at a service centre. Restoration of a LIC can only occur when LIC has been cancelled. If the Benefit Status line on the customer's record shows LIC has been rejected, the customer is required to commence the new claim process.
