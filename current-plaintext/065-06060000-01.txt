This document outlines information relating to the cancellation and
restoration of Commonwealth Seniors Health Card (CSHC).

Cancellation

The system will automatically cancel CSHC if the correct information was
updated on the appropriate CSHC screen. Some of the cancellation reasons
can be coded manually.

Cancellation codes

Codes are utilised when a decision is made to cancel a payment or to
reject a claim and is recorded on the Centrelink computer system. A
description of the code is used in correspondence with customers. The
list of codes is not available to the public.

Restoration

Restoration may be approved in a number of cases, for example customers
who have had their CSHC cancelled for the reason 'whereabouts unknown'
('WUK') and the customer contacts within 13 weeks providing their new
address or evidence their current address has not changed. See
Restoration of Commonwealth Seniors Health Card (CSHC) for further
information.
