Support services for separated families

Child Support provides separated parents with referrals to services that
provide support and products to help with their family situation. There
are many groups, websites, publications and services available to
support separated parents and their children. Some of these include:

Social workers

Third party services such as

-   the Family Relationship Advice Line
-   Mensline Australia
-   1800RESPECT
-   Financial Counselling Australia
-   Amica

The Services Australia website offers helpful tools such as:

-   a budgeting tool
-   the Payment and Service Finder

Online support products. These can provide customers and their families
with information and advice to help with changes to their situation
