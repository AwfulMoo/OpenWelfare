This page contains information about answering enquiries from service
providers, checking a veteran's eligibility, and the business rules for
processing Department of Veterans' Affairs (DVA) optical service claims
and relevant coding for Veterans' Affairs Processing (VAP).

Select the provider enquiry or check eligibility process or the relevant
processing optical service category from the drop down box below.
