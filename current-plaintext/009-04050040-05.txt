Forms

Carer Payment and/or Carer Allowance Care Needs Assessment (for a child
under 16) (SA394)

This is a printable and fillable PFD form that can be issued manually or
via the First Contact Service Offer (FCSO) workflow with the relevant
claim form.

Carer Payment and/or Carer Allowance - Care Needs Assessment (for a
child under 16 years) (SA408)

This is a pre-populated form that can be issued via FCSO with the
relevant claim form or via a prompt from the CDTT screen during a Carer
Allowance (child) claim assessment or re-issued on the History Summary
(HS) screen if necessary. To print a copy identical to the one the carer
has, Service Officers can produce a ‘Local Print’ from the HS screen.
The SA408 is available as a system generated form only.
