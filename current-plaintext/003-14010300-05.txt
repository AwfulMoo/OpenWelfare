The claiming period for the November 2015 Paris attacks has now closed.
For more details see Operational Blueprint - Australian Victim of
Terrorism Overseas Payment (AVTOP) claims lodged outside of the time
limit

This page contains links to Australian Victim of Terrorism Overseas
Payment (AVTOP) claim form, contact details for Emergency Management
Teams and Emergency Management Level 2 helpdesk, map of affected areas
and the Payment Query Template. Links on this page may be unavailable to
external audiences.
