Contact with the Commonwealth Bank

Letter requesting the return of a direct credit payment or Real Time
Gross Settlement (RTGS) made to a Commonwealth Bank account

Fax the letter directly to Commonwealth Bank Processing Services,
Sydney, if a Commonwealth Bank account requires a letter requesting the
return of:

-   a direct credit payment, or
-   RTGS

See Commonwealth Bank of Australia (CBA) for the fax number for:

-   requests for a refund from a deceased customer's Commonwealth Bank
    account
-   requests for the return of a direct credit or RTGS payment made to a
    Commonwealth Bank account (that is, incorrect recipient)

After faxing the request:

-   record a document on the customer's record
-   do not send the letter to the bank

Call the Commonwealth Bank to follow up any enquiries about requests for
refund or return.
