This page contains information about recording and reviewing
circumstances impacting compliance for job seekers and ParentsNext
participants who have compulsory requirements. The Circumstances
Impacting Compliance page is only available to Services Australia staff
when a job seeker or ParentsNext participant is receiving a
participation payment.

On this page:

Assessment

they are up to date' presents after completing a compliance workflow
