For staff in the Tasmanian Transport Programs (TTP) Team only

This page contains information on how to determine if a TFES
underpayment or overpayment has been made to a claimant and how to
correct the payment. An underpayment or overpayment maybe identified
when assessing a claim, as part of the quality assurance (QA) process,
advised by claimant, or when managing returns. etc.
