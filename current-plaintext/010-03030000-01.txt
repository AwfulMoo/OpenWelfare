This document outlines how to determine if a student is eligible for
AIC.

Purpose

AIC helps primary, secondary and certain tertiary students who:

-   are geographically isolated from their nearest appropriate state
    school, or
-   have special needs, or
-   are deemed isolated because of their circumstances
