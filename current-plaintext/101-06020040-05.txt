Shared care - level of care required

Customers who have shared care arrangements are required to have a
certain percentage of care to enable the shared care dependents to be
listed on the parent/guardian's concession card, including Health Care
Card (HCC), Pensioner Concession Card (PCC), Low Income Health Care
Cards (LIC), Foster Child HCC (FST), Commonwealth Seniors Health Card
(CSHC) and Ex-Carer Allowance (child) HCC (EHC).
