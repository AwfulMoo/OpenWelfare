AIC change of address

Information on changing an address for AIC applicants or students

This table describes information on changing an address for Assistance
for Isolated Children Scheme (AIC) applicants and/or students. Portions
of this process are done by Service Officers in Assistance for Isolated
children (AIC) only. Lookup local Processing Service details for:
Assistance for Isolated Children (AIC)
