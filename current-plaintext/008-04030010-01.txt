This document outlines instructions on how to locate Disability
Employment Services (DES) and Community Development Program (CDP) in the
Office Locator and Australian Disability Enterprises (ADE) via the ADE
website.

Office Locator

-   Provides a centralised point where staff can access information such
    as office locations, local services, opening hours, staff contact
    details and income management/third party organisation details
-   Office Locator is not available to the public

The Resources page contains a link to Office Locator.
