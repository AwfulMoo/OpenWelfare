This document outlines how to search and retrieve historical data in
relation to Child Care Benefit (CCB) previously issued to Approved
Service Providers (ASP).

CCB information in ACE

The Archiving and Culling Engine (ACE) contains copies of weekly Child
Care Benefit (CCB) statements, acquittal reports and bulk listings that
have been generated in the past and issued to ASP.

Copies of these can be printed and mailed or faxed to the ASP upon
request.
