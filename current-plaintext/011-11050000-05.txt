Most recent donation decision date

Most recent donation decision date is the date the donor signed the
registration form.

The most recent donation decision date must be entered when:

-   creating a new AODR registration
-   amending a Donor Only or AODR entitlement via a registration or
    Medicare Claim form (MS014) (an updated and more current signed
    consent signature date)
-   linking a donor only entitlement to a consumer record

Do not update most recent decision date an existing registration when
organ donor registration enquiries are received by phone or over the
counter at a Medicare Service Centre. Do not update when advised of date
of death with no change to donor decision type or organs and/or tissue.
In these circumstances only the Date of Last Contact is to be updated.

Note: if the form has not been signature dated, see Signature date
discrepancy.
