This document provides information on how to issue a Statement of Debt
to a customer.

Statement of Debt purpose

The Statement of Debt is different to the Account Payable Notice. If the
customer asks for a copy of the original debt advice, reissue the
Account Payable letter.

The Statement of Debt gives customers information about their debts and
includes:

-   any period where there were outstanding debts on their record
-   the payments received
-   new debts raised, and
-   adjustments and transactions related to quarantined debts

The Statement of Debt can be issued for periods up to 5 years (60
calendar months).
