This document outlines the process for the HPOS application process for
the PIP, PIP Individual Incentives and the WIP - Practice Stream.

Supporting documents

The practice must lodge the below supporting documents with an HPOS
application:

-   Practice Incentives Practice ownership details and declaration
    (IP008) form
-   Accreditation or registered for accreditation certificate
