This document outlines how to complete and finalise an appointment in
the Centrelink Appointment System using the Centrelink Appointment
System guided procedure.

Finalising an appointment

Once a customer has attended an appointment, it should be completed. If
the customer has not attended and has not contacted, the appointment
record should also be finalised accordingly.

The Resources page contains a full list of appointments and parameters
for rescheduling.
