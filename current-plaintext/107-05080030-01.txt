This document outlines the use of External Collection Agents (ECAs) in
recovering customer debts. It covers protocols for contact, roles and
collection actions.

Contracted ECAs

ECAs contracted to Services Australia:

-   Milton Graham
-   Probe Group
-   ARL Collect Pty Ltd (ARL)

ECAs recover debts of non-current customers on behalf of the agency.

The Collection Agent (OPMC) screen contains details about debts referred
to an ECA.

ECAs can only discuss a customer's debt with the Collection Agent
Liaison Officer.

The Resources page contains links to websites, contact details and
delegations.
