Quality check table

Table 2: This table details the fields that are subject to AODR quality
checking under each entitlement type.

Work items must be checked fully and feedback on identified errors given
to the Service Officers and errors returned for correction. Australian
Organ Donor Register (AODR) registrations with a Medicare number are
subject to Medicare QBBI quality checks for any information held in
Group and Personal. Any errors identified in these circumstances are not
recorded for AODR quality checking.
