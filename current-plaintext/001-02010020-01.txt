This document outlines how to access general employment opportunities,
advice and information for job seekers.

Role of Services Australia

The role of Services Australia is to inform and encourage job seekers to
make use of all services that may help them find employment or improve
their skills. This includes help from their employment services
provider.

It should be noted that Services Australia is not a referral agency in
this process.

Job seekers assistance options

This document lists some of the options available for job seekers to
access general employment. These include:

-   Job vacancies or referral for career planning
-   Work for the Dole (WFD) under Workforce Australia
-   Australian Apprenticeship Support Network providers
-   Indigenous Employment Program
