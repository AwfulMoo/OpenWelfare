
Cancellation, rejection, not effective, assessed and current zero rate codes for Parental Leave Pay (PPL) 007-09030010

-   Background
-   Process
-   References

[Expand/Collapse all sections]Expand all

This page contains information for Service Officers when determining the
correct code to use when cancelling, rejecting or making a claim for
Parental Leave Pay (PPL) not effective.

The tables below show the pre and post claim codes used for assessing a
PPL claim as NEF (not effective), REJ (rejected) or CAN (cancellation).

when cancelling, rejecting or making a claim for PPL not effective.

claims is always open.
