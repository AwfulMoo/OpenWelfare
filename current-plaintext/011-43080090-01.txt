This document outlines information on phone registrations for Medicare
Safety Net. Note: as at 1 July 2016 Norfolk Island is covered under
Medicare provisions.

Medicare Safety Net registration amendments and confirmation

Medicare Safety Net registrations, amendments and confirmations can be
undertaken over the phone, see Family registration and confirmation for
Medicare Safety Net and Medicare Safety Net.

Customers amending, registering or confirming a Medicare Safety Net
registration must be 14 years of age or older and a member of the
Medicare Safety Net family.

The Resources page contains links to Medicare Safety Net user help
guides.
