Delegations and authorisations

Service Officer (SO3) and above

-   Credit NAPs under s71, s71A and 71B, where there is:
-   no dispute
-   both parties can be contacted
-   Credit NAPs reported by the person owed the debt under s71, s71A,
    71B and 71C

Service Officer (SO4) and above

Credit NAPs under s71, s71A, 71B and 71C where there is:

-   a dispute
-   where either party cannot be contacted

Refuse to credit an amount under s71D

Note: this does not prevent an officer at a lower level who has the
support from their team leader from carrying out the preliminary steps.
However, the final decision to process a NAP must be made by an
authorised officer or higher.
