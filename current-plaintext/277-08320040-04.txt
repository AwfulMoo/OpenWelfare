Delegations and authorisations

Overseas team Service Officer is authorised to:

-   make a decision to accept or refuse a case referral. If the decision
    is to refuse, this must be reviewed by an International
    Non-Compliance Team Leader (TL)
-   request transmittal package be sent to Central Authority. A Service
    Support Officer (SSO) must review the transmittal package prior to
    sending to the Team Leader for approval
-   withdraw a transmittal

An Overseas TL is authorised to:

-   accept a transmittal package for translation
-   approve sending transmittal package to Central Authority
