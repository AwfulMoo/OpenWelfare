This page contains a list of eligibility document requirements to
register a customer for Medicare in the Consumer Directory Maintenance
System (CDMS), information on group and personal history categories,
contact details for the Eligibility and Enrolments team and the Medicare
Intertwined and Duplicate Resolution Team.

-   O = discrepancy in DOB
-   D = data out of range (document either expired, stolen, lost)
