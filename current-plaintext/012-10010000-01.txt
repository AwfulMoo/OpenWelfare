This document outlines information on the policies and procedures for
the Practice Incentives Program (PIP) and the Workforce Incentive
Program (WIP) - Practice Stream. The information is for Service Officers
to use for general enquiries, administration and general processing.

The Resources page contains links to contact details for the Practice
Incentive Program (PIP) and the Workforce Incentive Program (WIP) -
Practice Stream.
