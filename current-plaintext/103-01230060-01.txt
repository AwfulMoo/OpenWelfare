This document outlines the action required when a third party reports
they have found a BasicsCard.

Suspension of BasicsCard

The Service Officer is to suspend the BasicsCard if not already
suspended due to the customer notifying they had lost the card.

It is important the identification of the customer is not given to the
third party.

The Resources page contains links to Office Locator for the BasicsCard
and TPO Management Team contact details, and BasicsCard Alternative
Interface.
