This document outlines the requirements following a Carer Allowance (CA)
(child) change of care, where the carer is qualified for a Health Care
Card (HCC) only CA for the child.

Requirements when there is a change of care

With a change of care for HCC only CA:

The carer who is ceasing care for a care receiver will have no further
entitlement. HCC only CA should be cancelled.

The new carer is required to complete a new claim.

A new medical report is required when either:

-   the claim is made within 6 months of the next scheduled medical
    review of entitlement, or
-   there is reason to believe that the care receiver's medical
    condition has changed

Dispute between carers

If there is a dispute between two carers over the care being provided
