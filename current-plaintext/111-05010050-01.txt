This document outlines the guidelines when a customer who has called
Centrelink places themselves on hold before their call is answered.

Guidelines

These guidelines are also for when the customer asks to put a Service
Officer on hold during a call.

The general rule is that if the customer does not return to the call
within 30 seconds, the call is terminated.
