This document outlines the information available on the Income
Management Summary screen.

Income Management Summary screen

The screen allows Service Officers to:

-   view all customer information relating to Income Management in one
    place, and
-   launch workflows to undertake Income Management related activities

This screen is available through Customer First on the:

-   Workspace page, under the Income Management menu
-   ‘General Information’ box by clicking on the Income Management link
    under ‘Events’ on the Customer Summary page, or
-   Issues page when selecting an Income Management related activities.
    For example, a Work Item

The Resources page contains links to the National Transfer numbers in
Office Locator, and an example of how the Available Balance and Current
Balance displayed on the screen are calculated.
