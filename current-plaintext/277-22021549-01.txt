This document outlines process for Child Support to notify Centrelink
when a mutual customer dies and how to complete Liable Parent Deceased
(LPD) coding in Customer First.

This process is for Child Support Deceased Customer Management (DCM)
staff only.

Bereavement Service Offer: Notification of death to Centrelink and LPD coding Customer First

The Bereavement Service offer enables Child Support to share information
with Centrelink regarding the death of a mutual customer.

When notified of a death of a mutual customer, the information is shared
with Centrelink when:

-   child support are notified first, and
-   is not already recorded or actioned in Customer First

To check if the death has already been recorded in Customer First, see
Confirmation of death for Child Support customers, Table 2, Step 1.
