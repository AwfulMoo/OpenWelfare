This document outlines information relating to means tests for students,
trainees or Australian Apprentices.

Government intent

The means test for students, trainees or Australian Apprentices ensures
that the correct amount is paid in the next calendar year. It also
ensures that the student, trainee or Australian Apprentice does not miss
out on additional services or payments.

Means tests

Income support payments for students, trainees or Australian Apprentices
are subject to means testing. The means test that applies will depend on
the customer's circumstances.
