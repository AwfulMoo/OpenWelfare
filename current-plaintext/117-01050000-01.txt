This document outlines how to use the Compensation Estimator to
calculate estimates of preclusion periods and compensation charges.

Prior to accepting a compensation payment

Customers, their solicitors and financial advisers are encouraged to use
the Estimator.

Do not use the Estimator to calculate lump sum compensation payments
awarded:

-   by a court
-   by a tribunal after a contested hearing, or
-   for an arrears of periodic compensation

The Resources page contains a link to the Compensation online Estimator.
