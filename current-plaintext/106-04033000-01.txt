This document outlines specific information about the Agreement
including the process of making a claim for Australian payment under the
agreement and coding of Swiss payments.

Commencement date

The Agreement with Switzerland (Swiss Confederation) started on 1
January 2008.

Claiming and benefits covered

The Agreement generally allows customers to lodge a claim for payment
from either country. It also allows customers to add together periods of
residence in Australia and periods of social security coverage in
Switzerland, to meet the minimum requirements for payment.

Australian payments covered

-   Age Pension (Age)
-   Disability Support Pension (DSP) for the severely disabled
-   Parenting Payment Single (PPS)
-   Double Orphan Pension
-   Carer Payment (CP)

Notes:

-   CP can only be granted in Australia
-   Only ‘widowed persons’ can be granted PPS

Customers can claim Australian payments under the International
Agreement by contacting Services Australia or the Swiss authorities.

Swiss payments covered

-   Old-Age Pension
-   Disability Pension
-   Survivors Pension

Customers can claim a foreign pension by contacting Services Australia
or the Swiss authorities.
