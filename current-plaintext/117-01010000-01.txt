This document outlines a list of procedures relevant to the impact of
compensation on Centrelink payments.

Types of payments affected

Most social security income support payments are Compensation Affected
Payments (CAP), and include:

-   Age Pension
-   Disability Support Pension (including blind)
-   JobSeeker Payment
-   Youth Allowance
-   Carer Payment
-   Parenting Payment

Supplementary Payments are compensation affected when the primary
payment is a CAP.

The Resources page has a link to the Compensation and Damages form (Mod
C).
