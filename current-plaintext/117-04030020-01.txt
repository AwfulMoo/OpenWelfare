This document outlines how compensation Online Advice letters (OLA) are
created, viewed, managed and printed.

On this Page:

Access to OLA letters

Create Compensation Recovery letters manually via Online Advice (OLA).
These letters:

-   assist the Compensation Recovery Team (CRT) informing all parties
    involved in compensation matters
-   contain a shell of standard text which vary according to the type of
    letter being created

Viewing OLAs and associated activities

OLAs and associated activities:

-   can be viewed for up to 56 days from the date printed
-   move into the archive system for storage after 56 days

Retrieve archived OLA's from the Customer Archive Retrieval (CAR)
system.

The Resources page contains a list of compensation OLAs and codes.
