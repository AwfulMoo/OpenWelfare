This document outlines the Family Tax Benefit (FTB) review processes,
which are automatically or manually set up on a customer's record
depending on their circumstances. The procedures also indicate the
action that needs to be taken to finalise the review.

FTB reviews

These reviews are conducted to ensure that the customer receives their
correct entitlement from Centrelink.
