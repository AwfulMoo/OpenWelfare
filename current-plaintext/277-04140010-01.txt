This document outlines information about the role of a Service Officer
in setting up Employer Withholding (EW) linking.

Roles and responsibilities

Service Officers:

-   identifying cases suitable for EW/EWA
-   contacting the payer
-   identify appropriate employer
-   processing Elections for EW not to apply
-   referring appropriate cases to Employer Services (EW)
-   request the ending of EW/EWA

Employer Services Officers (ESO):

-   checking the referral to ensure suitability for EW/EWA
-   providing advice to Service Officers where the link is not
    appropriate
-   establishing and maintaining EW link at the request of a Service
    Officer
-   amending and ending EW with the employer
