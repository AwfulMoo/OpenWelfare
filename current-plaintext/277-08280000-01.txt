This document outlines the process for transmitting a case to the Inland
Revenue, New Zealand (NZIR). Links on this page may be unavailable to
external audiences.

Transmittal from Child Support to NZIR

This process is used to send Australian Child Support cases for
registration and collection by Inland Revenue, New Zealand (NZIR). This
is appropriate when Child Support has received information from NZIR
confirming the payer resides/lives in New Zealand.

Authorisations and delegations

For more information about authorisations and delegations see Child
Support Services, Delegations and Authorisations.
