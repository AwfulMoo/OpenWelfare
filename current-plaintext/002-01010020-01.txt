This document outlines what can be offered to a farmer who contacts to
advise they are planning to retire and wants to know if there is any
assistance available to them.

Products and services

The farmer may be invited to test their eligibility for the following
products and services:

-   Age Pension
-   Age Pension (Blind)
-   Commonwealth Seniors Health Card (CSHC)
-   Farm Household Allowance (FHA)

If any of the above payments or services are claimed, the farmer must
meet all eligibility criteria before payments/services can be granted.

Financial Information Service (FIS)

This is a free service and customers planning for retirement may benefit
from this service. The FIS Officer provides expert information to help
people make informed decisions about their investments and financial
affairs and the retirement options available to them.

Other services such as information, advice and financial assistance, are
also available through external providers. Information about these
services and contact details should be offered to the customer.
