This document outlines information about the options payers, payees and
employers have to make payments to Child Support.

Child support payment options

Flexible payment options are available for customers to suit their
individual circumstances. For payers, it provides guidance on adjusting
payment periods to suit a payer's circumstances.

Roles and responsibilities

Service Officers (SO3) and above can process payment options.
