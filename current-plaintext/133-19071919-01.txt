This document outlines information to help customers cancel their
current payment using their Centrelink online account.

Cancel my current payment service

Eligible customers can use the [] Cancel my current payment service
through their Centrelink online account to voluntarily cancel their
income support payment, when they no longer want to continue receiving
the payment.

Eligible customers

Customers are eligible to use the [] Cancel my current payment service
if they:

-   have an active Centrelink online account linked to their myGov
    account
-   are current on Youth Allowance (YA), Austudy, JobSeeker Payment
    (JSP) or Tertiary Access Payment (TAP)
-   do not have a nominee arrangement
-   are not income managed (unless in an income management exemption
    period)
-   do not have a partner on an income support payment. Note: does not
    apply to TAP
