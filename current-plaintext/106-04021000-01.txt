This document outlines specific information about the international
social security agreement with Latvia including the process of making a
claim for Australian payment under the Agreement and coding Latvian
payments.

On this Page:

Commencement date

The Agreement with Latvia started on 1 January 2013.

Claiming and benefits covered

The Latvian Agreement generally allows customers to lodge a claim for
payment from either country. It also allows customers to add together
periods of residence in Australia and periods of social security
coverage in Latvia, to meet the minimum requirements for payment.

Australian payments covered:

-   Age Pension

Customers can claim Australian payments under international agreements.

Latvian payments covered:

-   Old Age Security Pension (AGE)
-   State Social Security Benefit (AGE)
-   Survivor Pension (SUR)

Customers can claim a foreign pension by contacting Services Australia
or the Latvian authorities.

Information for international agreements
