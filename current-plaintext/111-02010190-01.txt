This document outlines the process of sending activities retrieved using
the Archiving and Culling Engine (ACE) application back to the
Centrelink system.

Purpose of functionality

This functionality was added to assist users to assess and determine
Families Assistance Office (FAO) reconciliation debts and other
associated activities.

It is important to note that once activities have been sent back to the
Centrelink system they will only remain there for approximately 7-13
days.

The Resources page contains a link to the Archiving and Culling Engine
(ACE).
