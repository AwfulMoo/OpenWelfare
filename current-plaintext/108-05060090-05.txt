This page contains contact details for the Financial Industry and
Network Support (FINS) Helpdesk and a link to the online version of the
Details of income stream product form (SA330). Links on this page may be
unavailable to external audiences.

Helpdesk

Contact Financial Industry and Network Support (FINS) Helpdesk via the
Level 2 Policy Helpdesk - online form, for help with a schedule or
income stream information and where unsure if the income stream is from
a Self-Managed Superannuation Fund (SMSF) or a small APRA Superannuation
Fund (SAF).
