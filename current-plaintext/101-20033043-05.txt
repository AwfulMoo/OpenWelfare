Coronavirus Supplement eligible payment between 25/09/2020 and 31/12/2020

Customers who were receiving an eligible Coronavirus Supplement payment
and had the 24th of September as part of their reporting period,
received a final $550 Coronavirus Supplement payment before
transitioning to the change rate of $250. See Coronavirus Supplement
Payment Rate Change Transition Matrix - September 2020 table.

Customers granted a Coronavirus Supplement eligible payment between
25/09/2020 and 31/12/2020 were eligible for the Coronavirus Supplement
at the rate of $250 per fortnight.

Note: for Benefit Transfers, see the Payment cancellation and benefit
transfers table on the Process page.

Coronavirus Supplement eligible payment on or after 01/01/2021

Customers who were receiving an eligible Coronavirus Supplement payment
and had the 31st of December 2020 as part of their reporting period,
received a final $250 Coronavirus Supplement payment before
transitioning to the change rate of $150. See Coronavirus Supplement
Payment Rate Change Transition Matrix - January 2021.

Customers granted a Coronavirus Supplement eligible payment on or after
1 January 2021 were eligible for the Coronavirus Supplement at the rate
of $150 per fortnight.

Coronavirus Supplement Transition

Coronavirus Supplement Payment Rate Change Transition Matrix - September 2020

Eligible customers with a regular fortnightly reporting period that
started on or before 24 September 2020 received a Coronavirus Supplement
payment of $550 with their regular payment.

Eligible customers continued to receive $250 per fortnight until 31
December 2020.

Coronavirus Supplement Payment Rate Change Transition Matrix - January 2021

Eligible customers with a regular fortnightly reporting period that
started on or before 31 December 2020 were entitled to receive a
Coronavirus Supplement payment of $250 with their regular payment. This
means that customers who were due to report on or before 13 January 2021
received $250 with their regular payment.

Customers whose regular reporting day was 14 January 2021 onwards,
received $150 per fortnight. Customers continued to receive $150 per
fortnight until 31 March 2021 as long as they remained eligible.

an eligible customer received based on their reporting date.

Coronavirus Supplement Payment Rate Change Transition Matrix - 31 March 2021

Customers whose regular reporting day is 14 April 2021 onwards, will no
longer receive the Coronavirus Supplement.

Customers continued to receive $150 per fortnight until 31 March 2021,
as long as they remained eligible.
