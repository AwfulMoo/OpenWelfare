This page contains a summary of how to view and manually adjust the
clock for Farm Household Allowance (FHA). The clock shows how many days
a customer has received FHA and how many days remaining out of the
maximum 4 cumulative years (1,460 days).

Manual adjustments are completed by only specialised Smart Centre FHA
Processing officers.
