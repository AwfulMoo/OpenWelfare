This document outlines the employer assisted dispute resolution process
for PP) disputes involving an employer determination or employer
obligations.

Assisted dispute resolution

Assisted dispute resolution action cannot be initiated:

-   within 14 days of the date of the employer determination, or
-   if an employer review of decision or appeal relating to the
    determination has been lodged by the employer, but has not been
    finalised by the Authorised Review Officer (ARO) or the
    Administrative Appeals Tribunal (AAT).
