This document outlines the process for when possible tax evasion is
reported by customers.

On this Page:

Overview

This document describes the process for all Service Officers to refer
possible tax evasion by individuals or their businesses to the
Australian Taxation Office (ATO).

Note: there is no requirement to refer customers or third parties to the
ATO to report tax evasion. Customers can independently decide to contact
the ATO. Customers can visit the ATO website for more information.

This document is not for:

-   tax return lodgement enforcement
-   fraud allegations and other offences

Child Support and ATO secrecy law, and privacy law, prevent disclosure
to third parties of details of any referral, investigation, or outcome
about possible tax evasion.

Roles and responsibilities

Service Officer

-   Receive and/or identify tax evasion information
-   Complete ATO tax evasion referral macro
-   Send tax evasion referral email to ATO
