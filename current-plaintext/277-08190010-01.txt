This document outlines information for identifying the process method
when amended certificates for New Zealand ‘In’ cases at the request of
Inland Revenue, New Zealand (NZIR) have been received. These are cases
where the payer resides in Australia and the payee in New Zealand (or
the payee has left New Zealand but there are still arrears owing). Links
on this page may be unavailable to external audiences.
