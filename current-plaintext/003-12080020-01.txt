For Services Australia social workers only.

This document outlines the principles and guidelines for social workers
recording participation and compliance referrals, consults, assessments
or interventions.

Social work documentation for participation and compliance related work

Social work documentation covers the recording of:

-   consultations, assessments, referrals and interventions in Social
    Work Information System (SWIS)
-   social work DOCs (in SWIS and using Fast Note)
-   social work case notes
-   Comprehensive Compliance Assessment (CCA) referrals and assessments
    in SWIS

For detailed instructions on how social workers record work for
Comprehensive Compliance Assessments, see Completing a Comprehensive
Compliance Assessment (CCA).
