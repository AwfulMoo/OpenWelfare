This document outlines information about simple Child Support errors and
the procedure to identify and correct a simple error. Errors include
decisions that have not yet been made or processed.

Definition of simple error

Simple errors are:

-   obvious errors which are straight forward and indisputable
-   those that do not meet any of the complex criteria including invalid
    decisions or those that require a review and alter, and
-   corrected at the Service Officer (SO3) level and above
