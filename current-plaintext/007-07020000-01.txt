
This document outlines information on how to complete the various claim
and review forms for Family Tax Benefit (FTB).

Online claiming

[] Customers should be encouraged to register and claim online.

Claim forms

Family assistance claim forms need to be completed and lodged by
parents, grandparents or guardians caring for children to test their
eligibility to receive payments.

[] Customers can claim online. A Claim for Paid Parental Leave and
Family Tax Benefit (FA100) or a Claim for an annual lump sum payment of
Family Tax Benefit for the 2022-2023 financial year (FA048) should only
be issued to customers who are unable to lodge an online claim.

The Details of your child's care arrangements (FA012) is completed by
FTB customers who share the actual care of a child with another person
and a pattern of shared care exists over a certain period.

The Claim for Bereavement Payment (FA008) is for claiming a payment
after a stillbirth or neo-natal death.
