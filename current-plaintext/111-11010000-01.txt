This document outlines information for Assessment Services staff using
the Department of Employment and Workplace Relations (DEWR), Employment
Services System (ESS) to arrange and conduct an Employment Services
Assessment (ESAt) or Job Capacity Assessment (JCA).

Employment Services System (ESS)

Assessors and administrative support staff can use the ESS and ESS
SmartClient to complete the Assessment Services end-to-end process for
the customer, such as:

-   set up site details and conduct searches (ESS Web)
-   maintain diary and calendar entries (ESS Web)
-   access customer information (ESS Web and ESS SmartClient)
-   create or reschedule appointment (ESSWeb)
-   record interview attendance (ESS Web)
-   view ESAt/JCA reports (ESS Web and ESS SmartClient)
-   complete ESAt/JCA reports (ESS SmartClient), and
-   make referrals by Job Capacity Assessment (JCA) Assessors to
    Employment Services (ESS SmartClient) and specialist assessments

Training

All Assessment Services staff will complete appropriate training before
completing any aspects of the ESAt/JCA process.
