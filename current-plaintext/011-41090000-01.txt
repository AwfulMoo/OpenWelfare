This document outlines information about the Medicare Teen Dental Plan
(MTDP).

Closure of MTDP

The Medicare Teen Dental Plan (MTDP) closed on 31 December 2013. The
Medicare Teen Dental Plan was replaced by the Child Dental Benefits
Schedule (CDBS) on 1 January 2014. Services provided to eligible
teenagers before the date of closure will receive Medicare benefits.

For assistance with all MTDP enquiries, contact the Child Dental
Benefits Schedule (CDBS) section.

The Resources page contains a link to contact details for Child Dental
Benefits Schedule (CDBS).
