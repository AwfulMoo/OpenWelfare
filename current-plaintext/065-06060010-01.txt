CSHC cancellation codes

This document outlines codes to use to cancel or reject a claim for
Commonwealth Seniors Health Card (CSHC) when a decision has been. A
description of the code is used in correspondence with customers.

Withdrawn claims

Sometimes, customers may withdraw their claims voluntarily. Withdrawn
claims are not rejected, rather, withdrawn claims are taken to have
never been made.

The Resources page contains a table of the cancellation and rejection
codes for CSHC.
