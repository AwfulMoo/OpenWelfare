This document outlines information about addresses for all of the
recognised overseas authorities the Child Support Program works with to
administer International Child Support.

Central Authority Address Listing

The Central Authority Address Listing is used to determine:

-   where to send a case for enforcement if the paying parent lives
    overseas, and
-   whether a request from another country for recognition and
    enforcement has been appropriately issued through the recognised
    overseas authority
