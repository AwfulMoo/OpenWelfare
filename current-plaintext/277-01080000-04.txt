Delegations and authorisations

Child Support Services, Delegations and Authorisations

Service Officer (SO1)

To make changes to the Register (limited to address and factual
information changes).

Service Officer (SO3 and SO4)

To make changes to the Register (all other changes).

Note: this does not prevent an officer at the SO2 level who has the
support of their team leader from carrying out this type of work.
However, the final decision to make changes must be made by an officer
at the SO3 level or above.

Regional Restricted Access Customer (RACS) Coordinator

To assign, change or remove Restricted Access Customers (RACS) levels.
