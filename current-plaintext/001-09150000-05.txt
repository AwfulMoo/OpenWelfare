Hierarchy of JSR/UPD activities

When identified that a JSR/UPD activity is required and there is already
a current JSR/UPD activity on the job seeker's Activity List (AL)
screen, the system will determine which JSR/UPD activity is higher
priority and auto complete the activity that has a lower priority.
