This document outlines information about imposing penalties.

Recovery Fee

Debts raised from 1 July 2006 may have a 10% Recovery Fee applied.

Interest Charge

From 1 January 2017, the Interest Charge (IC) applies to former
customers who have had a debt, and:

-   have the financial capacity to repay the debt
-   do not enter into and make the agreed payments under an acceptable
    repayment arrangement by the due date
-   are not honouring a payment arrangement

Penalty interest charge

The following information is historical only.

From 4 December 1999, penalty interest cannot be added to new debts.

Late payment charge and interest under the Student Assistance Act

Late payment charge and interest under the Student Assistance Act 1973
are no longer applied. Existing cases of late payment charges and
interest can be manually reduced, adjusted or ceased. See the References
page for historical information.

Administrative Charge

The Administrative Charge applies to debts raised between 1 September
1987 and 1 April 1994.

See the References page for historical information
