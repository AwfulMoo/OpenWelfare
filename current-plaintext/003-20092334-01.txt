The document outlines how to access and use the NECC portal during an
activation of the National Emergency Call Centre Surge Capability
(NECCSC).

Portal use

Use of the portal will depend on the type of NECCSC activation. When
told, Service Officers will use the portal to enter all details taken
during a call.

Access to portal

The Emergency Management Operations team, Operational Response Branch,
arranges access to the NECC portal when a NECCSC activation occurs that
uses the portal.

For a link to the NECC website, see the Resources page.

The Resources page contains a link to the NECC portal.
