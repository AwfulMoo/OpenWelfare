This document outlines the process for recording the Administrative
Appeals Tribunal (AAT) hearing and providing additional documents where
required.

On this Page:

Pre-hearing information

The AAT will advise Child Support when a hearing has been scheduled.

If Child Support has received further information or documents relevant
to the hearing, these will be forwarded to the AAT and customers prior
to the hearing date.
