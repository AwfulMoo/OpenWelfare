Privacy note

Specialist or Consultant privacy note for email agreement process for
patient signature

The following privacy notes are by the specialist or consultant issued
when a patient assigns their right to a Medicare benefit to a specialist
via email:

"Your personal information is protected by law, including the Privacy
Act 1988, and is collected by the Australian Government Services
Australia for the assessment and administration of payments and
services. This information is required to process your application or
claim.

Your information may be used by Services Australia or given to other
parties for the purposes of research, investigation or where you have
agreed or it is required or authorised by law. You can get more
information about the way in which Services Australia will manage your
personal information, including our privacy policy at
servicesaustralia.gov.au/privacy or by requesting a copy from Services
Australia."

"If you (the patient) agree to the assignment of the Medicare benefit
directly to the specialist (bulk bill), reply to this email including
the word 'YES' in the body of the email, and your (the patient's) name."

Example specialist email

The following is an example of an email sent by the specialist when a
patient to assigns their right to a Medicare benefit to a specialist via
email:

"Dear Mr Jones

Item number: 104 - Benefit amount: $71.35

Item number: 99 - Benefit amount: 35.00

Date and time of service: 01/01/2012 10:30am

Patient: Peter Jones

Provider: Jane Smith

If you (the patient) agrees to the assignment of the Medicare benefit
directly to specialist (bulk bill) reply to this email including:

-   the word 'YES' in the body of the email
-   your (the patient's) name

Regards

Dr Jane Smith
