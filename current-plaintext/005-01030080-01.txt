Profile of the Muslim community

It is extremely important for all Services Australia staff to be aware
of the issues that are significant to all customers from culturally and
linguistically diverse (CALD) backgrounds. The Multicultural Services
Branch has written a document for Services Australia staff to develop a
better understanding of the cultural and religious profile of the Muslim
community in Australia.

The Resources page contains a links to community profiles and
statistical data.
