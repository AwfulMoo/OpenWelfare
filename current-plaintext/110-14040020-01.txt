This document outlines the process for YA customers who are independent
and require entitlement verification for RSS reviews.

Customers do not qualify under the Independent criteria

Customers who do not qualify under any of the Independent criteria may
need a Parent(s)/Guardian(s) details - for the BASE tax year and CURRENT
tax year for dependent Youth Allowance or ABSTUDY customers form (MOD
JY) where parental income information is not already available on the
customer's record.

Independence categories

The Independent Rate categories include:

-   22 years of age or older
-   having or caring for a natural or adopted dependent child
-   previously had a natural or adopted dependent child
-   member of a couple or previously was a member of a couple
-   lived, or are living as a member of a couple for at least 12 months,
    or at least 6 months when the relationship ended due to special
    circumstances
-   orphan
-   parents unable to care for customer
-   unreasonable to live at home (UTLAH)
-   worked for on average 30 hours a week for a total of 18 months
    within a 2 year period
-   worked continuously for at least 15 hours a week for at least 2
    years since they last left secondary school
-   refugee without parents in Australia
-   was or is in State care
-   been out of secondary school for at least 14 months, have worked and
    earned in a 14 month period, 75% or more of Wage Level A of the
    National Training Wage schedule included in a modern award
-   earned at least $15,000 gross income in approved agricultural work
    anytime between 30 November 2020 and 31 December 2021
-   partial capacity to work
-   special safety net criteria for disadvantaged customers
