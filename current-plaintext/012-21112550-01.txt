Provisional Payments for the Practice Incentives Program (PIP)

Provisional Payments allow greater flexibility for Services Australia to
quickly deliver one off payments in response to requests from the
Department of Health and Aged Care and Government.

Provisional Payments for PIP can be for:

-   temporary measures with one-off payments to PIP practices
-   short term initiatives or additional payments to existing incentives

Provisional Payments for PIP are not adjustment payments for existing
incentives.

Internal users can view Provisional Payment records through the
Provisional Payments tab of PIP Online. External users can view
Provisional Payments data via a Provisional Payments link in their HPOS
PIP Online home screen.

The Process page contains the steps to view the provisional payments for
a practice.
