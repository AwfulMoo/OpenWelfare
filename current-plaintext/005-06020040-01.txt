This document outlines how to help customers who enquire about their
Status Resolution Support Service (SRSS) payment.

Purpose of contact

Customers may call to enquire about not being paid, to query their rate
of payment, or to request proof of income.

Viewing SRSS payments

View payment information in Customer First. Planned payments can be
viewed in a payment determined or approved Social Service Plan (SSP) on
the Planned Payment screen. This includes provisional or future payments
to be delivered once the SSP is approved.

The Process page contains instructions on how to view payment
information.

The Resources page contains contact details for the SRSS helpdesk, and a
link to the Multicultural and Tailored Services Branch intranet page.
