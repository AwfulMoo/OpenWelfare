Example

Customer receives an inheritance, which is invested on day 9 of their
payment cycle. Customer is receiving $10.00/day in investment income,
and has no other income. Customer has received $60.00 in TOI for the
fortnight, but will accrue Working Credit for the first 8 days of the
fortnight.
