This document outlines information about financial hardship assistance
for residential care recipients in the Aged Care Staff Portal (ACSP).

Aged Care Staff Portal (ACSP)

Service Officers can view and manage (register, update and delete)
financial hardship assistance records for residential care recipients in
the Aged Care Staff Portal (ACSP).

For home care recipients, financial hardship assistance records are
managed in the Aged Care Management Payment System (ACMPS). See Aged
care financial hardship assistance - assessment for more information.
