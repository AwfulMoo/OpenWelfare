This document outlines how Compensation Batch Laser Advices (BLA) are
created, viewed, managed and printed.

Compensation recovery decisions

The Compensation Management System (CMS) automatically sends a BLA to
all customers:

-   to advise of a compensation recovery decision, and
-   for every action that involves a reviewable decision

Viewing BLA and associated activities

BLA and associated activities can be viewed from the HS screen up to 62
days after printing. They then move into the archive system for storage.

The Resources page contains a list of all CMS BLA letters.
