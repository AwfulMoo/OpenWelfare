Farm Household Allowance (FHA)

ICT Service Desk referrals

If the correct date of event cannot be recorded for the business income
estimate, processing staff must submit the webform Farm House Allowance
(FHA) issues, select General Incidents and include the correct date of
event:

-   Go to mySupport
-   Search using key words or the name of the webform and select the
    relevant webform
-   Complete and Submit the required webform taking note of any receipt
    or reference code produced
