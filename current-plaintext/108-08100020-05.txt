Additional information

Other circumstances, which prevent payability under Social Security Act
1991:

-   is getting a transitional rate pension
-   is getting pension supplement, and their pension supplement amount
    is greater than the pension supplement basic amount
-   customer is being paid seniors supplement under the Social Security
    Act or either the senior supplement or veteran’s supplement the
    Veteran’s Entitlement Act
-   customer wants to get their pension supplement minimum amount on a
    quarterly basis,
-   customer is a member of a couple (other than an illness separated,
    temporarily separated or respite care couple) and the person's
    partner is getting veterans supplement, or
-   is a Commonwealth Seniors Health Card (CSHC) holder, regardless of
    whether they are getting the seniors supplement or have chosen not
    to get it

System related issues and support requests

Service Officers are required to help customers with common online
problems and seek help from their local Technical Support Officer or
Service Support Officer before submitting a request through mySupport.

Service Officers seeking to report systems issues on behalf of a
customer or themselves should:

-   Go to mySupport
-   Search using key words, error messages or application name and
    select the relevant webform
-   Complete and Submit the required webform taking note of any receipt
    or reference code produced
