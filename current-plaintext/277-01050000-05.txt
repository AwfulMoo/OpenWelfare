Privacy

Protect the customer's personal information during customer location
activities when third parties are contacted to assist in locating a
customer. Do not reveal or volunteer any information about the customer
to any sources. Accidentally revealing personal information may
constitute a privacy breach and must be reported to a Team Leader.

When making contact by telephone:

-   verify who is speaking
-   do not reveal any personal information to the other person, and
-   confirm any information obtained

For more information see the Information Privacy Principles.
