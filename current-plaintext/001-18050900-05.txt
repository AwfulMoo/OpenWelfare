The Department of Education, Skills and Employment jobactive helpline
will assist with general Career Transition Assistance Program enquiries
and advise the job seeker of any jobactive providers near them.

Job seekers wishing to participate in the Career Transition Assistance
Program are to be referred by Services Australia to a jobactive
provider.
