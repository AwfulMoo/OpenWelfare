This document outlines the processing instructions to register a Network
Organisation in the My Health Record system from the form owned by the
Australian Digital Health Agency (ADHA).

Background

The Australian Digital Health Agency owns the Application to register a
Network Organisation in the My Health Record system form. The form has
been removed from the Australian Digital Health Agency website, however
Services Australia still accepts them for processing.

Applicant requirements

The form must be completed and signed by either:

-   an Organisation Maintenance Officer (OMO) further up in the
    organisation network hierarchy, or
-   the Responsible Officer (RO) of the seed organisation that the
    relevant Network is requesting to be a part of

The Resources page contains contact details, a checklist and a link to
the Australian Digital Health Agency Website.
