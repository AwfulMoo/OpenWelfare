Risks to a customer's privacy have been identified. See Separating
Safely - protecting personal details to make sure the customer's
personal details are safe before progressing.

This document outlines topics which provide information regarding
procedures to be followed when customers advise of changes involving
their partner, children or other family members.

Government Intent

It is the Government intent that information received from customers
regarding changes involving their partner, children or other family
members are recorded in an accurate and timely manner. Changes in a
customer's family circumstances and caring responsibilities can impact
on their entitlement to different Centrelink payments and services.
