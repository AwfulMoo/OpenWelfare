This document outlines when and where scanning should occur by staff for
Medicare documents.

When and where scanning should occur

Scanning may occur at the department’s scanning provider, a smart centre
or a service centre. Documents received at smart centres and service
centres that require scanning should be scanned on the day they are
received unless scanning contingency processes are invoked. See Manage
Medicare scanning.
