This document outlines information about the bereavement assistance
available to Parenting Payment (PP) customers upon the death of the
customer, their partner or their child. The procedures explain how to
record details when a death is notified.

Confirmation and recording of death

Before any action is taken, confirmation of a death must be provided.
Once confirmed, it is important that the death be recorded as soon as
possible to ensure that customers receive their correct entitlement.

The procedures required to complete a death action vary according to
which family member has died and which payments the family were
receiving.
