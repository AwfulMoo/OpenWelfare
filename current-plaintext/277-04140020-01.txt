This document outlines information about the role of an Employer
Services Officer (ESO) in setting up, amending and ending an employer
withholding (EW) link.

Roles and responsibilities

Service Officers:

-   identify cases suitable for EW/EWA
-   contact the payer
-   identify appropriate employer
-   process Elections for EW not to apply
-   refer appropriate cases to EW
-   request the ending of EW/EWA

Employer Services Officers (ESO):

-   check the referral to ensure suitability for EW/EWA
-   provide advice to Service Officers where the link is not appropriate
-   establish and maintain EW link at the request of a Service Officer
-   amend and end EW
