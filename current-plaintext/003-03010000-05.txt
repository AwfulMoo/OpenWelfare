External websites

For information on specific emergency events and recovery assistance
following a disaster, see Disaster Assist.

To confirm a customer's home and/or work address see the White Pages or
the Australian Electoral Roll (AEC).

For more information on New Enterprise Incentive Scheme (NEIS) and the
NEIS Allowance, see Department of Education, Skills and Employment.

For more information to help in determining gross pay, see the
Australian Taxation Office (ATO) and search for 'tax tables'. Select the
right table - weekly, fortnightly or monthly.

For information on Beneficiary tax offset, see Government allowances and
payments and the beneficiary tax offset on the ATO website.
