This document outlines how customers can use self service terminals
(SST) to do their business online. These are in all Services Australia
service centres, myGov shopfronts and National Agents and Access Points.

What customers can do

Access the Services Australia website for information on payments and
services. They can also view guides to using self service

Sign in to myGov to do most of their business online with linked member
services

Access approved internet sites including:

-   bank and credit union websites
-   government, higher education and community sites for job search and
    payment related activities
-   most email providers

Look for work

Print statements, letters and other documentation

The Resources page contains links to websites, intranet pages,
publications and mySupport.
