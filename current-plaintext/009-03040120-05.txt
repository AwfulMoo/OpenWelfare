Carer Allowance (CA) rejection DOC

The DOC should contain all information relevant to the rejection of the
claim. For private home not shared by carer and care receiver
qualification, document the reason for rejection if it is:

-   Carer living separately - less than 20 hours personal care provided
-   Carer living separately - paid carer providing care
-   Carer living separately - co-residing carer providing care (such as
    the person who is living with the carer is providing care)
