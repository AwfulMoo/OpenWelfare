Payment Reconciliation Work Item Processing

The Payment Reconciliation is an automatic process that checks the
payments calculated for a customer are the actual payments delivered to
the customer via the Centrelink payment system. The system will perform
various checks, and where the payment amount is different to the
entitlement amount a DOC activity is created for further investigation.

Resources contains a link to the Payment Rates and Thresholds and a link
to an image containing an example of the PAYREC doc, the Payment Summary
screen and an explanation of the analysis undertaken.
