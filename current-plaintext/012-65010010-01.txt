This document outlines information about the Seventh Community Pharmacy
Agreement (7CPA).

About the 7CPA

The 7CPA started on 1 July 2020 and is in place for 5 years.

The 7PCPA is between the:

-   Commonwealth of Australia
-   Pharmacy Guild of Australia, and
-   Pharmaceutical Society of Australia

It supports access to Pharmaceutical Benefits Scheme (PBS) medicines and
related services through community pharmacies across Australia.

As part of the 7CPA Services Australia will administer the following
initiatives:

-   Continuation of the
    electronic prescription fee (EPF) until 30 June 2021

For more information see the Resources page for a link to the Department
of Health and Aged Care website.
