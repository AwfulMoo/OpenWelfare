This document outlines the Expense Management Summary Screen for
customers who are subject to Income Management (IM).

Functions of screen

The screen allows a user to:

-   view the customers available IM balance and Fortnight (14-days)
    balance details
-   launch workflows to manage voluntary Centrepay deductions, regular
    and one off Income Management expenses
-   launch the workflow to complete the priority needs discussion, and
-   view expense request, regular expense, one off expense, store card
    and adjustment histories

Location of screen

This screen can be reached from the menu list on the left hand side of
the screen under:

-   Income Management
-   Manage Expenses
-   Expense Management Summary

The Resources page contains an example of how the available balance and
current balance displayed on the screen is calculated and links to
Locator for the National Transfer numbers and customer 13 and 1800
numbers.
