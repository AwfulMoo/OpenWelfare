cardholders and in some cases their dependants.

| Scheme at the concession rate.    |                                   |
+-----------------------------------+-----------------------------------+
| Bulk-billed general practitioner  | -   HCC cardholder and dependants |
| appointments at the discretion of |     at the discretion of the      |
| the doctor.                       |     concession provider.          |
+-----------------------------------+-----------------------------------+
| An increase in benefits for       | -   HCC cardholder and            |
| out-of-pocket, out-of-hospital    |     dependants.                   |
| medical expenses above a certain  |                                   |
| threshold through the Medicare    |                                   |
| Safety Net.                       |                                   |
