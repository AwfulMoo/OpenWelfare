This document outlines how to handle enquiries for supply relating to
death reason codes 619, 620, 621 and 622, for the Pharmaceutical
Benefits Scheme (PBS).

Disclosure of information

Service Officers must not disclose a patient's deceased status to any
individual when responding to enquiries. This includes the enquiring
pharmacy. Disclosure of this additional information is not specifically
required in the performance of duties and may be in breach of the
secrecy provisions outlined in the National Health Act 1953 and Health
Insurance Act 1973. The References page contains links to these Acts.

The Resources page contains typical questions and appropriate responses
to death reason code questions for the PBS.
