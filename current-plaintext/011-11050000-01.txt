This document outlines information to assist with the processing rules,
field and button descriptions for Australian Organ Donor Register (AODR)
registrations and when inquiring on, searching or amending AODR
registration.

Service Officers must be in Amendment Search within CDMS when handling
calls from the AODR Information Line. Specific screens are required for
the AMP telephony line,

The Process page contains more information about general processing
rules including both minor and major discrepancies. The Resources page
contains:

-   examples of how to action conflicting donation decisions on forms
-   contact details, and
-   a link to Consumer Directory Maintenance System (CDMS) functions
