Agreements and arrangements

Services Australia deliver payments and services on behalf of other
government agencies. A range of bilateral agreements and Bilateral
Management Arrangements (BMA) governs our relationships with those
agencies.

For more information, contact the Department of Social Services (DSS)
Relationship Team.
