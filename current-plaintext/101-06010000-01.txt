This document outlines the concessions and discounts available to a
Commonwealth Seniors Health Card (CSHC) holder.

Health related concessions

The CSHC holder may be entitled to:

-   discounts on prescription medicines listed under the Pharmaceutical
    Benefits Scheme (PBS)
-   bulk-billed General Practitioner (GP) appointments at the discretion
    of the GP
-   reduced out-of-hospital medical expenses through the Medicare Safety
    Net

Concessions from other governments or providers

In some instances, additional health, household, transport, education
and recreation concessions may be offered by state or territory and
local governments and private providers. However, these providers offer
these concessions at their own discretion, and the availability of these
concessions may vary from state to state.

Note: the State Seniors Cards is a different card to the CSHC.

More Support for CSHC holders

CSHC holders may be eligible for Energy Supplement and the Economic
Support Payment 1 and 2. See Commonwealth Seniors Health Card (CSHC).
