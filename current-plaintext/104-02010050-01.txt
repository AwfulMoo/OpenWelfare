This document outlines information about the psychologist consultation
service and the referral process for forensic matters.

Consultation services

The Assessment Services Branch has established a consultation service to
provide professional advice and support for Services Australia staff
working with customers who have behavioural issues that may require an
enhanced level of support.

These psychologists are highly professional and experienced consultants
who have specialist knowledge in the practice of psychology working in a
range of forensic settings. These include correctional facilities, the
police service or in community corrections where they utilise their
clinical skills in understanding human behaviours.

Behavioural issues that the psychologists provide include:

advice on managing customers who are:

-   aggressive or vexatious
-   threatening suicide or homicide intent

psychological education and support for Services Australia staff working
with difficult customers

group training for Services Australia staff to build resilience, set
professional boundaries, understand specific mental health and substance
misuse issues and how these may impact on customer and staff behaviour,
and

advice about community corrections involvement in the management of
customers
