This document outlines information about the Agreement including the
process of making a claim and coding of Chilean payments.

Commencement date

The Agreement with Chile started on 1 July 2004 and was amended by
exchange of notes effective 1 October 2009.

Claiming and benefits covered

The Agreement generally allows customers to lodge a claim for payment
from either country. It also allows customers to add together periods of
residence in Australia and periods of social security coverage in Chile,
to meet the minimum requirements for payment.

Australian payments covered:

-   Age Pension
-   Disability Support Pension for the severely disabled

Customers can claim Australian payments under the Agreement by
contacting Services Australia or the Chilean authorities.

Chilean payments covered:

-   Old Age Pension
-   Disability Pension
-   Survivor Pension

Customers can claim a foreign pension by contacting the Services
Australia or the Chilean authorities.
