Rules about withdrawal of superannuation benefits on hardship grounds
are not part of social security legislation. The superannuation industry
has its own legislation, which governs the release of superannuation
benefits.

Superannuation Industry (Supervision) Regulations 1994

-   section 6.01(2), Commonwealth income support payment
-   section 6.01(5), Interpretation

Retirement Savings Accounts Regulations 1997

-   section 4.01(2), Commonwealth income support payment
-   section 4.01(5), Interpretation
