Carer Allowance adjusted taxable income details (SA489)

Forms issued with CA (child) HCC only review are system generated:

-   Review of Carer Allowance Medical Report including Functional
    Assessment (for a child under 16 years) (SA403)
-   Review of Carer Allowance Care Needs Assessment (for a child under
    16 years) (SA420)

The following forms can be printed and are identical to the above review
forms:

-   Medical Report including functional assessment (for a child under 16
    years) SA429
-   Review of Carer Allowance - Care Needs Assessment (for a child under
    16 years) SA423

Examples of backdating Carer Allowance

  --------- ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  Example   Scenario
  1         A carer is selected for a CA (child) HCC only review on 13 July. The carer lodges the review on 28 July. The review is processed on 02 August. It is identified that the care receiver's medical condition changed on 01 June resulting in the carer now being qualified for CA payment. The start day for payment is 28 July, as this was the day the carer lodged the review forms.
  2         A carer who is CA (child) HCC only contacts the agency on 1 November about a change in their child’s medical condition which was diagnosed on 3 September. A review is sent to the carer on 1 November. The carer lodges the forms on 13 November and is assessed as qualifying for CA payment. The start day for payment is 1 November as this was the day the carer notified the agency of the change. See Reassessment of CA Health Care Card (HCC) only entitlement to payment level Carer Allowance (CA) for a child.
