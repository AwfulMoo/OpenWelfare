This document outlines how to cancel a customer’s LIC entitlement.

Automatic or manual cancellation

Many benefits can be cancelled automatically or manually:

-   automatic cancellation may occur following reassessments where no
    continuing entitlement exists,for example, income precludes
    entitlement
-   manual cancellation may be required when a customer requests
    cancellation of the card, or a customer is imprisoned

A LIC cannot be suspended.

Unfavourable decisions

When making an unfavourable decision, speak to the customer:

-   explain the decision
-   give them a chance to provide more information, and
-   advise their review and appeal rights

Make 2 genuine attempts to contact a customer before finalising the
decision.
