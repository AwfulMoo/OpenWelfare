This document outlines the procedure for students who attend a Defence
Force Reserves training camp.

Training camps for Defence Force Reserve

A customer in receipt of ABSTUDY, Austudy or Youth Allowance (YA) as a
full-time student can attend a training camp as a member of the Defence
Force Reserve services during a vacation period. YA/ABSTUDY (full-time
students) may also attend a training camp as a member of the Defence
Force Reserve services during studies if they remain enrolled in their
course and are making satisfactory progress. The camps may be situated
within Australia or overseas. The customer is required to provide
evidence of their training camp in the form of their Reserves papers or
a letter from the Defence Force Reserves Service Commander (or similar
officer).
