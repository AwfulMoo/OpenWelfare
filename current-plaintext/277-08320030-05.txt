International agreements

Child support cases are transmitted under different International
Arrangements depending on which country the payer resides. The
arrangements are:

-   Agreement between the Government of Australia and the Government of
    the United States of America for the Enforcement of Maintenance
    (Support) Obligations
-   Hague Convention on the Recognition and Enforcement of Decisions
    Relating to Maintenance Obligations 1973
-   United Nations Convention on the Recovery Abroad of Maintenance
    (UNCRAM) 1956, and
-   Non Treaty Reciprocal Arrangements (Commonwealth)

For further details see Child Support Guide 1.5.2: International
agreements and conventions.
