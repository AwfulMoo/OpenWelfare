Income Management Summary screen balances

Balance display and calculations on Income Management Summary screen

This table contains an example of how the available balance and current
balance displayed on the Income Management Summary screen are
calculated.

+-----------------------------------+-----------------------------------+
| Current Balance @ Today           | $200.00                           |
+-----------------------------------+-----------------------------------+
| Credit Day 5                      | +$200.00                          |
+-----------------------------------+-----------------------------------+
| Balance at day 5                  | $400.00                           |
+-----------------------------------+-----------------------------------+
| Debit Day 10                      | $350.00                           |
+-----------------------------------+-----------------------------------+
| Available Balance                 | $50.00                            |
+-----------------------------------+-----------------------------------+

On day 5, the Current Balance would be $400 but this is not used as the
Available Balance because it is higher than the lowest balance during
the period.
