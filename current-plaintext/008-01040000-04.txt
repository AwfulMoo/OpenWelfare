Legislative instruments

For the instrument that allows a person to qualify for an annual EMEP:

-   subject to other eligibility criteria, if they or a person for whom
    they care uses specified essential medical equipment, see Social
    Security (EMEP - Essential Medical Equipment) Specification 2022
-   if they, their partner or a person for whom they care holds an
    energy account or contributes to paying an energy account for a
    specified form of energy that is supplied to the relevant EMEP
    residence, see Social Security (EMEP - Energy) Specification 2022
-   subject to other eligibility criteria, if they or a person for whom
    they care has a specified medical condition and requires additional
    heating or cooling, resulting in higher than average energy use, see
    Social Security (EMEP - Medical Conditions) Specification 2022
-   if they, as the person with medical needs, or they as the carer of
    the person with medical needs and the person for whom they care,
    reside in a home that is a specified residence, see Social Security
    (EMEP - Residences) Specification 2022
