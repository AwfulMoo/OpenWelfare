This document outlines general information about compensation payments.

Compensation Recovery Teams (CRT)

The CRT are the main contact how compensation payments are treated under
the Social Security Act 1991.

If no previous record of the compensation claim, Service Officers are to
record the details on the Compensation Management System (CMS).

The Resources page contains links to the Compensation and Damages form
(Mod C).
