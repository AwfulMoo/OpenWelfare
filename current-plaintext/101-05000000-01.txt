conduct the risk and referral process.

This document outlines the specialist supports available for customers
within Services Australia.

Specialist support

When a customer (whether on income support or not) is experiencing
difficult circumstances, and is in need of additional support, it may be
important to either:

-   consult with a specialist officer, or
-   refer the customer to a specialist officer or other internal support
    within the agency for help

The help provided by each specialist and other internal support service
varies according to their role, function, and the customer’s needs.

The following specialist services are available within Services
Australia:

-   Social work services
-   Indigenous Service Officers
-   Multicultural Service Officers
-   Financial Information Service
-   Assessment Services
-   Community Engagement Officers
-   Aged Care Specialist Officers

Other internal supports include:

-   Grandparent, Foster and Kinship Carer Advisers
-   Farm Household Case Officers
-   Complex Assessment Officers
-   Personalised Services
-   Mobile Service Centres

See Referral to external support services for information about external
referral options.
