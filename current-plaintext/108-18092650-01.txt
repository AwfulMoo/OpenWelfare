Types of rent the customer may be paying

For the purposes of RA, rent means amounts paid or payable on a regular
basis. This is a condition of occupying the principal home.

Types of rent could include:

private rent

lodging for accommodation

board and lodging where the money is for meals and accommodation

accommodation in a care situation

fees in a retirement village/care facility

site fees for a:

-   caravan or other vehicle
-   mobile home
-   transportable home
-   tent

mooring fees to moor a boat

An A-Z of all Rent Assistance codes and terms is in Rent Assistance
(RA).
