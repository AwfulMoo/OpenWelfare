This document outlines the process of requesting income information from
Inland Revenue, New Zealand (NZIR). Links on this page may be
unavailable to external audiences.

Overview

A New Zealand ‘Out’ case is where the payer resides in New Zealand and
the payee resides in Australia and Services Australia have asked NZIR to
register the case and collect on their behalf.
