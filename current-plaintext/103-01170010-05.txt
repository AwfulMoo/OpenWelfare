This page contains assessment examples.

On 24 April 2013 Services Australia is assessing whether the following
individuals who reside in the Northern Territory would be subject to
Income Management under the Disengaged Youth (DEY) measure. The Service
Officer will look at the number of weeks, since 24 October 2012, that
each individual has been receiving a relevant payment.
