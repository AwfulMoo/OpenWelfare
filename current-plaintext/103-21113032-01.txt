From 1 August 2022, Income Management (IM) customers in the Northern
Territory (NT) are unable to transition to the Cashless Debit Card (CDC)
program. For more information, read Operational Message New Cashless
Debit Card (CDC) participants will not start on the program.

This document contains information on how to help customers that have
started on CDC get access to residual IM funds that have not yet been
disbursed to their CDC account.
