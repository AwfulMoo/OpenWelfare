DRA and NZ DRA average weekly income cut-off points

Based on the Rate Calculator Determination 2020.

Average weekly income is the Average Weekly Ordinary Time Earnings
(AWOTE) for a full-time adult in Australia according to the Australian
Bureau of Statistics.

The Australian Bureau of Statistics updates the AWOTE in May each year.
It applies for the following year from 1 January to 31 December.

To assess a customer’s LGA

Open the NSW Live Feed App

Place the address given by the customer in the search box

Select Search (magnifying glass icon)

Select the payment the customer is claiming in the Layers menu on the
right hand side. Use AGDRP for NZ DRP claims and DRA for NZ DRA claims)

If the address provided by the customer is:

-   in a declared LGA, the name will show in the middle of the LGA.
    Note: staff may need to zoom out until the LGA name shows
-   not in a declared LGA, to identify the name scroll down the Layers
    menu on the right hand side and select LGA 2020

Continue to assess the claim:

-   Eligibility for AGDRP and NZ DRP claims
-   Assessing DRA and NZ DRA claims
