This page contains information on how to check the progress or result of
Family Tax Benefit (FTB) reconciliation or FTB lump sum claim as a
result contact from the customer.

On this page:

FTB Reconciliation summary and results

Claim pending or finalised or the customer requires more information
about the reconciliation or lump sum claim result

FTB Reconciliation summary and results

Note: this procedure does not apply if FTB reconciliation or lump sum
payment is parked, as reconciliation has already occurred. In these
cases, check the relevant Australian Taxation Office (ATO) information
has been received.
