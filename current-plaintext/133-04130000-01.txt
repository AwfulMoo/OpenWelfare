This document outlines how to find businesses and organisations that
provide services to Services Australia.

Search tools

There are 2 search tools:

Customers can use Find a business or organisation for these services:

-   Income Management
-   BasicsCard
-   Centrepay

Staff can use Advanced Organisation Search to find the above services
and:

-   Centrelink Confirmation eServices
-   Rent Deduction Scheme

Find a business and organisation search tool

The Find a business or organisation search tool allows customers to
find:

-   where they can use their income managed funds
-   businesses participating in Centrepay and get details to start
    deductions
-   the types of goods and services the business offers
-   the address of BasicsCard merchants

This Resources page contains a link to the Find a business or
organisation search tool for customers on the Services Australia
website.
