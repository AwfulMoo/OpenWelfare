This document outlines information on the Medicare Debt Recovery
functions.

For Medicare Debt Management Officers (DMO) only

Recovering debt

Medicare benefits can sometimes be paid incorrectly or obtained
fraudulently. When this occurs, the person or entity who obtained the
benefit is generally liable to repay the debt. This could be:

-   patients
-   Doctors
-   Medical Practices
-   Compensation companies
-   Private Health Insurance companies

Creating invoices

Medicare program debts are created in SAP Finance. Customers receive
invoices with an overpayment letter explaining the debt.

Dunning

Dunning is the process of automatically issuing a series of overdue
account reminder notices. See Dunning for Medicare debt for more
information.

Types of financial adjustment

Transactions in SAP may require financial adjustments. There are several
types of financial adjustments:

-   Debt write off
-   Refund
-   Funds transfer
-   Invoice reversal

See Financial adjustments for Medicare debt for more information.

General administration

Medicare Recovery complete a number of general tasks in their daily
work. See General administration for Medicare debt for more information.

Reports

Medicare Recovery need to report on a number of different work areas.
See Reports for Medicare debt.
