Policy

Bereavement payments and impact on other allowances/payments

There are a number of bereavement payments available to recipients of
Social Security payments depending on the circumstances. For details on
when they apply and how the customer's current payment is affected, see:

Family Assistance Guide, 2.1.1.110, Death of an FTB or regular care
child

Family Assistance Guide, 2.6.4, CCS eligibility - death of an eligible
child or recipient

Social Security Guide, 3.1.5.115, Death of PP child

Social Security Guide, 3.1.5, Bereavement Payment Provisions

Social Security Guide, 3.6.7.190, Continuation, Variation or Termination
of CA

Social Security Guide, 3.10.4.20, Notification Period for Bereavement
Events - 28 days

Social Security Guide, 2.2.6, Confirming a death

Social Security Guide, 6.1.3, Reviews - Death of a Recipient

persons' and deceased customers are not in this category.

Bereavement payments and definitions

A New Tax System (Family Assistance) Act 1999

-   section 31, Continued eligibility for family tax benefit if an FTB
    child dies
-   section 85DA, Eligibility for child care subsidy or additional child
    care subsidy in substitution for individual who has died.
-   section 85 EC, Only one individual eligible in substitution for
    individual who has died
