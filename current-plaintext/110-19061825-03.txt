Customer contacts about a compliance intervention decision

Employment income debts raised pre 2015, pre 7 December 2020 CAM changes
and debts raised with bank statements

This page has the process for Compliance Officers (CO) when a customer
contacts Services Australia about their compliance intervention
decision.
