This document outlines information about Employers and Overseas Entity's
ability to send and receive emails containing customer information with
Child Support using secure messaging.

Secure messaging

To enable Employers and Overseas Entities to communicate efficiently
with Child Support and exchange sensitive customer information, a secure
messaging service is available through an external provider - Symantec.

Once registered with Symantec, Employers and Overseas Entities can log
in to a secure site to communicate via email with Child Support.

The Resources page contains links to mySupport, Secure Messaging for
Employers and Overseas Entities.
