This document outlines the process for AIC Smart Centre staff to code
AIC payments to a third party (for example, the school, boarding
institution, private board provider, or the student) if this is
requested by the applicant.

Request and procedure

This request can be made when lodging a new claim or while in receipt of
AIC.

All applicant requests for update of AIC payment destination should be
referred to AIC Smart Centre staff. Look up Processing Service details
for: Assistance for Isolated Children (AIC).

Third party debts

AIC Scheme debts may be the responsibility of a third party. This could
be an educational institution, parent or guardian. Check to confirm
which party directly received the payment if an overpayment has been
identified.
