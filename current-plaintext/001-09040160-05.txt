Common cancellation reasons

Common reasons why a customer's payment may cancel before their 'due to
lodge day:

-   failing to lodge their Medical Review (SA012R), regardless of
    medical coverage
-   having mail returned, for example, whereabouts unknown (WUK)
-   missing a Services Australia appointment
