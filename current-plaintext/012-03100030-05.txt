This page contains information about Australian Health Practitioners
Regulation Agency (Ahpra) providers Healthcare Provider Identifiers -
Individual (HPI-I) and non Ahpra registered practitioner details that
can be amended and the possible action to the record. It also contains
information about the automatic status updates to amended records and
subsequent change to the Healthcare Identifiers (HI) Provider Directory
(HI HPD) entry, a link to the HI Service Application to amend a
Healthcare Provider record form and a link to the Ahpra website.

On this Page:
