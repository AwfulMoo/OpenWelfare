For ABSTUDY Smart Centre Processing Services staff only

This page contains steps to determine who is the payee for Away from
Home (AFH) allowances payable to students and Australian Apprentices,
including School Fees Allowance (SFA) (Group 2).

On this page:

Determining payee for AFH allowances for SFA (Group 2)

the permanent home
