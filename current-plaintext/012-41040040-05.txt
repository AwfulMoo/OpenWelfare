Forms

Application to register or amend a diagnostic imaging or radiation
oncology practice form (HW061)

Note: these forms and any supporting documentation can be uploaded and
submitted through Health Professional Online Services (HPOS), see
Services Australia's website. If the form has been received via HPOS
then correspondence will also need to be delivered via HPOS. Forms
received via HPOS can be identified, as the naming convention will
contain the word 'HPOS': for example YYYYMMDD.HPOS12345678_
WorkID.WorkType. For more details, see the Process page in Health
Professional Online Services Support Mail Centre – Managing emails from
health professionals.
