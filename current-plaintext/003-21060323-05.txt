Verbal claim

For every phone claim taken:

Right click on the link below, depending on the event the customer is
claiming

Select 'Save link as'

Staff must save the template to their desktop

Open the template

Complete a new template for each relevant period claimed where automatic
payments do not apply

Do not delete any questions in this template

Always access the version in Resources for future claims

-   Content is updated regularly. Using the current version prevents
    accidental use of customer data from a previous claim

Complete all questions as per your discussion with the customer by:

-   answering 'Yes' or 'No' as needed, and
-   documenting relevant information

Instructions in blue should be deleted once actioned otherwise the
template may exceed the character limit for Fast Notes

Use the hyperlinks to progress through the template

Copy and paste the completed template into the relevant Verbal Claim
Fast Note

When completing the template, follow Operational Blueprint

Use this template and Operational Blueprint together

To:

-   assess eligibility, see Assessing COVID-19 Disaster Payment claims
-   record and code a claim, see Coding COVID-19 Disaster Payment claims

Once completed, staff must
- copy and paste content below the instruction box into the relevant
Verbal Claim Fast Note in Customer
- delete the saved template
- start a new template from the below links for any new claim
