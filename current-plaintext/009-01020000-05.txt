Employment assistance for carers

This table outlines the types of employment assistance that CP and CA
customers may be eligible for.

  Employment assistance services                              Carer Payment   Carer Allowance
  Fully Eligible Participants (FEP)                           N               N
  Volunteer Job Seekers                                       Y               Y
  New Enterprise Incentive Scheme (NEIS)                      Y               N
  Disability Employment Services (DES)                        Y               Y pending in receipt of an income support payment.
  Additional Child Care Subsidy (ACCS) (Transition to Work)   Y               N
  Community Development Program (CDP)                         Y               Y
  ----------------------------------------------------------- --------------- ----------------------------------------------------
