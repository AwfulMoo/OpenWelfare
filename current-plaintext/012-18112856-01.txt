This document outlines information about practice payments in the
Practice Incentives Program (PIP) and the Workforce Incentive Program
(WIP) - Practice Stream. The information is for Service Officers to use
for payment related processing, administration and general enquiries.
PIP and WIP - Practice Stream payments are made to eligible general
practices. PIP Service Incentive Payments (SIPS) are payable to General
Practitioners that meet the eligibility requirements. These can be paid
directly to a practice where agreed with the General Practitioner.

The Resources page contains links to contact details for Practice
Incentives Program (PIP) and the Workforce Incentive Program (WIP) -
Practice Stream
