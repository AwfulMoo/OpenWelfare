This document outlines information on the Practice Incentives Program
(PIP) and Workforce Incentive Program (WIP) – Practice Stream for health
professionals and supporting systems.

Background

The PIP is aimed at supporting general practice activities that
encourage continuing improvements and quality care, enhance capacity and
improve access and health outcomes for patients. We administer the PIP
on behalf of the policy owners, the Department of Health (Health).The
PIP is not a legislative program and is delivered under program
guidelines.

The WIP – Practice Stream provides incentive payments to practices to
support an expanded and enhanced role of nurses employed in general
practice. We administer the WIP – Practice Stream on behalf of the
policy owners, the Department of Health (Health) and the Department of
Veterans’ Affairs (DVA). The WIP – Practice Stream is not a legislative
program and is delivered under program guidelines.

The Resources page contains contact details for the Practice Incentives
Program (PIP) and the Workforce Incentive Program (WIP) – Practice
Stream.
