This page contains information about Approval and ID numbers, and when a
Responsible Officer (RO) requires evidence of identity (EOI) to act on
behalf of an organisation.

On this Page:

Approval and ID numbers

The approved person is:

-   the owner of a pharmacy approval number - generally also the Duly
    Authorised Officer (DAO) for the pharmacy PKI location certificate
-   an authorised person (if noted as ‘director’ on the authorised
    person screen)
