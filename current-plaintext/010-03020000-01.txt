This document outlines procedures, eligibility and assessment criteria
for the Assistance for Isolated Children (AIC) Scheme.

AIC Allowances

AIC Scheme allowances are available for primary, secondary and certain
tertiary students who are unable to attend an appropriate state school
because of geographic isolation or special health-related or educational
needs.

Processing AIC claims

All AIC claims and forms are assessed by AIC Smart Centres. Staff in
other Service Centres are able to check AIC claims and forms for
completeness and assess eligibility for other payments and services.
