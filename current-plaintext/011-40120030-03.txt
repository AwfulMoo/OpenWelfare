There is a 4-tier support model for staff:

-   Tier 1 - staff must use Operational Blueprint
-   Tier 2 - help is provided onsite by a Team Coach/Team
    Leader/QDO/Buddy System to give basic troubleshooting and
    navigational support
-   Tier 3 - ask for help via the MsTeams Technical Support Chat and
    monitor frequently asked questions in the chat group
-   Tier 4 - if the above options are unable to resolve the customer's
    enquiry, follow the actions in the table below

Note: cold transfer calls to the relevant queue unless told to warm
transfer below.
