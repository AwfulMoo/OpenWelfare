This document explains specific information about the Agreement
including the process of making a claim for Australian payment under the
agreement and coding of Indian payments.

On this Page:

Start date

The Agreement with India started on 1 January 2016.

Claiming and benefits covered

The Agreement generally allows customers to lodge a claim for payment
from either country. It also allows customers to add together specific
periods of residence in Australia and specific periods of insurance in
India, to meet the minimum requirements for payment.

Australian payments covered:

-   Age Pension

Customers can claim Australian payments under the Agreement by
contacting Services Australia or the Indian authorities.

Indian payments covered:

-   Retirement (Age) Pension
-   Invalidity (Total and Permanent Disability) Pension
-   Survivors’ Pension

Customers can claim a foreign pension by contacting Services Australia
or the Indian authorities.
