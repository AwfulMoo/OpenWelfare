This document outlines the dosage calculators and charts for
Pharmaceutical Benefits Scheme (PBS).

Dosage calculators

The dosage calculators assist Service Officers to determine the total
quantity and length of supply to approve for Authority required
medicines. The calculators are an easy-to-use alternative to the dosage
chart.

Note: the results provided by the calculators must be considered in the
context of the individual circumstances of the authority application.
