This document outlines information on how to create a new objection or
update an existing objection to donate organs in the Australian Organ
Donor Register (AODR).

Objection to donate organs

A person can request to register an objection to donate by either:

-   submitting a new registration form and selecting the option in
    section 1 - Register my decision not to be a donor
-   registering using myGov or the Medicare Express Plus app and under
    the option 'organ and tissue donation' selecting 'I wish to register
    my decision not to be a donor'
-   over the phone and registering their objection to donate
-   amending an existing registration by submitting a registration form
    to amend an existing consent registration

The objection to donate covers bone tissue, eye tissue, heart, heart
valves, kidneys, liver, lungs, pancreas and skin tissue.

Note:

-   If the donor has an existing yes consent registration and requests
    to register their objection to donate, a new consent form must be
    completed and returned to Medicare
-   To avoid duplicates, complete Search for an existing registration
    before creating a new organ donor registration in Consumer Directory
    Maintenance System (CDMS)
