This document outlines how Hearing Australia and Services Australia
share the responsibility of providing hearing screening services for
customers.

Services provided

Services Australia and Hearing Australia have entered into a Memorandum
of Understanding to support elderly citizens by facilitating hearing
screenings with the objective of identifying hearing loss.

Screenings are conducted in over 120 service centres across the country.
See the Resources page for service centres list.

This service is offered to customers as part of their normal Services
Australia business and is conducted every 6 months. The department is
required to:

-   provide appropriate facilities and
-   arrange customer bookings for screening tests

For further information, see the Process page for the roles and
responsibilities of Services Australia and Hearing Australia regarding
hearing screenings.

The Resources page contains a list of designated Services Australia
service centres offering the hearing screenings.
