Information Packs and Plastic Card Wallets

BasicsCard Customer Information Packs are to be issued to
customers/payment nominees when they receive their first BasicsCard.
BasicsCard Customer Information Packs contain:

-   BasicsCard Customer Terms and Conditions
-   BasicsCard Factsheet
-   BasicsCard Card Wallet.

This is a combined product that can be ordered directly via Centrelink
Orders Online (COOL) by sites issuing BasicsCard. Sites are responsible
for maintaining supplies of this product. The Product Number in COOL is
9359 'BasicsCard - Helping you manage your money'.

Sites should also maintain separate stocks of card replacement wallets
for replacement BasicsCards. The Product Number in COOL is CO620
'BasicsCard Customer plastic card wallet'.
