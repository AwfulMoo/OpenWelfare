This page contains information on how an Assessing Officer (AO) is to
complete assessment of a MIOC AHHCCS initial or subsequent payment
application, or a Section 38 response, to determine if the AHHCCS
payment application meets the relevant eligibility requirements under
the AHHCCS scheme.

On this page:

Assessing MIOC AHHCCS S38 Responses
