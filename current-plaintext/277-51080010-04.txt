For the definitions of maintenance income and capitalised maintenance
income, see A New Tax System (Family Assistance) Act 1999, section 3,
Definitions.

For more about the meaning of maintenance income, capitalised
maintenance income, and exempt child disability expenses, see A New Tax
System (Family Assistance) Act 1999, section 19, Maintenance income.

Maintenance income received by a customer's child is included, see A New
Tax System (Family Assistance) Act 1999, Schedule 1, clause 19A,
Extended meaning of receiving maintenance income.

Maintenance income received by the customer's partner is included, see A
New Tax System (Family Assistance) Act 1999, Schedule 1, clause 21,
Maintenance income of members of couple to be added.
