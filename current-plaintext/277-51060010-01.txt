This document outlines how to locate the pre July 2000 Child
Support/Maintenance screens. These screens were used to record and
display Child Support/maintenance income assessment details.

Child Support Maintenance screens

The information available to a Service Officer via the pre July 2000
Child Support/Maintenance screens includes:

-   the annual amount assessed for Maintenance Income Test (MIT)
    purposes, as well as the amount actually paid by Child Support
-   the child support liability amount to be collected privately by the
    customer
-   the individual payday breakdown of the total child support paid to a
    customer through Child Support and shows where the payment was
    issued
-   a summary of all the child support income information.
