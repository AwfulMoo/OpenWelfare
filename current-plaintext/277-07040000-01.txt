This document outlines information relating to child support income
estimates from 1 July 2010, including, a Service Officer(s) role on a
customer's initial contact about their income, and the procedure for
taking and making a decision on an estimate election.

International cases

This document includes information about estimates lodged for
international cases.

Estimate elections

Where a customer's income varies significantly from that used in the
current child support assessment, the Child Support (Assessment) Act
1989 allows the customer to elect to have their assessment based on an
estimate of current income in some circumstances.

For details on reviewing or reconciling an estimate, see:

-   Child support estimates review (post 1 July 2010), or
-   Child support estimates - Reconcile (pre 1 July 2011)

Authorisations and delegations

Service Officer 3 (SO3) level or above

To accept or refuse an estimate election.

For authorisations and delegations see Child Support Services,
Delegations and Authorisations.
