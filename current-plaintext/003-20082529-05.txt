Pandemic Leave Disaster Payment - Victoria

Claim form

Period of self-isolation, quarantine or caring that starts:

-   between 9 December 2021 and 17 January 2022 (inclusive), use the
    Claim for Pandemic Leave Disaster Payment (EM252) form
-   before 9 December 2021, (that is, up to and including 8 December
    2021), take a claim for this period over the phone using the verbal
    claim template. If the customer is not able to submit a claim via
    phone, use the Claim for Pandemic Leave Disaster Payment - Victoria
    form (EM223) form

Scenarios - general

For periods of self-isolation, quarantine or caring that start:

-   from (on or after) 9 December 2021 (ending on 17 January 2022), see
    Scenarios - from 9 December 2021
-   before 9 December 2021 (that is, up to and including 8 December
    2021), see Scenarios - before 9 December 2021

Scenarios - payments customers residing in New South Wales or South Australia are eligible for

See Scenarios – Victoria Pandemic Leave Disaster payment, and where a:

-   New South Wales resident tries to claim the Victoria Pandemic Leave
    Disaster Payment, see scenarios about eligibility for either New
    South Wales or Victoria Pandemic Leave Disaster Payment on the
    Resources page of Pandemic Leave Disaster Payment - New South Wales
-   South Australian resident tries to claim the Victoria Pandemic Leave
    Disaster Payment, see scenarios about eligibility for either South
    Australia or Victoria Pandemic Leave Disaster Payment on the
    Resources page of Pandemic Leave Disaster Payment – South Australia
