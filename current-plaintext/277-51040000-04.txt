Family Tax Benefit (FTB) Part A is limited to the base rate for a child
if the customer or their partner is entitled to apply for child support
for the child but does not take reasonable action. See:

A New Tax System (Family Assistance) Act 1999

-   Schedule 1, clause 10, Effect of maintenance rights
-   Schedule 1, Part 2, Division 5, Maintenance income test

For FTB instalment customers, maintenance income is estimated during the
income year, see Family Assistance (Administration) Act 1999, section
31B, Variation of instalment entitlement determination to reflect
revised maintenance income estimates

Eligibility for Child Support collection

For collection of child support, see Child Support (Registration and
Collection) Act 1988, section 17, Liabilities in relation to children
that are registrable maintenance liabilities

For collection of spousal maintenance, see Child Support (Registration
and Collection) Act 1988, section 18, Liabilities in relation to parties
to marriages that are registrable maintenance liabilities
