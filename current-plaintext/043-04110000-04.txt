Policy

For information about the assessment of fixed (non-discretionary trusts
including those set up before 7.30 p.m. AEST 9 May 2000, refer to Guide
to Social Security Law, 4.12.3.20, Fixed (Non-discretionary) Trusts

For information about testamentary trusts - including savings provisions
for those activated on or before 31 March 2001, refer to Guide to Social
Security Law, 4.12.3.30, Testamentary Trusts

For information about trusts set up for individuals unable to handle
their own affairs (protective trusts), refer to Guide to Social Security
Law, 4.12.3.40, Protective Trusts

For information about limited appointorship concession for primary
producers (farmers), refer to Guide to Social Security Law, 4.12.11.20,
Qualification Provisions for the Primary Production Concession

Guide to Social Security Law, 4.12.11, Primary Production Controlled
Private Trusts & Controlled Private Companies
