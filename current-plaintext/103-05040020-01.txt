This document outlines information related to customers receiving
Parenting Payment Single (PPS) under Age Pension age or Disability
Support Pensioners (DSP) aged under 21 without dependent children who
are able to claim advance payments of Pharmaceutical Allowance (PhA).

Entitlement and rate

The PhA advance payment equals 7 times the fortnightly rate or the
number of paydays remaining in the entitlement year if this is less than
7.

An entitlement year for advance payments of PhA is a calendar year (01
January to 31 December in the same year).

The following criteria must be met for a customer to be eligible for the
advance:

-   Entitlement to PhA must exist
-   A customer's ordinary income must not exceed $20.50 per fortnight
-   Expenditure on prescriptions must not be less than the PhA received
    in that entitlement year

How to claim

A Claim for Advance of Pharmaceutical Allowance (SA140) form must be
completed.

The Resources page contains a link to the Claim for Advance of
Pharmaceutical Allowance (SA140) form.
