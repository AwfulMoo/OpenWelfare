This document has information for staff working on Deduction and
Confirmation programs only.

It outlines instructions on how staff who issue letters to Third Party
Organisations (TPO) can create, print, and send Online Advice (OLA)
letters to TPOs.

OLA letters to TPOs

The OLA letters sent to TPOs include:

notices of approval for contracts with Services Australia

withdrawal and termination of contracts

letters relating to programme assurance matters

contractual letters for programmes including:

-   BasicsCard
-   Income Management (IM)
-   Centrelink Confirmation eServices (CCeS)
-   Centrepay
-   Electronic Verification of Rent (EVoR)

The Resources page has a table listing the available OLA letters used by
staff issuing letters to Third Party Organisations (TPO), including
letter codes and a brief description for each letter. It also has
information on user settings required for issuing Online Advice (OLA)
letters.
