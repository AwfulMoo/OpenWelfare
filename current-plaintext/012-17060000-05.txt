DVA fee types and fees paid

Table 1: this table describes the DVA fees for medical services for
Veterans’ Affairs Processing.

+-----------------------------------+-----------------------------------+
| Fee type                          | Fee paid                          |
+-----------------------------------+-----------------------------------+
| Medicare Benefits Schedule (MBS)  | 100% of MBS scheduled Fee         |
+-----------------------------------+-----------------------------------+
| Rural Enhancement Incentive       | 10% Loading above scheduled fee   |
+-----------------------------------+-----------------------------------+
| Local Medical Officer (LMO)       | 115% of scheduled fee             |
+-----------------------------------+-----------------------------------+
| Repatriation Medical Fee Schedule | 135% of the scheduled fee for Out |
| (RMFS)                            | of Hospital consultations and     |
|                                   | 140% for procedures               |
+-----------------------------------+-----------------------------------+
| Relative Value Guide fees (RVG)   | RVG Fee Schedule                  |
+-----------------------------------+-----------------------------------+
| REI and LMO fee calculations      | 10% loading + 115% of scheduled   |
|                                   | fee                               |
+-----------------------------------+-----------------------------------+
| Derived fee calculations          | Calculated using Formula          |
+-----------------------------------+-----------------------------------+
| Medical accounts with GST         | N/A                               |
+-----------------------------------+-----------------------------------+
