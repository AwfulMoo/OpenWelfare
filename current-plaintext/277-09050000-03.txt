This page contains information about the process for the External
Complaints Child Support (ExCCS) team to manage complaints from
customers who write directly to the Minister or Prime Minister, or when
a parliamentarian writes to the Minister on behalf of a constituent.
When required, ExCCS Service Officers consult with Service Support
Managers (SSM) and Service Managers (SM).

On this page:

Managing complaints from the Minister’s office or another government
department

Refer complaint to another government department for management or input

Contact the customer to manage the complaint

Ministerial complaint requires written response

Finalise written responses and Ministerial complaints

Provide feedback for Ministerial complaints
