This document outlines a list of Customer Relationship Management (CRM)
system Hot Keys to be used by Service Officers when entering new tasks
within a Service Request (SR) in CRM.

Background

Using hot keys reduces keying and maintains consistency of language when
entering My Health Record provider information into CRM during incoming
and outgoing calls to applicants.
