This document outlines the tasks required to be performed on a weekly
basis when information is exchanged between the Child Support and Inland
Revenue, New Zealand (NZIR).

Weekly tasks

The following tasks are performed on a weekly basis:

-   Weekly 'In' exchange
-   Reporting for 'In' exchange - including NZF Master, and
-   Provide NZIR with confirmation

Authorisations and delegations

See the Background page in New Zealand Case Management - Exchange of
Information from NZIR to Child Support for information about
authorisations and delegations.
