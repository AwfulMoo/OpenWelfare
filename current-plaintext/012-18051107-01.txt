This document outlines details of the previous Pharmaceutical Benefits
Scheme (PBS) subsidised idelalasib for patients with chronic lymphocytic
leukaemia (CLL) and small lymphocytic lymphoma (SLL).

This program has been moved to the general schedule from 1 January 2022
and this page is for information purposes only.

Chronic lymphocytic leukaemia/Small lymphocytic lymphoma listing dates

Chronic lymphocytic leukaemia (CLL) and Small lymphocytic lymphoma (SLL)
are cancers of the blood and bone marrow.

Listing dates are as follows:

-   idelalasib - 1 September 2017

See Online Pharmaceutical Benefits Scheme (PBS) Authorities System (OPA)
for further information.
