This document explains how to assess and process the Additional
Incidentals Allowance Claim Form m (SY031).

When to use this form

Customers must complete the Additional Incidentals Allowance Claim Form
m (SY031) if they:

-   are full-time tertiary students and
-   require financial assistance for the costs involved with essential
    course costs/items

For information on eligibility for Additional Incidentals Allowance, see
ABSTUDY Additional Incidentals Allowance.

The SY031 has two sections:

-   Student details
-   School/Faculty Declaration.

Receipts and quotes

Students must provide official receipts and/or quotes within 3 weeks of
lodging the claim.

Official receipts must show:

-   the name of the business
-   details of each item purchased
-   the date and amount of each item purchased

Quotations must show:

-   the name of the business
-   the date the quote was supplied
-   a description of the items

Lodgement

The SY031 form can be lodged using the following options:

-   [A self service option is available for customers: Placed under
    section headings or general content where self service options are
    available.]Online - using the Upload documents service
-   Post - via a reply paid envelope or by posting to Services
    Australia. See Processing Service details for: ABSTUDY and ABSTUDY
    PES
-   In person - at a service centre, or in remote areas, with a
    Centrelink field officer

Processing

ABSTUDY Smart Centre Processing Team process ABSTUDY claims. See
Processing arrangements for ABSTUDY claims and enquiries.
