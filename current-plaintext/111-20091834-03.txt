Cards information

Table 7. Most information on Customer Overview display as cards. Cards
display the interactions between a customer and the agency in a
chronological order for the last 28 days. (With the exception of SMS and
push notifications displaying for only 7 days.) Cards aggregate for the
same activity type undertaken of the same day.

Cards assists by:

displaying letters and correspondence the customer has received from the
agency and a view of the letter as the customer views it (PDF)

displaying the interactions the customer has had in the Smart IVR prior
to reaching a Service Officer. This save the Service Officer from
repeating messages or completing activities the customer has already
undertaken or attempted to self-serve

displaying the previous interactions the customer has had with the
agency, such as Service Centre visit, online and app activities, phone
calls and push notifications highlighting the journey the customer has
already been on
