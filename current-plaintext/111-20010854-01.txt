This document outlines the quality checking process for staff who
complete work in Process Direct.

Staff and QMA

Staff are responsible for accessing their quality checking results and
for identifying their learning and development requirements.

Staff need to:

-   select the QMA Feedback tile each day in Process Direct to view
    results and comments from Quality Management Officers
-   acknowledge they have read the feedback
-   view and monitor identified errors
-   apply continuous improvement techniques to their own work
-   request training or support when needed
-   escalate disputes and supply supporting reference material

Reporting and Management data for QMA

For information about reporting and management data, see Quality
Management Application (QMA) in Process Direct.
