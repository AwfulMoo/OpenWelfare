The claiming period for this event has now closed, unless special
circumstances apply. For more details see Operational Blueprint -
Australian Victim of Terrorism Overseas Payment (AVTOP) claims lodged
outside of the time limit.

On this Page:

Forms

Claim forms for secondary victims are available for claimants on the
Services Australia Website.

Staff can access claim forms from Services Australia Forms on the
Intranet.

Contact details

Staff can contact the Emergency Management Level 2 Helpdesk if there are
any queries.

If staff receive a call in regards to an AVTOP, transfer the claimant to
the hotline on 1800 040 226.
