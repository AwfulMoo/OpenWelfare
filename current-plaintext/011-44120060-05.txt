This page contains links to contact details for Medicare Eligibility
Local Peer Support (LPS), the Newborn Child Declaration (FA081) and the
Medicare enrolment application form (MS004).

Note: the FA081 is for reference only and additional forms cannot be
distributed to the public. Where a parent has never received or has lost
their FA081 form, they will need to complete the Medicare enrolment
application form.
