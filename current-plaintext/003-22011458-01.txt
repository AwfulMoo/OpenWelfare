This document explains the internal review process for the Pandemic
Leave Disaster Payment.

Review

There is no time limit to request an explanation of a Pandemic Leave
Disaster Payment decision.

If a customer does not understand or agree with the reason/s for a
Pandemic Leave Disaster Payment decision, they can ask for an
explanation of the decision.

A Suitably Skilled Officer will provide an explanation of the decision.

The Suitably Skilled Officer will:

-   investigate the decision
-   correct any identified errors
-   undertake any reassessment, and
-   contact the customer to explain the decision

A review cannot be undertaken by:

-   an Authorised Review Officer (ARO), or
-   the Administrative Appeals Tribunal (AAT)

Timeliness standards

Services Australia aims to complete an explanation of decision within 14
days from the date of the customer’s request.
