For staff processing International Disbursements only.

This document outlines the process of sending bulk payments
electronically to New Zealand Inland Revenue Child Support (NZIR).

Schedules currently prepared

Schedules are created to send bulk payments out of Australia. The
following 10 schedules are currently prepared:

-   New Zealand
-   Sweden
-   Slovakia
-   British Columbia
-   Ontario
-   Norway
-   Netherlands
-   Germany
-   Switzerland
-   Payments Overseas (Cheque payment)
