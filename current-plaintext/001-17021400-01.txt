This document provides historical information about the PaTH Internship
Programme including the objectives of participation in the programme and
who was eligible to participate. PaTH was a 4 to 12 week internship
placement that sought to give job seekers under 25 years of age
employability skills and real work experience.

Ceassation of the PaTH Internship

Following a government announcement, from 1 July 2022 providers have
ceased making referrals to the PaTH Internship Programme. PaTH was
ceased with effect from 01 October 2022, as announced in the Federal
Budget on 25 October 2022. Participants who were current on 1 July 2022
continued in their placement until they reached their end date or were
exited for any reason.

Objectives of the PaTH Internship Programme

The objective of the PaTH Internship Programme was to address barriers
to employment by providing opportunities for job seekers that increased
their chances of obtaining sustainable employment. Participants received
the $200 fortnightly PaTH Internship payment.

PaTH Internship Programme eligibility

Job seekers with mutual obligation or participation requirements were
eligible to participate if they:

were receiving any of the following income support payments

-   JobSeeker Payment (JSP)
-   Youth Allowance (YA) (other)
-   Special Benefit (SpB)
-   Parenting Payment Single (PPS)
-   Disability Support Pension (DSP)

and

were aged 17 to 24 and already serviced in either:

-   Transition to Work (TtW)
-   Disability Employment Services (DES), or
-   Workforce Australia Provider Services

or

were aged 17 to 24 and already serviced in:

-   Workforce Australia Online for Individuals, and
-   had received 2 months (63 days) of Online services or had
    participated in an Employability Skills Training (EST) course or
    were participating in a Workforce Specialist activity

Coding

The PaTH Internship approved activity automatically appeared on the
Activity and Exemption Summary (AEX) screen as PTH and included start
and expected end dates. PaTH Internship payments appeared on the Payment
Summary (PS) screen as PII.
