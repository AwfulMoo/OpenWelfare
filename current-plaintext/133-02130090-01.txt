This document outlines the processing instructions for Tier 2 and Tier 3
staff on how to update the status of a Provider Digital Access (PRODA)
account. It also includes how to display pending records and flagged
identifiers in PRODA.

Self service

Service Officers can help users self-manage their PRODA account online.
This can include cancelling their PRODA account.

Other updates to the account status need to be completed by a Service
Officer.

Account status updates

Tier 2 and Tier 3 staff can complete the following updates on a user's
PRODA account:

-   reactivate cancelled or suspended accounts
-   suspend account
-   cancel account
-   retire account

The Resources page contains contact details for Tier 2 and Tier 3 staff.
