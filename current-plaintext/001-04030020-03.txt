If a child is in immediate danger or risk of harm you must act, and a
delegate must call 000.

This page contains the steps to take if a customer is currently in
receipt of YA or DSP, or lodges a claim for YA, DSP, SpB or TAP and has
applied under the UTLAH independence category.
