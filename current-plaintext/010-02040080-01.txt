This document outlines the procedure to determine if a student or
Australian Apprentice can be considered independent for ABSTUDY on the
grounds of meeting the age criteria.

Independence at 22 years of age

A customer applying for ABSTUDY will be considered independent if they
are 22 years of age or older. This category of independence is
permanent, and once granted the student or Australian Apprentice remains
eligible for independent status for this claim and any future claims.
