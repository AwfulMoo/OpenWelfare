Policy

A person must use the legal name at all times for the Services Australia
purposes, including issue and reissue of concession cards. See:

Social Security Guide, 3.9.2.10, Qualification for PCC

Social Security Guide, 3.9.1 HCC - Qualification Provisions

Social Security Guide, 3.9.3 CSHC - Qualification Provisions

Social Security Guide, 3.9.4.20, Non-cancellation of Concession Cards
for Temporary Overseas Absences

Social Security Guide, 2.2.2, Verifying Personal Details.
