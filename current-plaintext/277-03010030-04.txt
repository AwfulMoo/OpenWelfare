Delegations and authorisations

The authorisation to accept or refuse an application for administrative
assessment is at the Service Officer 2 (SO2) level.

The authorisation to determine that a non-parent carer is not an
eligible carer is at the SO4 level.

For more information about authorisations and delegations, see Child
Support Services, Delegations and Authorisations.
