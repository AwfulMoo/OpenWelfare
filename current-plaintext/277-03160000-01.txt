This document outlines tables and values used in child support
assessments including care levels, cost percentages, protected earnings
amount, annualised MTAWE, self-support amount, default income, maximum
basic amount for parenting payment single, minimum annual rate, fixed
annual rate and the costs of children. It also includes values for
periods prior to 1 July 2008 and for WA ex-nuptial children (pre 1 July
1999 amendments).
