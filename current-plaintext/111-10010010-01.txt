This document outlines how Classification Support staff locate, access
and classify Centrelink customer images that have been escalated in the
National Queue via Process Direct.

Classification Support Officer

A Classification Support Officer is responsible for:

-   resolving issues and classifying images that have been escalated
-   moving images to other queues in the National Queue

Table 1 on the Process page outlines classification guidelines.

Restricted Access Officer

A Restricted Access Officer is responsible for:

-   classifying Restricted Access documents
-   ensuring document upload is successful for Restricted Access records

Table 2 on the Process page outlines classification guidelines.

Index and the Staff Feedback Tool.
