Taxation

Under the Income Tax Assessment Act 1936, student assistance allowances
paid on behalf of, or directly to students under 16 years of age
(regardless of their status) are not treated as taxable income.

What is a debt?

A reference to a special education assistance overpayment relates to an
overpayment of a current or former payment of:

-   ABSTUDY
-   ABSTUDY Pensioner Education Supplement (PES), or
-   Assistance for Isolated Children (AIC) Scheme

A reference to a student assistance overpayment relates to an
overpayment of:

-   AUSTUDY (before 1 July 1998), or
-   Financial Supplement Loan (FSL)
