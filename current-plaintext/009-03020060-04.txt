General

For the definition of an Australian resident, see Social Security Act
1991, section 7(2), Australian residence definitions.

For the requirement to be in Australia to lodge a claim, see Social
Security (Administration) Act 1999, section 29, General rule.

Payment specific

For the qualification provisions for Carer Allowance for a child care
receiver, see Social Security Act 1991, section 953.

For the qualification provisions for Carer Allowance for an adult care
receiver, see Social Security Act 1991, section 954.
