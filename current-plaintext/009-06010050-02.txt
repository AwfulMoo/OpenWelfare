Exceeding respite bank procedure

This image provides a summary of the procedure when a carer exceeded
their temporary cessation of care (respite) and hospitalisation absences
in a calendar year and payment was cancelled. They reclaimed when
eligible (when the care receiver returned to their care) and were
re-granted. Later in the same calendar year, the care receiver enters
respite care again.
