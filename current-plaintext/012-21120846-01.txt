This document outlines information about the recognition for an allied
health professional as appropriately trained in focussed psychological
strategies (FPS).

Focussed psychological strategies (FPS)

FPS are specific mental health care management strategies, derived from
evidence based psychological therapies.

Recognition of additional skills and ongoing training in FPS are
important elements of maintaining high quality standards within the
provision of FPS for assessed mental health conditions.

Allied health professionals trained in FPS qualifies them to use:

-   cognitive behavioural therapy
-   relaxation strategies
-   skills training (including parenting skills)
-   problem solving
-   anger management
-   stress management
-   social skills

Eligibility for Recognition

FPS eligibility can be granted to the following allied health
professionals:

-   Occupational therapist, see Occupational Therapist – provider
    eligibility and registration
-   Psychologist, see Psychologist – provider eligibility and
    registration
-   Social workers, see Social worker- provider eligibility and
    registration

Occupational Therapist

Occupational therapists can undertake additional training in FPS
accreditation with Occupational Therapy Australia (OTA). Occupational
therapists must:

hold current Australian Health Practitioner Regulation Agency (Ahpra)
general or limited registration as an occupational therapist, and

be accredited by OTA as qualified to provide FPS as occupational
therapists:

-   have undertaken to observe the standards set out in the document
    published by the Occupational Therapy Board of Australia Australian
    Occupational Therapists Competency Standards in force on 1 January
    2019.

Psychologist

To be eligible to claim or bill FPS services under Medicare, the
psychologist must have a General registration with Ahpra.

Social Worker

To be eligible to claim or bill FPS services under Medicare, the Social
Worker must be an Accredited Mental Health Social Worker (AMHSW).

Application for Recognition

Occupational Therapist

To be recognised to provide FPS, Services Australia receives an email
notification of endorsement directly from Occupational Therapy
Australia.

Psychologist

No additional specialty code is required for the psychologist to provide
FPS services.

Social Workers

No additional specialty code is required for the AHMSW to provide FPS
services.
