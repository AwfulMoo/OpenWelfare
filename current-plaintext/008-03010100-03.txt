This page contains information about streaming and finalising a new
claim for Disability Support Pension (DSP) or referring for a Job
Capacity Assessment (JCA).

Staff trained in Process Direct, select the Process Direct tab. Staff
not trained, select Customer First.

Process Direct

On this page:

Work item received

Claim for DSP for a Terminal Illness (SA494) and /or Verification of
terminal illness (SA495)

Commence streaming of claim
