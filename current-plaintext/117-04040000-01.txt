This document outlines the archiving and culling of compensation claims
within the Compensation Management System (CMS).

Archived claims

Compensation claims convert to a status of archived:

-   12 months after the claim has been finalised or
-   12 months after the end date of the preclusion period of the claim

Compensation claims convert to a status of culled 3 years after the
claim has been finalised.

The Claim Summary screen in CMS will show if a claim has been:

-   Finalised
-   Archived or
-   Culled.
