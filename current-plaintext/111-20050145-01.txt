This document outlines how to manage suspected Information and
Communications Technology (ICT) issues using the Process Direct Digital
Assistant (Roxy) tool. It also advises what to do when Roxy is
unavailable.

Claim cannot be finalised

If a claim cannot be finalised in Process Direct due to a suspected ICT
issue, Service Officers must adopt the Recognise, Research, Support
process before escalating to ICT.

Add additional information:

-   via the ICT Interaction Viewer, or
-   by clicking on the link in the ICT acknowledgement email once
    received, and entering a comment/attachment

ICT referrals that do not contain enough information will be returned
un-actioned.

The Resources page has links to Process Direct current issues, ICT
Service Desk and Roxy Triage team contact details, and mySupport.
