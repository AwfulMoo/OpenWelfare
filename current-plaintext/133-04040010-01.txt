From 2 July 2018:
- Child Care Subsidy (CCS) replaces Child Care Benefit (CCB) and Child
Care Rebate (CCR)
- Additional Child Care Subsidy (ACCS) replaces Special Child Care
Benefit (SCCB) Child at Risk and Temporary Financial Hardship, Jobs,
Education and Training Child Care Fee Assistance (JETCCFA) and
Grandparent Child Care Benefit (GCCB)
For more information, see Child Care Subsidy (CCS) and Additional Child
Care Subsidy (ACCS).
Claims for CCB and CCR lump sum for approved care and CCB Registered
Care, for care received prior to 2 July 2018, can be lodged up until 30
June 2019.

This document outlines how customers receiving Child Care Benefit (CCB)
for approved care can access their Centrelink online account to view
information about their child care details and payments.

On this Page:

Express Plus Centrelink mobile app

[] Customers registered with an active online account can also access
their CCB information using the Express Plus Centrelink mobile app.

Types of CCB

There are 2 types of CCB:

-   Approved CCB, which means that the child is in the care of an
    approved service provider, such as a child care centre
-   Registered CCB, which means that the child is in the care of a
    registered carer, such as a relative or neighbour

This service only deals with approved CCB.

View Child Care Details and Payments page

This page provides current Approved CCB customers with information
about:

-   Approved CCB percentage
-   Work/Training/Study
-   Jobs, Education and Training Child Care fee assistance (JETCCFA)
    entitlements if applicable
-   Children currently in the customer's care
-   Childs schooling status
-   Child's Customer Reference Number (CRN)
-   Child care attendance
-   Child Care Rebate (CCR) payment results

Payment and Service Finder

The Payment and Service Finder, on the Services Australia website, can
be used by new and current customers, and, Service Officers to estimate
potential CCB and CCR entitlement.
