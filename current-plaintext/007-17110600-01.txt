This document outlines information about and links to immunisation
requirements for Child Care Benefit (CCB) and Family Tax Benefit (FTB).

Affected payments

Immunisation requirements apply to Child Care Benefit (CCB), approved
care, registered care and CCB lump sum claims, Child Care Rebate (CCR)
and Family Tax Benefit (FTB) for children under the age of 20.
