This document outlines information to help customers complete the Claim
for Pension Bonus Bereavement Payment (SA388).

Pension Bonus Bereavement Payment (PBBP)

This is a tax free lump sum payment for the surviving partner of a
Pension Bonus Scheme (PBS) member who died before successfully claiming
Age Pension or Pension Bonus.

Lodging a claim

Lodging a claim for the PBBP involves 3 steps:

-   Complete the form
-   Collect all the documents needed
-   Lodge the claim with support documents as required, for example
    identity confirmation, relevant modules, death certificate
