This document outlines information about the training requirements for
Service Officers who require access to process My Health Record opt-out
questions.

Training to process My Health Record opt-out questions

Training to process My Health Record opt-out questions on Services
Australia forms is not required; however Service Officers are encouraged
to familiarise themselves with Processing My Health Record options –
tips and error reporting.

Service Officers need to ensure they follow the appropriate procedures
when processing My Health Record opt-out questions.
