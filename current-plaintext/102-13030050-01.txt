For Data Quality Unit (DQU) staff only

This document outlines how delete a Child to Parent (CHI/PAR) link and
is for Data Quality Unit (DQU) Quality Assurance (QA) staff only.

Comprehensive Assessment of the record

Data Quality Unit Service Officers have access to delete child to parent
(CHI/PAR) links to resolve multiple records. Before a CHI/PAR link is
deleted, a comprehensive assessment of the record(s) must be undertaken.
For multiple child record(s), DQU staff are to refer to Multiple
Centrelink Child CRN Checks and Cleansing.

Note: this procedure is for multiple records. A separate procedure is
available for Intertwined records.

Child not displayed

After the CHI/PAR link has been deleted, the child will not display in
the parent(s)/guardian(s) record or be included in future
correspondence.

The Resources page contains a link to the Intertwined Referral form.
