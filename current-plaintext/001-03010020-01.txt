This document outlines the age requirements to qualify for JobSeeker
Payment (JSP) or Youth Allowance (YA) (job seeker).

JobSeeker Payment (JSP) age eligibility

To be of eligible age for JSP, a customer must be 22 years and over, and
under Age Pension age. The Resources page contains a link to the
Services Australia website for information on eligibility basics for Age
Pension.

Minimum age for YA (job seeker) eligibility

Minimum age for YA is 16 years (or 15 years if independent and above the
minimum school leaving age for the State/Territory).

Maximum age for YA (job seeker) eligibility

The maximum age to be eligible for YA is 21 years for job seeker
customers or 25 years for full-time students undertaking a course of
education. See also minimum and maximum ages of YA student and
Australian Apprentice.
