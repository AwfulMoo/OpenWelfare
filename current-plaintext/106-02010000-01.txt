This document outlines information about Centrelink's required
timeliness standards. For information about timeliness standards
relating to callbacks and ACTDOCs, see Timeliness standards for
callbacks and ACTDOCs.

Key Performance Indicators

The Department of Social Services (DSS) Partnership Agreement contains
Key Performance Indicators (KPI) and these contain some standards that
Centrelink is to perform to. In order to meet customer expectations and
the KPIs under the Balanced Scorecard, Centrelink has timeliness
standards set for the services it offers.
