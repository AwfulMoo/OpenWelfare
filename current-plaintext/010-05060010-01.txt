This document outlines the age qualification for Pensioner Education
Supplement (PES).

PES age requirements

To be eligible for PES, a student must be of PES age, which means:

-   at least 16 years of age, or
-    reached the minimum school leaving age under the law of their state
    or territory, and
-    be regarded as independent under section 1061PL of the Social
    Security Act 1991

There is no maximum age limit for PES.

To qualify for PES, a student must also meet all of the following:

-    be receiving a payment that attracts PES
-   satisfy the residence requirements
-   be undertaking qualifying study

The Resources page contains a table to assist in determining the PES
qualification date for those customers that may not have reached the age
of 16 years.
