This document outlines information to provide an awareness for Service
Officers about people with disabilities, the issues that they face and
provision of a high level of customer service to them.

Disabilities and medical conditions

Service Officers should be aware of the disabilities and medical
conditions that may impact customers including:

-   Musculoskeletal conditions
-   Mental health conditions
-   Intellectual impairments
-   Human immunodeficiency virus (HIV)/ Acquired immune deficiency
    syndrome (AIDS)
-   Acquired brain impairment
-   Vision and hearing impairments
-   Childhood disabilities
-   Common medical conditions for aged customers
