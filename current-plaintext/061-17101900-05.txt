Portability approval codes

This table contains a list of all portability reasons and the extended
reasons required for some approvals, a portability reason is optional
coding. It is not required for all payment types. Note: the Code NZL was
used to show a customer was intending to remain in New Zealand
long-term. The codes NZL and N50 have been end dated with effect 30 June
2017 and cannot be used after that date.
