This document outlines services, which may be available for people
affected by a major disaster.

This is a Family and Domestic Violence Interaction Point. If the
customer is with another person, on speaker phone, or already identified
with family and domestic violence concerns, continue with the current
business conversation. Otherwise, go to the Family and domestic violence
procedure to conduct the risk identification and referral process.

Available services

The following payments may be available to people experiencing a major
disaster:

-   Australian Government Disaster Recovery Payments (AGDRP)
-   Disaster Recovery Payment for New Zealand Special Category
    (subclass 444) visa holders (NZ DRP) - previously known as ex-gratia
    Disaster Recovery Payment (ex-gratia DRP)
-   Disaster Recovery Allowance (DRA)
-   Disaster Recovery Allowance for New Zealand Special Category
    (subclass 444) visa holders (NZ DRA) - previously known as ex-gratia
    Disaster Allowance (ex-gratia DRA)
-   Disaster Recovery Funding Arrangements (DRFA)
-   Australian Victim of Terrorism Overseas Payment (AVTOP)

The Resources page has links to the Disaster Assist and Family and
Community Services websites.
