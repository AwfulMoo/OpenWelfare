This document outlines extra functions available to staff with enhanced
access to Work Optimiser.

Functionality

WLM Team Leaders can:

-   view, manage and assign work for themselves and for staff who report
    to them
-   access operational information
-   use the information in Work Optimiser to manage staff productivity
    and performance

WLM Analysts can:

-   view, manage and assign work
-   access operational information, run queries and monitor dashboards
-   complete maintenance tasks
-   use the information in Work Optimiser to manage productivity and
    performance at a site or program level

Team Leader and Analyst access

Access to functions in Work Optimiser depends on the security roles
assigned in ESSentials.

The WLM Team Leader and WLM Analyst security roles give leaders access
to more information than staff with base level access.

WLM Team Leader level access needs line manager approval.

The WLM Analyst role needs line manager approval and role owner
approval.

Access to these functions depends on security roles assigned in
ESSentials.
