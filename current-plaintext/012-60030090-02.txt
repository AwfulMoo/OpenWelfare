High level process overview of the PBS HMC for PBS purposes

This image provides a summary of the PBS HMC. There are 2 versions of
the short stay chart and 1 version of the long stay chart, see the
Resources page. Although individual hospital settings may have specified
variations to this flowchart, when using the PBS HMC for PBS purposes
they must comply with PBS prescribing, supply and claiming rules.
Variations must comply with the Instrument of Approval.
