Formula

To calculate a MOB advance debt amount:

Amount of advance divided by 13, then multiplying this figure by the
number of paydays in the advance period.

Where the:

amount of advance means the amount of MOB advance paid to the recipient

number of paydays means the number of whole paydays in the period that:

-   starts on the day that the customer ceased to be qualified for MOB,
    and
-   ends at the end of the customer's advance payment period
