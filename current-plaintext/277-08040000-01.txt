For staff processing International Disbursements only.

This document outlines the process of sending bulk payments
electronically to the Child Support administrations of Sweden and
Slovakia.

Schedules currently prepared

Schedules are created to send bulk payments out of Australia. The
following schedules are currently prepared:

-   WIRE/EFT Transfers
-   Payments Overseas (Cheque payment)
-   Austria
-   Belgium
-   Canada, British Columbia and Ontario
-   Finland
-   Germany
-   Netherlands
-   New Zealand
-   Norway
-   Slovakia
-   Sweden
-   Switzerland

Delegations and authorisations

For more information about delegations and authorisations see Child
Support Services, Delegations and Authorisations.
