Digital Support Products

Online guides and videos show how customers use self service. Customers
can view these on the Services Australia website or YouTube Channel
(https://www.youtube.com/user/HumanServicesGovAU).

Staff can view them at Digital Support Products. Staff can also view
training simulations.

mySupport

Service centre staff can report issues with self service terminals by
submitting the Self Service Terminal (SST) issues webform:

-   Go to mySupport
-   Search using the webform name, key words, error message or
    application name, and select the relevant webform
-   Complete and Submit the form taking note of any receipt or reference
    code provided
