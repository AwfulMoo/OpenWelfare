Age eligibility for Survivor Pension

Survivor Pension age eligibility

Belgium is in a transitional period where the minimum age is increasing
each year by 6 months.

+-----------------------------------+-----------------------------------+
| Spouse died in                    | Minimum age of the surviving      |
|                                   | spouse at the time of death       |
+-----------------------------------+-----------------------------------+
| 2015 and previous years           | 45 years old                      |
+-----------------------------------+-----------------------------------+
| 2016                              | 45 years and 6 months             |
+-----------------------------------+-----------------------------------+
| 2017                              | 46 years                          |
+-----------------------------------+-----------------------------------+
| 2018                              | 46 years and 6 months             |
+-----------------------------------+-----------------------------------+
| 2019                              | 47 years                          |
+-----------------------------------+-----------------------------------+
| 2020                              | 47 years and 6 months             |
+-----------------------------------+-----------------------------------+
| 2021                              | 48 years old                      |
+-----------------------------------+-----------------------------------+
| 2022                              | 48 years and 6 months             |
+-----------------------------------+-----------------------------------+
| 2023                              | 49 years                          |
+-----------------------------------+-----------------------------------+
| 2024                              | 49 years and 6 months             |
+-----------------------------------+-----------------------------------+
| 2025                              | 50 years                          |
+-----------------------------------+-----------------------------------+

A transition allowance may be payable if a spouse does not meet the age
requirements but does meet the other conditions. It is payable for 12
months (without dependent children) or 24 months (with dependent
children).
