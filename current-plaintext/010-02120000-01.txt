This document outlines information about the procedure when an ABSTUDY
customer has a change of circumstances.

ABSTUDY

ABSTUDY helps with costs for Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander
Australians who are studying or undertaking an Australian
Apprenticeship.

Change of circumstances

ABSTUDY customers need to advise Services Australia when they have a
change of circumstances. Notifiable events include, but are not limited
to:

-   Change in personal circumstances
-   Change of address
-   Change of income
-   Failure to lodge tax return
-   Change in care arrangements
-   Change in study load
-   Planning to leave Australia
-   Change of applicant (for Schooling A Award recipients)

ABSTUDY customers are required to advise a change in circumstances
within 14 days of the event occurring. The reassessment of the
customer's eligibility and/or entitlements will occur from the date of
the change in circumstances, even if the customer has not notified
within 14 days.

The Resources page contains examples of changes in circumstances for
ABSTUDY customers.
