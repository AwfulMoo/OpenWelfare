Historical Medicare enrolment control lines

This table lists historical Medicare enrolment control lines. These
control lines should not be used.

  --------------- ---------------------------- ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  Control lines   Topic                        Function
  EASI            Name and address enquiry     Address search screen.
  EFAI            Medicare Safety Net family   Family Safety Net registration screen.
  EFRI            Medicare Safety Net family   Family audit trail screen.
  EFSI            Medicare Safety Net family   Family Safety Net confirmation screen.
  EFPI            PIN enquiry                  Flag PIN screen for the PIN keyed.
  EMGI            PIN merging                  Used to merge two PINs.
  ENSI            Name and address enquiry     Blank name search screen.
