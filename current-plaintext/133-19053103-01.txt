This document explains how to help customers fix problems they are
having with self service.

Helping customers

Service Officers should try to help the customer fix the problem when
they contact. Common problems are easy for the customer to fix with
troubleshooting assistance. Customers can often fix these themselves.

For troubleshooting and escalation processes, see Digital
Troubleshooting or the Digital Support Directory.

Follow escalation process if unable to fix an issue after using all
resources available.

Do not use this process for reporting technical issues with digital
services.

Contact us

The Phone us page on the Services Australia website lists numbers
customers can call for online support.

Customer feedback, suggestions or complaints

Customers can give feedback or make a complaint by:

-   calling the Complaints and feedback line
-   submitting a complaint or feedback on the Services Australia website
    or in their online account
