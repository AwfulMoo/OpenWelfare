This document outlines conversation guides to assist Service Officers to
discuss the estimate process with customers. This includes assisting the
customer to decide if an estimate is the best option, discussing the
estimate processes and provisions, ensuring the estimate is and will
remain accurate, and provides answers to the more difficult questions
that customers may pose.
