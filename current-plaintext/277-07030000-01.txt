This document outlines information about the use of international
incomes in child support assessments, including how to obtain income
information for customers living overseas and how to apply it. Up to
date income for parents is essential to ensure accurate assessments and
compliance.

Roles and responsibilities

Service Officer 2 (SO2):

-   To determine an income where taxable income is not readily
    ascertainable

Service Officer 4 (SO4):

-   To amend an assessment when a taxable income has been amended

The Resources page contains contact details for the International
Disbursements Team, macros and forms.
