This page contains information on how to help a person apply for a loan
under the Home Equity Access Scheme (the Scheme).

-   The self-managed tab explains how to help a person apply for the
    Scheme online
-   The assisted tab explains how to check a person's circumstances to
    ensure a new claim is required, complete an Assisted customer claim
    (ACC) with them, and lodgement of claims and documentation
-   The SA310/SA496 tab describes the information in the SA310/SA496
    paper application form including Parts A - F
