This document provides the processes for a review of decision for the
TFES.

TFES review process

There are 2 levels of review:

-   the initial review of decision is conducted by the Services
    Australia - Tasmanian Transport Programmes (TTP) team
-   if the claimant is not satisfied with the outcome of the initial
    review, a further review can be requested of the policy department,
    the Department of Infrastructure, Regional Development and Cities

Note: a request for a reassessment of a claim (unrelated to re-purposed
goods) is a review of decision request.

Review requirements and timeframes

The timeframe and process for a review of decision are contained in the
TFES Ministerial Directions.

The request for review must be:

-   requested by the claimant or the claimant's claims agent within 30
    days of receiving notification of the original decision
-   completed by the reviewer within 30 days of receipt of the request

The reviewer:

-   cannot be the same agency officer who made the original decision
-   must provide the outcome of the review and the reason for the
    decision to the claimant in writing
