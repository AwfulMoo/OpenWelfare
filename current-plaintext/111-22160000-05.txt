Error codes for Provider Registration quality checking

Table 2: A non-critical error code is to be applied in situations where
the error does not have an impact on the provider’s eligibility to
receive Medicare benefits; a provider’s payments and the error
identified presents no or low risk of harm to the reputation of Services
Australia.

Critical error codes are only to be applied when the error would have in
a critical impact to the provider’s eligibility, ability to claim
Medicare and/or may present a significant risk of harm to the reputation
of Services Australia.
