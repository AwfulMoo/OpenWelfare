This document outlines information on how to complete the Assistance for
Isolated Children (AIC) Application for Short Term Boarding Allowance
(SY068).

When to use this form

The Assistance for Isolated Children (AIC) Application for Short Term
Boarding Allowance (SY068) should be completed for any period of short
term boarding after an initial period of short term boarding.

Points to note in completing the SY068:

-   the person who completed the initial Claim for Assistance for
    Isolated Children (AIC) (SY040) for the first period of short term
    boarding should complete the SY068
-   all relevant questions should be answered
-   the declaration must be signed by the applicant.

Lodging the application

The applicant may lodge the application at any service centre, via the
Express Plus mobile app or by [] uploading documents online, or it can
be posted to the appropriate AIC Smart Centre. Details of how to lodge
the application are shown on the form.

When helping customers complete a claim form, Service Officers must not
make any notations, underline or make comments of any sort on a claim
form that is to be signed by a customer and lodged. Instead, any
notations or explanations to questions on a claim form should be made on
a separate sheet of paper and attached to the customer’s claim form.
