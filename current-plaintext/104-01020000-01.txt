This document outlines information on granting and recording parental
permission to enquire.

Authority

An ABSTUDY or Youth Allowance (YA) customer may authorise their
parent(s) to enquire about their claim and payments. This authority may
be unrestricted or be limited in terms of period and/or types of
information provided to the parent. The customer may provide this
authority in person, by phone or by mail. Details of the authority must
be recorded on a Display on Access DOC.

The Resources page contains links to the Release of Information,
Nominees and Permission to Enquire and Privacy intranet pages and the
relevant form.
