This document outlines the processes used by officers in the
International Central Authority (CA) team to manage the exchange of
information between Child Support and Inland Revenue, New Zealand
(NZIR).

New Zealand case management - Information exchange

Information is exchanged via the NZ secure messaging mailbox and is
recorded in a number of spreadsheets titled:

-   NZ Daily Exchange – request for information from NZIR and responses
    received from NZIR
-   Weekly ACERTS – notification of amended liabilities
-   Weekly Withdrawals Requests from transmitting authority to withdraw
    their case
-   Weekly Tracing Requests pre-transmittal/customer location requests
-   Weekly Transmittals – registration requests
-   Weekly Income requests – income requests
-   Weekly Enforcement Updates – request from transmitting authority for
    updates
-   Weekly General Withdrawal Requests CS to NZ – request to
    transmitting authority to withdraw their case
-   Weekly Potential Returns CS to NZ – request to transmitting
    authority for new information on cases which may be returned
-   Weekly Returns – notification to transmitting authority of returned
    cases
-   Weekly Requests for Amended Certificates – request to transmitting
    authority for an Amended Certificate

All spreadsheets are saved in a specific folder relevant to the process
in Hobart’s shared drive folder titled Int - NZ.
