Home Equity Access Scheme National

Delegate discretion in unique circumstances

For delegate discretion in unusual or special circumstances, Complex
Assessment Officers need to email the Older Australians Business
Processing team with details of the unique circumstances. The Older
Australians Business Processing team may refer these to the Real Estate,
Home Equity and Veteran Services Team prior to arranging repayment of a
loan.

Guide to Social Security Law, 3.4.5.90, Repayment of a HEAS loan - for
examples of when the delegate may exercise discretion when arranging
repayment of a Scheme loan.
