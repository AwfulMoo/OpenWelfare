This page contains information about eligibility for SPaR Income
Management.

Flowchart

Eligibility for supporting People at Risk (SPaR) Income Management is
determined by the Services Australia Income Management Contact Officer
(IMCO) when a customer, or their partner, is referred to the agency for
Income Management by a SPaR approved referring authority as suitable for
Income Management.
