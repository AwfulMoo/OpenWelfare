This page contains information on the procedure to request deletions of
digital images from Centrelink customer records. A deletion might be
required when documents (for example, letters or faxes) have been
scanned or faxed in error, or inappropriate documents have been uploaded
online and require deletion.

Deleting scanned, faxed or uploaded images
