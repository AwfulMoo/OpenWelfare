Approved Course status

All State/Territory government high schools are approved institutions
for Centrelink purposes, as are most, but not all, non-government high
schools. Approved Course status can be confirmed via the listing of
Approved courses of study for Youth Allowance (YA), Austudy and
Pensioner Education Supplement (PES).
