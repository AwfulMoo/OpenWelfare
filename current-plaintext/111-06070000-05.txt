ICT Service Desk referrals

If unable to access the Customer First Training Environment application,
complete and submit the webform, Customer First - Unable to access:

-   Go to mySupport
-   Search using webform title or keywords, select Support Catalogue and
    then select the relevant webform
-   When completing the webform, select Yes to Is this related to access
    to the Customer First Training Environment?
-   Complete and Submit the webform taking note of any receipt or
    reference code produced
