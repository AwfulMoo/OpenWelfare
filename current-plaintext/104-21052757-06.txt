Services Australia trains all service delivery staff to manage customer
aggression.

Some of the skills include:

-   understanding the relationship between customer service and
    preventing customer aggression
-   recognising the signs of escalating behaviour
-   using communication and negotiation techniques to defuse and resolve
    conflict situations
-   recognising when and how to disengage from a customer
-   techniques to reduce risk
-   reporting, recording, and escalating incidents

See the Managing Aggressive Behaviour page for information about
training resources for staff and Managers.
