Social Security Guide, 1.1.C.250, Compensation affected payment

For information about the requirement to pursue compensation, reasonable
action to obtain compensation, power to obtain information, and the
effect of periodic and lump sum compensation payments, see Social
Security Guide, 3.1.9, Compensation Provisions.

For information about transferring from a non-compensation affected
payment to a compensation affected payment, see Social Security Guide,
4.13.1, Overview of Compensation.

For information about the types of assessable income from compensation,
see Social Security Guide, 4.13, Compensation.

For information about recovery of compensation affected payments
following a compensation payout to a customer, see Social Security
Guide, 6.4, Compensation Recovery.
