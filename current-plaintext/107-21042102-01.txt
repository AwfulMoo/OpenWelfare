This page outlines information on the dunning process.

For Medicare Debt Management Officers (DMO) only

Dunning

Dunning is:

-   the automatic process of issuing a series of overdue account
    reminder notices
-   used when a customer's combined total of overdue accounts is equal
    to or greater than $100

When the combined overdue account balance is less than $100, Medicare
Debt Recovery staff manually issue an open item account statement using
SAP Finance.
