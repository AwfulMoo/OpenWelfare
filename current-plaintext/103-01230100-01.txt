This document outlines how to investigate a customer's BasicsCard
enquiry and the escalation process if the enquiry cannot be resolved at
first contact.

Customer's BasicsCard queries

There could be a number of issues the customer wants a resolution on,
including:

transactions that have occurred after their BasicsCard has been lost

balance on the BasicsCard

transactions they believe they are not responsible for

merchant issues, including:

-   transaction fees
-   minimum purchase amount
-   merchant holding card
-   unable to purchase goods

suspicion that the BasicsCard has been used fraudulently

a BasicsCard that has been incorrectly issued to a third party as a
result of Services Australia error
