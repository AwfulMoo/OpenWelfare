This document outlines the role of Services Australia in Supporting
People at Risk (SPaR) Income Management.

On this Page:

Income Management Contact Officer (IMCO)

The Income Management Contact Officer (IMCO) is responsible for liaising
with an approved referring authority about customers referred to
Services Australia who might benefit from Income Management.

Services Australia’s role

-   Action any requests from an approved referring authority received
    through the Unified Government Gateway (UGG)
-   Be the single point of contact to receive any referrals for Income
    Management (IM) sent by approved referring authorities
-   Once a customer has been identified, determine if the customer meets
    all eligibility criteria for SPaR Income Management
-   Contact eligible customers to book the Income Management Initial
    Assessment interview
-   Within the interview, explain the purpose of IM, including how to
    access income managed funds, and the process for disbursement of
    income managed funds
-   Manage the customer's IM funds to assist them in meeting their and
    their family's priority needs
-   Contact the approved referring authority 35 days prior to the end of
    the IM period to discuss whether IM should be extended or ceased
-   Undertake further reviews/contact during the IM period as
    appropriate
-   Facilitate the flow of information between Services Australia and
    the approved referring authorities in relation to the referred
    customer, for example, change in address or changes in payment
