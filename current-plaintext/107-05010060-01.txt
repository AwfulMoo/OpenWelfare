This document outlines what information can be disclosed when contacted
by a tax agent, who is acting on a customer's behalf.

Tax agent contact

Tax agents may contact Services Australia on behalf of a customer to:

-   query debts
-   determine if the agency will access tax refunds

Seek to discuss with customer

Staff must attempt to discuss debt information directly with the
customer. The agency must obtain written or verbal permission from the
customer before releasing any information.

The Resources page contains a link to the Office of the Australian
Information Commissioner's website, the Privacy and Information Access
page and a link to the Authorising a person or organisation to enquire
or act on your behalf (SS313) form.
