This page contains processing information about medical practitioners
affected by the legislation amendments in 2010 to section 19AB of the
Health Insurance Act 1973. That is, an Australian or New Zealand
permanent resident, or New Zealand citizen who completed their primary
medical degree at an accredited medical school in Australia or New
Zealand.

On this page:

Assess an initial or subsequent provider number application

Process initial stem

Assess application to determine level of access to Medicare benefits

Create an initial or subsequent provider number application
