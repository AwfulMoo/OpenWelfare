This document outlines topics that include answering, dealing with and
finalising calls in Centrelink.

Procedures and protocols

The procedures in these topics present the protocols, or procedural
rules, that are expected to be followed in telephone communication
situations, including the processes to be implemented for a business
disruption event or emergency situation.

These protocols are to be used by all staff.
