Use the above document as a guide only. Use tools such as Office Locator
and the relevant Operational Blueprint procedure for business processes.

Only specialist staff should use the specialist service reasons. To book
a specialist appointment, refer to the relevant Operational Blueprint
procedure and select the appropriate Appointment Type.

Some appointment types have different actions for staff and customers.
Staff complete actions through Customer First using the Centrelink
Appointment System. Customers complete actions using their Centrelink
online account through myGov or the Express Plus Centrelink mobile app.
