This document outlines how to select and display information for
customer debts in DMIS.

System protocols

All debts regardless of status are viewable.

Multiple debts display in the following order:

-   Undetermined and Assurance of Support (AoS) protected - listed from
    the earliest to the latest date registered
-   Determined - listed from the earliest to the latest date raised
-   Finalised - listed from the earliest to the latest date finalised

DMIS and Process Direct

Debts managed in Process Direct (such as Child Care Subsidy) can be
viewed in DMIS. Action updates in Process Direct. For more details see:

-   Raising Child Care Subsidy (CCS) debts in Process Direct
-   Recovering Child Care Subsidy (CCS) debts in Process Direct
