This document outlines how the AIC Marginal Distance Confirmation
(SY069) is assessed by AIC Smart Centre staff.

Establishing entitlement

This form is completed by an appropriate authority to establish an
applicant's entitlement to AIC when:

-   the distance or travel time of the journey from the student's
    principal family home to the nearest state school is close to the
    qualifying limits for AIC, or
-   it is not clear that an applicant's circumstances satisfy one of the
    geographical isolation rules

Completion of SY069

The SY069 should be issued to the applicant for completion by one of the
following authorities:

-   local bus company proprietor
-   local shire clerk engineer
-   local/state/national roads, water, or land management authority

It could be issued as part of the claim process or to review entitlement
after AIC has been rejected.

If the form is lodged at a service centre, check it is complete and
forward to the appropriate AIC Smart Centre.
