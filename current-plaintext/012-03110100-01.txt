This document outlines the policy and procedure to deactivate a
Healthcare Provider Identifier - Organisation (HPI-O) organisation in
the Healthcare Identifiers (HI) Service.

Deactivate HPI-O

A seed or network organisation can withdraw their request to participate
in the HI Service. To deactivate, the organisation must have a status of
Active.

Before a Service Officer can complete this procedure, the following must
be provided:

completed Application to deactivate, reactivate or retire a Healthcare
Organisation record form

the form must be signed by either the:

-   Responsible Officer (RO) of the seed organisation to be deactivated,
    or
-   Organisation Maintenance Officer (OMO) of the network organisation
    to be deactivated

When deactivation is complete:

-   the organisation is end-dated with the current date and time stamp
-   all network organisations under the deactivated organisation’s
    hierarchy are automatically deactivated
-   any Healthcare Provider Identifier – Individual (HPI-I), RO or OMO
    records remain linked with an open end-date
-   a Service Officer can search and view deactivated organisations, and
-   deactivated records do not display in the Healthcare Provider
    Directory (HI HPD)
