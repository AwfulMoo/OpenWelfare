This document outlines information about income rules applied by Cuba
based on the circumstances of the case. Using the most up to date income
for customers ensures accurate assessments and compliance.

Better income identified

When an income is added, either automatically or manually to the Client
Income window, Cuba will determine the best income for the child support
period. The assessment will update automatically and send the
appropriate letters. See Notify Child Support customers of income
changes.

Income issues in Cuba

For current Cuba issues, approved workarounds and timeframe for
resolution of issues relating to Cuba, see the Network News Update
(NNU).
