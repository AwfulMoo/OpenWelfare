Geospatial tool - 16-22 November 2021 activation and onwards

To assess if a customer lives or works in a relevant area:

Open the Northern Territory Live Feed App

Place the address given by the customer in the search box. Residential
and employer addresses may need to be checked

Select the correct address that populates in the drop down

In the Layers menu on the right hand side, for:

-   Relevant periods 1 and 2 for Katherine and Robinson River and
    homelands, select COVID-19 Disaster Payment – NT – 16-24 November
    2021
-   Relevant period 3 and onwards for Katherine, select COVID-19
    Disaster Payment – NT – 25 November 2021 and onwards

An address must be in the blue shaded area for the customer to be
eligible

Continue to assess the claim

Geospatial tool - 5-7 November 2021 activation

To assess if a customer lives or works in a relevant area:

-   Open the Northern Territory Live Feed App
-   Place the address given by the customer in the search box.
    Residential and employer addresses may need to be checked
-   Select the correct address that populates in the drop down
-   Select COVID-19 Disaster Payment – NT – 5-7 November 2021 in the
    Layers menu on the right hand side
-   An address must be in the blue shaded area for the customer to be
    eligible
-   Continue to assess the claim

Geospatial tool - August 2021 activation (closed)

To assess if a customer lives, works or was present in a relevant area:

Open the Northern Territory Live Feed App

Place the address given by the customer in the search box. Residential
and employer addresses may need to be checked

Select the correct address that populates in the drop down

Select COVID-19 Disaster Payment NT – August 2021 in the Layers menu on
the right

For relevant areas in:

-   Greater Darwin the address must lie in the blue shaded area to be
    eligible to receive a payment
-   Katherine the address must lie in the blue shaded area to be
    eligible to receive a payment

Continue to assess the claim
