Disallowable instruments - Department of Education, Employment and Workplace Relations (DEEWR) and Department of Families, Housing, Community Service and Indigenous Affairs (FaHCSIA)

The disallowable instrument specifies the class of debts that may be
waived. This enables the Secretary to waive the Commonwealth's right to
recover debts incorrectly raised against persons adversely affected by
the specified natural disasters of 2009, 2010 and 2011.
