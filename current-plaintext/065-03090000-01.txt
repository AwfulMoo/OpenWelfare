This document outlines information about customers not eligible for Age
Pension due to residence or age requirements who may be eligible for
other income support payments. Customers should be advised of the
payments they may qualify for and then helped to select the most
appropriate for their specific needs. Customers will need to meet the
eligibility criteria to receive the suggested payments or products.
