This document outlines how to complete the activity on a customer's
record. These activities must be completed to ensure customers are
advised of their Family Tax Benefit (FTB) assessment.

Manual letter activity

A manual letter activity is created as a result of an action that has
been taken on a customer's record and an automatic letter failed to
generate or was not available.

These activities can be monitored and processed through Work Optimiser.
By 'S'electing the activity, a Service Officer can complete an
appropriate online advice to be sent to the customer.
