This document outlines information for the Income Management Contact
Officer (IMCO) on the process for arranging a review with the Child
Protection Authority case worker. It also advises when to notify the
Child Protection Authority case worker if a Child Protection Income
Management customer fails to attend any scheduled appointments.

Case Worker notification

The Income Management Contact Officer (IMCO) will notify the case worker
in the following situations:

-   customer did not attend an appointment
-   arranging the mid-point review and pre-exit review
