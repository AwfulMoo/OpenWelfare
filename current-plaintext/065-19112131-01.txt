Aged Care Staff-Assist Portal

The Aged Care Staff-Assist Portal (ACSAP) allows Service Officers a view
only access to the Aged Care Provider Portal (ACPP). The ACSAP assists
Service Officers to replicate scenarios in the ACPP and resolve some
service provider queries. Service Officers do not have the ability to
submit, update or delete events.

To access the ACSAP, Service Officers must have the relevant security
role in ESSentials and access to the relevant Tivoli Access Manager
(TAM) groups.

The Process page describes how Service Officers can request access to
the ACSAP.
