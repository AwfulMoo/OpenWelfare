This document outlines functions and processes used by Medicare Debt
Management Officers on a daily basis.

For Medicare Debt Management Officers (DMO) only

General administration tasks

Medicare Debt Recovery undertake a range of administrative tasks each
day, including:

-   maintaining a group mailbox
-   registering and distributing mail (traditional post)
-   scanning customer documents, and
-   inbound and outbound telephone work

The Process page contains the steps to complete each task.

The Resources page contains Auxiliary codes, Medicare mainframe search
commands, contact details ,payment options, intranet links and search
commands.
