Agreements and arrangements

The agency delivers payments and services on behalf of other government
agencies. A range of bilateral agreements and Bilateral Management
Arrangements (BMA) governs our relationships with those agencies.

For more information, contact the Department of Social Services (DSS)
Relationship Team.
