This document outlines how customers can view their Work Bonus balance
history using their Centrelink online account.

Work Bonus option

Eligible customers can view their Work Bonus balance and history by
selecting:

-   the Report employment income option from the Welcome page, or
-   Manage payments > My payments from the menu options

The Resources page contains a link to the Services Australia website and
a screenshot of the online Work Bonus Balance screen.
