This document outlines information relating to customers who return to
Australia after a period of residence in another country and who are
granted (or transfer from assessment under an international agreement)
an affected pension payment under domestic qualification rules. These
customers are affected by the former resident provisions if they leave
Australia within a certain period, which is currently two years.

This procedure is no longer current.
It has been replaced by Former resident provisions 061-01060010.
