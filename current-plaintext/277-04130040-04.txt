Child Support (Registration and Collection) Act 1988, Part VA -
Departure prohibition orders

Delegations and authorisations

DPO Contact Officer (EL1 only)

-   issue DAC out of office hours where child support debt/overpayment
    is under $50,000

Specified EL2 Delegate, Intensive Collection Services

-   issue DPO, issue a Departure Authorisation Certificate (DAC) and
    revoke DPO where child support debt/overpayment (not including
    penalties) is under $50,000

National Manager, Intensive Collection Services

-   issue DPO, issue a DAC and revoke DPO where child support
    debt/overpayment (not including penalties) is $50,000 and above

For more information about authorisations and delegations, see Child
Support Services, Delegations and Authorisations.
