Urgent payments

Urgent payment is the early delivery of part of a customer's
entitlement. When a customer receives an urgent payment, their next
regular payment will be lower than usual.

Urgent payments and Crisis Payments are 2 different payments with their
own eligibility rules.

Urgent payments can be issued for the following reasons:

-   exceptional and unforeseen or extraordinary circumstances
-   Centrelink delay (including holiday processing)
-   expenses associated with a funeral
