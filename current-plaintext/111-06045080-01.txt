This procedure outlines how to action reviews for payments that are
assessed using Customer First SAP native technology - the term used
globally relating to software designed specifically for the platform on
which it is running (SAP HANA).

Review actions

In Customer First SAP native, there are 3 main review actions:

-   selecting a review on the customer's record
-   changing the date for a review
-   completing the review

See the relevant procedure for payment specific information about
reviews, including selection criteria, frequency, the assessment process
and how to conduct the review.
