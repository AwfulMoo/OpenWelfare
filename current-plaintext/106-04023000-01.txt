This document outlines specific information about the Agreement
including the process of making a claim for Australian payment under the
agreement and coding of Dutch payments.

Commencement date

The Agreement with the Netherlands started on 1 April 1992 and was fully
revised on 1 April 2003.

Claiming and benefits covered

The Agreement generally allows customers to lodge a claim for payment
from either country. It also allows customers to add together periods of
residence in Australia and periods of social security coverage in the
Netherlands, to meet the minimum requirements for payment.

Australian payments covered:

-   Age Pension
-   Disability Support Pension for the severely disabled

Note: existing Parenting Payment Single and Double Orphan Pension
customers may use the Agreement for portability purposes only.

Customers can claim for Australian payments under International
Agreement by contacting Services Australia or the Dutch authorities.

Dutch payments covered:

-   Old Age Pension
-   Disability Pension
-   Survivors Benefits
-   Child Benefits
-   Sickness Benefits

Customers can claim a foreign pension by contacting Services Australia
or the Dutch authorities.
