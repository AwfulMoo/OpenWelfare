Changing a CDC waiting period

Table 2: this table provides the required information for a staff to
change a customer’s initial waiting period and commence CDC earlier.

If any issues occur please contact the TPS or Access Line. If TPS or
Access are unable to resolve the issue, TPS or Access are to email the
CDC Transition mailbox.
