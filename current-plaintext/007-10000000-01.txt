This document outlines assistance and services for families in addition
to the main family payments and assistance. This can include programs
and extra payments to target disadvantaged families.

Government intent

The government supports families and children through the provision of
programs, services and payments.

Payments

Payments under the Assistance for Isolated Children (AIC) Scheme provide
help with the cost of schooling. Carer Allowance (CA) is also available
to carers of a child with a disability.

Participation

Assistance is available to help parents return to work, overcome
prevocational barriers to employment and increase their level of
participation in the community. Young parents are assisted to complete
their education.

Health of children

Australian Immunisation Register (AIR) is a national register that
records vaccinations given to people of all ages living in Australia.
